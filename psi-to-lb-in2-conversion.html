The diameter of a circle is 15 inches. What is the area of the circle?

A. 94.2 in2

B. 176.625 in2

C. 47.1 in2

D. 706.5 in2

[tex]A = \pi {r}^{2} \\ A = (3.14) {(7.5 \: in)}^{2} \\ A = (3.14)(56.25 \: {in}^{2} ) \\ A = 176.625 \: {in}^{2} [/tex]

26. a pressure of 1atm is equal to 1033g/cm3. Express this pressure in Lb./in2​


Answer:

1033 g/cm^2 = 14.66 lb/in^2

Explanation:

Do you mean 1033 g/cm^2? Pressure is equal to force/area so the unit of pressure should be in g/cm^2. The unit g/cm^3 is used for density.

Conversion factors:

1 in = 2.54 cm

1 lb = 0.4545 kg = 454.5 g

Solution:

Multiply 1033 g/cm^2 into conversion factors that would yield the needed units. Arrange it in fraction form and in such a way that the original units will be cancelled out, and the desired unit will remain.

[tex] = \frac{1033 \: g}{ {cm}^{2} } \times \frac{ {(2.54 \: cm)}^{2} }{{(1 \: in)}^{2}} \times \frac{1 \: lb}{454.5 \: g} [/tex]

Simplifying:

= 14.66 lb/in^2
