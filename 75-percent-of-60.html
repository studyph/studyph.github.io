17. Let's do this!Activity = 2Find the percentage and rate of the following.1.) 60% of 50-2.) 12 %% of 60-3.) 100% of 75 -4.) What percent of 8 is 25.) 40 is what percent of 120​


PERCENTAGE / RATE / BASE==============================

Formula for the Percentage:

[tex]\implies \sf P = R \times B[/tex]

Formula for the Rate:

[tex] \implies \sf R = \frac P B \\ [/tex]

==============================

Find the percentage and rate of the following.

1.) 60% of 50

[tex] \implies \sf60\% = 0.6[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf P = 0.6 \times 50[/tex]

[tex]\implies \orange{ \sf P = 30}[/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

2.) 12% of 60

[tex]\implies \sf12\% = 0.12[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf P = 0.12 \times 60[/tex]

[tex]\implies \orange{ \sf P = 7.2}[/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

3.) 100% of 75

[tex] \implies \sf100\% = 1[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf P = 1 \times 75[/tex]

[tex]\implies \orange{ \sf P = 75}[/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

4.) What percent of 8 is 2

[tex]\implies \sf R = \frac{2}{8} \\ [/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf R = \frac{1}{4} \\ [/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf \orange{ R = 25\% } [/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

5.) 40 is what percent of 120

[tex]\implies \sf R = \frac{40}{120} \\ [/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf R = \frac{1}{3} \\ [/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf \orange{ R = 33.33\% } [/tex]

==============================
