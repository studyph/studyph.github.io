20. if 5 teachers are assigned to take care of 120 preschoolers,how many teachers are needed to take care of 192 preschoolers?​


Answer:

To find out how many teachers are needed to take care of 192 preschoolers, we can use a proportion:

5 teachers / 120 preschoolers = x teachers / 192 preschoolers

We can solve for x by cross-multiplying:

120x = 5 * 192

x = 5 * 192 / 120

x = 8

Therefore, 8 teachers are needed to take care of 192 preschoolers.
