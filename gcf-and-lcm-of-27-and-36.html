GCF of 27 and 36 is 9.

GCF of 27 and 36 by Prime Factorization

Prime factorization of 27 and 36 is (3 × 3 × 3) and (2 × 2 × 3 × 3) respectively. As visible, 27 and 36 have common prime factors. Hence, the GCF of 27 and 36 is 3 × 3 = 9.

LCM of 27 and 36 is 108

LCM of 27 and 36 by Division Method

To calculate the LCM of 27 and 36 by the division method, we will divide the numbers(27, 36) by their prime factors (preferably common). The product of these divisors gives the LCM of 27 and 36.

Step 1: Find the smallest prime number that is a factor of at least one of the numbers, 27 and 36. Write this prime number(2) on the left of the given numbers(27 and 36), separated as per the ladder arrangement.

Step 2: If any of the given numbers (27, 36) is a multiple of 2, divide it by 2 and write the quotient below it. Bring down any number that is not divisible by the prime number.

Step 3: Continue the steps until only 1s are left in the last row.

The LCM of 27 and 36 is the product of all prime numbers on the left, i.e. LCM(27, 36) by division method = 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 3 = 108.
