10. what means level of discretion ?​


Answer:

The term "level of discretion" refers to the amount of freedom or flexibility that an individual or organization has to make decisions and take actions without being restricted by external factors such as rules, regulations, or guidelines. In other words, the level of discretion represents the extent to which someone is allowed to use their own judgment and make choices that may affect the outcome of a situation. A high level of discretion means that there is more room for decision-making based on personal judgment, while a low level of discretion means that decisions are more limited by external factors. The level of discretion may vary depending on the context, such as in the workplace, in legal proceedings, or in public policy.
