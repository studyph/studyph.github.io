17. 5. As a speaker, I should know my purpose and method of delivery before giving my speech so...​


Importance of knowing the purpose and method of delivering a speech

Answer:

As a speaker, I should know my purpose and method of delivery before giving my speech so that I could be able to identify the best way in which I could share my thoughts. For example, when delivering a speech to a group of students or graduates, it is important to motivate them to move forward and to inspire them to choose a career or degree in which they would be happy.

As a speaker, I should know my purpose and method of delivery before giving my speech so​ that I don't embarrass myself.
