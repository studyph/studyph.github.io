12. a coffee vending machine with 3 flavors has a capacity of 15 3/4 liters 1. if a cup contains 1/8 of a liter, how many cups of coffee can the machine dispense? 2.if each flavor has equal number of liters, how many liters will each flavor have?


Answer:

1. divide the total capacity by the size of the cup:

15 3/4 ÷ 1/8

= 15 3/4 x 1/8

= 126 cups

2. divide the total capacity by the number of flavors (3):

15 3/4 ÷ 3

= 15 3/4 x (1/3)

= 21/4 or 5.25 liters
