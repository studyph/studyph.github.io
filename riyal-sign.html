3. (c) Write an equation that converts Saudi Riyal (SAR) into peso.​


The equation to convert SAR to peso (PHP) is SAR * PHP exchange rate. The exchange rate represents the value of one currency in terms of another currency, and it changes constantly due to supply and demand and other economic factors. So the equation would look like this:

PHP = SAR * Exchange Rate

For example, if the exchange rate is 0.19 PHP/SAR, and you have 100 SAR, the equivalent amount in PHP would be:

PHP = 100 SAR * 0.19 PHP/SAR = 19 PHP
