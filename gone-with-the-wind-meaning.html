23. what is ever gone skinny dipping means?​

This idiom's etymology

In 1947, the phrase "skinny dipping" was first observed in use. Swimming naked was considered the norm and was performed worldwide up until this point. Due to developments in materials that could dry quickly after being wet, the practice of swimming naked was no longer thought to be required in 1947. The term "skinny dipping" was created in order to distinguish between the new and traditional ways of swimming. Skinny dipping, the practice of swimming by only dipping your skin in the water, was formerly popular. Nowadays, "skinny dipping" is frequently seen as criminal in several nations unless done in specific places.
