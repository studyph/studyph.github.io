8. There are 128 students taking Biology (A). There are 100 students taking Spanish (B). There are 80 students taking Art (C). There are 30 students taking Biology and Spanish. There are 40 students taking Spanish and Art. There are 28 students taking Biology and Art. There are 10 students taking all three subjects. a. How many students are taking Biology and Spanish, but not Art? b. How many students are taking Biology and Art, but not Spanish? c. How many students are taking Art, but not Biology or Spanish? d. How many students are taking at least one of the three courses? ​


Answer:

Let A be the set of students taking Biology, B for the set of students taking Spanish, and C for the set of students taking Arts respectively.

From the given, we have

n(A)= 128, n(B)= 100, n(C) = 80

n(A ∩B ) = 30, n(A∩C) = 28, n(B∩C) = 40, n(A∩ B ∩ C)= 10

a. How many students are taking Biology and Spanish, but not Art?

n(A ∩B ) - ,n(A∩ B ∩ C)

30 - 10 = 20

b. How many students are taking Biology and Art, but not Spanish?

n(A∩C) - n(A∩ B ∩ C)

28 – 10 = 18

c. How many students are taking Art, but not Biology or Spanish?

No. of students who had taken only Arts

n(C) = n(C) [n(A∩C) + n(B∩C) - n(A∩ B ∩ C)]

= 80 – [28 + 40 – 10]

= 22

d. How many students are taking at least one of the three courses?

From the basic stuff, we have

No. of students who had taken only Biology

n(A) = n(A) – [n(A∩C) + n(A∩B) - n(A∩ B ∩ C)]

= 128 – [ 28 + 30 – 10]

= 88

No. of students who had taken only Spanish

n(B) = n(B) – [n(A∩B) + n(B∩C) - n(A∩ B ∩ C)]

= 100 – [ 30 + 40 – 10]

= 40

No. of students who had taken only Arts

n(C) = n(C) [n(A∩C) + n(B∩C) - n(A∩ B ∩ C)]

= 80 – [28 + 40 – 10]

= 22

Total no. of students who had taken at least one of the three course

= 88 + 40 + 22

= 150

22. What is biology?What is the meaning of biology?Whose invented the biology? ​


What is biology?

Biology is the natural science that studies life and living organisms, including their physical structure, chemical processes, molecular interactions, physiological mechanisms, cognition, and even evolution.

What is the meaning of biology?

The concept that drives this field. Biology examines the cell as the basic unit of life, the gene as the basic unit of inheritance, and evolution as the engine that drives the reproduction and extinction of species. Living organisms are open systems that survive the transfer of energy and the reduction of their local entropy to maintain the slow and important condition called "homeostasis".

Whose invented the biology?

Aristotle was a Greek philosopher and polymath during the Classical period in Ancient Greece. Taught by Plato, he was the founder of the Lyceum, the Peripatetic school of philosophy, and the Aristotelian tradition and he called it History of Biology.
