Question:

which of the following statement about metallic property is true?

Choices:

a. the metallic property decrease from top to bottom of a group

b. metallic property increase across a period

c. metallic property increase decrease the metallic property decreases across a period

Answer:

c. metallic property increase decrease the metallic property decreases across a period

Explanation:

Metallic character decrease as you move across a period in the periodic table from left to right. This occurs as atoms more readily accept electrons to fill a valence shell than lose them to remove the unfilled shell. Metallic character increases as you move down an element group in the periodic table
