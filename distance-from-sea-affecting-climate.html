13. 1.Describe Climate & Weather.2. How does... a. Latitude affect climate? b. Altitude affect climate? c. Distance from sea affect climate? d. Wind system affect climate e. El Niño & La Niña affect climate? 3. How will you prevent / lessen the effect of Climate Change in your own way?​


1.) Describe Climate & Weather.

Climate refers to the long-term patterns and trends in temperature, precipitation, and other weather factors in a specific region, while weather refers to the short-term atmospheric conditions in a particular area at a particular time.

2.) How does...

a. Latitude affect climate?

Latitude affects climate through its impact on the amount and angle of sunlight received. Areas near the equator receive more direct sunlight and thus have warmer temperatures, while areas nearer the poles receive less sunlight and have cooler temperatures.

b. Altitude affect climate?

Altitude affects climate through its impact on temperature. As altitude increases, temperature decreases due to thinner air and lower atmospheric pressure.

c. Distance from sea affect climate?

Distance from the sea affects climate by moderating temperature changes. Areas near the sea have more moderate temperatures, while areas farther inland have more extreme temperature changes.

d. Wind system affect climate?

Wind systems affect climate through their impact on temperature and precipitation patterns. For example, prevailing winds can bring moisture from oceans, leading to higher precipitation in some areas.

e. El Niño & La Niña affect climate?

El Niño and La Niña affect climate by altering ocean temperatures and currents, which can impact weather patterns and precipitation levels around the world.

3.) How will you prevent / lessen the effect of Climate Change in your own way?

To prevent or lessen the effects of climate change in my own way, I can make changes to reduce my carbon footprint, such as using public transportation, consuming less meat, reducing energy use, and properly disposing of waste. I can also participate in local environmental initiatives and advocate for policies that prioritize sustainability and environmental protection.
