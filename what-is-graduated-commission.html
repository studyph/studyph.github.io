P2,500.00

Solution:

1. Commission on 1st 5K:

P5000 x 5% = P250

2. Commission on next 15K:

P15000 x 10% = P1500

3. Commission in excess of 20K:

P5000 x 15% = P750

4. Total commission (add answers on 1-3 above):

P250 + P1500 + P750 = P2,500
