18. what is the sum of all even integers between 15 and 85 solution plss​


✏️ARITHMETIC SERIES==============================

Problem: What is the sum of all even integers between 15 and 85?

Solution: Identify all the given variables that can be substituted to the formula to find the sum.

• The first term [tex] (a_1) [/tex] will be 16 since it is the first even number that is between 15 and 85, same as 84 being the last even term [tex] (a_n) [/tex].

[tex] a_1 = 16 [/tex]

[tex] a_n = 84 [/tex]

• The common difference [tex] (d) [/tex] is 2 since it is the sequence of consecutive even or odd integers.

[tex] d = 2 [/tex]

• The number of terms [tex] (n) [/tex] is not given which is needed to the arithmetic series formula. Solve it using this formula:

[tex] \begin{align} & \bold{Formula:} \\ & \boxed{d = \frac{a_n - a_k}{n - k}} \end{align} [/tex]

[tex] 2 = \frac{a_n - a_1}{n - 1} \\ [/tex]

[tex] 2 = \frac{84 - 16}{n - 1} \\ [/tex]

[tex] 2 = \frac{68}{n - 1} \\ [/tex]

[tex] 2n - 2 = 68 [/tex]

[tex] 2n = 68 + 2 [/tex]

[tex] 2n = 70 [/tex]

[tex] \frac{\cancel2n}{\cancel2} = \frac{70}{2} \\ [/tex]

[tex] n = 35 [/tex]

• Thus, there are 35 terms in the sequence. Find the sum of terms using the arithmetic series formula.

[tex] \begin{align} & \bold{Formula:} \\ & \boxed{S_n = \small \frac{n}{2} \normalsize ( a_1 + a_n )} \end{align} [/tex]

[tex] S_{35} = \small \frac{35}{2} \normalsize ( 16 + 84 ) \\ [/tex]

[tex] S_{35} = \small \frac{35}{2} \normalsize ( 100 ) \\ [/tex]

[tex] S_{35} = 35 ( 50 ) [/tex]

[tex] S_{35} = 1,\!750 [/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] The sum of all even integers between 15 and 85 is...

[tex] \Large \underline{\boxed{\tt \purple{1,\!750}}} [/tex]==============================
