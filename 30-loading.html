29. calculate the work done by a force of 30 n in lifting a load of 2kg to a height of 10m(g=10ms-2)


#BrainlyFast

Problem:

calculate the work done by a force of 30 n in lifting a load of 2kg to a height of 10m(g=10ms-2)

Note:

The gravitational acceleration in the given problem is 10 m/s².

Given Data:

m ( mass ) = 2 kg

h ( Height ) = 10 m

a (gravitational acceleration ) = 10 m/s²

W ( Work ) = ? unknown

Solution:

The formula for the Potential Energy is ( W = m × g × h ).

W = m × g × h

      = 2 kg × 10 m/s² × 10 m

      = 200 kg·m²/s²

      = 200 J

Answer:

The work done is 200 kg·m²/s² or  200 J
