How many square decimeter is the cube A. 61.5 dm² B. 63.375 dm² C. 64.5dm² D. 65.5 dm² 2. A 5 by 9 by 3.5-inch block of wood will be painted on all faces. How many square inches surface will be painted? A. 183 square inches B. 185 square inches C. 187 square inches D. 188 square inches cone with the radius of 2 cm and D. 70.24 cm² C. 60.24 cm² 4. Jasper is wrapping a present. The box he is using is rectangular prism With the length of 18 inches, a width of 10 inches and the height of 4 inches. Find how many square inches of paper he needs to wrap the entire box? A.584 square inches B. 585 square inches 3. Find the surface area of an ice cream the slanted height of 6cm. A. 40.24 cm² B. 50.24 cm² C.586 square inches D. 587 square inches 5. A certain music box has the shape of a cube. Each side of the music box is 15 centimeter long. What is the surface area of the box? C. 1450 cm² C. 1250 cm² B. 1350 cm² D. 1550 cm²​


Answer:

1. B. 63.375 dm²

To find the surface area of the cube, we need to find the area of each face and add them together. Since all faces of a cube are the same, we just need to find the area of one face and multiply by 6.

Area of one face = 3.25 dm x 3.25 dm = 10.5625 dm²

Surface area of cube = 6 x 10.5625 dm² = 63.375 dm²

2. B. 185 square inches

The block has 6 faces, so we need to find the area of all 6 faces and add them together.

Area of each face = length x width = 5 in x 9 in = 45 in²

There are 2 faces with dimensions 5 in x 9 in, 2 faces with dimensions 9 in x 3.5 in, and 2 faces with dimensions 5 in x 3.5 in.

Total area = 2(45) + 2(9 x 3.5) + 2(5 x 3.5) = 185 in²

3. A. 40.24 cm²

We need to first find the slant height of the ice cream cone using Pythagoras theorem. The slant height is the hypotenuse of a right triangle where one leg is half the height and the other leg is the base.

Base = 2 cm (radius)

Height = 6 cm

Slant height = √(2² + 6²) = √40 = 6.32 cm

Now we can find the surface area using the formula: πr² + πr(l)

l is the slant height in this case.

Surface area = 2πr² + πr(l) = 2π(2²) + π(2)(6.32) = 40.24 cm²

4. A. 584 square inches

To wrap the box, we need to find the area of the 6 faces and add them together.

Area of each face = length x width

Top and bottom faces = 18 in x 10 in = 180 in² (2 faces)

Front and back faces = 18 in x 4 in = 72 in² (2 faces)

Left and right faces = 10 in x 4 in = 40 in² (2 faces)

Total area = 2(180) + 2(72) + 2(40) = 584 in²

5. C. 1350 cm²

The surface area of a cube is found by finding the area of one face and multiplying by 6. Since all faces are the same on a cube, we just need to find the area of one face and multiply by 6.

Area of one face = 15 cm x 15 cm = 225 cm²

Surface area of cube = 6 x 225 cm² = 1350 cm²
