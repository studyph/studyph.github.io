30. who wants to have corrupt politicians who prioritize self-comfirt than those of the people in the community ​


Some people want corrupt politicians who prioritize self-comfort over those of the people in the community. They believe that these politicians will be more likely to care about their own interests than the interests of the community.

I think there's a problem with your premise.  

The "people" you are referring to is not actually the people, but rather the government and its representatives (i.e., elected officials). So if we're talking about corruption from a governmental perspective, then it makes sense that the term would be used for this purpose. But I don't see any reason why the same word wouldn't apply when discussing corruption within an organization or institution instead of just by individuals.

The idea that "self" is a subset or part of one's country, and thus that it should be prioritized over other parts of society, is not new.  The US has had an attitude like this since at least 1865 with regard to African Americans (and even earlier).  There are many examples from history of European countries favoring their own citizens over others; for example, Britain favored its own farmers during World War II while condemning German ones as being unpatriotic and disloyal.
