V = πD²/4 · h

find the Volume (V)

V = 141.37cu.ft. x 7.48gal. / 1cu.ft.

V = 1,057.46 gallons

23. Jen picked 3/4 of a gallon of strawberries in half an hour. if she keeps picking strawberries at the same rate, how many gallons will she have picked in 2 hours


Answer:

Fractions

Jen will be able to pick 3 gallons of strawberries.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given that Jen can pick 3/4 gallon of strawberries in half an hour, compute how many half an hour are there in two hours. If there are two half hours in an hour then there will be four half hours in two hours. Use this ratio in order to compute for the number of gallons of strawberries Jen could pick for that period of time. Thus, 3/4 gallon must be multiplied to 4 since there are 4 half hour in 2 hours. Writing this as an equation will give 4(3/4) = 12/4 = 3. Therefore, there Jen will have 3 gallons of strawberries after 2 hours.
