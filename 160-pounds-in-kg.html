The mean weight of five complete computer stations is 167.2 pounds.

The weights of four of the computer stations are 158.4 pounds, 162.8 pounds, 165 pounds, and 178.2 pounds.

We have to determine the weight of fifth computer station.

Let the weight of fifth computer station be 'x' pounds.

Mean is calculated by sum of all the observations divided by the total number of observations.

So,

x= 171.6 pounds

The weight of fifth computer station is 171.6 pounds

30. 1 kg is equivalent to approximately 2.2 pounds what is the total weight in pounds of two pieces of luggage which weigh 14.8 kg and 18.9 kg, respectively?​


• Problem:

1 kg is equivalent to approximately 2.2 pounds. What is the total weight in pounds of two pieces of luggage which weigh 14.8 kg and 18.9 kg, respectively?

• Solution:

Since you're tasked to find the total weight, you can just add first the given weights and then convert the answer from kilograms to pounds.

Thus, here's the solution.

[tex]\large \boxed{ \begin{array}{} \tt14.8 \: kg + 18.9 \: kg \\ \tt33.7 \: kg\end{array}}[/tex]

Then, to convert, multiply 33.7 by 2.2 pounds.

[tex]\large \boxed{ \begin{array}{} \tt33.7 \times 2.2 \\ \tt 74.14 \: pounds\end{array}}[/tex]

• Answer:

The total weight of two pieces of luggage is 74.14 pounds.
