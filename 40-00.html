10. The elapsed time between 3:40 a.m. and 2:00 p.m.?


3:00 am to 12:00 pm = 9 hours

9 hours - 40 minutes (for 3:40) = 8 hours and 20 minutes

12:00 pm to 2:00 pm = 2 hours

8 hours and 20 minutes + 2 hours = 10 hours and 20 minutes

Therefore, the elapsed time between 3:40 am to 2:00 pm is 10 hours and 20 minutes.

21. What is the duration of 06:00 to 23:40 (24hour clock)


Answer:

To calculate the duration between 06:00 and 23:40, we need to subtract the earlier time from the later time.

First, we need to convert both times to a 24-hour clock format.

6:00 in 24-hour clock format is 06:00.

23:40 in 24-hour clock format is 23:40.

Now we can subtract the two times:

23:40 - 06:00 = 17:40

Therefore, the duration between 06:00 and 23:40 is 17 hours and 40 minutes.
