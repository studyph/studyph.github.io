Fixed assets are long-term tangible assets used in business operations, these are assets that the company intends to keep and use for a period of time. They are also referred to as property, plant, and equipment and are recorded on a balance sheet.

24. If an asset exchange has commercial substance, a gain or loss is recorded based on the difference between the value of the asset given up and the market value of the asset received.


Answer:

An exchange has commercial substance if, as a result of the exchange, future cash flows are expected to change significantly. For instance, if a company exchanges a building for land (a dissimilar exchange), the timing and the future cash flows are likely to be different than if the exchange had not occurred.
