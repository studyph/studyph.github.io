8. A girl leaves a history classroom and walks 25 meters north to a drinking fountain. Then she turns and walks 16 meters south to an art classroom. What is the girl’s total displacement from the history classroom to the art classroom? Given: Required: Equation: Solution: Answer:


Given:

The girl walks 25 meters north to a drinking fountain.The girl walks 25 meters north to a drinking fountain.Then she turns and walks 16 meters south to an art classroom.

Required:

The girl's total displacement from the history classroom to the art classroom.

Equation:

Total displacement = final position - initial position

Solution:

Let's assume that the history classroom is the initial position (or reference point) and the positive direction is towards north. Then, the girl's northward displacement to the drinking fountain would be +25 meters, and her southward displacement to the art classroom would be -16 meters.

Therefore, the girl's total displacement from the history classroom to the art classroom would be:

Total displacement = final position - initial position

Total displacement = (-16 meters) - (25 meters)

Total displacement = -41 meters

Note that the negative sign indicates that the girl's displacement is in the opposite direction of the initial position (i.e., towards south).

Answer:

The girl's total displacement from the history classroom to the art classroom is 41 meters south.

P = 2(l+w)

w = l - 3

Substitute

A = lw

54 = l(l-3)

54 = l^2 - 3l

Transpose

l^2 - 3l - 54 =0

(l -9)(l + 6) = 0

l - 9 = 0

l = 9m

l = -6

Note: Take only the positive value as we are dealing with dimensions

w = l -3

w = 9 - 3

w = 6m

Definition of Courseware

Courseware refers to software programs specifically designed for educational purposes. Originally available on CD as a course aid to instructors and to help them manage lectures, courseware has been revolutionized. Most are now available online with a variety of resources and supporting materials (reference books, research papers and journals).

Courseware is primarily used for online courses and certifications such as Microsoft Certified Professional courses.

Coverage in courseware

Courseware educational material studied with the help of a computer. It can be in the form of flash drives, compact disks or material placed online. Courseware can include:

Instructions on how to divide courses into lectures Notes to the instructor on leading a class Information for independent and computer-based learning Useful Web URLs for related materials
