9. what is virtue of fidelity​


Answer:

Fidelity is the virtue of consistency. It is the basis for reliable thought, reason, morality, trust, and loyalty. It allows us to predict future behavior based on the history of past behavior. Fidelity is most valuable when it is applied to the most worthy ideas, deeds, principles, or affiliations.
