7. 8. How many centimeters are there in 1 meter? A. 100 centimeters B. 50 centimeters C. 10 centimeters D. 75 centimeters​


A. 100 centimeters

1 meter is equivalent to 100 centimeters.

Solution:

1 cm = 1/100 m or 0.01 m.

Hence, to get the value of 100 cm in terms of 'meter', we multiply it with 0.01.

100 cm = 100 × 0.01 m = 1 m
