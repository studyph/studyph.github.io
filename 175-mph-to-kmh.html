2. 41. Sarah drives at an average of 35 mph on a journey of 175 miles. How long does the journey take?​


Answer:

So Sarah's journey of 175 miles at an average speed of 35 mph will take approximately 2 hours and 46 minutes.

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this problem, we can use the formula:

Distance = Speed x Time

In other words, the time (t) it takes to travel a certain distance (D) is equal to the speed (s) divided by the distance.

We can substitute the given values of Sarah's journey:

D = 175 mi

S = 35 mph

and we want to solve for t, the time it takes to make this journey:

t = (175 mi) / (35 mph)

t = (175 mi) / (5280 ft/mi) * (1 hr/3600 sec)

t = 2.76 hours
