QUESTION: The acute angles of right triangles are

ANSWER: a. Complementary

Before I explain why they are complementary, let's learn the information below:A right triangle is a kind of triangle that has 1 right angle. A right angle is an angle that measures EXACTLY 90 degrees. The sum of interior angles of a triangle is 180 degrees.Complementary angles are an angle pair whose measures add up to 90.

Now, here is my explanation:Since there is a 90 degree angle (aka the right angle) in the right triangle, we will subtract it to 180. 180 - 90 = 90The remaining angles will have a sum of 90 degrees, therefore being complementary.
