What does "đum spiro, spero." mean?

Đum spiro, spero means "While I breathe, I hope". It is a Latin phrase used as a motto. It means that as long as an individual lives, he/she will keep hoping and striving for success. The motto "đum spiro, spero" means not giving up as long as we're living.

26. Partial Differentation. If u xy/x^2+y^2 + sin^-1 (x/y) evaluate du/dy and x du/dx + du/dy​


[tex]\mathbb{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{u=\dfrac{xy}{x^2+y^2}+sin^{-1}\left(\dfrac{x}{y}\right)}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{\partial}{{\partial}x}\left[u=\dfrac{xy}{x^2+y^2}+sin^{-1}\left(\dfrac{x}{y}\right)\right]}[/tex]

treat y as a constant

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}x}=\dfrac{(x^2+y^2)y-xy(2x)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}+\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-\left(\dfrac{x}{y}\right)^2}}\left(\dfrac{1}{y}\right)}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}x}=\dfrac{x^2y+y^3-2x^2y}{(x^2+y^2)^2}+\dfrac{1}{y\sqrt{\dfrac{y^2-x^2}{y^2}}}}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}x}=\dfrac{y^3-x^2y}{(x^2+y^2)^2}+\dfrac{\sqrt{y^2}}{y\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}x}=\dfrac{y(y^2-x^2)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}+\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{\partial}{{\partial}y}\left[u=\dfrac{xy}{x^2+y^2}+sin^{-1}\left(\dfrac{x}{y}\right)\right]}[/tex]

treat x as a constant

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}y}=\dfrac{(x^2+y^2)x-xy(2y)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}+\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-\left(\dfrac{x}{y}\right)^2}}\left(-\dfrac{x}{y^2}\right)}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}y}=\dfrac{x^3+xy^2-2xy^2}{(x^2+y^2)^2}-\dfrac{x}{y^2\sqrt{\dfrac{y^2-x^2}{y^2}}}}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}y}=\dfrac{x^3-xy^2}{(x^2+y^2)^2}-\dfrac{x\sqrt{y^2}}{y^2\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}y}=\dfrac{x(x^2-y^2)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}-\dfrac{x}{y\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{x\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}x}+\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}y}=x\left[\dfrac{y(y^2-x^2)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}+\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\right]+\left[\dfrac{x(x^2-y^2)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}-\dfrac{x}{y\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}\right]}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{x\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}x}+\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}y}=\dfrac{xy(y^2-x^2)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}+\dfrac{x}{\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}+\dfrac{x(x^2-y^2)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}-\dfrac{x}{y\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{x\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}x}+\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}y}=\dfrac{xy(y^2-x^2)+x(x^2-y^2)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}+\dfrac{xy-x}{y\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\mathbb{ANSWER:}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\mathsf{\dfrac{{\partial}u}{{\partial}y}=\dfrac{x(x^2-y^2)}{(x^2+y^2)^2}-\dfrac{x}{y\sqrt{y^2-x^2}}}}[/tex]
