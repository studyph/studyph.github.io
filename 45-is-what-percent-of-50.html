Answer:

Here are the solutions to each problem:

1. To find the rate, divide the part (14) by the whole (56) and multiply by 100%.

n% = (14 / 56) * 100%

n = 25%

2. To find the rate, divide the part (45) by the whole (60) and multiply by 100%.

n% = (45 / 60) * 100%

n = 75%

3. To find the rate, divide the part (10) by the whole (50) and multiply by 100%.

n% = (10 / 50) * 100%

n = 20%

4. To find the rate, divide the part (45) by the whole (60) and multiply by 100%.
