Answer:

Leaders are people who leads group of people to achieve a common goal. A leader leads, not commands. They inspire and motivate people to do their work and put the their group in action.

Tips: How to be a good leader?

1. Be a good communicator

---> You must be able to convey your thoughts effectively. Otherwise, your team won't be able to achieve good results as they didn't understand your message clearly. Also, it also helps you in motivating your members as words are powerful when used correctly.

2. Be honest and have integrity

---> People are more inclined to trust and follow you when they know that you can be trusted and have strong moral principles.

3. Inspire and bring up morale

---> People tend to lose hope and become less effective on their work when lacking morale. Morale is very important in a team and you must maintain it by inspiring them.

4. Be committed

---> Being committed on your work as a leader inspires your team to work harder. This shows them that you are also working hard for your goals like them.

5. Be humble

---> Don't act like you are above your members. They tend to dislike people like that and won't do their job properly. So, make them feel that you are still humble no matter your position.

6. Be wise and confident

---> Decisions are often made by leader and sometimes they are hard to make. You must be wise on what decisions you have picked and be confident in it to give off the impression to your members that you have picked the right.

21. explain the three dimensions namely intimacy, commitment and passion​


Answer:

three dimensions of love include passion, intimacy, and commitment. Passion is described by Sternberg as an intense physical and emotional feeling characterized by excitement and euphoria which in turn activate hormones and neurons in the body and mind. Intimacy is described as the feeling of closeness and connectedness in addition to sharing secrets and nakedness. Lastly, commitment ties the other two dimensions and is described as mutual caregiving, shared possessions, and forgiveness. Furthermore, commitment is a decision to remain with one another with future plans that takes a lot of dedication and work. An example of a type of love that is probably common at the beginning of relationships is romantic love in which passion and intimacy are present. Romantic love begins with passion, leads to intimacy and eventually to commitment, if the relationship continues to develop. An example of a type of love that is probably more common as the relationship develops is companionate love characterized by a combination of intimacy and commitment. Because passion fades, intimacy may grow and stabilize meanwhile commitment may also develop, particularly increasing when children are born.
