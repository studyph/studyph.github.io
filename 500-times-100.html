2. [tex]500 \times 3 - 500 + 600 - 100[/tex]​


Answer:

[tex]1500[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

MDAS rule

Firstly, We have to Multiply.

[tex]500 \times 3 -500 +600 -100 \\ 1500 -500 +600 -100[/tex]

Secondly, We Divide. Since we can't see any Division operation we proceed to the next step.

Thirdly, We Add.

[tex]1500 -500 +600 -100 \\ 1500 +100 -100 \\ 1600 -100[/tex]

Lastly, We Subtract.

[tex] 1600 -100 \\ 1500[/tex]

Answer:

C. Php 8,100

Step-by-step explanation:

"Php 2,250 - 5 hours"

First, identify how much the employee earns if he will work for 1 1 hour, so;

2,250 ÷ 5 (hours) = Php 450

So he/she earns Php 450 in an hour.Then, multiply the "Php 450" in 18 (hours);

450 × 18 = Php 8,100

So "Php 8,100" is the answer, you're welcome!
