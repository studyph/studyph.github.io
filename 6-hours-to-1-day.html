======

ANSWER

======

[tex]P109,167.75[/tex]

======

======

[tex]=192[/tex] × [tex]5=960[/tex]

[tex]=960[/tex] + [tex]255[/tex](minutes in 4.25 hours) [tex]=1,215[/tex]

[tex]=1,215[/tex] × [tex]89.85 = 109,167.75[/tex]
