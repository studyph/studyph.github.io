8. what's the answer to this (pleas with solution)(3y+2)(3y-2)​


(3y+2)(3y-2) can be solved using the FOIL method, which involves multiplying the first terms, the outer terms, the inner terms, and the last terms:

(3y+2)(3y-2) = 3y(3y) + 3y(-2) + 2(3y) + 2(-2)
= 9y^2 - 6y + 6y - 4
= 9y^2 - 4

Therefore, (3y+2)(3y-2) simplifies to 9y^2 - 4
