M = [tex]\frac{F}{A}[/tex]

M - Mass

F - Force

A - Acceleration

====================

Force - 500 N

Acceleration - 60 km/h

====================

[tex]M=\frac{F}{A}[/tex]

[tex]M = 500N[/tex] ÷ [tex]60 km/h[/tex]

[tex]M = 8.33[/tex] kg

So the mass of the car is 8.33 kg

19. MP and QS are divided proportionally by point O on MP and point R on QS. if QR is 12 cm, RS is 300 cm and MP is 104 cm, find MO and OP. ​


Answer:

MO is 4 cm and OP is 100 cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Reduce 12:300 into lowest termby dividing each value by their GCF 12:

(12÷12)/(300÷12) = 1: 25

Then the ratio of MO to OP is 1 : 25.

Find the common factor, x:

1x + 25x = 103

26x = 104

26x/26 = 104/26

x = 4

MO ⇒ 1x = 1(4) = 4

OP ⇒ 25x = 24(4) = 100

MO is 4 cm and OP is 100 cm.

Segment addition postulate:

MO + OP = MP4 cm + 100 cm  = 104 cm104 cm = 104 cm  (True)
