25. A clerk can sort 375 sheets per hour. If there are 225 sheets in an inch, how long will it take her to file 125 inches of loose sheets


We will first find the number of sheets in 125 inches. If there are 225 sheets in an inch, then there are also 28,125 sheets in 125 inches because
225 sheets/inch × 125 inches = 28,125 sheets.

Now, we will find how long it will take her to file 125 inches of loose sheets.
If a clerk can sort 375 sheets per hour, then she will take 75 hours to sort 28,125 sheets because
28,125 sheets ÷ 375 sheets/hour = 75 hours

Therefore, a clerk can sort 125 inches of loose sheets in 75 hours.
