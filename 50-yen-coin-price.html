29. Yen-yen got an average grade of 91 on her 4 subjects. What must be her grade on the fifth subject to get an average of 92?


✏️ANSWER

[tex] ➣96 [/tex]

✏️SOLUTION

[tex](x + 91 \times 4) \div 5 = 92[/tex]

[tex] \frac{x + 91 \times 4}{5} = 92[/tex]

[tex] \frac{x + 364}{5} = 92[/tex]

[tex] \frac{(x + 364) \times 5}{5} = 92 \times 5[/tex]

[tex]x + 364 = 92 \times 5[/tex]

[tex]x + 364 = 460[/tex]

[tex]x = 460 - 364[/tex]

[tex] = 96[/tex]

So the Correct Answer is 96.
