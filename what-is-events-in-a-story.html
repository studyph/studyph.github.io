Explanation:

which is a reference to the Philippine-American War of 1899-1902. The war resulted in the US gaining control of the Philippines and establishing a colonial government there.

The significance of this event in the story is that it serves as a reminder of how Filipinos have been oppressed by foreign powers throughout their history. 14. What is Events in story Cinderella​


Answer:

1.Cinderella is a young girl living with her stepmother and stepsisters, who treat her poorly and make her do all of the household chores.

2.One day, the royal family announces that they will be holding a ball, and every eligible young woman in the kingdom is invited to attend.

3.Cinderella's stepmother and stepsisters prevent her from going to the ball by tearing up her invitation and ruining her dress.

4.Cinderella's fairy godmother appears and transforms a pumpkin into a carriage, mice into horses, and Cinderella's rags into a beautiful ball gown.

5.Cinderella goes to the ball and meets the prince, who is immediately captivated by her.

6.As the ball is ending, Cinderella has to leave early and loses one of her glass slippers on the steps of the palace.

7.The prince searches the kingdom for the owner of the glass slipper, trying it on every eligible young woman in the kingdom.

8.When he arrives at Cinderella's house, the slipper fits her perfectly, revealing her to be the mystery princess the prince has been searching for.

9.Cinderella and the prince marry and live happily ever after.
