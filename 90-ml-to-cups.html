Answer:

1. 15mL = ____ teaspoon

1mL = 0.2029 teaspoon (divide by 4.929)

15 × 0.2029 (15 ÷ 4.929)

= 3.04 teaspoon

2. 2L = ____ cups

1L = 4.227 cups

2 × 4.227

= 8.454 cups

3. 45mL = ____ tablespoon

1mL = 0.0676 tablespoon (divide by 14.787)

45 × 0.0676 (45 ÷ 14.787)

= 3.04 tablespoon

1gram = 0.0353 ounce (divide by 28.35)

90 × 0.0353 (90 ÷ 28.35)

= 3.18 or 3.17 ounce

5. 3kg = ____ pound

1kg = 2.205 pound

3 × 2.205

= 6.615 pound
