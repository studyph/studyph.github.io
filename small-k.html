7. From the bottom of the lake where the temperature is 281 K and the pressure is 6.40 atm,a small bubble rises to the water's surface,where the temperature is 298 K and the pressure is 1.00 atm.calculate the final volume of the bubble if its initial volume was 0.00210 L


Given:

[tex]P_{1} = \text{6.40 atm}[/tex]

[tex]T_{1} = \text{281 K}[/tex]

[tex]V_{1} = \text{0.00210 L}[/tex]

[tex]P_{2} = \text{1.00 atm}[/tex]

[tex]T_{2} = \text{298 K}[/tex]

Unknown:

[tex]V_{2}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]\frac{P_{1}V_{1}}{T_{1}} = \frac{P_{2}V_{2}}{T_{2}}[/tex]

[tex]V_{2} = V_{1} × \frac{P_{1}}{P_{2}} × \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}[/tex]

[tex]V_{2} = \text{0.00210 L} × \frac{\text{6.40 atm}}{\text{1.00 atm}} × \frac{\text{298 K}}{\text{281 K}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{V_{2} = \text{0.01425 L}}[/tex]
