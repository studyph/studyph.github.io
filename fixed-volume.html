1. which of the following correctly describes a gasa. it has a fixed volume and shapeb. it has a fixed volume but does not have a fixed shapec. it does not have a fixed volume but has a fixed shaped. it does not have a fixed volume nor a fixed shape​


Answer:

d. it does not have a fixed volume nor a fixed shape

Explanation:

Gases don't have a fixed shape or a fixed volume. The particles move around all the time and spread out. This is why a gas fills its container. A gas can be compressed into a very small space – this pushes the particles closer together.
