1. A type of living organism that creates body chemical reactions to help support life.​


Metabolism

Explanation:
The sum of all the biochemical reactions in an organism is referred to as metabolism. Metabolism includes both exothermic (heat-releasing) chemical reactions and endothermic (heat-absorbing) chemical reactions.

2. 2. What type of chemical reaction took place in the activity? Support your answer.​


Answer:
