13. 1. You’re picking an outfit for school. In your closet, you find 4 pairs of pants, 8 shirts, and 6 belts. Assuming everything matches, how many outfits can you create? ​


Answer:

192 outfits

Step-by-step explanation:

You can create 192 outfits. This is because you can combine each pair of pants with each shirt in 4 x 8 = 32 ways, and then combine each of those 32 outfits with each belt in 32 x 6 = 192 ways.
