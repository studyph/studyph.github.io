How to draw a cartoon dog:

1. Start by drawing a large oval for the dog's head.

2. Add two smaller ovals for the eyes and fill them in.

3. Draw a triangle for the nose and add a line for the mouth.

4. Add two small triangles for the ears and connect them to the head.

5. Draw the body by adding another larger oval underneath the head.

6. Add four legs by drawing rectangular shapes.

7. Draw a small tail at the back of the body.

8. Add details such as fur by drawing small lines around the body.

Voila! You have successfully drawn a cartoon dog.
