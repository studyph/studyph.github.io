12. what method of separating mixtures will you use if you want to remove small shells of coffee from ground coffee​


Answer:

Filtration

Explanation:

Filtration takes advantage of this size difference of these particles by passing the mixture over a surface that has holes that are small enough to allow only the liquid or gas through. A coffee filter is a great example of this process.

Answer:

Sieving

The method of separating mixtures that you need to use if you want to remove small shells in a grind/ground coffee is sieving,

Explanation:

Sieving is very effective when trying to remove solids from solids. For example, if you are trying to remove small shells from a ground coffee, or if you wanted to remove rocks from sands, then sieving if the thing that you should do.

The sieve is a device that is usually hollow and has a base that has many holes in it (for example, chicken wire). You need to put the solid mixture inside, and then gently shake the sieve to let the smaller particles pass through, while the larger particles are left behind.

Answer:

B. Fossil fuels cannot be replaced once they are used up, and they are a finite resource. It took millions of years for fossil fuels to form from the remains of ancient plants and animals, and at the current rate of consumption, they will be depleted within the next century or two. Conserving fossil fuels is important because they are a major source of energy for our modern society, and if we do not conserve them, we will eventually run out of them. In addition, the use of fossil fuels contributes to climate change, air pollution, and other environmental problems, so reducing our consumption of these fuels can help to mitigate these issues.
