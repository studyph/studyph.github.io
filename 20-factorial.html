4. zero factorial to 20 isusulat daw Namin sa 1/2 index card pa help Naman po​


Factorial is the product of all positive integers less than n. So:

0! = 1 (No positive values less than 0)
1! = 1 (Factorials consider 1 as the last number)
2! = 2*1 = 2
3! = 3*2*1 = 6
4! = 4*3*2*1 = 24
5! = 5*4*3*2*1 = 120

and so on. So your equation would be:
n! = n*n-1*n-2*n-3*…1

12. Rossie worked in a bag factory and earned P7,875 for 15 days. How much will she earn in 20 days?


Answer:

₱10,500.00

Step-by-step explanation:

use ratio and proportion

7875:15=X:20

Let x be the amount of she earns in 20 days.

multiply the extreme

7875×20= 157,500

then divide it by the mean

157,500÷15= 10,500

She earns ₱10,500.00 in 20 days.

Final Answer: Rossie will earn P10,500 in 20 days

Solution:

1. Identify the parts of the equation

Amount of money earned: P7,875

Number of days worked: 15 days

Number of days Rossie WILL work: 20 days

Salary after 20 days = ?

2. Calculate

Formula:

Amount of money earned ÷ Number of days worked x Number of days Rossie will work = Salary after 20 days
