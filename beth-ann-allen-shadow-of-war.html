20. Ma. Jo Ann is 1.6 m tall. Her father is 2.0 tall. Her father casts a shadow of 5 m. How long would Ma. Jo Ann's shadow (d) be? 2.0m -5m 1.6m​


Answer:

We can use proportions to find the length of Ma. Jo Ann's shadow (d).

We know that Ma. Jo Ann's height is 1.6 m and her father's height is 2.0 m. We also know that her father's shadow is 5 m.

Using proportions, we can set up the following equation:

father's height / father's shadow = Ma. Jo Ann's height / Ma. Jo Ann's shadow

Substituting the values we know:

2.0 m / 5 m = 1.6 m / d

Simplifying:

d = (5 m x 1.6 m) / 2.0 m

d = 4 m

Therefore, Ma. Jo Ann's shadow would be 4 meters long.

23. a sum of money was shared among ann beth and Don. ann received 1/8 of the money. Been received 1/4 of the money. don recieved the remaining 250 pesos. how much was the sum of money​


Answer:

400 pesos

Step-by-step explanation:

First, add the 2 fractions.

1/8 + 1/4 = 3/8

The remaining fraction is equal to 250 pesos.

To get the remaining fraction.

1 - 3/8 = 5/8

5/8 = 250 pesos

So, 250 times 5 divided by 8 is equal to 50 pesos. therefore, 1/8 is equal to 50 pesos. (250 x 5/8 = 50)

To get the value of 1/4, just multiply the value of 1/8 twice.

50 x 2 = 100

1/4 = 100 pesos

1/8 + 1/4 + 5/8 = n

50 + 100 + 250 = 400
