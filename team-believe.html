12. Do you believe trust-building is a learned skill? Why? How to develop the trust-building skill? What values are most important to you as a leader? What can you do to motivate a team?​


Answer:

Yes, I believe trust-building is a learned skill. Building trust requires communication, consistency, and the ability to demonstrate genuine care and concern for others. To develop the trust-building skill, an individual must practice active listening, be authentic, and be willing to compromise and make sacrifices. Additionally, they must be open and transparent with their intentions, and be willing to build relationships and collaborate with others. Values that are important to me as a leader include integrity, respect, fairness, open communication, and collaboration. To motivate a team, I believe in fostering a positive and supportive environment, providing recognition and rewards, setting clear expectations and goals, identifying areas of growth and development, and allowing for autonomy and creative freedom.
