LCD (35/8, 7/2) = 8

(35/8 × 1/1) + (7/2 × 4/4)

= 35/8 + 28/8

35 + 28/8= 63/8

63/8 is the same as 63 ÷ 8

Convert to a mixed number using  long division for 63 ÷ 8 = 7R7:
