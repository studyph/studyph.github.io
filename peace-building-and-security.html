12. why migration, poverty and health security becomes an issue in global peace and security ​


Migration, poverty, and health security can all have a significant impact on global peace and security for a number of reasons.

Migration, whether it is voluntary or forced, can lead to social and economic disruptions in both the origin and destination countries. It can also strain resources and lead to conflicts over access to these resources. In addition, large-scale migration can also lead to cultural tensions and misunderstandings, which can contribute to social unrest and conflicts.

Poverty is another important factor that can impact global peace and security. When people are living in poverty, they may be more likely to engage in criminal activity or join extremist groups in order to survive. Poverty can also lead to social unrest and political instability, as people may become disillusioned with their government's ability to address their needs and may turn to violent means to bring about change.

Health security is also a major factor that can affect global peace and security. The spread of infectious diseases can have serious economic and social consequences, as well as lead to political instability and conflict. In addition, the lack of access to healthcare and other basic services can lead to social unrest and contribute to conflicts.

Overall, migration, poverty, and health security are all interconnected and can have a major impact on global peace and security if not addressed effectively.

15. why migration becomes an issue in global peace and security? ​


Answer:

Migration, poverty, and health security can all contribute to global peace and security in a number of ways.

Migration can have an impact on global peace and security because it can lead to conflicts over resources and territory, and it can also contribute to cultural and social tensions in host countries. For example, if large numbers of people are moving to a new country and competing for limited resources, it can lead to social and economic tensions that can escalate into violence. Additionally, if people are fleeing conflict or persecution in their home countries, they may be vulnerable to exploitation and abuse, which can contribute to social unrest and conflict in the host country.

Poverty can also have an impact on global peace and security because it can lead to social and economic inequality, which can fuel social and political tensions. When people are living in poverty, they may be more likely to engage in illegal activities or to join extremist groups, which can contribute to instability and conflict. Additionally, poverty can make it more difficult for people to access essential services, such as education, healthcare, and clean water, which can lead to social unrest and conflict.
