4. what is the difference of the poetry in the book of psalms and in the book of proverbs​


Answer:

Proverbs are very different. The Psalms are vertical to God while the Proverbs are horizontal about people. The Psalms are divine counsel from God whereas the Proverbs are practical advice to people. The Psalms include a collection of prayers and hymns, while the Proverbs are wise sayings.

A. Let x= total sales
p= poetry books sold,

[tex]50\%(x) = 114 \\ x = 228 \: books[/tex]
[tex]76 + 114 + p = 228 \\ p = 228 - 190 \\ p = 38 \: books[/tex]

B.
[tex] \frac{38}{228} = 0.1667 \\ 0.1667 \times 100\% = 16.67\%[/tex]
