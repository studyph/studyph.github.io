22. what is the ratio of 25 cm to 1 m​

Step-by-step explanation:

Question: what is the ratio 25 cm to 1 m

Solution: We convert the units to make it similar.

Converting meter into centimeter:

1 m/meter = 100 cm/centimeter

Ratio: 25 cm : 1 cm → 1 cm : 4 cm

Therefore, the ratio 25 cm : 1 m in simplest form is 1 : 4
