10. Sin weakens the love relationship that we have with God. a personal sin b venial sin c original sin d mortal sin


Answer:

The correct answer is letter A

Explanation:

The statement "Sin weakens the love relationship that we have with God" applies to different types of sin, but the closest match to this statement is option A, "personal sin."

Personal sin is an individual's deliberate choice to act in a way that is contrary to God's will, which weakens the individual's relationship with God. Personal sin can be either venial (a less serious offense) or mortal (a grave offense that completely severs the individual's relationship with God).
