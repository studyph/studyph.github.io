Answer:

To find the perimeter of the object, we need to add up the lengths of all the sides.

Given:

T = 78 in

U = 84 in

V = 63 in

W = 130 in

X = 104 in

Y = 16 in

Z = 105 in

Adding up all the lengths:

78 + 84 + 63 + 130 + 104 + 16 + 105 = 580

So, the perimeter of the object is 580 in.

Therefore, the correct option is:

A. 580 in
