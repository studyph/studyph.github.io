17. How can you tell if a painting is an abstract?


Answer:

Understand History and Significance

One of the final and most important aspects of determining the quality of abstract paintings is the history of the piece. The painter and their reputation as an abstract painter will be the easiest way to determine if the piece is of quality or not. Dates are important as well.

Abstract art is not "about nothing". At its basis, it is about form, color, line, texture, pattern, composition and process. These are the formal qualities of artwork, because they describe what the art looks like and how it is created. Abstract art is an exploration of these formal qualities. Meaning is derived from how these formal qualities are used to create a visual (and/or visceral, cerebral, emotional, etc) experience.
