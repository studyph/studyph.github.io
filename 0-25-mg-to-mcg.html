Step-by-step explanation:

A and I are midpoints, so they split MG and GC into two equal parts

Since A is the midpoint of MG, MA and AG have the same length

Since I is the midpoint of CG, IC AND GI have the same length

AG = 2y - 1 which means MA is also 2y - 1

Since they are equal, adding their lengths would result in the length of the whole line

MA + AG = MG

MG = (2y - 1) + (2y - 1)

MG = 4y - 2

IC = y + 5 which means GI is also y + 5

Since they are equal, adding their lengths would result in the length of the whole line

GI + IC = CG

CG = (y + 5) + (y + 5)

CG = 2y + 10
