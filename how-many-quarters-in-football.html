21. The number of football balls of Rick's collection is divisible by 2,5 and 10. He has more that 15 football balls but fewer than 25. How many football balls does Rick have?


Answer:

20 footballs

Step-by-step explanation:

Numbers divisible by 2, 5, and 10, more than 15 but less than 25 is 20.

20 ÷ 2 = 10

20 ÷ 5 = 4

20 ÷ 10 = 2

There is no remainder, therefore, it is divisible.

20 is in range of 15 and 25.

Therefore, there are 20 footballs Rick have.
