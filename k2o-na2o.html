25. if 10 grams of k2o is made to reactwith 10 grams Hci, what is the limiting reaction?​


Answer:

K2O is the limiting reactant.

Explanation:

Write the balanced equation.

K2O + 2HCl → 2KCl + H2O

Compute for the number of moles of KCl formed.

For K2O

n KCl = 10 g K2O × (1 mol K2O / 94 g K2O) × (2 mol KCl / 1 mol K2O)

n KCl = 0.21 mol

For HCl

n KCl = 10 g HCl × (1 mol HCl / 36.5 g) × (2 mol KCl / 2 mol HCl)

n KCl = 0.27 mol

Determine the limiting reactant.

K2O is the limiting reactant.
