Answer:

The general objective of this research is to identify the social media usage of Grade 12 Fidelis Senior High Students and their acquired effective communication skills predominantly in their verbal, non-verbal, and written communication skills. The researchers selected the most appropriate design for the study which is quantitative research, specifically the descriptive research design which aims to accurately and systematically describe a population, situation, or phenomenon. The target respondents of this study will come from Grade 12 Fidelis Senior High STEM, ABM, HUMSS, TVL, SPORTS, and ARTSDES students. The total population of Grade 12 FSH students is 1115 in which 637 respondents were identified through the use of stratified random sampling which involves division of population into smaller groups or strata and Slovin’s formula which calculate the appropriate sample size from the population. Survey questionnaire will be used as the research instrument of this study. A total of a twenty-item (20) survey questionnaire will be used to answer and determine how social media usage impacts the respondents’ effective verbal, non-verbal, and written communication skills.
