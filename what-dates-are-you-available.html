Simple interest is a way to find the amount of interest charged on a sum at a given rate and for a given period of time. In this type of interest, the principal amount is always the same, unlike the compound interest. In the compound interest, we add the interest of previous years principal to calculate the interest of the next year. When investing money in the bank, the bank provides interest on that amount. Simple interest is just one of the many types of interest applied by the bank.
