1. Subtract: 12/25 - 3/25 = 12 - 3/25 = 9/25

For adding, subtracting, and comparing fractions, it is suitable to adjust both fractions to a common (equal, identical) denominator. The common denominator you can calculate as the least common multiple of both denominators - LCM(25, 25) = 25. In practice, it is enough to find the common denominator (not necessarily the lowest) by multiplying the denominators: 25 × 25 = 625. In the following intermediate step, the fraction result cannot be further simplified by canceling.

In other words - twelve twenty-fifths minus three twenty-fifths = nine twenty-fifths.

Add: the result of step No. 1 + 6/25 = 9/25 + 6/25 = 9 + 6/25 = 15/25 = 5 · 3/5 · 5 = 3/5
