Ideas grow into startups, which grow into mature enterprises, in four distinct stages, with a distinctive task to be accomplished in each: customer validation, operational validation, financial validation and self-sustainability. Further, the enterprise must proceed through these stages in sequence

What happens in the growth phase of a business?

During the growth phase, companies start seeing a profit and positive cash flow, which evidences their ability to repay debt. The corporations' products or services have been proven to provide value in the marketplace

What is business development phase explain each phase?
