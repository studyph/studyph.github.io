8. define slide mass movement


Answer:

A slide happens when a section of soil or rock suddenly gives way and moves down a slope. The material moves as a single mass along a slippery zone. The slippery zone is often made up of wet sediment. Translational slides move along a flat slippery zone and rotational slides move along a concave (bowl shaped) slippery zone.

30. The visual effect of movement that occurs when one slide changes into another slide is called:


Visual Effect of slide movements

Answer:

The answer is slide transitions. Slide transitions refer to the visual effect that we apply whenever we move from one slide to another. There are different types of transitions that are helpful in order to make our presentation more lively and easier to understand. Some transitions are effective in catching other people's attention so it is important to make a good plan in applying slide transitions.

In order to apply a slide transition, go to the transition options in the gslide or ms powerpoint presentation. Click your chosen transition and click apply. There are various transitions available for use and we can also modify the timing, length, etc of these transitions.
