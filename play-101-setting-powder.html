24. Nerio was asked to prepare 15 sets of 3/4 kg coffee powder to be given to thier expected guest how many kilograms of coffee powder will he prepare in all


Answer:

Nerio will have to prepare 11 1/4 kilograms of coffee powder.

Step-by-step explanation:

You just have to multiply 15 by 3/4 to get the answer.

In multiplying whole numbers and fractions, you can multiply the whole number and the numerator first:

15×3=45

after getting the product between the whole number and numerator, divide the product by the denominator:

45÷4= 11 1/4.

You can also divide the whole number first by the denominator:

15÷4= 3 3/4

then multiply the quotient by the numerator:

3 3/4×3= 11 1/4.
