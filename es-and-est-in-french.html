17. Select the correct answer. Which verb form correctly completes this conversation? Fidel: ¿Te gusta esta pintura? Geraldo: No. Es que yo no __________ este estilo de arte.


The dialog Fidel: ¿Te gusta esta pintura?
Geraldo: No. Es que yo no __________ este estilo de arte.
translates to
Fidel: Do you like this painting?
Geraldo: No, it's that, I don't ______ this style of art (or art style).

So, the verb would likely be "quiero" which means want... but if I were to change the sentence, I'd rather say:
1.) "No quiero este estilo de arte" (I don't want this style of art) Note: I took "yo" because it's kind of redundant when you say yo no quiero, because the verb "quiero" is already conjugated in its "yo" form...

2.) "No me gusta este estilo de arte" (I don't like this style of art)
or

3.) "No me encanta este estilo de arte" (I don't love this style of art)

Hope that helped :)
