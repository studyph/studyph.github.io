6. how many radius are ther in circle?​


Answer:

A CIRCLE CAN HAVE MORE THAN ONE RADIUS

Explanation:

The distance between the center of the circle and its circumference is referred to as its radius. We can draw as many radii to a point on the circle by joining the center. Thus every circle has an infinite number of radii.

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{QUESTION:}}[/tex]

Eight children join and form a circle. In how many ways could the circle be formed

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{P_{circular}=(n-1)!}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{P_{circular}=(8-1)!}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{P_{circular}=7!}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{P_{circular}=7×6×5×4×3×2×1}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{P_{circular}=5040}[/tex]

There are 5040 ways they can form a circle

12. at a given point on the circle how many lines can be drawn that is tangent to the circle​


Answer:

Two

Step-by-step explanation:

No tangent line can be drawn through a point within a circle, since any such line must be a secant line. However, two tangent lines can be drawn to a circle from a point P outside of the circle.
