12. Suppose the population of a certain bacteria in a laboratory sample is 500. If it doubles in population every 6 hours, what is the growth rate? How many bacteria will there be in two days?


Answer:

There will be 64,000 or  6.4 × 10⁴  bacteria after two days.

Step-by-step explanation:

Geometric Progression:

an = a₁(r)ⁿ⁻¹

Where:

n = term  ⇒  8  (that is, 48 hours/6 hours=8)

a₁ = the first term ⇒  500

r = ratio  ⇒ 2

(A) What is the growth rate?

Find the growth rate in percent:

Rate (%) = ratio × 100

Rate (%) = 2 × 100

Growth Rate  = 200%

(B) How many bacteria will there be in two days?

n = 8

an = a₁(r)ⁿ⁻¹

a₈ = 500(2)⁸⁻¹

a₈ = 500 (2)⁷

a₈ = 500 (128)

a₈ = 64,000  or  6.4 × 10⁴  bacteria

There will be 64,000 bacteria in two days or 48 hours.

RATIO AND PROPORTION: MCQ

Answers with Step-by-step explanation:

1. The ratio of boys to girls is 4:3. There are 24 boys, how many are girls?

A. 17 B. 19 C. 16 D. 18

Answer: The correct answer is letter D. 18.

Solution:

boys : girls = 4 : 3 :: 24 : X

24 ÷ 4 = 6 x 3 = 18 girls

2. Three people can paint a fence in 4 hours, how many people it will take to paint it in 6 hours?

A. 2 B. 5 C. 10 D. 8

Answer: The correct answer is letter A. 2.

Solution:

original number of people : new number of hours = new number of people : original number of hours

3 : 6 = X : 4

The product of the means equals to the product of the extremes

3 x 4 = 12 ÷ 6 = 2 people

3. In a partitive proportion 2:3 = 500, what is the value of the bigger number?

A. 200 B. 300 C. 100 D. 400

Answer: The correct answer is letter B. 300.

Solution:

2 + 3 = 5

500 ÷ 5 = 100

3 is bigger than 2, so: 3 x 100 = 300.

4. The heart beats 15 times in 10 seconds. How many times does it beat in one minute?

A. 70 times B. 80 times C. 90 times D. 100 times

Answer: The correct answer is letter C. 90 times.

Solution:

1 minute is equivalent to 60 seconds

15 : 10 :: X : 60

60 ÷ 10 = 6 x 15 = 90 times

5. Ten workers can assemble nine car engines in 18 days. How many days will it take 15 workers to assemble the same number of engines?

А. 12 days B. 13 days C. 14 days D. 15 days

Answer: The correct answer is letter A. 12 days.

Solution:

10 x 18 = 180 ÷ 15 = 12 days

6. The ratio of red roses to white roses in a florist shop is 5:2. If there are 588 roses, how many are white?

A. 148 B. 168 C. 420 D. 84

Answer: The correct answer is letter B. 168.

Solution:

588 ÷ (5+2) = 588 ÷ 7 = 84

84 x 2 = 168 white roses

7. Andrea saves Php 45.00 a day. At this rate, how long will she be able to save Php 270.00?

A. 4 B. 5 C. 6 D. 7

Answer: The correct answer is letter C. 6.

Solution:

270 ÷ 45 = 6 days

8. A bag of dog food can feed 4 dogs for 10 days. How long will it feed 5 dogs?

A. 4 B. 8 C. 10 D. 12

Answer: The correct answer is letter B. 8.

Solution:

4 x 10 = 40

40 ÷ 5 = 8 days
