What is the Basic Command of Taekwondo? (English to Korean)

Arc Hand – Ageumson Arm – Pal Attention – Charyut (sound like Chari-yut) Back or Backwards – Dwi (i.e. Dwi Chagi = Back Kick) Basic – Kibon Begin – Sijak Belt – Ti Block – Makgi (sounds like Mah-key) Bow – Kyungnet Breaking – Gyeok-pa, Kyeok-pa, Kyuk-pa, etc.

What is the Tenets of Taekwondo?

Courtesy Integrity Perseverance Self-control Indomitable Spirit

What is the Basic Stances of Taekwondo?

Attention Stance Closed Stance Front Stance Back Stance Ready Stance Rear Foot Stance Sitting Stance

What is the Basic Kicking of Taekwondo?

Front Kick Side Kick Hooking Kick Back Kick Roundhouse Kick

What is the Basic Blocks of Taekwondo?

Absorbing Block Deflecting Block High Block Inside Block Kicking Block Knife Hand Block Single Middle Knife Hand Block Double Middle Knife Hand Block Low Block Middle Block Mountain Block Single Mountain Block Outside Block Outside Forearm Block Double Outside Block Pressing Block Scissors Block Supporting Block or Assisted Block Tripping Block X-Block or Cross Block
