8. Find the volume of a fish tank that is 8 feet long and 5 feet wide, if the height is 5.5 feet?​


Volume

[tex]______________________________________[/tex]

Find the volume of a fish tank that is 8 feet long and 5 feet wide, if the height is 5.5 feet?

==============================

Short Discussion:

Volume of a rectangular prism is the product of it length, width and height. When finding volume of a solid figure, we will ise cubic unit (cubic centimeter or cm³, cubic meter or m³).

V = l⋅w⋅h

Where,

L = Length

W = Width

H = Height

============================

Solving:

Volume of a Rectangular Prism

Solution

Step 1: List down the given

L = 8 ft

W = 5 ft

H = 5.5 ft

Step 2: We will ise the given to substitute on the formula

V = l⋅w⋅h

= 8 ft ⋅ 5 ft ⋅ 5.5 ft

= 40 ft² ⋅ 5.5 ft

V = 220 ft³

Answer:

Therefore, the volume of the fish tank that is 8 feet long, 5 feet wide and 5.5 high is 220 ft³.

10. 5. The closet is 6 feet wide, 5 feet deep and 8 feet tall. In the closet, there is a suitcase that is 2 feet​


Answer:

The 5 feet deep is taken to be the length. It is quite confusing using "deep" and "tall" in the statement.

Volume of the closet, Vc = l × w × h

Vc = 5 × 6 × 8 = 240 ft³

Volume of the suitcase, Vs = l × w × h

Vs = 3 × 2 × 4 = 24 ft³

Volume of the remaining space in the closet, Vr = Vc - Vs

Vr = 240 - 24 = 216 ft³
