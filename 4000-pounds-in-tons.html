5. 432 pounds to tons w/ solution


1 pound (lb) is equal to 0.45359237 tons (t).

1 lb = 0.00045359237 t

The mass m in tons (t) is equal to the mass m in pounds (lb) times 0.00045359237:

m(t) = m(lb) × 0.00045359237

432 lb × 0.00045359237

= 0.19595190384 t
