Step-by-step explanation:

There is no distance so we will multiply the speed and the time

Which is pedro's speed (10kph)

And the time it took mario to catch up with pedro (20min)

10 x 20 = 200

And you could get the speed by dividing distance over time before mario started running at same direction.

200 ÷ 5 = 40

Answer:

D. 9 hrs & 20 min.

Step-by-step explanation:

If the flight was delayed and the plane took off 1 hour and 30 minutes later, then the plane took off at 6:15 + 1:30 = 7:45 A.M

Now, we will convert the time to minutes so it will be easier to solve.

7:45 means 7 hours and 45 minutes. An hour consists of 60 minutes, therefore 7:45 is 7 × 60 + 45 = 465 minutes.
