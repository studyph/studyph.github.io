2. 4.On the figures at the right, ZYVA and ZAVN are complementary angles. IfmzYVA = 25, then what is the measure of m<RLA?A. 285⁰B. 105⁰C. 15⁰D. 5⁰​


Answer:

D. 5⁰​

Step-by-step explanation:

Based on the given information, we know that ZYVA and ZAVN are complementary angles, which means their measures add up to 90°.

mzYVA + mZAVN = 90°

25° + mZAVN = 90°

mZAVN = 90° - 25°

mZAVN = 65°

Now, we can see that angle RLA is vertical to angle ZYVA, which means they have the same measure.

mRLA = mzYVA = 25°

Therefore, the measure of angle RLA is 25°
