Answer:

Your radial pulse can be taken on either wrist. Use the tip of the index and third fingers of your other hand to feel the pulse in your radial artery between your wrist bone and the tendon on the thumb side of your wrist. Apply just enough pressure so you can feel each beat.

3. what is the difference between parallel-line development and Radial-line development as identified methods of pattern developments?​


Answer:

Parallel-line method is employed in case of prisms and cylinders in which stretch out-line principle is used. Radial-line development is used for pyramids and cones in which the true length of the slant edge or the generator is used as a radius.
