Direction: Analyze the given procedure and identify the health related and skill related exercises or activities to be applied. Write your answer on the space provided before the number.

1. Lie flat on the mat, knees bent with heels about six inches from the buttocks, feet slightly apart. Raise yourself up until you assume the starting position and without resting, repeat the movement as many times as you can.

2. Lie face down with your arms at your sides and your hands under your thighs. Look straight ahead and lift your upper body slowly upward to a maximum height of 30 centimeters.

3. Lie face down with your hands on the floor and your thumbs in line with your shoulders Push your trunk off the floor and assume the starting position. Bend your arms at the elbows and lower yourself until your arms are at a right angle position

4. Sit on the floor with the meter stick/ tape measure between your legs/ knees. Extend your arms forward over the meter stick/ tape measure.

5. Sit on the bench with your knees forward and bent and your hands resting on the thighs, with both feet flat on the floor. Make sure that the meter stick is positioned down the middle of your back, approximating the line made by the spine down your back.

6. Stand beyond the take off line, about six centimeters from the left end of the tape. Crouch, swing your arms backward and jump as far as you can.

7. Start at the starting line, at the signal "Go!" sprint toward the finish line.

8. With your right hand, reach over the back of your right shoulder and at the same time, place your left hand behind your back and try to touch the fingers of your right hand.

9. Two-meter sticks of at least three meters in length and accurate to 0.1 cm are mounted horizontally on a wall. The tape measure should be parallel to each other, with the lower one approximately 1 meter above the floor and the upper one at least 1.5 meters above the floor. 10. Sit on the floor with the meter stick/ tape measure between your legs/ knees Extend your arms forward over the meter stick/ tape measure.
