She planned to hire additional 5 sewers. How manychildren dresses will XYZ Garments produce with the addition of 5 sewers?​


Answer:

120 total per day

Step-by-step explanation:

> currently 100 units of children dress per day divided by 25 workers is equal  to 4 per person

> so additional five sewers times 4 per head is equal to 20 additional dresses

> therefore, with additional 5 sewers XYZ Garments will produce 120 units of children dress per day
