9. Construct your own sentence using them. Make sure to write in a complete sentences. -On the other hand-Besides-Assuming that-Because-Since-Despite the fact that-Therefore-Consequently-Thus-Although​


CONSTRUCTING SENTENCES

Construct your own sentence using them.

1) On the other hand

My brother bought an ice cream for dessert. On the other hand, he thought that it would have been nice to buy a coffee jelly.

2) Besides

I feel lazy to start painting; besides, I ran out of art materials.

3) Assuming that

I ran as fast as I could towards my respective classroom; assuming that the teacher is done checking attendance.

4) Because

Me and my mother went out to grocery because we have incomplete ingredients at home.

5) Since

Since there is no classes, I lay down the couch to sleep.

6) Despite the fact that

My little brother couldn't stop laughing; despite the fact that everyone is serious at the moment.

7) Therefore

I concentrated during the examinations; therefore; the teacher gave me high grades.

8) Consequently

My older sister didn't like her writing on the paper; consequently, she crumpled it.

9) Thus

I really like writing essays and stories, thus, I wanted to be a writer.

10) Although

Although there is a loud noice last night, I managed to sleep soundly.
