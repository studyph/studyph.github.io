10. Learning Task 1Direction: Convert the following units of measurements. show your solution/computation on a separate sheet of paper1. 96 inches = _________ feet2. 5 feet = _________ centimeter3. 22 inches = _________ centimeter4. 55 dccimeters = _________ meter5. 3 yard = _________ inches​


Answer:

1. 96 inches = ____ feet

1 inch = 0.0833 feet (divide by 12)

96 × 0.0833 (96 ÷ 12)

= 8 feet

2. 5 feet = ____ centimeter

1 foot = 30.48 centimeters

5 × 30.48

= 152.4 centimeters

3. 22 inches = ____ centimeter

1 inch = 2.54 centimeters

22 × 2.54

= 55.88 centimeters

4. 55 decimeters = ____ meter

1 decimeter = 0.1 meter (divide by 10)

55 × 0.1 (55 ÷ 10)

= 5.5 meters

5. 3 yard = ____ inches

1 yard = 36 inches

3 × 36

= 108 inches
