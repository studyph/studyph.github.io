25. Can someone help me? plss need na Talaga thnkyou What is your reflection about the quotes? “It is easy to add but difficult to maintain” .-french quotes ​


I think this quote speaks to the idea that it's often easier to start something new than it is to sustain it over the long term. This can apply to many different areas of life, whether it's starting a new habit, launching a new project, or building a new relationship.

Adding something new can be exciting and energizing, and there's often a sense of momentum that comes with starting something fresh. But as time goes on, it can become more challenging to maintain that initial level of enthusiasm and effort. It takes consistent effort and dedication to keep something going over the long term, and this can be more difficult than simply starting something new.

This quote is a reminder that while it's important to start new things and take risks, we also need to be mindful of the effort required to maintain them over time. It's important to be realistic about the long-term commitment required and to be prepared to put in the work to sustain our efforts and achieve our goals.
