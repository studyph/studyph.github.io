Step-by-step explanation:

Let y = bending of beam

x = mass

k = constant

Direct variation formula:

y = kx

First, find the value of constant 'k'

Using the formula, we have

k = y/x

k = 20/40

k = 1/2 ( this is the constant)

now, we can solve for y (in the problem)

y = kx

y = 100(1/2)

y = 50 (final answer)

14. 7/10 cm = ________ mm?1/4 kg = ________ g?1/10 t = _________ kg?​


Answer:

1.) 7

2.) 250

3.) 300

Step-by-step explanation:

Convert the fractions to decimal

7/10 will be 0.7

1/4 will be 0.25

1/10 will be 0.1

Since 1 cm = 10 mm, thus 0.7 = 10(0.7) mm = 7

Since 1 kg = 1,000 g, thus 0.25 = 1,000(0.25) g = 250

Since 1 t = 1,000 kg, thus 0.1 = 1,000(0.1) = 100 kg
