27. The length of a rectangle is 3cm less than 3 times its width. If the perimeter of the rectangle is 194 cm, what are its dimensions?


Answer:

Width=25cm

Length=72cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Let X be the width:

X=Width

3X-3=Length

Perimeter of rectangle

2L + 2W

Substitute the values

2(3X-3) + 2(X)=194cm

Simplify

(6X-6)+2X=194cm

8X-6=194cm

Transpose

8X=194+6

8X=200

Divide both sides by 8

8X/8=200/8

X=25

Go back to step 1

Width=X

Length= 3X-3

Substitute

Width=25cm

Length=3(25)-3 or 75-3 which is 72cm
