4. What were the directions for reforms that guided the Aquino administration's reorganization efforts?​


The Aquino administration's reorganization efforts

1. Streamlining of the bureaucracy to make it more efficient and responsive to the needs of the people.

2. Rationalization of government agencies to eliminate redundancy and overlapping functions.

3. Decentralization of powers and resources to local government units to promote local autonomy and development.

4. Strengthening of the civil service system to ensure merit-based recruitment, promotion, and retention of employees.

5. Promotion of transparency and accountability in government through the implementation of anti-corruption measures and the strengthening of oversight mechanisms.

6. Improvement of the delivery of basic services such as health, education, and social welfare to the poor and marginalized sectors of society.

7. Promotion of economic growth and development through the implementation of sound economic policies and programs.

8. Protection of human rights and promotion of peace and security through the strengthening of the justice system and the promotion of peace negotiations with armed groups.

6. What is the social reorganization plan proposed by fray Juan de plasencia in 1582


Answer:

Juan de Plasencia proposed that the Indios (or Filipino natives) were taught Spanish while the missionaries should learn the local language.

Explanation:

Juan de Plasencia was a Franciscan friar from Spain who arrived in the Philippines in 1578. He spent his time visiting various places in Luzon establishing towns, spreading Christianity, and writing religious literature.

He wrote the first book to be published in the country which was Doctrina Cristiana. He made sure to provide a translation of the Spanish book in Tagalog, in Latin script, and also in Baybayin. This was in line with his vision and proposal that both sides should be able to understand each other perfectly for Christianity to be successfully instilled in the Filipinos.

He is an important figure for the preservation of Filipino history. His written works were documentation of the Filipino tradition, culture, and beliefs in local towns. In a way, some of his writings served as a civil code of the time.

27. How is information simplified, reorganized, synthesized, and evaluated to expand, review or update knowledge?​


Benjamin Bloom have constructed a systematic classification of cognitive skills and learning behavior which was referred to as the Bloom’s Taxonomy.  Taxonomy is defined as classification.  The structure, a pyramid in form, shows an order of progressive level of thinking which is as follows:

1. Knowledge – primary level

2. Comprehension

3. Application

4. Analysis

5. Synthesis

6. Evaluation – highest level

Knowledge is the basic level in the hierarchy which is the simplest way of acquiring knowledge like memorizing and taking down notes.  This kind of knowledge acquisition progresses as the intellectual ability becomes more capable of putting value in small pieces and interpreting them in whole.

However, the taxonomy was then revised and re-labeled as:

1. Remembering

2. Understanding

3. Applying

4. Analyzing

5. Evaluating

6. Creating

It is noted that the nouns have been converted to verbs to imply that thinking is an action.
