19. what is 1/4 of 120 added to 1/3 of 120​


Answer:

70

Explanation:

1/4 of 120 is 30,

1/3 of 120 is 40,

40 + 30 = 70.

Answer:

70

Explanation:

Before getting the sum, we must get the products of 1/4 and 120, and 1/3 and 120 respectively.

Let's break down the equation first into parts-

Equation number one: 1/4 x 120 = a

•1/4 and 120 are the coefficients

•1/4 is the fraction coefficient

•120 is the whole number coefficient

•a is the product

Equation number two: 1/3 x 120 = b

•1/3 and 120 are the coefficients

•1/3 is the fraction coefficient

•120 is the whole number coefficient

•b is the product

To get the products for both equations here are the steps:

1.) divide the whole number coefficient with the denominator (number located below the fraction) of the fraction coefficient. So let's apply this with the two equations-

equation number 1:

•the denominator of 1/4 is 4, the whole number coefficient is 120,

•120 divided by 4 = 30

equation number 2:

• the denominator of 1/3 is 3, the whole number coefficient is 120

•120 divided by 3 = 40

2.) Then, we multiply the quotient with the numerator (the number located above the fraction) to get a and b, individually.

Equation number 1:

• the numerator of 1/4 is 1

• the quotient is 30

•. 30 x 1 = a

• a = 30

Equation number 2:

• numerator of 1/3 is 1

• quotient is 40

• 40 x 1 = b

• b= 40

3.) Lastly, we add a and b to get the answer to the question

•a + b = n

•30 + 40 = 70

70 is 1/4 of 120 added to 1/3 of 120
