Step-by-step explanation:

1. The tent resembles a square pyramid.

2. The formula to find the lateral area of a square pyramid is L = (1/2)Pl, where P is the perimeter of the base and l is the slant height of each triangular face. The total amount of canvas used for the tent would be the lateral area plus the area of the base.

3. The perimeter of the base is 4 x 5 = 20m. To find the slant height, we can use the Pythagorean theorem: h^2 = 4^2 + (5/2)^2 = 25.25. Therefore, the slant height is approximately 5.02m. The lateral area is L = (1/2)(20)(5.02) = 50.2m^2. The area of the base is simply 5^2 = 25m^2. Therefore, the total amount of canvas used is 50.2 + 25 = 75.2m^2.

27. Peter and Eric pitched a tent in a shape of a pyramid. The base of the tent is 3meters wide by 4meters long .the tent had a slanted height of meter.what was the surface area of the tent


Answer:

In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebraic expression and basic mathematics especially in solving word problems about surface area.

Problem

Peter and Eric pitched a tent that had a shape of a pyramid. The base of the tent is 3 meters wide by 4 meters long. The tent was 5 meters high. What was the surface area of the tent?

Given

Base of tent is 3 m by 4 m

h = 5 m

Solution

Surface area of triangle prism is the sum of all area of faces.

Triangular Face 1 = 1/2BH → 1/2(3)(5) = 7.5 m²

Triangular Face 2 = 1/2BH → 1/2(4)(5) = 10 m²

Rectangular Base = LW → (3)(4) = 12 m²

Surface Area = 2(7.5) + 2(10) + 12

Surface Area = 47 m²

THE CORRECT ANSWER IS 47 m²
