9. novelty of the research about the advantage and disadvantage of social media to the student in senior high school to the performance of the academic performance senior high school ​


The novelty of the research topic is that it focuses specifically on the impact of social media on the academic performance of senior high school students. While there have been many studies conducted on the general effects of social media on students, this research aims to delve deeper into how it specifically affects senior high school students.

The research problem of this topic could be:

1. What are the advantages and disadvantages of using social media among senior high school students in relation to their academic performance?

2. How does social media use affect the study habits and time management skills of senior high school students?

3. What are the factors that contribute to the positive and negative effects of social media on the academic performance of senior high school students?

The chosen research problem is important because it addresses a relevant issue among senior high school students, who are at a critical stage in their education. The results of the research could provide insights to educators and parents on how to properly regulate the use of social media to maximize its advantages and minimize its disadvantages in relation to academic performance.

18. Why is novelty included as a requirement in patenting?​


ANSWER: An invention is not new and therefore not patentable if it was known to the public before the filing date of the patent application, or before its date of priority if the applicant claims priority of an earlier patent application. The purpose of the novelty requirement is to prevent prior art from being patented again.

24. Why novelty is not an essential part of innovation?


Answer:

Novelty is essential for innovation; but it is not the definitive factor for its success.

Truly novel ideas that are proven to solve a major problem in a unique way, create business opportunities, and may also enrich the state-of-the-art. Technical novelty usually leads to Intellectual Property and differentiators for products and services.

However, novelty depends on the point of view, and it is not binary — it needs to be expressed using a degree, a level of newness. Real-world products and solutions are complex — they encapsulate multiple ideas some of which may be known, while others may be appearing for the first time. The degree of newness reflects how many of those essential features are, indeed, novel.
