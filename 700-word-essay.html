19. write a 700 words essay about Why does oral presentation related to communication for work purposes?​


Answer:

Oral presentations are an important part of communication for work purposes. Oral presentations provide a platform for individuals to share their ideas, opinions, and experiences in a structured and organized manner. They also allow for the exchange of ideas between individuals in a professional setting.

Oral presentations are effective for communication in the workplace because they provide a face-to-face interaction between the presenter and the audience. This allows for a more personal connection and better understanding of the message being conveyed. Oral presentations also allow for a more detailed explanation of the topic at hand, which can be especially important when discussing complex topics or ideas.

Oral presentations also allow for the audience to ask questions and provide feedback. This is beneficial because it allows the presenter to gain a better understanding of the audience’s thoughts and opinions on the topic. This feedback can then be used to adjust the presentation accordingly, ensuring that the message is effectively conveyed.

Oral presentations also provide an opportunity for the presenter to practice their public speaking skills. Presenters can learn how to effectively engage their audience, use body language to convey their message, and use visual aids to help illustrate their points. These skills are essential for effective communication in the workplace.

Finally, oral presentations are a great way to build relationships in the workplace. By presenting to an audience, the presenter is able to establish a rapport with the audience. This can help to build trust and respect between the presenter and the audience, which can be beneficial for future collaborations and projects.

In conclusion, oral presentations are essential for effective communication in the workplace. They provide a face-to-face interaction between the presenter and the audience, allow for a more detailed explanation of the topic, provide a platform for the audience to ask questions and provide feedback, and provide an opportunity for the presenter to practice their public speaking skills. Additionally, oral presentations can help to build relationships in the workplace. For these reasons, oral presentations are an important part of communication for work purposes.
