1. 1: What is the purpose of planned maintenance(PM)? 2: What is the purpose of Condition Based Maintenance(CBM)?3: What is the purpose of Corrective maintenance? ​


1.What is the purpose of planned maintenance(PM)?

The purpose of planned maintenance is to reduce downtime and improve the efficiency of operations. It is usually carried out to have all the necessary resources on hand to minimize downtime.

2. What is the purpose of Condition Based Maintenance(CBM)?

This strategy involves carefully analyzing an asset's condition and its purpose is to determine when maintenance is needed.

3. What is the purpose of Corrective maintenance?

The purpose of corrective maintenance is to correct and repair faulty equipment and systems.

3. what are the purpose of preventive maintenance??​


Answer:

Preventive maintenance emphasizes regularly scheduled maintenance tasks. The goal of preventive maintenance is to give an asset the care it requires while it's still running. This approach actively minimizes the chance of failure, costly repairs and unscheduled downtime.

8. What is the main purpose of having a vehicle undergo regular vehicle maintenance inspection?


The main purpose of having a vehicle undergoes regular vehicle maintenance inspection is to ensure that the vehicle is in safe and proper working condition.

Explanation :

Carrying out proper vehicle inspections and routine maintenance can reduce the risk of traffic accidents. Inspectors will help ensure all vehicle materials are operating properly and safely. Besides that, the vehicle owner also has an obligation to ensure that the work vehicle is inspected and maintained properly.

Regular vehicle maintenance inspection also can help prevent breakdowns, as well as extend the lifespan of the vehicle. In addition regular vehicle maintenance inspections also help to identify and address any potential issues before they become more serious and costly to repair. Under OSHA laws, employers are responsible for maintaining a safe workplace for their employees.
