Step-by-step explanation:

Logic Pattern

The given above is an example of logic pattern. Answering logic pattern is tricky. It involves logical thinking and reasoning skills.

To find the next number in the pattern above. We need to find the relationship of the given numbers. We need to come up with a rule and apply it to find the next logical number.

Let us analyze the given pattern above.

11 + 11 = 4

22 + 22 = 16

33 + 33 = ?

Based on every answer, it is very clear that we cannot use the given addition sign to come up with the answer.

From the first two equations, we can say that the rule is to add the two digits in each addend and multiply them.

11 + 11 = 4

(1 + 1) × (1 + 1) = 2 × 2 = 4

22 + 22 = 16

(2 + 2) × (2 + 2) = 4 × 4 = 16

33 + 33 = ?

(3 + 3) × (3 + 3) = 6 × 6 = 36

Therefore, the missing number is 36.
