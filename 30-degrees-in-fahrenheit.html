6. Convert 35.6 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit Select one: a. 96.1 degrees Fahrenheit b. 2 degrees Fahrenheit c. 308.75 degrees Fahrenheit d. 69.1 degrees Fahrenheit


35.6 °C = 96.1 °F

Further explanation  

Temperature is one of the principal quantities that shows the degree of heat or cold from an object/space  

Temperature is used as a guide to the level of heat energy from objects  

Temperature measures thermal energy  

The temperature conversion is  :

°F = (9/5) °C + 32  

°R = (4/5) °C  

°K = °C + 273  

°C = 5/9 (°F-32)  

35.6 °C to °F

[tex]\rm ^oF=(\dfrac{9}{5}\times 35.6)+ 32=\boxed{\bold{96.1^oF}}[/tex]
