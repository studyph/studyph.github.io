28. A steel rod, 31 cm long is to be cut into three parts, two of which parts are to be of equal lengths. The lengths of each of the two equal parts is 5cm more than the third part. Find their lengths


Answer:

The first and second part is 12cm long while the third part is 7cm long.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x represent the length

(x + 5) + (x + 5) + x = 31

Combine like terms.

3x + 10 = 31

Transpose 10 and change its sign.

3x = 31 - 10

Subtract.

3x = 21

Divide both sides by 3.

3x/3 = 21/3

x = 7

The length of the third part is 7cm.

x + 5

7 + 5 = 12 cm

The length of the first and second part is 12cm.

Checking:

(x + 5) + (x + 5) + x = 31

(7 + 5) + (7 + 5) + 7 = 31

12 + 12 + 7 = 31

31 = 21
