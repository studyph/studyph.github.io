4. How a metabolic pathway can be self-regulating


Metabolic Control

The interrelated complexity of the metabolic pathways. To maintain homeostasis, or a steady set of variables within cells, metabolism's reactions must be carefully controlled in response to changing settings. Organisms can respond to signals and engage in active environmental interaction thanks to the control of metabolic pathways.

How can a metabolic pathway be self-regulating?

The reactions self-regulate to react to variations in the quantities of substrates or products for intrinsic metabolic pathway regulation. For instance, a reduction in product quantity may result in an increase in the metabolic pathway.  An example of this is a feedback mechanism.Increasing or lowering an enzyme's sensitivity to signals is one way that metabolic pathways can be regulated.Control requires keeping track of the impacts that these variations in an enzyme's activity have on the pathway's overall rate. For instance, a highly regulated enzyme may exhibit significant fluctuations in activity, but if these changes have little impact on the flux of a metabolic route, the enzyme is not implicated in the pathway's control.

To ensure that the output of metabolic pathways matches biological demand and that energy in the form of ATP is not wasted by having opposing pathways running concurrently in the same cell, exquisite systems that regulate the flux of metabolites via these pathways have evolved.

12. spiritual and mental self regulations examples​


Answer:

01

Emotional Self-Care

Activities that help you connect, process, and reflect on a full range of emotions.

Examples: Seeing a therapist, writing in a journal, creating art, playing music etc.

02

Practical Self-Care

Tasks you complete that fulfill core aspects of your life in order to prevent future stressful situations.

Examples: Creating a budget, taking professional development classes, organizing your closet, etc.

03

Physical Self-Care

Activities you do that improve the well-being of your physical health.

Examples: Taking a walk during lunch breaks, sleeping eight hours a day, staying hydrated, etc.

04

Mental Self-Care

Any activity the stimulates your mind or your intellect.

Examples: Reading a book, solving a puzzle, playing chess, going to a museum, etc.

05

Social Self-Care

Activities that nurtures and deepens the relationships with people in your life.

Examples: Brunch with friends, going on a date, making time to call your mom regularly, etc.

06

Spiritual Self Care

Activities that nurtures your spirit and allows you to think bigger than yourself. Spiritual self care does not have to be religious, although for some it is.

Examples: Meditation, yoga, going to a place of worship, be in nature, dedicate time for self-reflection, etc.

15. Tagalog explanation at example ng Regulating Markets.?


Explanation:

Ang "Regulating Markets" ay ang pagpapatakbo ng pamahalaan sa mga merkado upang maprotektahan ang mga mamimili at mapangalagaan ang kalagayan ng mga negosyante. Ito ay ginagawa upang maiwasan ang mga hindi makatarungang praktisyo tulad ng pag-abuso ng mga negosyante sa kanilang kapangyarihan sa merkado at pagpapataas ng presyo ng mga produkto ng sobrang taas.

Isang halimbawa ng regulasyon sa merkado ay ang pagpapataw ng limitasyon sa presyo ng mga pangunahing bilihin tulad ng bigas, gulay, at karne. Sa ganitong paraan, hindi maaaring magtayo ng sobrang taas na presyo ang mga negosyante dahil mayroong limitasyon ang presyo na itinakda ng pamahalaan.

Ang regulasyon sa merkado aylnagalayong matulungan ang mga mamimili na makatipid at maipaglaban ang kanilang karapatan bilang konsumers. Sa kabilang banda, ito ay nagbibigay rin ng proteksyon at oportunidad sa mga negosyante na makapag-operate ng patas at magkaroon ng makatwirang kita sa kanilang negosyo.

Sa kabuuan, ang pagpapatakbo ng merkado ay isang mahalagang gawain ng pamahalaan upang maprotektahan ang interes ng lahat ng panig at mailayo sa anumang uri ng pang-aabuso sa merkado.
