The perimeter of the glider is to be trimmed with a special material. What is the minimum length of material needed? A. 54 feet B. 58 feet C. 64 feet D. 78 feet


Answer:

To find the minimum length of material needed, we need to find the distance between the two given points and add it to the sum of the distances from each point to the x-axis.

Using the distance formula, we can find the distance between (12,7) and (24,2) as:

sqrt((24-12)^2 + (2-7)^2) = sqrt(144 + 25) = sqrt(169) = 13

The distance from (12,7) to the x-axis is 7, and the distance from (24,2) to the x-axis is 2. So the total length of material needed is:

13 + 7 + 2 = 22
