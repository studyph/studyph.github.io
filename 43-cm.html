11. what is the magnification of a convex mirror if it produced a 43 cm high image from a 55 cm high object​


The magnification of a mirror can be calculated using the formula: magnification = -(image height / object height)

Given that the image height is 43 cm and the object height is 55 cm, we can calculate the magnification as follows:

magnification = -(43 cm / 55 cm) = -0.78

The magnification of a convex mirror is -0.78. This means that the image is smaller than the object, and it is also flipped horizontally.
