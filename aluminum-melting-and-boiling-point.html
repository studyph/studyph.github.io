28. 2. A mixture is a material consisting of po two or more kind of matter, each of which retains its own characteristic properties and has its own respective boiling and melting point. Which of these substances is an example of mixture? O aluminum foil O seawater


Answer:

seawater

Explanation:

Seawater is a mixture of water (H2O), salts and other materials in trace amounts. It has its own boiling point. Seawater of 35 psu has a specific heat of 0.932 compared with 1.000 for pure water. When salt is added, the freezing point is lowered and the boiling point is raised. Freshwater freezes at 32°F (0°C), but the freezing point of seawater is lower: about 28°F (-2° C). The saltier the water is, the colder it has to be to freeze.
