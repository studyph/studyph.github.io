28. he frequency of a vibrating string varies inversely as its length. A string 3 feet long vibrates 175 cycles per second. Find the frequency of a 5-foot string.


Answer:

A 5-foot string vibrates 105 cycles per second.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let Frequency = f, length = l

Finding value of k:

f = k/l (because of inverse variation)

175 = k/3 (substitute given value)

k = 525 (multiply both sides by 3)

Solving for asked:

f = 525/5

f = 105
