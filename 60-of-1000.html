17. calculate the kenitec energy of a 1000 kg ac traveling at 60 km/h​


Answer:

KE = 138,894.445 J

Explanation:

Let:

KE = kinetic energy

m = mass

v = velocity

Given:

m = 1000 kg

v = 60 km/hr

Solution:

First, we need to convert the velocity to m/s so that the units are consistent

v = 60 km/hr (1 hr/3600 s)(1000 m/1 km)

v = 16.667 m/s

KE = (1/2)mv²

KE = (1/2)(1000)(16.667)²

KE = 138,894.445 J (answer)
