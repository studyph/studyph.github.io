Answer:

1. Dynamic stretching means active, energetic, or vigorous, so dynamic stretching involves movement – usually of more than one muscle group.

Dynamic stretching examples:Lunge with a Twist, Knee to Chest,

2. Static stretching means stationary, so static stretches isolate one muscle group at a time and hold a position rather than moving through a range of motion.

static stretching examples: Biceps stretch, Head-to-knee forward bend, Cobra Pose, Seated butterfly stretch.
