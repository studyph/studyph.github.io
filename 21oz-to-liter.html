30. a car travels 140km on 7liters of petrol. how far would travel in 3 liters of petrol


Question

    A car travels 140 km on 7 liters of petrol. How far would it travel in 3 liters of petrol?

Answer

    The answer for the question is 60 km.

Explanation

    This type of question is an example of a Direct Proportion. In Direct Proportion, when one quantity increases, the other quantity increases at the same rate and vice versa.

Solving

    For every liter of petrol, you can travel 20 km. Based on the question that is given, we have 3 liters of petrol.

Formula

    140 ÷ 7 = 20 (km)

    20 x 3 = 60 (km)

Conclusion

    The car can travel 60 km in 3 liters of petrol.
