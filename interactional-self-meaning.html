What is interactional self means?

Interactional self means the portrayed personality among the society where the coping mechanisms happen just to comply with the standards of the group regardless of your personal point of view and standard. Due to the need to interact with others, one might consider other person's actions, decisions, and philosophies so that smooth, harmonious working relationship is maintained.
