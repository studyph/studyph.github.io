12. Find the surface area of this wooden box 20 cm, 20cm and 23cm


Answer:

The surface area of the wooden box is 2640 cm².

Step-by-step explanation:

Given :

The surface area of the cuboidal wooden box is 20 cm, 20 cm, and 23 cm.

To Find :

Total surface area of the wooden box.

Solution :

By using the formula of Total surface area of cuboid, we will find the Total surface area of the wooden box. Here, l = 20 cm, b = 20 cm, h = 23 cm.

According to the question :

[tex] \sf \longrightarrow {TSA\ =\ 2(lb\ +\ bh\ +\ hl)} [/tex]

[tex] \sf \longrightarrow {TSA\ =\ 2(20 \times 20\ +\ 20 \times 23\ +\ 23 \times 20)} [/tex]

[tex] \sf \longrightarrow {TSA\ =\ 2(400\ +\ 460\ +\ 460)} [/tex]

[tex] \sf \longrightarrow {TSA\ =\ 2 \times 1320} [/tex]

[tex] \sf \longrightarrow {\underline{TSA\ =\ 2640\ cm^2}} \bigstar [/tex]

∴ Hence, the required surface area of the wooden box is 2640 cm².

22. a piece of paper measure 23cm by 30cm. what is it's area in cm2?​


AREA

PROBLEM

A piece of paper measure 23cm by 30cm. What is it's area in cm²?

ANSWER

The area of the given piece of paper is 690 cm².

SOLUTION

We know that a piece of paper is a rectangular shape. Thus, we will use the formula for the area of a rectangle which is A = L × W.

Since the given measurements are in its eight unit already, we can just substitute it to the formula.

A = L × W

A = 23 cm × 30 cm

A = 690 cm²

Therefore, the area of the given piece of paper is 690 cm².
