9. Why are concave mirrors used in car headlights and flashlights?​


Answer:

The light emanating from the bulb used in a flashlight or a car headlight spreads out is all directions. So we need something to ensure that most of the rays of light converge towards the desired direction. For this purpose we need a converging mirror and hence concave or parabolic mirrors are used.
