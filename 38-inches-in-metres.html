10. Mr. Garces had trapezoidal field planted with mongo. If the field has bases of 12 metres and 15 metres and a height of 7 metres. What is the area of the field?


Trapedoid Formula:

[tex]a = \frac{1}{2} h \times (b _{1} + b_{2}) \\ a = 3.5m \times 27m \\ a =94.5 [/tex]

Answer:

94.5 square meters

Computation:

Given,

[tex]a=12\:m\\b=15\:m\\h=7\:m\\A=\:?[/tex]

Solving for the area of the trapezoidal field.

[tex]A=\frac{a+b}{2}h\\\\A=\frac{12\:m+15\:m}{2}(7\:m)\\\\A=\frac{27\:m}{2}(7\:m)\\\\A=13.5\:m(7\:m)\\\\\boxed{A=94.5\:m^2}[/tex]

20. In three minutes Gabrielle can walk 300 metres. In five minutes Mara can walk 600 metres. They started walking at the same time along the same track. How far apart were they after 4 minutes? A 80 metres B 100 metres C 120 metres D 300 metres


The Answer is A 80 metres


Gabriel's Speed = 300m/3min = 100m/min

Mara's Speed = 600m/5min = 120m/min

Speed Difference = 120 - 100 = 20m/min

Distance = Speed Difference X Time = 20m/min X 4min = 80m

Distance each walked in 4 minutes:

Gabrielle:

3:300 = 4:x

3x/3 = 1,200/3

x = 400 meters

Mara:

5:600 = 4:x

5x/5 = 2,400/5

x = 480 meters

Difference of distance walked in 4 minutes:

480 - 400 = 80

ANSWER: (A) 80 meters
