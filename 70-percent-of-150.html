30. 31. of the 250 students, 70% were able to join the field trip. How many students were not able to join it?A 75B. 125C.95D.802 na Math quiz, 30 out of 150 contestants qualified for the final round. What percent of the contestants​


Answer:

A. 75

Step-by-step explanation:

Given

250 = total number of students

70% = percentage of students who joined the trip

N = number of students who did not join

Solution:

250 × 70% = N

70% ÷ 100 = 0.7-----> convert the percentage to decimal first.

Find the exact number of students that joined first

250 × 0.7 = 175

175 = the exact total number of students that joined the trip

250 - 175 = 75

N = 75, the number of students that were not able to join.

A shorter solution:

250 is the total of all students, so if all of them joined it would be 100%.

But 70% only joined, so there's 30% left

Divide 30 over 100, you get 0.3

250 × 0.3 = 75
