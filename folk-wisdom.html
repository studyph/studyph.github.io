8. Differentiate practical wisdom to theoretical wisdom?​


Answer:

Theoretical wisdom is wisdom that is concerned with unchanging truth. It is not concerned with things that we can change. Practical wisdom is about the things that we can change. But it is not just a matter of applying a bunch of theoretical rules to a particular situation
