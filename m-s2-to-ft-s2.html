22. What is the acceleration of a ball with a mass of 0.20 kg is hit with a force of 16N?A. 0.08 m/s2B. 0.8 m/s2C. 80 m/s2D. 800 m/s2​


ACCELERATION

==================================

Problem

What is the acceleration of a ball with a mass of 0.20 kg is hit with a force of 16N?

Solution

Using the acceleration formula to determine the acceleration itself

[tex]\large\tt{ Acceleration = \frac{Force(n) }{Mass (kg) }}[/tex]

[tex]\large\tt{Acceleration = \frac{16\:n}{0.20\: kg}}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\large\tt\red{Acceleration = 80\: m/s² }}[/tex]

Final Answer

Therefore, the acceleration produced by the ball that has a force of 16 n is 80 m/s²Option C || 80 m/s

==================================
