12. based on your understanding, write a narrative report about the main objective of the game volleyball ​


Answer:

The main objective of the game volleyball is to score points by hitting the ball over the net and into the opposing team's court. The team that is able to do this successfully and prevent the other team from returning the ball wins the point. The team that wins the most points wins the game.

The game is played on a rectangular court, with a net in the middle that divides the court into two halves. Each team consists of six players, and the objective is to hit the ball over the net and into the opposing team's court without the other team being able to return it. The ball is hit with the hands or arms, and must be returned in three hits or less.

To score a point, the ball must be served from behind the back line, and must land within the opposing team's court. The team that wins the point gets to serve the next one, and the game continues until one team reaches the predetermined number of points to win the game.

Volleyball requires a combination of physical skill, teamwork, and strategy. Players must be able to move quickly and jump high to hit the ball, and must work together to communicate and coordinate their movements to effectively defend and attack. The game is fast-paced and exciting, and can be played both indoors and outdoors.

Overall, the main objective of the game volleyball is to score points by hitting the ball over the net and into the opposing team's court, and to prevent the other team from returning the ball. Through physical skill, teamwork, and strategy, players compete to win the most points and emerge victorious.
