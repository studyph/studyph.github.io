The first reason why its important to know our clients, is because, as a marketing team we as a whole have an understanding the client and what they actually want to achieve. This knowledge is vital as we develop our market and our marketing, to ensure the best outcomes for our clients.

The market profile of a client is key and should be included with all other aspects of their strategy in order to ensure they are able to reach the targeted/desired market. 21. give an example of a company or brand that practice market skimming.Difine or profile the early address who are willing to pay the premium for getting the product earlier than most.​


Answer:

One example of a company that practices market skimming is Apple Inc. Apple is known for launching new products at a premium price point and gradually lowering the price over time as the product becomes more widely adopted.

When Apple launches a new product, the early adopters who are willing to pay a premium to be the first to have the product are typically tech-savvy individuals who value innovation and are willing to spend money on high-end technology. These early adopters are often seen as trendsetters and influencers within their social circles, and they may be willing to pay more for the prestige and exclusivity of being the first to own a new Apple product.

Apple's marketing strategy is designed to target these early adopters through carefully crafted product launches, limited edition releases, and exclusive events that generate hype and anticipation around new products. By leveraging the enthusiasm of early adopters, Apple is able to generate significant revenue and establish a strong brand image that emphasizes innovation, quality, and exclusivity.
