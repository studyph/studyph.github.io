8. how fast did you go if your family traveled 1500 km in 2 hours​


Answer:

The speed at which your family was traveling was 750 kilometers per hour.

Step-by-step explanation:

If your family traveled 1500 km in 2 hours, then the speed at which you were traveling can be calculated by dividing the distance by the time.

So,

Speed = Distance / Time

Speed = 1500 km / 2 hours

Speed = 750 km/hour

to calculate speed, you can use the formula: Speed = Distance / Time. In this case, the speed would be:

Speed = 1500 km / 2 hours = 750 km/h

So if a family traveled 1500 km in 2 hours, their average speed would be 750 km/h.
