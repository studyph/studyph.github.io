For Grade 10: Activity 1 for the record: This is a two part activity that highlights the recording and/or the broadcasting technologies as an old yet ever evolving popular application of the relationship between electricity and magnetism which is also known as electromagnetic induction. Electromagnetic induction or sometimes referred as induction is a process wherein a conductor placed in a changing magnetic field (or a conductor moving through a stationary magnetic field) causes the production of a voltage across the conductor.

Infrared radiation
