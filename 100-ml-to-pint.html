10. Acyclovir (ZOVIRAX) suspension con- tains 200 mg of acyclovir in each 5 mL... How many milligrams of acyclovir are contained in a pint (473 ml.) of suspen- sion


Answer:

To determine the amount of acyclovir contained in a pint (473 mL) of suspension, you need to multiply the concentration of acyclovir in the suspension by the volume of the suspension. The concentration of acyclovir in the suspension is 200 mg/5 mL, and the volume of the suspension is 473 mL.

So, to find the amount of acyclovir contained in a pint of suspension, you can perform the following calculation:

(200 mg/5 mL) x (473 mL) = <<200/5*473=9460>>9460 mg

Therefore, a pint (473 mL) of acyclovir suspension contains approximately 9460 mg of acyclovir.
