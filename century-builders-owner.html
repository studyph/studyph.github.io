1. Example of 21st century nation builders


A great example is former US president Barack Obama.He was raised largely in Hawaii, Obama also spent one year of his childhood in Washington State and four years in Indonesia. 
After graduating from Columbia University in 1983, he worked as a community organizer in Chicago. In 1988 Obama enrolled in Harvard Law School, where he was the first black president of the Harvard Law Review. After graduation, he became a civil rights attorney and professor, and taught constitutional law at the University of Chicago Law School from 1992 to 2004. In 2008, Obama was nominated for president, a year after his campaign began, and after a close primary campaign against Hillary Clinton. He was elected over Republican John McCain, and was inaugurated on January 20, 2009. Nine months later, Obama was named the 2009 Nobel Peace Prize laureate. After winning re-election by defeating Republican opponent Mitt Romney, Obama was sworn in for a second term in 2013.

During his first two years in office, Obama signed many landmark bills. Main reforms were the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (often referred to as "Obamacare", shortened as the "Affordable Care Act", the Dodd–Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act, and the Don't Ask, Don't Tell Repeal Act of 2010. The American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 and Tax Relief, Unemployment Insurance Reauthorization, and Job Creation Act of 2010 served as economic stimulus amidst the Great Recession, but the GOP regained control of the House of Representatives in 2011. After a lengthy debate over the national debt limit, Obama signed the Budget Control and the American Taxpayer Relief Acts. In foreign policy, Obama increased U.S. troop levels in Afghanistan, reduced nuclear weapons with the United States–Russia New START treaty, and ended military involvement in the Iraq War. He ordered military involvement in Libya in opposition to Muammar Gaddafi, and the military operation that resulted in the death of Osama bin Laden. 
During his second term, Obama promoted inclusiveness for LGBT Americans, with his administration filing briefs that urged the Supreme Court to strike down same-sex marriage bans as unconstitutional (United States v. Windsor and Obergefell v. Hodges). Obama advocated for gun control in response to the Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting, and issued wide-ranging executive actions concerning climate change and immigration. In foreign policy, Obama ordered military intervention in Iraq in response to gains made by ISIL after the 2011 withdrawal from Iraq, continued the process of ending U.S. combat operations in Afghanistan, promoted discussions that led to the 2015 Paris Agreement on global climate change, initiated sanctions against Russia following the invasion in Ukraine and again after Russian interference in the 2016 United States elections, brokered a nuclear deal with Iran, and normalized U.S. relations with Cuba. Obama left office in January 2017 with a 60% approval rating. He currently resides in Washington, D.C. His presidential library will be built in Chicago.

2. II. TITLE OF THE ACTIVITY: “Digital Transformation” III. ACTIVITY: Bilang isang 21st Century Nation Builder, anong modernong solusyon ang maaari mong imungkahi upang matugunan ang mga pangangailangan sa hinaharap? Sumulat ng isang sanaysay tungkol dito at talakayin ang iyong plano kung papaano mo ipapatupad ito sa inyong pamayanan. "I am a 21st Century Nation Builder" ______________________________​


Bilang isang 21st Century Nation Builder, isa sa mga modernong solusyon na maaring kong imungkahi upang matugunan ang mga pangangailangan sa hinaharap ay ang "Digital Transformation". Ang digital transformation ay ang proseso ng paggamit ng mga teknolohiya at digital na kahaliling mga pamamaraan upang baguhin at mapabuti ang mga tradisyunal na paraan ng gawain at serbisyo sa isang organisasyon o pamayanan.

Sa pamamagitan ng digital transformation, ang aking plano ay ipatupad ang mga sumusunod na hakbang sa aming pamayanan:

1. Pagpapalawak ng access sa internet: Upang maabot ang digital transformation, kailangan ang malawakang access sa internet sa aming pamayanan. Ito ay maaaring makamit sa pamamagitan ng pagpapalawak ng imprastruktura ng internet, pagtatayo ng mga Wi-Fi hotspots sa mga pampublikong lugar, at pagbibigay ng subsidyo o tulong sa mga indibidwal o komunidad na walang kakayahang magbayad ng internet service.

2. Pagkakaroon ng digital literacy programs: Upang matiyak na ang lahat ng mamamayan ay maaaring makibahagi sa digital transformation, kailangan ng mga programang naglalayong palawakin ang kaalaman sa digital literacy. Ito ay maaaring isagawa sa pamamagitan ng mga pampublikong paaralan, komunidad, o pribadong sektor na nagbibigay ng mga libreng kurso o pagsasanay sa paggamit ng mga teknolohiya tulad ng computer, internet, at iba pang digital na kahalili ng mga tradisyunal na paraan ng komunikasyon at gawain.

3. Pagtulong sa mga lokal na negosyo sa digital transformation: Ang digital transformation ay maaari ring magbigay ng oportunidad sa mga lokal na negosyo na mapalawak ang kanilang market reach at mapataas ang kanilang kita. Bilang isang Nation Builder, maaari kong ipatupad ang mga programa na naglalayong tulungan ang mga lokal na negosyo na makaagapay sa digital transformation. Ito ay maaaring kasama ang mga pagsasanay sa e-commerce, online marketing, at digital financial management.

4. Pagpapalaganap ng mga digital na serbisyo sa pamahalaan: Ang pamahalaan ay maaaring maging modelo ng digital transformation sa pamayanan. Maaari kong ipatupad ang mga programa na naglalayong mapabuti ang mga serbisyong pampubliko sa pamamagitan ng digital na pamamaraan tulad ng online pagbabayad ng buwis, online application ng mga permits at lisensya, at iba pang online na transaksyon sa mga kahaliling paraan ng gawain.

5. Pagsulong ng cybersecurity at data privacy: Sa panahon ng digital transformation, mahalaga rin ang kaligtasan ng mga indibidwal at organisasyon laban sa mga cybersecurity threats at paglabag sa data privacy. 9. 1. If a body builder does 6000) of work in 1 minute and 15 seconds, what is the body builders power output?


The power output is 80 watts

Explanation:

Note: Check the unit of work. If the unit of work is in Joules, refer to the solution below:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

W = 6000 J

t = 1 min and 15 s = 60 s + 15 s = 75 s

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Power

Step 3: Solve for Power

Solution:

The formula for power is given by:

P = W/t

where: P - Power

W - work

t - time

Substituting the value of W and t in the formula,

P = (6,000 Nm)/(75 s)

P = 80 J/s or 80 watts
