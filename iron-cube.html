11. What is the density of a 64 gram iron cube that displaces 8 mL of water?


Answer:

To solve for density, we use the formula density = mass / volume also known as p = m / V.

When we substitute the given, we get:

p = 64 g / 8 mL = 8 g/mL or 8 g/cm^3

Therefore, the density of a 64 g iron cube that displaces 8 ml of water is 8 g/cm^3.
