18. How to understand (FDT) Frequency Distribution Table?​


To understand Frequency Distribution Table (FDT),let us first define frequency.

Frequency tells you how often something happened.The frequency of observation tells you the number of times the observation appears in the data.For example,in the following list of numbers

1,. 2,. 3,. 4,. 9,. 6,. 6,. 8,.

5,. 1,. 1,. 6,. 6,. 0,. 9,. 6,.

Number 6 appears in the list.So, the frequency of the number 6 is 5.

Frequency Distribution Tables (FDT) also allows you find patterns in your data.

To understand Frequency Distribution Tables (FDT) let's us organize the data.The following are the steps:

Step 1: Create a table with 3 columns.Label each column accordingly as to Title of the categories Scores,Tally and Frequency.

Step 2: Write the categories of the result gathered (scores) in the first column

Note:You can stop at 25 since it is the lowest score.

Step 3:Tally the numbers in each category based on the given data.

Step 4:Count the tally marks and write the frequency in the 3rd column

Step 5: Finalize the table,make sure to right the title
