15. Y varies directly as x and y = 40 when x = 16. Find x when y = 30. WITH SOLUTION​


Answer:

The value of x when y equals 30 is 12.

Step-by-step explanation:

Mathematical Equation:

[tex]\LARGE\text{$y=kx$}[/tex]

For the constant of variation (k):

Given:   [tex]y=40[/tex],   [tex]x=16[/tex]

Find:   [tex]k=?[/tex]

Formula:   [tex]y=kx[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]y=kx\\40=16k\\\frac{40}{16}=\frac{16k}{16}\\k=\frac{40}{16}\\k=\frac{5}{2}[/tex]

For the value of x:

Given:   [tex]k=\frac{5}{2}[/tex],   [tex]y=30[/tex]

Find:   [tex]x=?[/tex]

Formula:   [tex]y=kx[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]y=kx\\30=\frac{5}{2}x\\5x=(30)(2)\\5x=60\\\frac{5x}{5}=\frac{60}{5}\\\boxed{\boldsymbol{x=12}}[/tex]
