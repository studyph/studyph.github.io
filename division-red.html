(Hint: What is the total number of red blood cells in the body? What fraction of them must be replaced each day if all are replaced in 120 days?)


Answer:

In the bloodstream, iron picks up oxygen in the lungs and drops it off in the tissues; the amino acids in hemoglobin then transport carbon dioxide from the tissues back to the lungs. Erythrocytes live only 120 days on average, and thus must be continually replaced.

Two

There are two ways cell division can happen in humans and most other animals, called mitosis and meiosis. When a cell divides by way of mitosis, it produces two clones of itself, each with the same number of chromosomes. When a cell divides by way of meiosis, it produces four cells, called gametes.

Each circulation takes about 60 seconds (one minute). Approximately 84% of the cells in the human body are 20–30 trillion red blood cells. Nearly half of the blood's volume (40% to 45%) is red blood cells.

Blood cells are flexible and contain a lot of hemoglobin, the protein which binds to oxygen. Due to this loss of a nucleus and other organelles, blood cells cannot repair themselves when damaged; this limits their lifespan to about 120 days.

12. Learning Task #1: Find the probability of the following problems.A bowl contains 15 chips numbered 1 to 15. If a chip is drawn randomly from the bowl, what is the probability that it is1. 7 or 15?2.5 or a number divisible by 3?3. even or divisible by 3?4. a number divisible by 3 or divisible by 4?5. Dario puts 44 marbles in a box in which 14 are red, 12 are blue, and 18 are yellow. If Dario picks one marble atrandom, what is the probability that he selects a red marble or a yellow marble?​


PROBABILITY

1. The probability of 7 or 15 is 2/15.

Explanation:

Because the chips only have one label 7, the probability is 1/15. Because the chips only have one label 15, the probability is 1/15.

    When the probabilities of 7 and 15 are added, the answer is 2/15.

Solution:

                  [tex]\frac{1}{15}+\frac{1}{15}=\frac{2}{15}[/tex]

2. 5 or a number divisible by 3 has a probability of 2/5.

Explanation:

The probability is 1/15 due to the fact that there is only one label 5 in the chips.The list of numbers that is divisible by 3 fro 1-15 are 3,6,9,12 and 15. So there are 5 numbers and the probability is 5/15 if we simplify it will become 1/3.

By adding the probability of 5 and the number divisible by 3 the answer is 2/5

Solution:

                  [tex]\frac{1}{15}+\frac{5}{15}=\frac{6}{15}=\frac{2}{5}[/tex]

3. The Probability of an even number and a number divisible by 3 is 2/5.

Explanation:

     -The list of even numbers from 1-15 are 2,4,6,8,10,12,14. There are 7 numbers so the probability is 7/15.

     -The list of numbers that is divisible by 3 from 1-15 are 3,6,9,12 and 15. So there are 5 numbers and the probability is 5/15 if we simplify it will become 1/3.

      - There are numbers that are even and numbers that are divisible 3 which must be eliminated. The numbers are 6 and 12, therefore the probability is 2/15.

   By adding the Probability of an even number a

                 [tex]\frac{7}{15}+\frac{5}{15} =\frac{12}{15}-\frac{2}{15}=\frac{10}{15} =\frac{2}{3}[/tex]

4. The Probability of A number divisible by 3 and divisible by 4 is 7/15

Explanation:

The list of numbers that is divisible by 3 from 1-15 are 3,6,9,12 and 15. So there are 5 numbers and the probability is 5/15 if we simplify it will become 1/3.The list of numbers that is divisible by 4  from 1-15 are 4,8 and 12. There are 3 numbers so the probability is 3/15.There's a numbers that is divisible 3 and 4 which must be eliminated. The number is 4, therefore the probability is 1/15

     By adding the probability of a number that divisible by 3 or divisible by 4 is:

                          [tex]\frac{5}{15}+\frac{3}{15}=\frac{8}{15}-\frac{1}{15} =\frac{7}{15}[/tex]

5. The Probability of Dario selects a red marble or a yellow marble is 8/11.

Explanation:

The Probability that Dario selects a red marble is 14/44.The Probablity that Dario selects a yellow marble is 18/44.

    By adding all the probability the answer of Dario selecting a red marble or a yellow marble in a box is:

            [tex]\frac{14}{44} +\frac{18}{44}=\frac{32}{44}=\frac{8}{11}[/tex]
