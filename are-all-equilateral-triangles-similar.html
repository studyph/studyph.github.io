21. All equianular triangle are equilateral is that TRUE or FALSE


Answer:

True

Explenation:

Equiangular Triangle is having equal angles as same as the Equilateral Triangle


Answer:

TRUE. All equiangular triangles are also equilateral triangles.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us first define what "equiangular" and "equilateral" means.

EQUIANGULAR means having angles of equal measure. Meanwhile, EQUILATERAL means having all its sides on the same length.

Thus, an equiangular triangle is a triangle having all angles equal. While Equilateral triangle is a triangle having all sides equal.

But let us recall the PROPERTIES OF EQUIANGULAR AND EQUILATERAL TRIANGLES.

Equiangular Triangle

1. All angles are equal. Since the sum of the interior angles of a triangle is 180 degrees, then each angle measures 60 degrees.

2. Since all angles of an equiangular triangle are equal, then we can say that all of its sides are also equal.

3. If we draw an incircle and a circumcircle inside and outside the equiangular triangle respectively, then we can see that the radius of the incircle is half of the radius of the circumcircle.

Equilateral Triangle

1. All sides of an equilateral triangle are congruent (same in length).

2. Thus, if all of its sides are equal, then we can say that all of its angles are also equal and measures 60 degrees each.

3. If we draw an incircle and a circumcircle inside and outside the equiangular triangle respectively, then we can see that the radius of the incircle is half of the radius of the circumcircle.

Remember that when we draw a polygon, if its sides are equal in length, then its angles would be equal. So we do it reversely, if its angles are equal, then its sides are also equal.

Thus we say, that all equiangular triangles are also equilateral triangles.
