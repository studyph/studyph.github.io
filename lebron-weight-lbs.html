James decided to lose extra weight to keep himself healthy. From his 250 lbs. frame, his weight went down to 190 lbs. in 6 months. What was his average decrease in weight per month?

Answer:A. 10 Ibs.

Solution:

250 - 10= 240 (1 month)

240 - 10= 230 (2 months)

230 - 10= 220 (3 months)

220 - 10= 210 (4 months)

220 - 10= 200 (5 months)
