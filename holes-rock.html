12. 21. How does gravity contribute to weathering?a. by expansion of rocksb. by abrasion of the rocksc. by creating holes in rocksd. by splitting rocks particles​


Answer:

A.

Explanation:

Gravity causes erosion because it pulls things downward. Rockslides are a form of erosion caused by gravity. Water, wind, and ice help gravity to erode rock. Once erosion carries away weathered rock, the rock left behind begins to weather, starting the process once again.
