Circle the letter of the transition word that will best complete each sentence. 1. A survey reveals that the new president enjoys massive public support in his decision to stop the former president from leaving the country. _________,there are those who are still discomfited by the manner by which the former president was treated.

a. However

b. Still

c. For instance

d. And

Answer

The correct answer among the choices is letter a. However is the word appropriate to connect the main idea of the sentences.  To complete the sentences, the complete answer would be:

A survey reveals that the new president enjoys massive public support in his decision to stop the former president from leaving the country. However, there are those who are still discomfited by the manner by which the former president was treated.

Transition Words

-These are the words used to connect words, phrases or sentences to support the main idea and the next supporting idea to form a coherent sentences or paragraphs.

Kinds of TransitionsAdditive  Transitions - these are words connected to add, introduced  or give similarity to other ideas,.A dversative Transitions - these are the words connected in order to show conflict, dismissal or contradiction.Causal Transitions - these are words used to connect relationships such as cause and effect and reason and result.Sequential  Transitions - these are the words use to connect words in a chronological or logical sequence.
