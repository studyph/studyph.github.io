22. the all-star womens record for a race was 5 minutes, 37 3/10 seconds. Actress kim chi ran the race in 4 minutes, 49 1/2 seconds. By how many seconds did kim chi miss trying the record?


Record time(in seconds)

= (5×60) + (37 3/10) seconds

= (300) + [ (370+3)/10 ]

= 300 + 37.3 seconds

= 337.3 seconds.

The Time Kim completed the race(in seconds)

= (4×60) + (49 1/2) seconds

= (240) + [ (98+1)/2 ]

= 240 + 49.5 seconds.

= 289.5 seconds.

Kim broke the record by : 337.3 - 289.5 = 47.8 seconds.
