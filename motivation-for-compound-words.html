Action: Klosh-kwan is a respected elder in the community who acts as a mentor and guide to Keesh.

Words: Klosh-kwan speaks with wisdom and experience, using his words to teach and advise Keesh.

Motives: Klosh-kwan's motives are to pass on his knowledge and skills to the next generation, particularly to Keesh, whom he sees as a potential future leader of the community. He wants to ensure that his people have a strong and capable leader to guide them in the future.
