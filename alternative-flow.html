3. what do the alternating stripes on the ocean floor flow?​


Answer:

On both sides of mid-ocean ridges, scientists found a pattern of alternating magnetic “zebra stripes.” “Black” stripes represented rocks imprinted when Earth's magnetic field was in a normal direction, as it is today, and “white” represents rocks imprinted when the field was in a reversed direction.

Explanation:

The striped magnetic pattern develops because, as oceanic crust pulls apart, magma rises to the surface at mid-ocean ridges and spills out to create new bands of the ocean floor. ... Later, after the planet's magnetic field flips again, the next stripe of new ocean floor aligns its polarity in the opposite direction.

Positive effects of globalization:

Increased economic growth and development through international trade and investment

Increased job opportunities and wages in many parts of the world

Greater access to goods and services, including technology, medicine, and education

Increased cultural exchange and diversity

Greater awareness and concern for global issues, such as climate change and human rights

Negative effects of globalization:

Greater income inequality and exploitation of workers in some countries

Negative environmental impact due to increased resource consumption and pollution

Loss of cultural traditions and identity due to increased homogenization and westernization of culture

Greater risk of global economic crises due to interconnectedness and dependence on other countries

Greater risk of spread of disease and pandemics due to increased travel and trade
