Value of the Random Variable T Probability PIT)​


The sample space S for three tosses of a coin is:

S = {HHH, HHT, HTH, THH, HTT, THT, TTH, TTT}

Let T be the random variable representing the number of heads in three tosses of the coin. To find the probability distribution of T, we can list the possible values of T and their corresponding probabilities.

Possible values of T and their corresponding probabilities are:

T = 0, P(T = 0) = P(TTT) = 1/8

T = 1, P(T = 1) = P(HTT or THT or TTH) = 3/8

T = 2, P(T = 2) = P(HHT or HTH or THH) = 3/8

T = 3, P(T = 3) = P(HHH) = 1/8

The probability distribution for T is:

T P(T)

1 3/8

2 3/8

3 1/8
