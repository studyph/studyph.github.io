29. Simplify: 3x – 5 + 23x – 9


Answer:

26x-14 or 2(13x-7)

Step-by-step explanation:

Simplifying algebraic expression, it means writing it in the most efficient and simpliest manner but without changing the value of the expression. This mainly involves collecting and combining like terms including constanst which means that we add together anything that can be added together. The rule here is that only like terms can be added together, and just simplify and reduce to the lowest possible form if there is a fraction.

Take note, like terms are those terms which contain the same powers of in the same variables.

In the given algebraic expression 3x – 5 + 23x – 9

Tips: Put-in in one side those like terms, or include in one side.

3x + 23x -5 -9

Tips: To make your self perfect in simplifying algebraic expression you need to master first your skills in fraction and integers.

Now, 3x + 23x, add the two like terms, and (-5) + (-9), add the two constant. In simplifying just combine all like terms.

It will give you 26x -14.

Therefore, the answer here is 26x-14, but if you want other way of simplifying, it can be written as 2(13x-7).
