1. Identify and assign variables:

$12.60 = price after discount10% = discountLook for: original price of shoes, let this be "y"Look for: discount price, let this be "x"

2. Covert this into equation form and solve for y and x:

$12.60 = y - (y*0.1)$|12.60 = 0.9yy = $14 ; this is the original price of shoes. x = y(.10)x = $14 (0.1)x = $1.4; this is the discount.
