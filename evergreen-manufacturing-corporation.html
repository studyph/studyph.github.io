19. The different types of business according to activities are a. Manufacturing, Corporations, Service b. Manufacturing, Merchandising, Service c. Service, Partnerships, Sole Proprietorships d. Corporations, Partnerships, Sole Proprietorships


Answer:

B

Explanation:

Manufacturing, Merchandising, and Service are business types according to activities. Manufacturing are the companies that produce finished goods consumption. They use raw materials to create and finish their products. Merchandising companies are similar to trading, wholesaling/retailing and buy and sell companies. They do not need raw materials to produce the items they will sell. They buy the finished goods already and sell on a mark-up. Service companies are the companies that sell services for a business. Examples are repair shops, agencies, parlors and spa/massage businesses.
