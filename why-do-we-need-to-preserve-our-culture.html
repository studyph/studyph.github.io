It's great that Filipinos are rich in culture and traditions. It's important to both preserve and promote these cultural elements in order to maintain a strong sense of national identity and heritage. There are many cultural traditions that are worth preserving and promoting, including the various languages and dialects spoken in the Philippines, the traditional arts and crafts, the cuisine, and the music and dance styles. Philippine Cultural Heritage

Philippine Cultural Heritage is the ways of living of the ancestors passed down to the present generation of Filipinos. Philippine Cultural Heritage is a peek at the practices and environment of the past. Philippine Cultural Heritage is displayed to show that Filipinos are unique from farming to socializing, from sports to dances.

Since it is the only thing left from the past that gives Filipino identity, it must be preserved. Filipinos must understand, value, and enjoy it. Filipinos must, most of all, pass it on to the next generation. If given the chance, it must also be shared with others from other cultures.

Examples of Philippine Cultural Heritage Values (hospitality, resilience) Artistic Expressions (Tinikling, Batek) Objects (Manunggul Jar, Kampilan) Places (Banaue Rice Terraces, Mayon Volcano) Sports (Sipa, Arnis) Customs (greeting parents and elderly) Practices (Bayanihan)
