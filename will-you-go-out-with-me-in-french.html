4. In order to select new board members, the French club held an election. 8 out of the 80 members of the French club voted in the election. What percentage of the members voted?​


[tex] \huge\sf{ \pink H \pink I!}[/tex]

===========================================

[tex]\underline{ \large\tt{\pink Problem:}}[/tex]

In order to select new board members, the French club held an election. 8 out of the 80 members of the French club voted in the election. What percentage of the members voted?

[tex] \\ [/tex]

[tex] \underline{\large\tt{\pink Solution:}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{part = percentage \: of \: whole}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{8 = \frac{n}{100} \times 8} [/tex]

[tex] \sf{8 = \frac{80n}{100} } [/tex]

[tex]\sf{8 = 0.8n} [/tex]

[tex] \sf{8 \div 0.8 = 0.8n \div 0.8}[/tex]

[tex] \sf{10 = n}[/tex]

[tex] \\ [/tex]

[tex] \underline{\large\tt{\pink Answer:}}[/tex]

10% of the members voted
