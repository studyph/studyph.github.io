•Human resources

Human resources are human resources involved in activities within a company. They become an important part of the company because without them, the company's activities are maintained. Therefore, to maintain it, each division is interconnected with one another. If there is inequality, it will have an impact on other divisions.

•Compliance

Compliance is the process of ensuring that business processes, operations, and practices run according to a set of predetermined or agreed-upon norms. It was also explained that historically, compliance was initially considered a burden. However, early on and over time, compliance has shown indications of an opportunity to improve its business processes and operations.

• Recruiting

Recruitment is the process of finding and selecting prospective employees to fill certain positions or positions. The main key to creating professional Human Resources (HR) lies in the process of recruitment, selection, training, and development of prospective employees. It is not easy to find qualified employees. This recruitment process is important in determining whether or not applicants apply for jobs at a company.

• Finance

Finance is a job related to the arrangement and management of finances in a company. Thus, they work to monitor the entry and exit of company funds, as well as improve the allocation of these funds and the financial system in case of problems.
