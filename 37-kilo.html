(e) Check if your result will satisfy the problem​


Answer:a) If x is the number of kilos of mango and the number of kilos of atis, then the equation is:

x + x = 20

(b) Equation for the cost of fruits:

60x + 40x = 1000

(c) The system of equations are

x + x = 20 and 60x + 40x = 1000.

(d) Solve by any method.

60x + 40x = 1000

100x = 1000

100x/100 = 1000/100

x = 10

Mother bought 10 kilos of mango and 10 kilos of atis.

(e) Check if your result will satisfy the problem.

x + x = 20

10 + 10 = 20

20 = 20 ✔

60x + 40x = 1000

60(10) + 40(10) = 1000

600 + 400 = 1000

1000 = 1000 ✔
