2. what percent of 1500 is 75?​


Answer:To find out what percent of 1500 is 75, you can use the following formula:

% = (part/whole) x 100

Where:

part = 75 (the value we want to find the percentage of)whole = 1500 (the total value)

So, substituting the values in the formula, we get:

% = (75/1500) x 100

% = 0.05 x 100

% = 5%

Therefore, 75 is 5% of 1500.

12. 30% is what percent of 1500​


Question:

30 is what percent of 1500?

Answer:

30 of 1500 is 2%

Steps to solve "what percent is 30 of 1500?"

[tex]30 \: of \: 1500 \: can \: be \: written \: as \\ \frac{30}{1500} \\ \\ \frac{30}{1500} \times \frac{100}{100} \\ \\ = ( \frac{30 \times 100}{1500} ) \times \frac{1}{100} = \frac{2}{100} \\ \\ therefore \: the \: answer \: is \: 2\%[/tex]

If you are using calculator, simply enter 30 ÷ 1500 × 100 which will give you 2 as the answer.
