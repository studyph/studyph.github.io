Answer:

RDAs apply to vitamins and minerals from food and daily supplements. The purpose of these guidelines is to inform you how much of a specific nutrient your body needs on a daily basis. It is important to meet your daily recommended dietary allowances so that your body gets everything it needs to function.

Answer:

The RDA is designed to satisfy the nutrient requirement for most healthy individuals and thus, exceeds the nutrient need for most people. The "D" does not mean daily, but dietary. A person does not need to meet the RDA every day but is the average intake needed every 3-7 days.

23. what is the meaning recommended dietary allowance (RDA)​


Recommended Dietary Allowances (RDAs) are the levels of intake of essential nutrients that, on the basis of scientific knowledge, are judged by the Food and Nutrition Board to be adequate to meet the known nutrient needs of practically all healthy persons.

ANSWER:OCLC: Online Computer Library Center, a global library cooperative that provides shared technology services and library resources. "Enhanced records" refer to records that have been updated or improved in some way, such as the addition of a new field, the adoption of new cataloging standards (RDA), or an increase in the encoding level, which refers to the level of detail or granularity in the record.947: Refers to a specific type of field in a MARC record, which is a standardized format used to represent bibliographic information. In this case, the 947 field is used to describe the reason for an update to a record.945: Another type of field in a MARC record, used to indicate local edits made to a record by a specific library or institution. This sentence is saying that these local edits will be retained even when the record is replaced or updated.946: Yet another type of field in a MARC record, used to indicate that a record has been updated or overlaid with a new version of the same record. In this case, the 946 field is specifically used to identify that the update was made by OCLC."Holdings" refers to the library's physical or electronic copies of a particular item, and "attached" means that the holdings have been associated with the bibliographic record in the library's system. This sentence is simply saying that updates to records can appear as soon as the day after holdings have been attached, meaning that the library's system is constantly being updated with new information.
