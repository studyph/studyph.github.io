18. Classify the following art forms. Use the legends below:Classification of Arts.BA - business artPA - practical artCA - civic art,MA medical artAA - agricultural ArtIA - industrial artPA - practical artFA - fishery artGA- graphic artCA - commercial art​


1. Medical art

The medical arts cover a wide range of applications from patient communication and information to medical teaching and training.It is also used by the pharmaceutical industry to help explain their products and in court rooms to help jury members understand complex topics.Medical artists produce work in a variety of media including traditional illustration, as well as interactive 3D models, and animation.

2. Civic art

Civic art is the sum total of the architecture, public spaces, monuments, urban design, and landscape of a city, but it is far more than the sum of the parts.

3. Graphic art

Graphic art is a branch of art whose work is made using printing techniques, usually on paper. Except for the Monotype technique, the process is able to create many copies of the same work, this is called the printing process. Each copy of the work is known as an 'impression'.

4. Business art

Art Business Info for artists is a compendium of advice, information and resources for artists about different aspects of the art business.

5. Practical art

Practical works of art are works of art whose main purpose is intended as disposable objects, for example, household furniture, tables, chairs and textiles.

6. Agriculture art

Agricultural arts are taught at primary and secondary schools and colleges across the country, especially in agricultural regions.

7. Industrial art

Industrial arts is an educational program that features the fabrication of objects in wood or metal using a variety of hand, power, or machine tools

8. Fishery art

Agri-fishery Arts strand is one of the programs under the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track in senior high school. This strand is designed to provide students with adequate knowledge and skills relating to agriculture and aquaculture

9. Commercial art

Commercial art which means art whose main purpose is to produce works for sale.

10. Applied art

Applied art is a work of art used in everyday life which contains certain functional values ​​in addition to its artistic value. The function of works of art can be divided into two, namely the aesthetic function and the practical function.

30. IndonesiaFolk ArtsMalaysiaFolk ArtsThailandFolk ArtsVietnamFolk ArtsCambodiaFolk ArtsSingaporeFolk ArtsLaosFolk ArtsBruneiFolk Arts​


Answer:

Indonesia - It is quite difficult to define Indonesian art, since the country is immensely diverse.

Thailand - Traditional Thai art is primarily composed of Buddhist art and scenes from the Indian epics. Traditional Thai sculpture almost exclusively depicts images of the Buddha, being very similar with the other styles from Southeast Asia, such as Khmer.

Malaysia - Malaysia draws on the varied cultures of the different people of Malaysia. The first people to live in the area were indigenous tribes that still remain; they were followed by the Malays, who moved there from mainland Asia in ancient times.

Vietnam - Vietnamese art is visual art that, whether ancient or modern, originated in or is practiced in Vietnam or by Vietnamese artists.

Vietnamese art has a long and rich history, the earliest examples of which date back as far as the Stone Age around 8,000 BCE.

Cambodia - stretches back centuries to ancient times, but the most famous period is undoubtedly the Khmer art of the Khmer Empire (802–1431), especially in the area around Angkor and the mainly 12th-century temple-complex of Angkor Wat, initially Hindu and subsequently Buddhist.

ingapore’s Traditional Arts - are made up of deeply rooted cultural and artistic expressions specific to the major ethnic groups. Each of the Traditional Arts practice provides a focal point for Singaporeans to explore their roots and achieve a deeper connection with their communities.

Laos - Lao art involves the myriad of forms creative, cultural expression originating from Laos. This includes both ancient artefacts and recent productions.

Bruneian art - is art from the country of Brunei. Brunei's art includes paintings, jewelry, and clothing.
