Question:

In how many ways can 8 people line up in a cafeteria line?

Answer:

There are 40,320 possible ways 8 students

Step-by-step explanation:

Think about building up the line one student at a time. You initially have 8 to choose from. Then for the second position you have 7 to choose from. Then 6 etc until the last student. So there are 8×7×6×5×4×3×2×1=8!=40320 different ways
