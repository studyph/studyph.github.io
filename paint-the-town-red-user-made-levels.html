22. A van and a car left Town A for Town B at the same time. The car travelled at an average speed of 56 km per houand reached Town B in 3 hours. The van was 3 km awag from Town B when the car completed the journey. What was the average speed of the van for the journey made in 3 hours? ​


Answer:

165 km per 3 hours.

Step-by-step explanation:

car: average speed is 56 km per hour

van: average speed ?

car: 56 km per hour * 3 hours= 168 km per 3 hours.

168 km per 3 hours -3 km= 165 km.

van: 165/3=55

van: average speed of van is 55 km per hour.
