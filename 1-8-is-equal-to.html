.
Getting the common difference. We can get the common difference by simply subtracting two consecutive terms.
d = a9 - a8 = 60 - 54 = 6
.
Now, let us get the value of the first term.
a9 = a1 + (n - 1)d
60 = a1 + (9 - 1)6
60 = a1 + 48
60 - 48 = a1
12 = a1
.
Therefore, the value of first term is 12.
