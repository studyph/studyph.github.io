16. Help po, sa mga matatalino dyan Calculate the % by mass and volume the following solutions: A. 2 grams of sucrose in 80 grams water B. 20 grams sodium hydroxide (NaOH) contained in 500mL solution C. 10 mL ethanol in 50mL solution D. 20mL og NaCI in 180mL water


Problem 1:

A.

Given: 2g C12H22O11

80g H2O

Unknown: % by mass = ?

Formula: M% = Msol / Msoln × 100%

where in, M% = percentage by mass

Msol = mass of solute

Msoln = mass of solution

Solution:

Msoln = mass of solute + mass of solvent

= 2g C12H22O11 + 80g H2O

= 82g

M% = Msol / Msoln × 100%

= 2g C12H22O11 / 82g × 100%

= 0.0244g × 100%

= 2.44%

Final Answer: The percentage by mass of the solution is 2.44%

B.

Given: 20g NaOH

500mL solution

Unknown: % by volume = ?

But the problem is, NaOH is in grams!!

Note: To convert the mass of NaOH to volume, we must know it’s density first. (Density of a certain mixture or compound can be found in Periodic Table of Elements, or by using the formula D = m/V, m = mass, and V = volume)

NaOH (d = 2.13g/cm³)

Now, we will derive the formula “D = m/V” so we can convert NaOH mass into volume, by following these steps:

• D = m/V ← divide both sides by m

• m/D = m/V/m ← cancel like terms

• m/D = V ← final formula

Solution of NaOH mass to volume:

V = m/D

= 20g / 2.13g/cm³

= 9.39cm³

Note: 1cm³ = 1mL, therefore, 9.39cm³ = 9.39mL

Now, we have the volume for NaOH, so let’s continue!

Formula: V% = Vsol / Vsoln × 100%

where in, V% = percentage by volume

Vsol = volume of solute

Vsoln = volume of solution

Solution:

V% = Vsol / Vsoln × 100%

= 9.39mL / 500mL × 100%

= 0.01878mL × 100%

= 1.878%

Final Answer: The percentage by volume of the solution is 1.878%

C.

Given: 10mL C2H5OH

50mL solution

Unknown: % by volume = ?

Formula: V% = Vsol / Vsoln × 100%

Solution:

V% = Vsol / Vsoln × 100%

= 10mL C2H5OH / 50mL × 100%

= 0.2mL × 100%

= 20%

Final Answer: The percentage by volume of the solution is 20%

D.

Given: 20mL NaCl

180mL H2O

Unknown: % by volume = ?

Formula: V% = Vsol / Vsoln × 100%

Solution:

Vsoln = vol of solute + vol of solvent

= 20mL NaCl + 180mL H2O

= 200mL

V% = Vsol / Vsoln × 100%

= 20mL NaCl / 200mL × 100%

= 0.1mL × 100%

= 10%

Final Answer: The percentage by volume of the solution is 10%
