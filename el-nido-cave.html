The Cultural Tour in El Nido, Palawan: A Must-Do!

The Cultural Tour in El Nido will take you during a day to meet and discover the life of the local peoples of northern Palawan. The tour starts at 8am and we’ll come back to town around 5pm. El Nido Resorts operates a state-of-the-art sewage treatment plant (STP) combined with native reed bed filters. This ensures that no raw sewage and grey water are discharged into sea.
