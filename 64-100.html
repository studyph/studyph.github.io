Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Rule in addition of integers:

If the addends have like signs, add.If the addends have unlike signs, subtract. The result follows the sign of the bigger number.

Rule in subtraction of integers:

Change the sign of the subtrahend (or the number to be subtracted)Proceed to addition rule

Rule in evaluation multi-operation expression or PEMDAS:

Evaluation first those grouped or inside the parenthesis/parentheses.Exponents (follow the exponent rule)Multiplication or Division whichever comes first from left to rightAddition or Subtraction

Evaluate:

(+64 - +36) - (-100 + 46) + 130​

= (+64 - (+36)) - (-100 + 46) + 130

= (+28) - (-54) + 130

= (+28) + (+54) + 130

= 212

7. Continue this pattern: 144, 121, 100, 81, 64


The pattern is a decreasing pattern of the perfect squares starting from 12.

Following the pattern,

144, 121, 100, 81, 64,

The next numbers would be
49, 36, 25, 16, 9, 4, 1The numbers were decreased by:
23 (144 to 121)
21 (121 to 100)
19 (100 to 81)
17 (81 to 64)

We can assume that the next number will be decreased by 15 because the difference of 23, 21, 19, & 17 are 2 and 17-2 will be 15.

64 - 15 → 49

Thus, the next number will be 49.
