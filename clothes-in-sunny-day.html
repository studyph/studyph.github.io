16. - Question: If you suddenly encounter a hot sunny day in August while preparing for school, would you still wear the same clothes in the closet? If yes, why? If not, encircle the proper attire for that school day.​


Answer:

I would likely not wear the same clothes in my closet that I would wear on a cooler day. Instead, I would choose lightweight and breathable clothing that would help me stay cool and comfortable throughout the day. Some appropriate clothing options for a hot sunny day in August might include:

-A loose-fitting t-shirt or blouse made from lightweight and breathable fabrics such as cotton or linen

-short, skirts or lightweight pants that allow air to circulate

-Sandals or open-toed shoes to let my feet breathe

-A hat or cap to protect my face and head from the sun

-Sunglasses to protect my eyes from UV rays

It's important to dress appropriately for the weather to avoid heat exhaustion or other heat-related illnesses.
