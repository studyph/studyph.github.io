8. In your own understanding, differentiate luxury transport vs. recreational transport. ​


Answer:

Luxury transport and recreational transport are two distinct types of transportation that serve different purposes.

Luxury transport refers to high-end and exclusive modes of transportation that are designed to provide comfort, convenience, and luxury to their passengers. Examples of luxury transport include private jets, luxury yachts, and high-end limousines. These modes of transportation are typically used for business travel, high-profile events, and for wealthy individuals who prioritize comfort and exclusivity in their travel arrangements.

On the other hand, recreational transport refers to modes of transportation that are designed for leisure and entertainment purposes. Examples of recreational transport include RVs, camping trailers, and boats for fishing or water sports. These modes of transportation are typically used for family vacations, outdoor adventures, and other recreational activities.

While luxury transport and recreational transport may both offer amenities and features that enhance the travel experience, they differ in their primary purpose and target market. Luxury transport is geared towards affluent individuals and corporate clients who prioritize luxury and exclusivity, while recreational transport is geared towards families and individuals who seek adventure and leisure.

In summary, luxury transport and recreational transport are two distinct types of transportation that cater to different needs and preferences. Luxury transport prioritizes exclusivity and comfort, while recreational transport prioritizes leisure and entertainment.

"Dedicated transport," on the other hand, typically refers to modes of transportation that are designed to serve a specific purpose or group of people, such as a company shuttle that transports employees to and from work sites. Step-by-step explanation:

We know that there are 2960 students in the school. Let's find out how many students come to school by each method:

20% of the students walk to school:

20% of 2960 = (20/100) * 2960 = 592 students

60% of the students come to school by public transportation:

60% of 2960 = (60/100) * 2960 = 1776 students

15% of the students come by school bus:

15% of 2960 = (15/100) * 2960 = 444 students

The remaining students come by private car:

Total number of students - (students who walk + students who come by public transportation + students who come by school bus) = 2960 - (592 + 1776 + 444) = 148 students

29. Public Administration vs Private Administration


Public Administration versus Private Administration

The difference between public administration and private administration is that while public administration is a political activity that is run by the government with a service motive, private administration is a business activity that is run by a group of individuals not related to government in any way whatsoever with the sole motive of profit.

Main differences between Public Administration and Private Administration

Public administration is set up by the government. While Private administration is not set up by the government instead it is set up by a group of private individuals.

Public administration follows a bureaucratic approach while private administration follows an egalitarian approach.

Public administration follows a pluralistic approach of decision making while private administration follows a monopolistic approach to decision making.

Public administration has a service and welfare motive. Private administration is profit-oriented i.e the sole motive here is profit maximization.

Public administration is accountable to the general public while private administration is accountable to its employees.

Public Administration earns revenue through taxes, fines, fees, etc. while private administration earns revenue through profit.

Administrators working in a public administration are paid salaries while administrators working in a private administration earn their remuneration through profits.

Public administration is political activity while private administration is a business activity.
