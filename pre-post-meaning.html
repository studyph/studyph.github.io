1. what is pre natal care meaningand post natal care​


Answer:

Prenatal care refers to the care that a mother gets during her pregnanacy. Prenatal care is essential for ensuring the overall health of the newborn and their mother. This includes the checkup during pregnancy.

Postnatal care refers to the care given to the newborn and mother after birth. This kind of care started when the child gets delivered, from implementing Unang Yakap, proper timing for cord clamping and to Essential Intrapartum and newborn care (EINC). This is to ensure that the mother and the child will achieve the best health as possible.

3. in your activity notebook write the meaning of pre-natal and post natal care​


Pre-natal care is the care given to pregnant women before and after the birth of the baby. Post natal care is the care taken by mothers after giving birth to ensure that they are fully recovered from childbirth and are ready to start caring for their babies. It includes checking that the mother’s health is good enough to deal with the demands of her child. Note: This is a one sentence answer.
