15. A ball was thrown with an initial velocity of 25.0 m/s at the angle of 30m0 above horizontal. Find the total time that the ball to reach the apex, The maximum height reach by the ball and the range of the flight of the ball


Answer:

a. The total time to reach the maximum height is 1.275 seconds

b. The maximum height reached by the ball is 7.972 meters

c. The range of the flight of the ball is 55.23 meters

Explanation:

Formula to be use for a projectile motion of the ball

a. [tex]t_{m} =\frac{v_{0}Sin0 }{g}[/tex]           time of flight of the ball to reach a maximum height

b. [tex]y_{m} =\frac{(V_{0}Sin0)^2 }{2g}[/tex]       maximum height

c. [tex]R= \frac{V_{0}^2Sin20 }{g}[/tex]          Range or maximum horizontal distance traveled by the

                                ball

Given information

[tex]V_{0} =25m/s[/tex]

θ [tex]=30[/tex]°

a. [tex]t_{m} =?[/tex]

b. [tex]y_{m} =?[/tex]

c. [tex]R=?[/tex]

Solving the problem

a. Use the formula for time to reach the maximum height

[tex]t_{m} =\frac{v_{0}Sin0 }{g}[/tex]

[tex]t_{m} =\frac{25Sin30}{9.8}[/tex]

[tex]t_{m} =1.275s[/tex]

b. Use the formula to solve the maximum height

[tex]y_{m} =\frac{(V_{0}Sin0)^2 }{2g}[/tex]

[tex]y_{m} =\frac{(25Sin30)^2 }{2(9.8)}[/tex]

[tex]y_{123m=7.972m[/tex]

c. Use the formula the range

[tex]R= \frac{V_{0}^2Sin20 }{g}[/tex]

[tex]R= \frac{25^2Sin2(30) }{9.8}[/tex]

[tex]R= \frac{25^2Sin60 }{9.8}[/tex]

[tex]R=55.23m[/tex]
