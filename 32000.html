17. Eugene and elite are partners with capital balances of 32000 and 48000 respectively. Their partnership earned a net profit of 8000.find teh share of each using their capital ratio


Answer:

Eugene and elite are partners with capital balances of 32000 and 48000 respectively. Their partnership earned a net profit of 8000. Find the share of each using their capital ratio.

Eugene earned 3200 and elite earned 4800.

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

Find the share of each using their capital ratio.

What are given?

Eugene's capital - 32000

elite's capital - 48000

net profit - 8000

To find the amount that each of them earned from their net profit, we need to know the ratio of their capital first.

Capital Ratio

32000:48000

2:3

(The greatest common factor is 16000.)

(To know how to get the ratio, visit the link.

https://brainly.ph/question/247831)

Now that we already know the capital ratio, we can already proceed in finding the amount of their shares from the net profit.

Remember that the net profit is 8000 and the ratio is 2:3.

8000 ÷ 5 = 1600

(divided by 5, because of 2+3)

Eugene's share

1600 × 2 = 3200

elite's share

1600 × 3 = 4800

So, with 8000 net profit, Eugene earned 3200 and elite earned 4800.
