Answer:

If all the family members know how to construct electrical gadgets, it could have several potential benefits:

Increased efficiency: Having multiple family members with electrical expertise could increase the efficiency of building electrical gadgets. The workload can be distributed among family members, and each person can contribute their skills and knowledge to the project.

Cost savings: The ability to construct electrical gadgets could potentially save the family money on repair costs or the need to hire outside contractors.

Promote creativity: With a shared knowledge and interest in electrical gadgets, family members can collaborate and work together to design and build new and innovative projects.

Enhance safety: With more family members skilled in electrical work, it could improve the safety of electrical devices in the home. Family members could identify potential hazards and correct them before they become dangerous.

Overall, having all family members knowledgeable about constructing electrical gadgets could lead to increased productivity, cost savings, creativity, and enhanced safety in the home.
