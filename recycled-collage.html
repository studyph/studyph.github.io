1. how to make a photo collage using a recyclable materials


QUESTION

HOW TO MAKE A PHOTO COLLAGE USING A RECYCLABLE MATERIALS

ANSWER:

The recyclable materials needed:

Glue (used/old one)Used cardboardOld magazinesNewspapersOld textbook and old projectsTansan / bottle capsCrumpled scratch papers

You can make your own photo collage using recyclable materials.

First, you need to gather your materials such as glue (your used glue), scissors, used/old magazines, newspaper, old textbooks or projects.

Get a used cardboard as the board for your collage. Use your scissors and cut pieces of pictures from your magazines, newspapers, old projects or textbooks. (Ex. pictures related to Nature)

After collecting, place the pictures on your cardboard in a shape you like (Ex. Heart, triangle, circle) And then, get your used glue and stick the pictures on where you placed them.

After gluing, get your used crayons or used oil pastel to add designs on the background of the collage. You can also use tansan or plastic caps and crumpled scratch paper to glue it on the board.Then you can tie it using a ribbon to make it attractive.
