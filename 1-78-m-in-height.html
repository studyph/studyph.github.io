7. If m varies directly as n and m=35 when n=42 find m when n is 78


DIRECT VARIATION

If m varies directly as n and m=35 when n=42, find m when n is 78.

Answer:

The value of m is [tex]\: \: \LARGE \bold{65}[/tex].

Step-by-step explanation:

First, find the constant of variation using the equation [tex]m=kn[/tex], since m varies directly as n.

[tex]m=kn \\ 35 = 42k \\ \frac{35}{42} = \frac{\cancel{42}k}{\cancel{42}} \\ 0.833=k[/tex]

Then, find m when n is equal to 78 using the equation of variation [tex]m = 0.833n[/tex]

[tex]m = 0.833n \\ m = (0.833)(78) \\ m = 65[/tex]

Thus, the value of y is [tex] 65 [/tex].
