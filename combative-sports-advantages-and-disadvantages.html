10. Advantage and Disadvantage of combative spirts​


Advantage and Disadvantage of Combative Sports

Combative sports build confidence in an individual. It promotes physical activity that is good for an active lifestyle. Combative sports teach self-discipline while learning self-defense.  Meanwhile, engaging in combative sports needs a lot of money and time for training. Unfortunately, injuries are possible when playing combative sports.

Combat Sports

These are the common combat sports:

boxing (including many styles of kickboxing) wrestling (including Sumo wrestling fencing modern-era mixed martial arts (MMA) indigenous martial arts,(like judo,savate, Muay Thai)

Combat Sport Injuries

These are the common combat sports injuries:

Head injuries. Hand and wrist injuries. Neck and shoulder injuries. Dislocations. Lower limb injuries.
