    One of the core subjects is math. Math is a subject that is concerned with statistics, forms, facts, ratios, and logical processes. Every aspect of our lives, including medicine, engineering, finance, the natural sciences, economics, etc., is greatly affected by it. The universe of mathematics is all around us.

     In simple terms, the word "mathematics" allows us to learn, educate, or acquire knowledge. Mathematical ideas and theories assist us in comprehending and resolving a wide range of issues in both academic and practical contexts. It is a logical topic. STRENGTHS AND WEAKNESSES

     Each person must have both assets and liabilities. Some individuals are skilled at managing their flaws. Others continue to conceal their flaws and do not want assistance. I've been one of the persons that constantly hides and denies having vulnerabilities.

     My numerical series, which covers proportion, logical problems, decimals, fractions, and many other topics, is my area of strength in mathematics. I tend to comprehend things better, and one of my hobbies is to keep trying to find solutions.
