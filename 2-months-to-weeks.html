19. 1) 28 days = _____ weeks 2) 330 days = _____ months 3) 8 weeks = _____ days 4) 14 months = _____ days 5) 49 days = _____ weeks


1) 1 day = 1/7 weeks
[tex]28days* \frac{ \frac{1}{7} wk}{1day} =28* \frac{1}{7} wk=4 weeks[/tex]
2) 1 day = 1/30 months
[tex]330days* \frac{ \frac{1}{30} months}{1day} =330* \frac{1}{30} months=11months[/tex]
3) 1 week = 7 days
[tex]8 wk* \frac{7days}{1 wk} =8*7days=56days[/tex]
4) 1 month = 30 days
[tex]14months* \frac{30days}{1month} =14*30days=420days[/tex]
5) 1 day = 1/7 weeks
[tex]49days* \frac{ \frac{1}{7} wk}{1 days} =49* \frac{1}{7} wk=7weeks[/tex]
Conversion:
1 week=7 days
1 month=30 days(approximately) or 4 weeks

Convert:
1)[tex]28 days(\frac{1 week}{7 days})=4 weeks[/tex]
2)[tex]330 days(\frac{1 month}{30 days})=11 months[/tex]
3)[tex]8 weeks(\frac{7 days}{1 week})=56 days[/tex]
4)[tex]14 months(\frac{30 days}{1 month})=420 days[/tex]
5)[tex]49 days(\frac{1 week}{7 days})=7 weeks[/tex]
