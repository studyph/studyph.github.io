24. an aquarium 100 cm long. 40 cm wide and 60 cm high is the aquarium is half-full how many cubic centimeters of the water does it hold?​


QUESTION:

An aquarium 100 cm long. 40 cm wide and 60 cm high If the aquarium is half-full how many cubic centimeters of the water does it hold?

ANSWER:

Volume of rectangular aquarium,

V = L × W × H

where

V = Volume = ?

L = Length = 100 cm

W = Width = 40 cm

H = Height = 60 cm

solve for Volume of whole aquarium,

V = L × W × H

V = 100 × 40 × 60

V = 240,000 cm³

Since aquarium is half full, we divide it by 2,

V half full = 240,000 ÷ 2

V half full = 120,000 cm³
