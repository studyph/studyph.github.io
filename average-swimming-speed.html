7. Use the GRESA method to answer the following problems.1. A triathlete swims a distance of 3 kilometers in 1 hour, then bikes a distance of 50 kilometers in 3 hours, and finally then runs a distance of 12 kilometers in 1 hour. What is his average speed?2. A plane travels 395,000 meters in 9000 seconds. What was its speed?3. A trucks goes from Haryana to Bangalore with an average speed of 60 km/hr. The journey takes 30 hours. It returns from Bangalore to Haryana on the same road with an average speed of 40 km/hr. What was the average speed of the truck during the road trip?4. What is the distance covered by a car traveling at a speed of 147 km in 5 hours?5. It took the lion 5 seconds to reach a distance of 47 meters.​


Answer:

G - Given: Write down the given information in the problem.

R - Required: Write down what is being asked in the problem.

E - Equation: Write down the formula or equation that can be used to solve the problem.

S - Substitute: Substitute the given values in the equation.

A - Answer: Solve for the unknown and write down the answer with the correct units.

1. G:

Swimming distance = 3 kmSwimming time = 1 hourBiking distance = 50 kmBiking time = 3 hoursRunning distance = 12 kmRunning time = 1 hour

R: Average speed

E: Total distance / Total time

S: Total distance = 3 km + 50 km + 12 km = 65 km

Total time = 1 hour + 3 hours + 1 hour = 5 hours

A: Average speed = Total distance / Total time = 65 km / 5 hours = 13 km/hr

2. G:

Distance = 395,000 meters

Time = 9000 seconds

R: Speed

E: Speed = Distance / Time

S: Distance = 395,000 meters

Time = 9000 seconds

A: Speed = Distance / Time = 395,000 meters / 9000 seconds = 43.89 m/s

3. G:

Average speed from Haryana to Bangalore = 60 km/hrTime is taken from Haryana to Bangalore = 30 hoursAverage speed from Bangalore to Haryana = 40 km/hr

R: Average speed during the entire trip

E: Average speed = 2 * A * B / (A + B)

where A = speed from Haryana to Bangalore, B = speed from Bangalore to Haryana

S: A = 60 km/hr, B = 40 km/hr

A: Average speed = 2 * 60 km/hr * 40 km/hr / (60 km/hr + 40 km/hr) = 48 km/hr

4. G:

Speed = 147 km/hrTime = 5 hours

R: Distance

E: Distance = Speed * Time

S: Speed = 147 km/hr

Time = 5 hours

A: Distance = Speed * Time = 147 km/hr * 5 hours = 735 km

5. G:

Time = 5 secondsDistance = 47 meters

R: Speed

E: Speed = Distance / Time

S: Distance = 47 meters

Time = 5 seconds

A: Speed = Distance / Time = 47 meters / 5 seconds = 9.4 m/s
