One dimension of a cube is increased by 1 inch to form a rectangular block. Suppose that the volume of the new block is 150 cubic inches. Find the length of an edge of the original cube:​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

The Area of the rectangle is 60

1.)  Let:

S =  shorter side

L = longer side

Area of  Rectangle formula = S x L

60 = S x L <======== eq. 1

(see attached diagram)

Use Pythagorean theorem:

(S+8)^2 = L^2 + S^2

simplified into:

S+8 = L + S

L = 8

subtitute to eq.1

60 = S x L

60 = S x 8

S = 7.5

therefore the dimensions are 8 and 7.5
