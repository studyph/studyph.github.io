3. the diameter of cylindrical well is 4ft. and the water depth 9 meters how many gallons of water are there in the well if there are 7.48 gallon to a cubic foot


SOLUTION:

Step 1: Convert the water depth from meters to feet.

There is 0.3048 meter in 1 foot.

[tex]\begin{aligned} h & = \text{9 m} \: \times \frac{\text{1 ft}}{\text{0.3048 m}} \\ & = \text{29.53 ft} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Determine the radius of the well.

[tex]\begin{aligned} r & = \frac{d}{2} \\ & = \frac{\text{4 ft}}{2} \\ & = \text{2 ft} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 3: Calculate the volume of water in the cylindrical well.

[tex]\begin{aligned} V & = \pi r^2h \\ & = \pi (\text{2 ft})^2(\text{29.53 ft}) \\ & = \text{371.08 ft}^3 \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 4: Convert the volume from cubic foot to gallon.

[tex]\begin{aligned} V & = \text{371.08 ft}^3 \: \times \frac{\text{7.48 gal}}{\text{1 ft}^3} \\ & = \boxed{\text{2,775.68 gal}} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, there are 2,775.68 gal of water.

∅ = 75 feet

1 gallon = 0.13398 cubic feet

Solution:

π = 3.14

r = 75/2 = 37.5 feet

Volume of sphere

Volume = 4/3 π r³

Volume = 4/3(3.14)(37.5)³

Volume = 220 781.249 cu.ft.

if 1 gallon = 0.13398 cubic feet

V = 220 781.249 cu.ft. x  1 gal / 0.13398 cubic feet
