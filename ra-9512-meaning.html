4. 6.) RA 10354 or Responsible Parenthood and Reproductive Health Act of 2021__________________________________________________________7.) RA 8750 or Seat Belts Use Act of 1999_________________________________________________________8.) RA 9512 or National Environment Awareness and Education Act of 2008_____________________________________________________________9.) RA No. 9165 Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002​


Answer:

6.The Responsible Parenthood and Reproductive Health Act of 2012, also known as the Reproductive Health Law or RH Law, and officially designated as Republic Act No. 10354, is a law in the Philippines, which guarantees universal access to methods on contraception, fertility control, sexual education, and maternal care.

7.8750: AN ACT REQUIRING THE MANDATORY COMPLIANCE BY MOTORISTS OF PRIVATE AND PUBLIC VEHICLES TO USE SEAT BELT DEVICES, AND REQUIRING VEHICLE MANUFACTURERS TO INSTALL SEAT BELT DEVICES IN ALL THEIR MANUFACTURED VEHICLES. The Act is also known as the "Seat Belts Use Act of 1999."

8.This Act provides for the promotion of environmental awareness through environmental education which shall encompass environmental concepts and principles, environmental laws, the state of international and local environment, local environmental best practices, the threats of environmental degradation and its impact on human well-being, the responsibility of the citizenry to the environment and the value of conservation, protection and rehabilitation of natural resources and the environment.

9.The Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002, or R.A. No. 9165, is a consolidation of Senate Bill No. 1858 and House Bill No. 4433. It was enacted and passed by the Senate of the Philippines and House of Representatives of the Philippines on May 30, 2002 and May 29, 2002, respectively.
