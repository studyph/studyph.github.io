Answer:

For example, f [g (x)] is the composite function of f (x) and g (x). ... Given the functions f (x) = x2 + 6 and g (x) = 2x – 1, find (f ∘ g) (x). Solution.

Answer:

(f • g) (x) = 84x³ + 24x²

Step-by-step explanation:

(f • g) (x) = f (x) • g (x)

= 6x² (14x + 4)

= 84x³ + 24x²
