10. The ratio between the present ages of Patrick and Roger is 5:7 respectively. If the difference between Roger's current age and Patrick's age after 6 years is 2. What is the sum of Patrick & Roger's current age? A. 52 years B. 36 years C. 48 years D. None of the above E. Insufficient data


Answer:

C.48 years

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the common multiple in the ratio 5:7;

Patrick's current age =5x&

Rogers current age =7x

As per given information

7x-(5x+6)=2

Solving this equation we get ;

7x-5x-6=2

2x=2+6

2x=8

Patrick current age =5x=5x4=20&

Rogers current age 7x=7x4=28

So the sum of of Patrick's and Rogers current age will be;

20+28 =48

So the answer is Letter C.
