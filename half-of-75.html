3x = (1/2)x + 75

To solve for x, we can simplify this equation by first subtracting (1/2)x from both sides:

(5/2)x = 75

Then, we can isolate x by dividing both sides by (5/2):

x = (75)/(5/2)

Simplifying the right side, we can rewrite this as:

x = 75 * 2/5

x = 30

Therefore, the number is 30.

30. the half life of a radioactive substance is 194 days how many days will it take for 75% of the substance to decay​


Answer:

The half-life of a radioactive substance is the time it takes for half of the initial amount to decay.

If the half-life of the substance is 194 days, after 194 days, half of the initial amount will have decayed. After another 194 days, half of the remaining amount will have decayed, leaving 1/4 of the initial amount.

So, it will take (2 x 194) = 388 days for 75% of the substance to decay.
