24. 16a²b⁴-25c²=?pa answer po​


Answer:

(4ab²+5c)(4ab²-5c)

Explanation:

Rewrite 16a²b⁴ as (4ab²)².

(4ab²)²-25c²

Rewrite 25c² as (5c)².

(4ab²)²-(5c)²

Since both terms are perfect squares, factor using the difference of squares formula, a²-b²=(a+b)(a-b)

where a=4ab² and b=5c.

(4ab²+5c)(4ab²-5c).
