10. Why does it hurt more when you fall on a concrete floor than on a wooden floor?


Answer:

Why does it hurt more when you fall on a concrete floor than on a wooden floor? ... Both floors provide the same impulse – the amount of force needed to bring your body to zero. If contact time is less for a concrete floor, the amount of force exerted must be greater to give the same impulse.

18. What is easier to push, a wooden cabinet in concrete flooring or a wooden cabinet in tiled flooring? Why is that so?​


A wooden cabinet in tiled flooring.

Why?

It is easier to push a wooden cabinet in tiled flooring because tiles are smooth than concrete. Objects that move in smooth surfaces move faster and better than those that are moving on rough surfaces. So basically, there's more friction on a rough surface than there is on a smooth surface.
