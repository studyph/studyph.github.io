21. explain the process of delegation of authority​


Explain the process of delegation of authority​

The process of delegation of authority are as follows:

1.Assignment

2.transfer

3.Acceptance

4.Accountability

The manager or the leader delegate the authority to their employees by means of deciding what type of duties and responsibilities they want to perform by their members. The first step is that the duties are assigned to the subordinates as per their job roles.Next step is transfer. The leader or the manager assure that they delegate the tasks to ensure that it can perform well by the employees.The third step is acceptance.  The member of the organization can either accept or rejects the tasks assigned to him by his superior. The last step is the accountability. If the member of the group decided to accept the responsibility, it can  automatically accountable to the superior for their performance.
