3. how does the poem function as a product of the time in which it was written? from the poem of nymph complaining for the death of her fawn by Andrew marvell. ​


Answer:

The poem "The Nymph Complaining for the Death of Her Fawn" by Andrew Marvell was written in the 17th century, a time when the natural world was highly valued and often romanticized. The poem reflects this trend by depicting the fawn as a symbol of innocence and beauty, and the nymph as a mournful figure lamenting the loss of a creature she deeply loved.

At the same time, the poem also reflects the Christian worldview that dominated Western society during this period. The fawn's death is portrayed as a tragic event, but the nymph is comforted by the thought that the fawn is now in heaven, reunited with its creator. This reflects the belief that death was not an end in itself, but a transition to an afterlife.

Overall, the poem reflects the values, beliefs, and concerns of the society in which it was written. It illustrates the cultural importance of nature, the romanticization of innocence, and the role of religion in shaping people's understanding of life and death.

19. Manuel Anselmo is ten times older than his son, Andrew. In 20 years, Manuel’s age will be 4 more than twice Andrew’s age. How old was he when Andrew was born?/in linear equation


Answer:

Let's start by assigning variables to the unknowns in the problem.

Let's say Manuel's current age is M and Andrew's current age is A.

From the problem, we know that:

M = 10A (since Manuel is ten times older than Andrew)

In 20 years, Manuel's age will be M+20 and Andrew's age will be A+20.

Also, we know that:

M+20 = 2(A+20) + 4 (since Manuel’s age will be 4 more than twice Andrew’s age in 20 years)

Now we can substitute the first equation into the second equation to get:

10A + 20 = 2(A + 20) + 4

Simplifying this equation, we get:

10A + 20 = 2A + 44

8A = 24

A = 3

So Andrew's current age is 3, which means he was born 3 years ago.

Therefore, Manuel's age when Andrew was born was:

M = 10A = 10(3) = 30

So Manuel was 30 years old when Andrew was born.

27. 2. Andrew starts his soccer practice at 8:35 am. If the practice ends at 2:40 Andrew practice Soccer pm, how long did Andrew practice soccer​


To find out how long Andrew practiced soccer, we need to subtract the starting time from the ending time.

End time = 2:40 pm

Start time = 8:35 am

To convert the start time to pm, we need to add 12 hours since 8:35 am is in the morning.

Start time = 8:35 am + 12 hours = 8 + 12 : 35 = 20:35 pm

Now we can subtract the start time from the end time:

2:40 pm - 8:35 pm = 6 hours

Therefore, Andrew practiced soccer for 6 hours.
