21. A. At the beginning of the current year, Margarita Company purchased for P10,800,000, including appraiser's fee of P100,000, a warehouse building and the land on which it is located. The following data were available concerning the property: Current Land Warehouse building Appraised value 4,000,000 6,000,000 10.000.000 Seller's original cost 2,800,000 5,600,000 8.400.000​


To calculate the cost of the land and the warehouse building, you can use the appraised value and the seller's original cost.

For the land:

Appraised value: P4,000,000

Seller's original cost: P2,800,000

Cost of land: P4,000,000 - P2,800,000 = P1,200,000

For the warehouse building:

Appraised value: P6,000,000

Seller's original cost: P5,600,000

Cost of warehouse building: P6,000,000 - P5,600,000 = P400,000

Therefore, the total cost of the land and the warehouse building is:

P1,200,000 (cost of land) + P400,000 (cost of warehouse building) = P1,600,000.
