1. A landscaper is putting in a brick patio. The area of the patio is 110 square meters. If the bricks measure 10 cm by 20 cm, how many bricks will it take to make the patio? with solution ​


Answer:

it will take 5,500 bricks to make the patio.

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Find the area of each brick

Each brick is 10 cm × 20 cm

Each brick is 10 cm × 20 cmThat is 200 cm² per brick.

2. Find the area of the patio in square centimeters

110 m² = 1,100,000 cm²

3. Find out how many 200 cm² bricks it would take to fill in an area

1,100,000 cm² ÷ 200 cm² = 5,500
