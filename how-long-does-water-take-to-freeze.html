22. If an ice cube takes 10 secs. to freeze in -150°C, how long will it take to freeze an ice cube in -360°C?


Answer:

24 sec


Step-by-step explanation:

-150ºC = 10 sec


Find amount of time to freeze 1ºC:

-1ºC = 10 ÷ 150 = 1/15 sec


Find the time needed to freeze to -360ºC:

-360ºC = 1/15 x 360 = 24 sec


Answer: 24 sec
