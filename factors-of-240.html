10. what is the prime factor ng 240


240 is a composite number.Prime factorization: 
240 = 2 x 2 x 2 x 2 x 3 x 5,
 which can be written 2⁴ x 3 x 5
The exponents in the prime factorization are 4, 1 and 1.
 Adding one to each and multiplying we get
(4 + 1)(1 + 1)(1 + 1) = 5 x 2 x 2 = 20.
Therefore 240 has 20 factors.Factors of 240:
1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10, 12, 15, 16, 20, 24, 30, 40, 48, 60, 80, 120, 240
Factor pairs: 240 = 1 x 240, 2 x 120, 3 x 80, 4 x 60, 5 x 48, 6 x 40, 8 x 30, 10 x 24, 12 x 20, or 15 x 16
Taking the factor pair with the largest square number factor, we get √240 = (√16)(√15) = 4√15 ≈ 15.492

Continuous division: 
240/5 = 48
48/2 = 24
24/2 = 12
12/2 = 6
6/2 = 3
