The definition of evaluation

An evaluation is an assessment. Evaluating means judging a work by its advantages and disadvantages. An evaluation of a work is generally called a review. The following is an explanation of the short story review.

6 Ways to Evaluate a Text

1. Text selection

Choose a short story that will be reviewed as needed or that has just been published, is of public concern, and is beneficial to the community. In other words, look for short stories, novel, books, etc that deserve reviews.

2.Mastering the content of the text

Read the short story carefully, several times if necessary, to enjoy, understand, and master its content.

3. Identify the text's content.

By reading, you will recognize the parts that are the advantages and disadvantages.

4. Summarize the content of the text.

The parts that have been identified, you rearrange briefly and systematically (making a synopsis).

5. Assessing text content

Give feedback by assessing the quality of the content of the text.For example, what are the advantages. What are the drawbacks.What are the benefits for readers. What is the book suitable for. Compare it with other books on the same topic.

6. Concluding

The last step is to draw conclusions by giving suggestions and considerations to the reader.
