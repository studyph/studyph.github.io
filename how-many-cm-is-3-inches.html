11. 4. How many 3-cm ribbons can be cut from an 11-inch ribbon?


Answer

[tex]\Large \boxed{\bold {9\ pieces}}[/tex]

Question

How many 3 cm  ribbons can be cut  from an 11" ribbon?

Solution

First, convert the 11" into centimeters.

[tex]\frac{11\ inches}{1}\times \frac{2.54\ cm}{1\ inch}=\boxed{27.94\ cm}[/tex]

Since the given values are now of the same units, divide [tex]27.94\ cm[/tex] by [tex]3\ cm[/tex].

[tex]27.94\ cm\ \div\ 3\ cm=9.31 \Rightarrow \Large \boxed{9}[/tex]

Therefore, you can cut 9 pieces of 3 cm ribbons from a 11" ribbon.
