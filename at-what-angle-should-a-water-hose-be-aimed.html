30. "At what angle should a water hose be aimed in order for the water to land with the greatest horizontal range? a. 0Â° b. 30Â° c. 45Â° d. 60Â° "


Answer:

At what angle or position should a water hose be aimed in order for the water to land with the greatest horizontal range?

The hose should be angled at 45 degrees in order for the water to reach the greatest horizontal range, so the answer would have to be the letter C.

Explanation:

With water’s low viscosity, having the hose positioned at 45 degrees would hit the farthest because, at the middle of the arch that is formed, vertical displacement takes place, which is when gravity asserts itself and forces the sprayed water return to earth. And it is by positioning the hose at 45 degrees that would give the greatest horizontal range.

This answer does not take into account the distance from the target, the amount of pressure available in the area and in the case of a garden hose, how much the user's thumb is controlling the spray.
