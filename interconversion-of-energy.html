Melting = Solid to liquid (e.g. kandilang natutunaw sa apoy)Evaporation = Liquid to gas (e.g. tubig sa sinaing kapag inapuyan)Condensation = Gas to liquid (e.g. mga tubig sa labas ng baso ng malamig na tubig kaoag hinayaan)Solidification = Liquid to solid (e.g. yelo)Sublimation = Solid to gas (e.g. dry ice)Deposition = Gas to solid (e.g. frost)

Answer:

Statement: An even number is divisible by two.

If-then: If a number is even, then it is divisible by two

Converse: If a number is divisible by two, then it is an even

Inverse: If a number is not even, then it is not divisible by two

Contrapositive: If a number is not divisible by two, then it is not even
