20. give examples to illustrate how human persons make sense of thier world and experiences through the language of signs and symbols (long answers pls)​


Humans make sense of their world and experiences through the language of signs and symbols by using them to represent ideas, objects, and emotions. Signs and symbols are used to communicate meaning without the need for words. They can be found in all aspects of life, from art to literature, from religion to politics.

Signs are physical objects that represent something else. For example, a stop sign is a physical object that tells us to stop our vehicle when we see it. Symbols are abstract representations of ideas or concepts. For example, a heart symbol is often used to represent love or affection.

Signs and symbols can be used in many different ways to convey meaning. They can be used as metaphors or allegories to illustrate an idea or concept in a more vivid way than words alone could do. They can also be used as visual cues that help us remember something important or provide guidance on how to act in certain situations.

Signs and symbols also help us make sense of our experiences by providing us with a common language that we can use to communicate with each other. By understanding the meaning behind certain signs and symbols, we can better understand each other’s thoughts and feelings without having to use words. This helps us build relationships with others and create a shared understanding of our world.
