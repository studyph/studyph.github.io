Describe a registered voter. ​

Voter registration (or enrollment) is a requirement in electoral systems that a person who is otherwise qualified to vote must register (or enroll) on an electoral roll, which is usually a requirement for being entitled or permitted to vote.

The laws governing registration differ from one jurisdiction to the next.

Voting and registration are optional in most countries, although in others (such as Australia), registration and voting are required for citizens of voting age. In some jurisdictions, enrollment may necessitate the submission of an application by an eligible voter, as well as the re-registration or updating of registration details when a person's address or other relevant information changes.

In some jurisdictions, an enrollment agency may receive change of address information from other government agencies, which is used to automatically update voter information; for example, when a person registers a change of residence with a government agency for a driver's license, the government agency may forward the information to the electoral agency, which will update voter registration details.

Some jurisdictions need "election day registration," while others do not, or may require the provision of proof of voting eligibility at the time of voting.

10. there are 135 new voters in a barangay if this is 20% of the voting population how many registered voters are there in that barangay​


Problem:

there are 135 new voters in a barangay if this is 20% of the voting population how many registered voters are there in that barangay

Solution:

[tex]p = 135[/tex]

[tex]r = 20\%[/tex]

[tex] \red{b = 135 \div 20\%}[/tex]

[tex]b = 135 \div \frac{20}{100} [/tex]

[tex]b = 135 \times \frac{100}{20} [/tex]

[tex]b = 27 \times \frac{100}{4} [/tex]

[tex]b = \frac{2700}{4} [/tex]

[tex] \purple{b = \boxed{675}}[/tex]

Checking:

1.

[tex]b = 675[/tex]

[tex]r = 20\%[/tex]

[tex] \purple{p = 675 \times 20\%}[/tex]

[tex]p = 675 \times \frac{20}{100} [/tex]

[tex]p = 27 \times \frac{20}{4} [/tex]

[tex]p = \frac{540}{4} [/tex]

[tex] \red{p = \underline{135}}[/tex]

2.

[tex]p = 135[/tex]

[tex]b = 675[/tex]

[tex] \purple{r = \frac{135}{675} \times 100}[/tex]

[tex]r = \frac{135}{27} \times 4[/tex]

[tex]r = \frac{540}{27} [/tex]

[tex] \red{r = \underline{20\%}}[/tex]

Final Answer:

675 registered voters are there in that barangay

Always remember that:To find the percentage:

[tex] \blue{p = b \times r}[/tex]

To find the base:

[tex] \blue{b = p \div r}[/tex]

To find the rate:

[tex] \blue{r = \frac{p}{b} \times 100}[/tex]
