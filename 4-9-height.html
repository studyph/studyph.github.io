Solution:

k = constant of variation

V = khr²

h = 8 cm

r = 2cm

33.5 cm³

V = khr²

33.5 = k(8)(2)²

33.5 = k(8)(4)

33.5 = k(32)

k = 33.5/32

V = khr²

V = (33.5/32)(6)(4)²

V = (33.5/32)(6)(16)

V = 100.5 cm³

The base of the parallelogram is thrice its height. If the area is 192 cm², find the base and height.

Solution:

Let the height of the parallelogram = x cm

then the base of the parallelogram = 3x cm

Area of the parallelogram = 192 cm²

Area of parallelogram = base × height

192 = 3x × x

⇒ 3x² = 192

⇒ x² = 64

⇒ x = 8

Therefore, 3x = 3 × 8 = 24

Therefore, Base of the parallelogram is 24 cm and height is 8 cm.
