1. Ratio 540 students in 20 rooms


Answer:

540 : 20 = 27 : 1

The ratio is not in lowest terms so we found an equivalent ratio by simplifying.

Solution:

We can simplify the ratio 540 : 20 by dividing both terms by the greatest common factor (GCF).

The GCF of 540 and 20 is 20.

Divide both terms by 20.

540 ÷ 20 = 27

20 ÷ 20 = 1

Therefore:

540 : 20 = 27 : 1
