30. Why do we need to study physical science?


Studying Physical Science is important because it helps humans to explain and understand a natural phenomenon by observing their physical properties and replication in the environment. For example, ancient Greeks assumed a rainbow after a rainstorm was a blessing from the gods until repeated observation of their occurrence was combined with the study of properties of light using prisms. Physical science uses practice experimentation and the scientific method to study and measure numerous physical effects. It emphasizes the essence of learning scientific method to make studies and draw conclusions about the matter, energy, and their interactions.

Branches of Physical Science

Physical science is a wide-ranging discipline concerned with natural phenomena of the earth, atmosphere, and space. It comprises a variety of fields that includes:

Astronomy  Physics, ChemistryEarth science

Sample Career Path for Physical Science Major

Chemist- a person engaged in chemical research understanding chemical properties.

Meteorologist-  a person responsible for studying the atmospheric condition.

Astronaut - a person trained to transport in a spacecraft.
