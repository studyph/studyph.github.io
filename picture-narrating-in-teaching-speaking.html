8. The narrator or the story teller is directly speaking to the reader.


Point of View (POV)

There are three (popular) forms

First Person-  In this point of view, a character  is telling the story. You may notice "I" , "me" or "we" in first person narrations.

Second Person- The author uses a narrator to speak to the reader. In the second narration, you can notice a lot of "you", "your" or "yours".

Third Person-  an external narrator is telling the story. You'll notice a lot of "he," "she," "it," or "they" in this form of narration.
