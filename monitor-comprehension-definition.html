13. comprehensive definition of research and qualitative research. Prepare a brief explanation for each definition.​


Answer:

Research refers to the systematic and scientific investigation of a specific topic or problem in order to discover new knowledge or understand existing knowledge better. Research can be either quantitative or qualitative in nature.

Qualitative research is a research method that aims to understand and interpret social phenomena through the collection and analysis of non-numerical data, such as interviews, observations, and written or visual materials. It is used to explore and understand the meaning and experiences of individuals and groups. The goal of qualitative research is to provide a rich, detailed, and in-depth understanding of the topic or phenomenon being studied.
