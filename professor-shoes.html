5. Ano ang professor emeritus


In some cases, the term is conferred automatically upon all persons who retire at a given rank, but in others, it remains a mark of distinguished service, awarded to only a few on retirement. 9. In a university faculty center, the ratio of female to male professors is 5:3. If the total number of professors in the university is 136, how many male professors are there?


Answer and Step-by-step explanation:

Let the common factor between the number of female and male professors be x.

The ratio of female professors to male professors is 5:3, so we can write:

5x + 3x = 136

Simplifying the equation, we get:

8x = 136

Dividing both sides by 8, we get:

x = 17

Therefore, there are 3x = 3(17) = 51 male professors in the university.

19. A college faculty consists of 10 professors in a department. How many different ways can a committee be selected consisting of two groups of faculty, one with group A with 6 professors, and the other group B with 4 professors?​


To determine the number of ways to select two groups of faculty, one with 6 professors and the other with 4 professors, we can use the combination formula:

C(n,r) = n! / (r!(n-r)!)

where n is the total number of professors in the department, and r is the number of professors in each group.

For the first group (Group A), we need to select 6 professors out of 10, so we can use the combination formula as follows:

C(10,6) = 10! / (6!(10-6)!) = 10! / (6!4!) = 210

There are 210 ways to select a group of 6 professors from the 10 professors.

For the second group (Group B), we need to select 4 professors out of the remaining 4, so we can use the combination formula again:

C(4,4) = 4! / (4!(4-4)!) = 1

There is only 1 way to select a group of 4 professors from the remaining 4 professors.

To determine the total number of ways to select two groups of faculty, we can multiply the number of ways to select Group A by the number of ways to select Group B:

210 x 1 = 210

Therefore, there are 210 different ways to select a committee consisting of two groups of faculty, one with 6 professors and the other with 4 professors, from a department with 10 professors.
