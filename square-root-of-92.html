29. The sum of the roots of quadratic equation is 92.If one of the roots is -4,what could be the equation?​


PROBLEM

The sum of the roots of quadratic equation is 92. If one of the roots is -4,what could be the equation?

ANSWERThe equation could be x² - 92 - 384 = 0

SOLUTION

Identify the roots then convert it into a Quadratic Equation

Given

Sum = 92

r1 = -4

Let r1 and r2 be the roots of the Quadratic Equation

r1 + r2 = sum

-4 + r2 = 92

r2 = 92 + 4

r2 = 96

We have now the roots, which is -4 and 96. Converting into a Quadratic equation, we have,

x = - 4 | x = 96

x + 4 = 0 | x - 96 = 0

(x + 4)(x - 96) = 0

x(x) + x(-96) + 4(x) + 4(-96) = 0

x² - 96 + 4x - 384 = 0

x² - 92 - 384 = 0

Therefore, the equation could be x² - 92 - 384 = 0
