21. What is the approximate circumference of a circle with a diameter of 36 yards? (Use 3.14 as an approximation of pi.)


The circumference of a circle can be calculated using the formula C = πd, where C is the circumference, π is the value of pi, and d is the diameter of the circle.

In this case, the diameter is 36 yards, so we can plug that into the formula and get:

C = πd

C = 3.14 x 36

C = 113.04 yards

Therefore, the approximate circumference of a circle with a diameter of 36 yards is about 113.04 yards.
