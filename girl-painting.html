11. A girl, A, took 5 hours to paint a wall. Her friend, B, took twice as much time as A to paint a wall of the same dimensions. How long would the task have taken the girls if they had worked together?


Number of hours to finish the task if the friends worked together: x
Number of hours for Friend A to finish the task: 5
Number of hours for Friend B to finish the task:  2(5) = 10

Equation for Combined Work (task):
1/5 + 1/10 = 1/x

LCM (Least Common Multiple): 10x

Multiply each term by 10x, then simplify:
(10x)(1/5) + (10x)(1/10) = (10x)(1/x)
2x + x = 10
3x = 10
3x/3 = 10/3
x = 10/3   or  3 ¹/₃ hours

ANSWER: It would take 3 ¹/₃ hours for the two friends to finish the task of painting the wall of the same dimensions.

14. What's the value of this painting called "A girl with a watering can" by Auguste Renior


Answer:

In 1876, Renoir began to paint anecdotal depictions of women and children, subjects in which he excelled. A Girl with a Watering Can, typical of these works, displays a mature impressionist style attuned to the specific requirements of figure painting. Renoir's colors reflect the freshness and radiance of the impressionist palette, while his handling is more controlled and regular than in his landscapes, with even brushstrokes applied in delicate touches, especially in the girl’s face. Brilliant prismatic hues envelop the child in an atmosphere of warm light and charmingly convey her innocent appeal.

Specific identifications have been proposed for the girl, but none is convincing. More likely, Renoir depicted a neighborhood child whose pretty features pleased him. A girl with similar curly blond hair, sparkling blue eyes, plump pink cheeks, and smiling red lips appears, dressed the same way in other paintings by Renoir, suggesting she was a favorite figure in the artist’s repertory. A Girl with a Watering Can is a showcase of the grace and charm of the artist's work.

29. Which of the sentences uses the word PAINTING as noun in the sentence? A. I am painting now. B. Artists really love painting. C. Karin, a 4 year old girl, is painting her red dress.


PAINTING AS NOUN

WHICH OF THE SENTENCES USES THE WORD PAINTING AS A NOUN IN THE SENTENCE?

A. I AM PAINTING NOW.

B. ARTISTS REALLY LOVE PAINTING.

C. KARIN, A 4 YEAR OLD GIRL, IS PAINTING HER RED DRESS.

ANSWER:

The answer is B. Artists really love painting. where the painting is used as a noun.

"Artists really love painting." is the correct answer because the subject of the sentence is painting.

❌ I am painting now is not the answer because painting is used as verb in the sentence.

❌ Karin, a 4 year old girl, is painting her red dress is also not the answer because painting is used as an action verb.
