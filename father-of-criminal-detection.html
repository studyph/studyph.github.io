Explanation:

SOCO is to locate, collect, preserve and catalogue evidence from a crime scene. They are also known as crime scene investigators (CSIs). A scene of crime officer will work on a wide variety of crime scenes, from burglaries and vandalism to murders and sexual offences.

While CIDG monitors, investigates, prosecutes all crimes involving economic sabotage, and other crimes of such magnitude and extent as to indicate their commission by highly placed or professional criminal syndicates and organizations.
