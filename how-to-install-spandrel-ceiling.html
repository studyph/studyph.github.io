13. A whispering gallery has a semielliptical ceiling that is 9 m high and 30 m long. How high is the ceiling above the two foci?


Hello!


First of all, we get the equation of the ellipse.

[tex] \frac{(x-h)^2}{a^2} + \frac{(y-k)^2}{b^2} =1 [/tex]


We can put the center (h,k) at the origin, (0,0), to make things easier.

Then, from the given, since the max height is 9, that is b.

Since the width is 30, and a is from the center to one side, then a =15.

Substituting all of these, we get:


[tex] \frac{x^2}{225} + \frac{y^2}{81} =1 [/tex]


Now, we get the foci.

Since we have a and b, we can use the formula:

c^2 = a^2-b^2

c^2 = 225 - 81 = 144

c = 12


This means our foci are at (12,0) and (-12,0).


Now to get the height at the foci, all we have to do is to substitute x=12 to the equation:


[tex] \frac{12^2}{225} + \frac{y^2}{81}=1
\\
\frac{y^2}{81} = \frac{225-144}{225} = \frac{81}{225}
\\
y^2 = \frac{81^2}{225}
\\
y = \frac{81}{15} [/tex]


Well, technically, y could be the positive or negative square root, but since we are looking for a height, it must be positive. Hope this helps!
