REACTION PAPER

Reaction papers are designed to process the opinions or feelings of individuals on what they have seen, heard, or read. Reaction papers are essential responses on a given matter. The forms of reaction papers are appeals, protests, and reflections. Reaction papers could apply critical approaches such as Formalist, Gender, Historical, Reader-response, Media, Marxist, and Structuralism.

Here are some facts involving reaction papers.

D. A sports report is not a form of a reaction paper. A. Specific sources are cited to make the paper supported and reliable. B. Feminism is a critical approach that emphasizes the ways roles in gender are shown in the literature. D. Marxist is a critical approach that emphasizes the works in the society like class, roles, and power relations. A. Reader-response is the critical approach that emphasizes the personal account of the reader’s reaction.

"Love Letter for Mother Earth"

We are all aware that Earth is the only planet on which life exists.

Since of now, it is apparent that this planet has been a gift to us humans and other living things, as it provides us with all of the resources we require.
