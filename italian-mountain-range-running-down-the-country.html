22. In a group of tourists, 50% are English, 25% are Germans and the rest are Italian. Find the number of tourists of each country if the total number of tourists is 48.


Answer:

There are 24 English tourists, 12 German tourists, and 12 Italian tourists.

Step-by-step explanation:

We simply multiply the percentage and the total number of tourists.

English tourists: 50% x 48 = 24

German tourists: 25% x 48 = 12

For the Italian tourists, we add the answers. Then we subtract it from 48.

24 + 12 = 36
48 - 36 = 12

For verification, we add all our answers.

24 + 12 + 12 = 48
