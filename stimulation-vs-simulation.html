What is the difference between a model and a simulation?              &&. Simulation versus model

¬ A computer model is the algorithms and equations used to capture the behavior of the system being modeled. By contrast, computer simulation is the actual running of the program that contains these equations or algorithms. Simulation, therefore, is the process of running a model.

24. gamit Ng stimulants or use of stimulants​


Answer:

Stimulants are drugs that increase activity in the central nervous system, resulting in heightened alertness, attention, and energy. While stimulants can be helpful in certain medical contexts, such as treating attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) or narcolepsy, the non-medical use of stimulants can be dangerous and even deadly.

Here are some potential consequences of using stimulants:

Addiction: Stimulants can be highly addictive, leading to cravings and dependence.

Physical health problems: Stimulants can raise blood pressure, heart rate, and body temperature, and can lead to heart attacks, strokes, seizures, and other serious health problems.

Mental health problems: Stimulant use can cause anxiety, paranoia, aggression, and even psychosis.

Poor decision-making: Stimulants can impair judgment, leading to risky behavior such as driving while under the influence.

Academic or professional setbacks: While stimulants can improve focus and productivity in the short term, long-term use can lead to burnout, decreased performance, and even expulsion or termination from school or work.

Overall, the use of stimulants without medical supervision is risky and can have serious consequences. If you are struggling with drug use, it is important to seek help from a healthcare professional or addiction specialist
