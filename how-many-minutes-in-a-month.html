930 ÷ 60 = 15.5

Add 15 to the 217 hours

Leaving us with 0.5, since the half of 60 is 30, we will add 30 minutes on our answer

Let x = number of 15¢ stamps

Let y = number of 20¢ stamps

Total = quantity × value

quantity value total

20¢ stamps y 20¢ 20y

together 50 950

Step 3: Add down each column to get the equations

x + y = 50 (equation 1)

15x + 20y = 950 (equation 2)

Use Substitution Method

Isolate variable x in equation 1

x = 50 – y (equation 3)

750 – 15y + 20y = 950
