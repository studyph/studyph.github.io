2. blinder describe how magnetic force works on these​


Answer:

All magnets have north and south poles. Opposite poles are attracted to each other, while the same poles repel each other. When you rub a piece of iron along a magnet, the north-seeking poles of the atoms in the iron line up in the same direction. The force generated by the aligned atoms creates a magnetic field.

9. how does a blinder workpls answer it correctly ​


Answer:

History of blenders

How it works

1. Let's see how it works. Suppose you drop a strawberry in the blender. You push a button to start the blender, and the motor begin to turn the blades. The circular whirring motion creates a vortex or a spiral movement in the fluid. In the blender, the fluid includes both liquid ingredients and air. The vortex causes a vacuum at the centre of the jar, which pulls the strawberry toward the middle, much as a tornado does.

2. As the strawberry combines with the other ingredients and begins to liquify, the liquid follows the blade in a whirling motion around the container, forming a well near its centre. The well in the centre of a blender's vortex is shallow, so it displaces the contents as they're drawn toward the axis at the centre of the blade.

3. The whirling motion and lack of space below the blades forces the liquified strawberry up and on the sides. This circular pattern continues, whipping air into the contents, which helps mix the ingredients more quickly, until you stop the blender. The final result is a frothy strawberry smoothie with a homogenised blend of ingredients and a consistent texture.

Explanation:

BLENDER VS FOOD PROCESSOR

Food processors are appliances used for solid food, and blenders are better for drinks. Most food processors operate at a single speed, which is slower than a blender, but they have a pulse function that gives you greater control over chopping, kneading and cutting. Food processors aren't suited for crushing ice or making frozen drinks, and blenders are still best for pureeing soups

10. Take the blinders from your vision. what does the line from the poem by maya angelou mean?


Answer:

When the poem says, "Take the blinders from your vision, take the padding from your ears, and confess you've heard me crying and admit you've seen my tears.." It is referring to white people ignoring and blocking out African-Americans by 'blinding' their vision and 'padding' their ears.

Explanation:

Blinding means causing or seeming to cause blindness; making sight difficult or impossible.

Padding means to fill or cover something with soft materials to protect its content.

13. This is an effect in which instructors often unknowingly expect moreproductive expectations in certain individuals.a. The Double Blinders Effectb. The Rosenthal Effectc. The Altruism Effectd. The Blame Game​


Answer:

Rosenthal Effect

Ang Rosenthal Effect ang tinutukoy sa tanong, kaya ang tamang sagot ay letrang B.

Explanation:

Ang Rosenthal Effect, o kilala rin sa tawag na Pygmalion Effect, ay tumutukoy sa isang psychological na pangyayari kung saan ang pagkakaroon ng mataas na mga ekspektasyon ay nagiging sanhi ng mas magandang performance.

Ang Pygmalion ay nagmula sa mitolohiyang Griyego kung saan nahulog ang isang iskultor sa istatwang kanyang ginagawa dahil sa kagandahan nito. Ang Rosenthal naman ay nagmula kay Robert Rosenthal, na siyang nag-aral sa pangyayaring ito kasama ni Lenore Jacobson.

Ayon sa kanilang pag-aaral, ang mataas na ekspektasyon ng mga guro sa kanilang mga mag-aaral ay nagkakaroon ng isang kapansin-pansing epekto sa kanilang performance.
