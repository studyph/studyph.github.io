24. why is the ugliest sea creature is the manatee


Answer:

Manatees

Saying that manatees are the ugliest sea creature is an unpopular opinion. People often see them as sweet looking, cuddly and cute creatures.

Manatees are also called as sea cows. They have a stubbly stout and flat tails. They are slow-moving mammals found on shallow coastal areas.

They only feed on sea grasses, algaes and mangrove leaves, making them herbivores. Manatees belong in the order of Serinia and their closest living relatives are Elephants.
