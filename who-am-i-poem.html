Can you please help me critique this poem using feminism approach?​


CRITIQUE USING THE FEMINISM APPROACH

From a feminist point of view, this poem can be seen as a celebration of women's empowerment and their power to take control of their own lives and speak out for themselves. Nowadays, women don't have enough voice to acknowledge their position in society.

In despite discrimination and adversity, the poem shows women to be strong, resilient, and unshakable.

This poem is a stunning portrayal of a woman's resilience. It draws attention to the difficulties women experience in society and the various caring for others, parenting, and protective roles they play. It also highlights the idea of women as fighters who never give up in the face of suffering.

It clearly emphasizes the numerous hardships and obstacles that women confront in society. It demonstrates how women frequently shoulder the responsibility of providing care for others and forgoing their own needs to support their families and loved ones.

Strength, resiliency, and resolving issues surrounded by difficulties can be considered as a powerful celebration for women with feminist principles. Honoring women's accomplishments in this situation creates a big impact on social justice and gender equality.
