Fractions that are greater than 0 but less than 1 are called proper fractions. In proper fractions, the numerator is less than the denominator. When a fractionhas a numerator that isgreater than or equal to the denominator, the fraction is an improper fraction. An improper fraction is always 1or greater than 1.
