30. how largw exactly is a billion? how long will it take to spend 1 billion pesos if you spend 1 pesos per second?​


Problem:

How large exactly is a billion? how long will it take to spend 1 billion pesos if you spend 1 pesos per second?​

Answer:

1 billion pesos can be spend in 1 billion seconds if you spend 1 peso per second.

Here are the answers on how long will it take to spend 1 billion pesos if you spend 1 peso per second in terms of minutes, hours, days, years.

Solution using minutes:

Given:

60 seconds = 1 minute

1,000,000,000 s × 1 min / 60 s = 16,666,666.667 minutes

Solution using hours:

Given:

3600 s = 1 hr

1,000,000,000 s × 1 hr / 3600 s = 27,777.78 hours

Solution using days:

Given:

86400 s = 1 day

1,000,000,000 s × 1 day / 86400 s = 1,1575.07 days

Solution using years (considering we have 365 days in one year)

Given:

31,536,000 s = 1 year

1,000,000,000 s × 1 year / 31,536,000 s = 31.71 years
