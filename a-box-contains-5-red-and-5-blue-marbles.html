Answer:

Therefore, the probability of getting not blue is 13/30

Step-by-step explanation:

According to problem, we have a box and it contains:

10 red marbles3 white marbles17 blue marbles

So, we want to get the probability that we cannot get the blue marbles. Using the formula below:

[tex] \boxed{\bold{P(A) = \frac{Number \ of \ red \ and \ white \ marbles}{Number \ of \ marbles \ in \ the \ box}}}[/tex]

Since, the problem doesn't want us to get the blue marble. The probability will be:

[tex] \rm{P(not \ blue) = \frac{Red \ + \ White}{Red \ + \ White \ + \ Blue} = \frac{13}{30}} [/tex]

Therefore, the probability of getting not blue is 13/30
