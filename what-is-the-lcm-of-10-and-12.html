24. what is the long method of the lcm of 10 and 12​


Answer:

LCM is 60

Step-by-step explanation:

The Least Common Multiple (LCM) for 10, 12 and 15, notation LCM(10,12,15), is 60.

Explanation:

* Multiples of 10: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50, 60

* Multiples of 12: 12, 24, 36, 48, 60

* Multiples of 15: 15, 30, 45, 60

Because 60 is the first number to appear on both lists of multiples, 60 is the LCM of 10, 12 and 15.
