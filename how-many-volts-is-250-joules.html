6. 30. A 5kg remote-controlled car is moving 10m/s. What is the toy car's kinetic energy?A 2 JoulesC. 250 JoulesB. 50 JoulesD. 500 Joules​


C. 250 J

Explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

m = 5 kg

v = 10 m/s

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Kinetic Energy

Step 3: Solve for Kinetic Energy

Solution:

The formula for Kinetic Energy is given by:

KE = ½ mv²

where: KE - Kinetic Energy

m - mass

v - velocity

Substituting the value of m and v in the formula,

KE = (½)(5 kg)(10 m/s)²

KE = 250 kgm²/s² or 250 Nm or 250 J
