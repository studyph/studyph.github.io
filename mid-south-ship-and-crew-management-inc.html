16. A ship travel 200km to the south and then 400km to the west. At what angle west of south should the ship headed to arrive the same place in straight path?


Answer:

SW = 63.43°

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the angle, use pythagorean theorem
tan (SW) = 400km/200km

tan (SW) = 2

SW = arctan 2

SW = 63.43°
