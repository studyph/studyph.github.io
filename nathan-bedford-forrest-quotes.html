5. nathan left how at exactly 6:30 in the morning and reached their school at exactly 7:15.if nathan traveled 15km per minute? what is the distance of the school and nathan's home ?​


Answer: the distance between Nathan's home and school is 675 kilometers.

Explanation:

Nathan traveled for 45 minutes (from 6:30 to 7:15), and if he was traveling at a speed of 15 kilometers per minute, we can find the total distance he covered using the formula:

distance = speed x time

distance = 15 km/minute x 45 minutes = 675 kilometers

Therefore, the distance between Nathan's home and school is 675 kilometers.

25. Fred is 4 times as old as Nathan and is also 27 years older than Nathan. Let f be Fred's age and let n be Nathan's age.


Answer:

Fred is 36 years old and Nathan is 9 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's find first Nathan's age:

[tex]4n = n + 27 \\ \\ 4n - n = 27 \\ \\ 3n = 27 \\ \\ \frac{3n}{3} = \frac{27}{3} \\ \\ n = 9[/tex]

Now, for Fred's age:

[tex]f = 4n \\ \\ f = 4(9) \\ \\ f = 36[/tex]

Fred = 36

Nathan = 9

30. Fred is 4 times as old as Nathan and is also 27 years older than Nathan. Let f be Fred's age and let n be Nathan's age. Which system of equations represents this situation?


Answer:

Linear equation represents the problem and the system of equations can be solved through substitution method

Step-by-step explanation:

Let f be Fred's age

n be Nathan's age

f = 4n -eqn 1

f = n + 27 -eqn 2

The problem represents linear equations. On this case you can use substitution method in solving the system of equations

substitute equation 2 to 1

n + 27 = 4n

27 = 4n - n

27 = 3n

n = 27/3

n = 9

Substitute n=9 to either equation 1 or 2

f = 4(9)

f = 36

or

f = 9 + 27

f = 36
