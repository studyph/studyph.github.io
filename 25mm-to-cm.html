1. Complete the chart Length width height volume12 cm 10 cm 5cm 9cm. 6m. 540 m25mm. 9mm. 7mm​


Answer:

COMPLETING THE CHARTVolume of a Rectangular Prism

1. [Length = 12cm, Width = 10cm, Height = 5cm, Volume = ?]

[tex]\boxed{\tt{V_{rectangular \: prism} = length × width × height}}[/tex]

[tex]\tt{V_{rectangular \: prism} = 12 \: cm × 10 \: cm × 5 \: cm}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\tt{V_{rectangular \: prism} = 600 \: cm³}}[/tex]

2. [Length = 9 cm, Width = 6 cm, Height = ?, Volume = 540 cm]

[tex]\boxed{\tt{H = \frac{volume}{length × width}}}[/tex]

[tex]\tt{H = \frac{540 \: cm}{9 \: cm × 6 \: cm}}[/tex]

[tex]\tt{H = \frac{540 \: cm}{54 \: cm}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\tt{H = 10 \: cm}}[/tex]

3. [Length = 25mm, Width = 9mm, Height = 7mm, Volume = ? ]

[tex]\boxed{\tt{V_{rectangular \: prism} = length × width × height}}[/tex]

[tex]\tt{V_{rectangular \: prism} = 25 \: mm × 9 \: mm × 7 \: mm}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\tt{V_{rectangular \: prism} = 1,575 \: mm³}}[/tex]
