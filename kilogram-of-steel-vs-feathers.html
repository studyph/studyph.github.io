27. Two thousand kilogram of steel containing 8% of nickel is to be made by mixing steel containing 14% nickel with another steel containing 6% nickel. How much of the steel containing 14% nickel is needed? ​


Let the quantity of the steel containing 14% nickel be a and the one containing 6% be b.

1. a + b = 2,000

Multiply each by their percentage.

(14%)a + (6%)b = (8%)2000

2. 14a + 6b = 16,000

Use system of equations to find a. Multiply equation #1 to 6 so we can cancel out b.

14a + 6b =  16,000

-6a  - 6b = -12,000

8a           =   4,000

a = 500

You need 500 kg of steel containing 14% nickel.


28. A steel cylinder has a volume of 246 cm3 and a density of 7.93 g/cm3. What is its mass in kilograms?


Mass is 1.95 kg

Explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

V = 246 cm³

ρ = 7.93 g/cm³

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Mass

Step 3: Solve for Mass

Equation:

Find the mass using the density of water which is by using the formula for density:

ρ = m/v

where: ρ - density

m - volume

v - volume

Solution:

Substitute the value of ρ and v in the equation:

m =(7.93 g/cm³)(246 cm³)

v = 1,950.78 g or 1.95 kg
