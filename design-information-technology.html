3 Examples of Information and its Data

1. Weather Data - Information about the weather conditions such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation can be collected and stored as data. This data can be analyzed to make predictions about future weather patterns, inform decision-making, and inform the public about current and expected weather conditions.

2. Customer Data - Information about customers, such as their names, addresses, purchase history, and preferences, can be collected and stored as data. This data can be used to personalize marketing campaigns, improve customer service, and better understand customer behavior and preferences.

3. Sales Data - Information about sales transactions, such as the products sold, the date of sale, and the amount of the sale, can be collected and stored as data. This data can be used to track sales trends, identify best-selling products, and make informed decisions about inventory management and pricing strategies.

24. in a graphic organizer of your own design or choosing, show the advancement of information technology from the old to the new and its eefects to society


With the advancement of information technology, society has seen a great change in the way information is accessed and used. Old methods of accessing and using information, such as libraries and newspapers, are no longer the only sources of information. New methods, such as the Internet and social media, have made it possible for people to access and use information in new ways. The Internet is a global network of computers that allows users to access vast amounts of information. The Internet has made it possible for people to access information and communicate with others around the world, at any time.

 

Social media is a wide range of tools and applications that allow users to communicate, share information, or interact with other people. Social media can be used as a source of information. For example, users can access news and current events through social media.

The effects of this change in information technology on society have been both positive and negative. On the positive side, it has made it possible for people to access a wealth of information that was previously unavailable. On the negative side, it has also made it possible for people to spread false or misleading information more easily. There are various theories of information technology, which are concerned with the social impact of this development in technology. The most basic theory is that of McLuhan, who states that new technologies can only be understood in terms of their relationship with old technologies.

It can be concluded that the advancement of information technology from the old to the new has an influence on society. If previously the sources of information came from libraries and newspapers, now with the Internet and social media, it has allowed people to access and use information in new ways. The effects of this change in information technology on society are positive and negative.
