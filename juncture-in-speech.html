16. Define the following persodic features of Speech give their importance. 1.Pitch 2. Juncture 3. Stress 4. Intonation 5. Rate of Speech​


Answer:

1. PITCH- the degree of highness or lowness of a tone.

2. JUNCTURE- is the matter of moving (transition) between two successive syllables in speech. /Juncture refers to breaks or pauses in speech that indicate words or other grammatical units.

3. STRESS- the degree of emphasis given a sound or syllable in speech.

4. INTONATION- use of changing (rising and falling) vocal pitch to convey grammatical information or personal attitude.

5. RATE OF SPEECH- is how fast you talk in words per minute (wpm) and is an important component of innovative presentations.
