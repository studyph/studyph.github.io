16. A rectangle has a perimeter of 32 cm and an area of 63 square cm. Find its dimensions.


Answer:

9 x 7

Step-by-step explanation:

perimeter = 2l+ 2w = 32

area = l x w = 63

l = (32-2w)/2

l = 16 - w

(16 - w) x w = 63

16w - w^2 = 63

w^2 - 16w + 63 = 0

(w - 9)(w - 7) = 0

the two roots are 9 and 7

plug in to see which one works

if w = 9

then l = 16 - w = 16 - 9 = 7

l x w = 9 x 7 = 63

2(7) + 2(9) = 32?

14 + 18 = 32 so yes that works

if w = 7

then l = 16 - 7 = 9

l x w = 9 x 7 = 63

2(9) + 2(7) = 32

18 + 14 = 32

so the dimension of this rectangle has dimensions of 9 x 7
