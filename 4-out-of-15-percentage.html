5. B=P÷R

You can find Base by dividing Percentage and Rate:

Ex:

20% is 8 of what number?

P=8

R=20

Turn the rate into decimal and then divide the rate by percentage,

20% of N= 8

=8÷0.20

=40

The Base is 40

20% of 40 is 8
