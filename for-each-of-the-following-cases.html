14. Classify each of the following as a heterogeneous mixture or homogeneous mixture. explain your rationale in each case. brainly


HOMOGENEOUS AND HETEROGENEOUS MIXTURES

First you should remember what homogeneous and heterogeneous mixtures are before answering this question. The mixture is homogeneous if it only contains one phase. Aside than that, it's a bit of a mishmash.

So, based on the aforesaid information, the given mixtures will be classified as homogeneous or heterogeneous.

Soda water, air, vinegar, and filtered tea are the example of homogeneous.

Because the particles in this matter are not visible and behave as one phase, it is classified as a homogeneous mixture.

Wood and soil are both example of heterogeneous.

Because the particles in this stuff are clearly visible, it falls under the concept of a heterogeneous mixture. These are made up of two or more particles, as we can see. As a result, it has multiple phases.
