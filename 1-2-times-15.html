17. What time was it 3 and 1/2 hours before 7:15 AM?


Answer:

3:45 AM

Step-by-step explanation:

In simple equations like this we could solve by using fractions,

whereas we simply turn the 7:15 into 7 and 1/4 hours (since 15 is a quarter of 60) and we use the 3 and 1/2 (which is 3:30)

Solving

[tex]3 \frac{1}{2}[/tex]  - [tex]7 \frac{1}{4}[/tex]  = n

turn into Improper fractions

7/2  -  29/4  = n

Find the Lcm

(2)7/(2)2 - (1)29/(1)4 hrs = n

Equate

14/4 - 29/4 = n

15/4 = n

Simplify

[tex]3\frac{3}4}[/tex] = n

[tex]3 \frac{1}{2}[/tex] hrs - [tex]7 \frac{1}{4}[/tex] hrs = [tex]3 \frac{3}{4}[/tex] hrs

In hour unit 3 and 3/4 hrs would be 3 hours and 45 minutes.

Therefore, It was 3:45 AM 3 and 1/2 Hours before 7:15 AM
