8. to find the area of a composite figure, ________ the figure into simpler shapes whose area can be found​


Answer:

Area of Composite Shapes

Area is the amount of space inside a figure. If two figures are congruent, they have the same area (Congruent Areas Postulate). A composite shape is a shape made up of other shapes. To find the area of such a shape, simply find the area of each part and add them up.
