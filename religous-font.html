22. IS SINULOG FESTIVAL RELIGOUS?


Yes, Sinulog Festival is a Religious Festival.

What is Sinulog Festival?

Sinulog Festival is held on the third Sunday of January in Cebu City. It is one of the most famous festivals in the Philippines. It was celebrated to honor the Santo Niño (child Jesus). The festival is done by a dance ritual. The word "Sinulog" means "graceful dance" the dance represents the "sulog" (current) of a river in Cebu. The Sinulog Festival is a dance ritual that reminisces the time when Filipinos embraced Christianity.

24. enumarate the religous institutes​


A religious institute is a type of institute of consecrated life in the Catholic Church where its members take religious vows and lead a life in community with fellow members.[1] Religious institutes are one of the two types of institutes of consecrated life; the other is that of the secular institute, where its members are "living in the world.

Societies of apostolic life resemble religious institutes in that its members live in community, but differ as their members do not take religious vows. They pursue the apostolic purpose of the society to which they belong, while leading a life in common as brothers or sisters and striving for the perfection of charity through observing the society's constitutions.[3] In some of these societies the members assume the evangelical counsels by a bond other than that of religious vows defined in their constitutions.
