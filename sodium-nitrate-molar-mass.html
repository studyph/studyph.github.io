Proust states the Comparative Law that compounds are formed from elements with the same Mass Comparison, so that compounds have a constant composition of elements  

Empirical formula is the mole ratio of compounds forming elements  

In the same compound, although from different sources and formed by different processes, it will still have the same composition/comparison

14. what is the percent CO2 of the molar mass of sodium bicarbonate


Sodium Bicarbonate comes from the absorption of CO2 into NaOH

TR = 2NaOH + CO2 -> NaHCO3 + H2O

Each mole of NaHCO3 comes from the absorption of 1 mole in CO2.

Molecular weight NaHCO3  =  84 g/mol

Molecular weight CO2 = 44 g/mol

It is that 84 g of NaHCO3 had 44 g of CO2

By weight ratio, it is 52% CO2 in Sodium Bicarbonate.

Therefore, the percentage of CO2 is 52%.
