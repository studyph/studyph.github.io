Mirrors do not flip left to right. They flip front to back. Mirrors reflect light rays such that the angle of reflection equals the angle of incidence. For a three-dimensional object standing in front of the mirror, there is an image of the object created behind the mirror with the right side still on the right, the left side still on the left, the top side still on the top, the bottom side still on the bottom, but the front side is flipped to the back and the back side is flipped to the front. This can be easily seen by tracing the rays from significant points on the object and seeing where they end up in the mirror image. 27. SCIENCE AND HEALTH Illustrate what is being described. 1. The apple is on the left side of the box. 2.The apple is in front of the box. 3. The apple is between the boxes.​


Answer:

1. Apple is to the left of the box.

2. Apple is in front of the box.

3. Apple is between the boxes.

Explanation:

1. When it is said that the apple is on the left side

of the box, it means that if you were standing in front of the box and looking at it, the apple would be to your left. So, the apple is not directly in front of you, but to the side.

2. If the apple is in front of the box, it means that if you were standing in front of the box and looking straight ahead, the apple would be in your line of sight. So, the apple is directly in front of you.

3. When it is said that the apple is between the boxes, it means that there are two boxes and the apple is located in the space that separates them. So, the apple is not on the outside of either box, but rather in the middle of them.
