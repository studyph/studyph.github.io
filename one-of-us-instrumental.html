20. one instrument from a Southeast Asian country. Explain the use


TWO-STRINGED LUTE

This instrument shares many similarities with the guitar in terms of basic characteristics. It has two strings, a hollow boat-shaped body, and a neck with two tuning knobs. Like the guitar, two-stringed lutes lack a large round hole in the front. Its hole is normally located in the back, therefore the sound it produces comes from there.
