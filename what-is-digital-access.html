TECHNOLOGICAL AND DIGITAL

Technological and digital resources such as cameras, cell phones and computers are the most used for accessing, appreciating and sharing art, especially theatrical.

How can technology facilitate access, appreciation and sharing of art?

Previously, to keep up with theatrical artistic practices, people had to attend the theater, pay for tickets and queue to enjoy classic stories or a traveling play that circulated through cities, something that was not always within everyone's financial reach.

Current technology aims to democratize access to these works, while it is enough for someone to film them and make them available on large networks or paid platforms, if this is possible due to copyright, so that all those who have cell phones or computers can consume these arts, very easily and investing less money.
