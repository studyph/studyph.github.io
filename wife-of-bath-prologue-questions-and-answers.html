1. the wife of bath tale conclusion​


Answer:

The Wife of Bath's Tale concludes with the knight finally understanding the true meaning of what women desire most: sovereignty over their own lives. He gives the old woman the choice to have youth and beauty but be a lesser wife, or to have age and ugliness but also have sovereignty over him as her husband. The old woman, moved by the knight's kindness, transforms into a beautiful and virtuous wife. The knight and his new wife live happily ever after, and the Wife of Bath concludes her tale with the message that women desire sovereignty and equal treatment in marriage. She also reminds the audience that the story was told by a woman, and urges men to listen and learn from women's wisdom.


2. moral lesson of wife of bath's tale​


Answer:

The morals in the Wife's tale are usually said to be that (1) women desire dominance over men, or, to use the Old English word, women desire "sovereintee" over men and that (2) granting women dominance over men is in the best interest of men.

<h1>This is a Heading</h1>

<P>This is a paragraph.
