20. If x^2 - kx + k = 0, what is the value of k?​


Answer:

4

Step-by-step explanation:

Suppose x²-kx+k has two factors x-r and x-s

Then

x²-kx+k = (x-r)(x-s) = x² - (r+s)x + rs

Clearly

r+s = k (sum of roots is k)

rs = k (product of roots is k)

Clearly 2 has the both properties: its sum is the same as its product

-----------------------

Notice (x-2)²

(x-2)² = x² - 4x + 4

Therefore k = 4
