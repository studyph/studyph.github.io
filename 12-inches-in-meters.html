24. Convert 8.65 feet to (a) meter, and (b) inches. Conversion factors: 1 meter = 3.28 feet 1 foot = 12 inches


ANSWERS:(A). 2.637 meters

(B) 103.8 inches

SOLUTIONS: Solution for letter A:

➠ To find the answer, we just need to divide the given (8.65 feet) by 3.28.

[SOL]

N = 8.65 feet ÷ 3.28N = 2.637 meters

FINAL ANSWER: Hence, if you convert 8.65 feet into meters the result will be 2.637 meters.

Solution for letter B:

➠ To find the answer, we just need to multiply the given value which is 8.65 feet to 12.

[SOL]

N = 8.65 feet • 12N = 103.8 inches

FINAL ANSWER: Hence, if you convert 8.65 feet into inches the result will be 103.8 inches.
