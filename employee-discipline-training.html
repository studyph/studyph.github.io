28. what is the impact of outsourcing disciplines on employees who are being outsourced and employees who may stay​


Answer:The impact of outsourcing on employees who are being outsourced can be significant, as they may lose their jobs or be required to transition to a new role with a different employer. This can lead to feelings of insecurity, stress, and uncertainty about the future. On the other hand, for employees who may stay, outsourcing can also have a negative impact as they may feel apprehensive about the stability of their own jobs and the future of the company. However, outsourcing can also have positive impacts, such as cost savings and improved efficiency for the company, which can potentially lead to job growth and career opportunities in the long term. Ultimately, the impact of outsourcing will depend on various factors, such as the specific industry, the nature of the work being outsourced, and the strategies employed by the company to mitigate the effects on its employees.
