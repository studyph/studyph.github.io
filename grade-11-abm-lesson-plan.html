18. Grade 11 Scope and Delimitation Title: effectiveness of the ABM strand In developing entrepreneurship skills?​


Answer:

The scope of the study titled "Effectiveness of the ABM Strand in Developing Entrepreneurship Skills" focuses on the ABM (Accountancy, Business and Management) strand in senior high school and its impact on the development of entrepreneurship skills among its students. The study will investigate the following:

1. The extent to which the ABM strand curriculum covers entrepreneurship education and its components.

2. The level of entrepreneurship skills development of students who took ABM strand as compared to other strands.

3. The perceptions of ABM students on the effectiveness of the ABM strand in developing their entrepreneurship skills.

4. The challenges and factors that affect the development of entrepreneurship skills among ABM students.

The delimitations of this study include the following:

1. The study will only focus on the ABM strand and its impact on entrepreneurship skills development among senior high school students in a specific school or area.

2. The study will only use a self-administered questionnaire as the research instrument to gather data from the respondents.

3. The study will not cover other factors that may affect entrepreneurship skills development among students, such as family background, personal experience, and extracurricular activities.

4. The study will not explore the impact of the ABM strand on the academic performance of the students.
