16. Shelly sleeps for 7 hours 30 minutes in a day. For how many hours did she sleep in the month of January?


Let Us Solve!

The month of January has 31 days.

Solution:

Multiply 31 days by 7 hours and 30 minutes separately.

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: 31 \\\times \: \: \: \: 7 \\ - - - - - - - \\ \: \: \: \: 217[/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: 31 \\\times \: \: \: \: 30\\ - - - - - - - \\ \: \: \: \: 930[/tex]

Divide 930 minutes by 60

930 ÷ 60 = 15.5

Add 15 to the 217 hours

217 + 15 = 232

Leaving us with 0.5, since the half of 60 is 30, we will add 30 minutes on our answer

So,

232 hours + 30 minutes = 232 hours and 30 minutes

Final Answer:

232 hours and 30 minutes
