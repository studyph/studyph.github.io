[tex] \frac{200}{4} = \frac{\cancel4k}{\cancel4}\\[/tex]

[tex]k = 50 [/tex]

[tex]t = (50)(5)[/tex]

[tex]t = 250[/tex]

250 km can travel in 5 hours

B. Inverse Proportion

If 6 men can do a certain job in 10 days,how many men willl be required to do the same job in 12 days?

FORMULA

[tex]\large\color{black}{{{\boxed{\tt\red{} \:\:\:\:\: m = \frac{k}{d} \:\:\:\:\:\:\:\ }}}}[/tex]

[tex]6 = \frac{k}{10}\\[/tex]

[tex]\frac{6}{1} = \frac{k}{10}\\[/tex]

[tex]k = 60[/tex]

[tex]m = \frac{60}{12}\\[/tex]

[tex]m = 5[/tex]

5 men will required to do same job in 12 days
