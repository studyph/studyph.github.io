Answer:

Given:

a = 46 ft.b = 53 ft.c = 83 ft.

Since only the sides are given, it is a Side-Side-Side or SSS case. Therefore, we should use the Law of Cosines.

c² = a² + b² - 2ab cos(C)

b² = a² + c² - 2ac cos(B)

a² = b² + c² - 2bc cos(A)
