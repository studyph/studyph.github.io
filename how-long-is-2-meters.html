29. the area of a rectangular streamer is 20 square meters. It's with is 2 squaroot of 2 meters .How long is the streamer​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving word problems about solving area of a figure.

Given

area of a rectangular streamer is 20 square meters.

width is 2 squaroot of 2 meters (2√2)

Solution

Use the formula for finding area of rectangle A = lw

A = lw → 20 = l(2√2)

20 = l(2√2) → l = 20/2√2

2√2 = 2(2)^½

20 = 10×2^1

Law of Exponents (Quotient Rule)

10×2^1 ÷ 2(2)^½

→ 10 ÷ 2 [2]^(1-½)

→ 10 ÷ 2[2]^½

→ 5[2]^½ = 5√2

Answer

5√2 meters
