Answer:

To determine the total number of canned goods from the worksheet about food donations, you will need to follow these steps:

Open the worksheet and locate the column that contains information about the canned goods.

Scan through the column and add up the number of canned goods that have been donated.

Once you have a total number, you can record it or use it as needed.

Alternatively, if the worksheet has a table or spreadsheet format, you can use a formula to calculate the total number of canned goods. For example, if the column containing the canned goods is labeled "Canned Goods" and the row numbers are labeled in column A, you can use the following formula in an empty cell: "=SUM(C2:C100)" (assuming that the canned goods are listed in rows 2 through 100). This formula will add up all the values in the "Canned Goods" column and display the total in the cell where the formula is entered.
