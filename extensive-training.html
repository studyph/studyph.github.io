Extensibility is a measure of the ability to extend a system and the level of effort required to implement the extension. 19. Why is it bad practice to plug in two or three more extension cord to another extension cord?​


Once you have two identical extension leads connected in series, one after the other, the extension cord's resistance effectively increases. This results in greater heating of the cord and possible damage to the insulation, which can be the source of fires and electrical shock.

Dynamics

Dynamics refers to the volume of a sound or note. The term is also applied to the written or printed musical notation used to indicate dynamics. Dynamics are relative and do not refer to specific volume levels.

Traditionally, dynamic markings are based on Italian words, although there is nothing wrong with simply writing things like “quietly” or “louder” in the music. Forte means loud and piano means soft.
