3. The secondary voltage is 220 Volt and primary voltage is 110 volt, then the number of turns ratio is?​


Answer:

A transformer with a primary winding of 1000 turns and a secondary winding of 100 turns has a turns ratio of 1000:100 or 10:1. Therefore 100 volts applied to the primary will produce a secondary voltage of 10 volts.

5. what is the resistance of a circuit of a potential difference of 110 volts processor current of 10 a​


Resistance is 11 Ω

ΩExplanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

I = 10 A

V = 110 V

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Resistance

Step 3: Solve for Resistance

Equation:

The formula for Resistance is given by:

R = V/I

where: R - Resistance

I - Current

V - Voltage

Solution:

Substituting the value of V and I in the formula,

R = (110 V)/(10 A)

R = 11 Ω
