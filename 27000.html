2. 27000 written in scientific notation is ?​


Answer:

27,000 is written 27 × 10.

Length: 0.000 000 14m = 1.4×10

Height: 0.000 275m = 2.75×10

Width: 0.000 002 56m = 2.56×10

Answer:

2.7 x 10^4

Explanation:

Calculating scientific notation for a positive integer is simple, as it always follows this notation:    a x 10^b

Follow the steps below to see how 27,000 is written in scientific notation.

Step 1

To find a, take the number and move a decimal place to the right one position.

Original Number: 27,000

New Number: 2.7000

Step 2

Now, to find b, count how many places to the right of the decimal.

New Number:              2      .   7 0 0 0

Decimal Count:                             1 2 3 4

There are 4 places to the right of the decimal place.

Step 3

Building upon what we know above, we can now reconstruct the number into scientific notation.

Remember, the notation is: a x 10b

a = 2.7 (Please notice any zeroes on the end have been removed)

b = 4

Now the whole thing:

2.7 x 10^4

Step 4

Check your work:

10^4 = 10,000 x 2.7 = 27,000
