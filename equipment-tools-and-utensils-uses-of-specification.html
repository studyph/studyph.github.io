12. [TLE G8]COMMONLY USED EQUIPMENT, TOOLS AND UTENSILS IN FISH PROCESSING AND ITS USE OF SPECIFICATION(NOW NA PO PLS)​


Explanation:

Here are some commonly used equipment, tools, and utensils in fish processing and their uses/specifications:

Fillet knife - used for removing the bones and skin from the fish. It should be made of high-quality stainless steel and have a blade length of 6-9 inches.

Fish scaler - used for removing the scales from the fish. It can be a manual or electric scaler and should have a firm grip and durable construction.

Cutting board - used for cutting and preparing fish. It should be made of food-grade material, non-porous, and easy to clean and sanitize.

Fish gutting knife - used for gutting the fish. It should be made of high-quality stainless steel and have a blade length of 6-8 inches.

Fish pliers - used for removing the bones from the fish. It should be made of high-quality stainless steel and have a sturdy construction.

Fish net - used for catching and transferring fish. It should be made of durable material and have a good grip.

Chopping knife - used for cutting and chopping the fish. It should be made of high-quality stainless steel and have a sharp edge.

Fish descaler - used for removing the scales from the fish. It can be a manual or electric descaler and should have a firm grip and durable construction.

Fish skinning knife - used for removing the skin from the fish. It should be made of high-quality stainless steel and have a sharp edge.

Food thermometer - used for checking the temperature of the fish to ensure it is cooked to the correct temperature. It should have a clear display and be accurate.

These are just some of the commonly used equipment, tools, and utensils in fish processing. It is important to choose high-quality, durable, and safe tools to ensure the quality of the processed fish and the safety of the workers
