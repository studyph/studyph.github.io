12. KE = 1/2 (70 kg)(6.7m/s)²​


Kinetic Energy Calculation

Answer:

               1, 571.15 J or 1. 57 KJ

Explanation:

Formula:

KE=  [tex]\frac{1}{2} mv^{2}[/tex]

m=mass of object

v=velocity of the object

Given:

m= 70 kg ; v= 6.7 m/s

Solution:

   KE =  [tex]\frac{1}{2} (70 kg)(6.7m/s)^{2}[/tex]

         =  [tex]\frac{1}{2} (70 kg)(44.89m^{2} /s^{2} )[/tex]

        =  [tex]\frac{1}{2} (3,142 kg m^{2} /s^{2} )[/tex]

         = 1,571.15 J or 1. 57 KJ
