••Nutrition Assessment.

••Nutrition Diagnosis.

••Nutrition Intervention.

••Nutrition Monitoring and Evaluation.

The Nutrition Care Process Model (NCPM) describes the NCP by presenting the workflow of professionals in diverse individual and population care delivery settings. It illustrates the steps of the Nutrition Care Process as well as internal and external factors that impact application of the NCP.
