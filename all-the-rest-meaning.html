What could be the meaning of tortuous and cynosure in these sentences?​


Okay, let's analyze the meaning of the bolded words in those sentences:

Tortuous: winding, twisting, convoluted. In this sentence, it means a winding, twisting road that curves and bends in a complicated manner.Cynosure: something that attracts attention or admiration. In this sentence, it means the center of attention or focus. The new Miss Philippines was the cynosure (focus) of all attention and eyes.

Some synonyms for tortuous could be winding, twisting, zigzag, convoluted.Some synonyms for cynosure could be focus, center of attention, focal point.

So in summary:

Tortuous road = winding, twisting road

Cynosure of all eyes = center of attention, focus of all eyes
