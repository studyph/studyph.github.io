30. the average speed? 3. What will be the average speed in km/h if the car travels 130 km in 2 hours and 30 minutes? show your solution. Pasagot po plss​


Answer:

To find the average speed, we can use the formula:

Average speed = total distance ÷ total time

We are given that the car travels 130 km in 2 hours and 30 minutes. We need to convert the time to hours, so:

2 hours and 30 minutes = 2 + 30/60 = 2.5 hours

Now we can substitute the values into the formula:

Average speed = 130 km ÷ 2.5 hours

Average speed = 52 km/h

Therefore, the average speed of the car is 52 km/h.
