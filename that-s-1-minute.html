28. Arunner runs 400m dash in 1 minute 2 secondsWhat is his average speed in m/s?A. 6.45 m/sB. 6.48 m/sC. 7.50m/sD. 7.55 m/s​


Answer:

A. 6.45 m/s

Step-by-step explanation:

The problem is asking for his average speed in meters per second, so convert the time in seconds first. 1 minute and 2 seconds is 60 + 2 = 62 seconds Afterwards, you'll use the speed formula.

                          Speed = Distance ÷ Time

Then, substitute values.

                          Speed = 400 m ÷ 62 s

                              Speed = 6.45 m/s

Thus, the runner's average speed is 6.45 m/s.
