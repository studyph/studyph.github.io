10. if your pencil is 20cm long.how long is its meter.?


Answer:

The equivalent of 20 centimeters in meters is 0.2.

Further Explanation

The process to solve this problem is to apply the concepts of units conversion.

Converting of units is the process of transforming between different units of measurement for an equivalent value by using conversion factors.

Conversion factors are used to change units of measure without altering its value.

Two Types of Conversion Processes

1. Metric System

2. English System

Some Basic Length Conversions

1.  1 mm = 0.1 cm = 0.001 m

2. 10 mm = 1 cm  = 0.01 m

3. 1000 mm  = 100 cm = 1 m

4. 1000000 mm = 100000 cm = 1000 m

5. 25.4 mm = 2.54 cm = 0.0254 m

To solve the equivalent meter of 20 cm, multiply 20 cm by a conversion factor which is [tex]\frac{1\:\text{m}}{100 \:\text{cm}}[/tex] since there are 100 cm in 1 m.

         

                           [tex]20\:\text{cm}\times{\frac{1\:\text{m}}{100\:\text{cm}}}=20\times\frac{1\:\text{m}}{100}=\frac{20}{100}\:\text{m}=0.2\:\text{m}[/tex]

This means that based on the solution above the equivalent of 20 cm in meters is 0.2.
