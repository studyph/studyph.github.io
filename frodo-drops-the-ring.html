10. Frodo's average score in all his four (4) tests is 89. If he is to take another set of test, what score he needs to target so that his average will reach 90? Select one: a. 92 b. 89 C. 90 d. 94​


Answer:

94

Step-by-step explanation:

(89+89+89+89+X)/5 =90 (assume that in 4 tests he gets 89 all)

5(89+89+89+89+X)/5=90(5)

356+X = 450

X= 450-356

X = 94
