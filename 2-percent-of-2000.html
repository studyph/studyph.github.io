4. the population of hamden was 350,000 in 1990. by 2000, the population had decreased to 329,000. what percent of decreased is this?​


Answer:

The population is decreased BY 6%.

The population decreased TO 94%.

Step-by-step explanation:

We can use the formula:

[tex]\frac{V1-V2}{V2} \\[/tex] x 100 = ?

V1 = Final value

V2 = Initial value

**multiplied by 100 to get the percentage

[tex]\frac{329, 000-350, 000}{350, 000}[/tex]x 100 = 0.06 or 6%

The computed percentage is the percentage decrease from 1990 to 2000.

However, we can also say that the population decreased TO 94%. The population in 1990 which was 350, 000, is considered 100%. Therefore, if we deduct the 6% decrease, we now have a population that decreased to 94%.

11. how many years will you pay 2000 pesos that accumulates an interest total of 240 pesos with 6 percent interest rate?


Step-by-step explanation:

You can use the formula for compound interest:

A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)

Where:

A = the final amount

P = the principal (starting amount)

r = the interest rate (as a decimal)

n = the number of times the interest is compounded per year

t = the time (in years)

We can rearrange this formula to solve for t:

t = (log(A/P)) / (n * log(1 + r/n))

Plugging in the given values, we get:

A = P + I = 2000 + 240 = 2240

r = 0.06

n = 1 (compounded annually)

t = (log(2240/2000)) / (1 * log(1 + 0.06/1)) ≈ 3.46 years

Therefore, it would take approximately 3.46 years to pay 2000 pesos with a 6 percent interest rate and accumulate a total interest of 240 pesos.
