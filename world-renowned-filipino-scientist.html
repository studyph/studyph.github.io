2. who is the filipino investment banker turned fashion designer, world renowned for luxury lingerie and lounge wear


Answer:

Explanation:

            In the different fields of investment banking and design, Josie Natori was an incredibly successful business woman.             Natori started a line of women's clothes that can be worn as intimate clothes or high fashion, like sleepwear or outer garments, acting on a comment by a purchasing agent, one who selects clothes that a store will sell, that a blouse Natori was selling would be more attractive as a nightshirt.

28. one of the most internally renowned piece of modern filipino


Answer:

The Spoliarium is the most valuable oil-on-canvas painting by Juan Luna, a Filipino educated at the Academia de Dibujo y Pintura (Philippines) and at the Academia de San Fernando in Madrid, Spain. With a size of 4.22 meters x 7.675 meters, it is the largest painting in the Philippines. A historical painting, it was made by Luna in 1884 as an entry to the prestigious Exposicion de Bellas Artes (Madrid Art Exposition, May 1884) and eventually won for him the First Gold Medal
