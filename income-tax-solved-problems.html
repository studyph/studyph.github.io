29. the following are the country’s problems in taxation discuss and explain your solution about this •Tax money is not represented fairly • Income tax rate is too high


Answer:

1. Tax money is not represented fairly: One potential solution to this issue is to implement a progressive taxation system. Under a progressive taxation system, higher income earners pay a higher percentage of their income in taxes. This can help to ensure that those who are able to pay more in taxes do so, while also providing a greater degree of support to those who are lower income earners. Other potential solutions could include implementing a wealth tax, which taxes the accumulated wealth of individuals rather than just their income, or implementing a progressive consumption tax, which taxes goods and services at higher rates for those who are able to afford them.

2. Income tax rate is too high: There are several potential solutions that could help to address the issue of high income tax rates. One option is to offer tax credits or exemptions to certain groups of taxpayers, such as low-income earners or small business owners. Another option is to undertake a comprehensive review of the tax system and identify areas where reform is needed, such as simplifying the tax code, reducing the number of tax brackets, or eliminating unnecessary deductions and exemptions. Additionally, improving the efficiency and effectiveness of the tax collection process could help to reduce the burden on taxpayers by ensuring that all individuals and businesses are paying their fair share of taxes.
