10. Evaluate ﻿limit as t rightwards arrow 0 of fraction numerator 1 minus cos space 7 t over denominator 4 t end fraction﻿.​


Answer:

The limit as t approaches 0 of the expression (1 - cos(7t)) / (4t) can be evaluated using L'Hopital's rule.

L'Hopital's rule states that if the limit of a fraction approaches 0/0 or ∞/∞ as x approaches a value, then we can differentiate the numerator and denominator and evaluate the limit of the fraction again. If the resulting fraction still approaches 0/0 or ∞/∞, then we can differentiate again, and so on.

The derivative of (1 - cos(7t)) is 7sin(7t), and the derivative of 4t is 4. Therefore, the limit as t approaches 0 of the fraction (1 - cos(7t)) / (4t) is equal to the limit as t approaches 0 of (7sin(7t)) / 4, which is 7 * 1 / 4 = 7/4.

So, the limit as t approaches 0 of (1 - cos(7t)) / (4t) is 7/4.

23. What number must be added to the numerator and denominator of the fraction4/11so the value of the new fraction is 1/2


The solution is:
1/2 - 4/11
Get their LCD (least common denominator) which is 22.
For 1/2, divide its denominator by 22, and you'll get 11. Multiply it to 1 and you'll get a fraction of 11/22.
Next for 4/11, divide its denominator by 22 and you'll get 2. Multiply it to 4 and get a fraction of 8/22.
Since they have the same denominators you can simply subtract both fractions:
11/22 - 8/22 = 3/22 :)

A. What is 3 1/4 x 3 1/3?

Convert mixed to improper fractions:

3 1/4 = 13/14

3 1/3 = 10/3

multiply:

13/4 x 10/3 = 130/12

convert to a mixed number:

130/12 = 10 10/12

and simplify:

10 10/12 = 10 5/6

Here it is in one line:

3 1/4 x 3 1/3 = 13/4 x 10/3 = 130/12 = 10 10/12 = 10 5/6

The answer is: 10 5/6

B. Find the quotient. Write it in simplest form.

Given: 2 1/6 ÷ 1 1/5

First, write the mixed numbers as improper fractions.

2 1/6 = 13/6

1 1/5 = 6/5

So, the expression becomes

13/6 ÷ 6/5

Multiply by the reciprocal of 6/5, which is 5/6.

13/6 ÷ 6/5 = 13/6 • 5/6

Multiply the numerators and multiply the denominators.

= 65/36

Write the improper fraction as a mixed number.

1 29/36

So,

2 1/6 ÷ 1 1/5 = 1 29/36
