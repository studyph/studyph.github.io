4. Three times one number equals ¼ another number. If the sum of 4 times the first number and 12 equals three times the second number, what is the number?​


Answer:

First number: 3/8

Second number: 9/2

Step-by-step explanation:

3x = 1/4y

12x = y

Substitute 12x to y

4x + 12 = 3y

4x + 12 = 3(12x)

4x + 12 = 36x

12 = 32x

12/32 = x

3/8 = x

Substitute 3/8 to x

3(3/8) = 1/4y

9/8 = 1/4y

36/8 = y

9/2 = y
