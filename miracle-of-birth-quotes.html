18. explain the message it had been saved by a miracle -a miracle of faith and love​


Answer:

The phrase "it had been saved by a miracle - a miracle of faith and love" refers to the ability of anything or someone to be delivered from a trying or life-threatening circumstance. The term "miracle" implies that this redemption was unanticipated or improbable and that it can be attributed to a higher power or energy. The expression "miracle of faith and love" alludes to the fact that everyone involved's belief and concern contributed to the outcome, proving the strength of these two qualities to overcome even the most formidable obstacles. Overall, the message emphasizes the value of love and faith in helping us get beyond whatever challenges we may encounter in life.

30. what is Miracle crusade


Answer:

The Jesus Miracle Crusade International Ministry (JMCIM) is an Apostolic (Oneness) Pentecostal religious group in the Philippines which believes particularly in the promotion of miracles, and faith in God for healing.

They currently claim 1,500,000 members in the Philippines and 15 other countries. It is founded by  WILDE E ALMEDA.

With 40 assemblies outside the Philippines, the bulk of their membership is within the country.

It has members in the following countries:

Philippines, United StatesCanadaEuropeMiddle EastAustraliaSingaporeJapanChina, and several parts of Asia .

JMCIM is the largest Oneness Pentecostal organization in the Philippines, holding several services weekly at the Amoranto Sports Complex in Quezon City, Metro Manila, with Sunday attendance in the tens of thousands at that location alone.
