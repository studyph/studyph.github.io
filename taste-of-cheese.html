7. A food company produces grated cheese made from two types of cheese. One type of cheese costs P29 per kilogram and the other costs P31 per kilogram .How much of each type of cheese was used in making 200kg of cheese worth P29.50?


Hello po,

So let's say the company uses x kilograms of the first type of cheese.

Since they made 200kg of cheese, they used 200-x kgs of the second type.

That's why the equation would be:

29x + 31(200-x) = 29.50(200)

This is just equating the prices (price/kg x kg) of each type of cheese.

Simplifying the above equation:

29x + 6200 - 31x = 5900

-2x = -300

x = 150 kg of the first cheese (P29/kg)

This means that we have

200 - 150 = 50kg of the second cheese (P31/kg).

We can check this:

150kg x P29/kg + 50kg x P31/kg = P5,900 for 200kg, or P29.5/kg.

FYI, this is what is commonly called a "mixture problem". Just in case you want to search for other practice problems.

Hope this helps!

28. Refers to the savory taste of meat stock, cheese, and mushrooms, that some scientists believe is another type of taste bud.


It is savoriness, commonly known as umami (うま味), it is the fifth (5th) of the basic tastes. Umami (うま味) literally means "pleasant savory taste". It was in 1908 when Dr. Kikunae Ikeda, a Japanese chemists, coined the word which is also translated as yummy or deliciousness.

The most common food seasoning that contains the unique umami taste is the Monosodium Glutamate (MSG).
