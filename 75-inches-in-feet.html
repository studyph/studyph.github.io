What are the dimensions of the room? 3. The length of a side of a square field is 275 m. What will be cost of levelling the field at a rate of 5 pesos per square meter? 4. Find the area of a trapezoid with bases of 9 centimeters and 7 centimeters, and a height of 3 centimeters. 5. The area of a trapezoid is 75 square inches and the bases are 17 inches and 33 inches. Find the height. 6. Find the area of a parallelogram with a base of 7 inches and a height of 10 inches. 7. The area of a parallelogram is 24 square centimeters and the base is 6 centimeters. Find the height.

Area of a Rectangle = length x width

Area of Classroom (Floor area) ÷ Area of each Floor tile
