2. Give 7 examples of quadrilaterals and its characteristics.

Parallelogram

A parallelogram is a simple quadrilateral with two pairs of parallel sides.

Rhombus

A rhombus is a 2D shape with four straight, equal sides with both pairs of opposite sides being parallel.

Kite

A kite is a quadrilateral whose four sides can be grouped into two pairs of equal-length sides that are adjacent to each other.

Rectangle

A rectangle is a quadrilateral with four right angles. It can also be defined as: an equiangular quadrilateral, since equiangular means that all of its angles are equal.

Trapezoid

A trapezoid is a quadrilateral with one pair of opposite sides parallel.

Square

A square is a four-sided regular polygon which is also known as a quadrilateral with four equal sides.

Isosceles Trapezoid

An isosceles trapezoid is a convex quadrilateral with a line of symmetry bisecting one pair of opposite sides.
