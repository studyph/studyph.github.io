3. The number of pages that carolyn wrote in her journal each day from monday to friday is shown below:9,8,12,6,10 what is the mean number of pages she wrote per day out?A.5B.6C.9D.11​


Answer:-

Given terms

9,8,12,6,10Number of terms=5

We know

[tex]\boxed{\sf Mean=\dfrac{Sum\:of\:terms}{Number\:of\:terms}}[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf\longmapsto Mean=\dfrac{9+8+12+6+10}{5}[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf\longmapsto Mean=\dfrac{45}{5}[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf\longmapsto Mean=9[/tex]
