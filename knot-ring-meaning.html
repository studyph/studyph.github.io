24. which knot is the simplest type of knot and is used to make a knob in a rope, string, or cord? a.bowline knot b.slip knot c.square knot d.overhand knot​


Answer:

D. overhand knot

Explanation:

The overhand knot is the simplest type of knot and is used to make a knob in a rope, string, or cord. It is used for tying packages, to keep rope ends from fraying, and as a first step in making more complex knots such as the surgeon's knot and the square knot.


25. what is the meaning of french knot​


Answer:

A Stitch

Explanation:

French Knot - A stitch in which the thread is wound around the needle, which is then passed back through the fabric at almost the same point to form a small dot. The French knot is one of several knotted stitches used in surface embroidery and produces a knot similar to a colonial knot. French knots are used as fillings for flower centres, for tiny powdered patterns and anywhere when the effect of a single dot is required.
