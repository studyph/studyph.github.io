15. how would you relate the story of romeo and juliet in your friends​


Answer:

Well, the story of Romeo and Juliet is a classic tale of young love and tragedy. It tells the story of two young lovers from feuding families in Verona, Italy. Despite their families' bitter feud, Romeo and Juliet fall deeply in love and are determined to be together.

Their love is forbidden, and they must keep it a secret. However, their families' hatred for each other leads to a tragic turn of events, and the young couple's love story ends in heartbreak and death.

The story of Romeo and Juliet shows the power of love and the destructive consequences of hate and revenge. It teaches us that love can conquer all, but it also warns us of the dangers of holding onto grudges and letting hatred consume us.
