CREATE A TOPIC SENTENCE

1. An endangered species

Topic sentence: The most common reason why there are endangered species is habitat loss, invasive species, and poaching

Main idea: Reasons why endangered species exists

Controlling idea: habitat loss, invasive species, and poaching are the common reasons why there are endangered species.

2. The cost of fuel

Topic sentence: Petroleum costs are continuing to rise

Main idea: The continuous rise of petroleum costs

Controlling idea: Petroleum costs are persisting on rising

3. The legal drinking age

Topic sentence: Their group said that 21 is the legal drinking age for everyone because, at that point of age, they might control themselves from getting drunk

Main idea: The group's point of view about the legal drinking age

Controlling idea: 21 is the legal drinking age for everyone because, at that point of age, they might control themselves from getting drunk.

4. A controversial film or novel

Topic sentence: A film or novel becomes controversial because of its millennial based genre and updated theme

Main idea; Reasons why a film or novel becomes controversial

Controlling idea: Because of the updated theme and millennial based genre of a film or novel It becomes famous and controversial.

12. What is the meaning of cohesion in writing? a. It is the unity of words in a sentence. b. It is the clarity of ideas at the idea level. c. It is the connection of ideas at the sentence level. d. It is the oneness of ideas and meanings.


C.

Cohesion concerns the flow of sentences and paragraphs from one to another. It involves the tying together of old information and new. When we write academic essays, particularly in the humanities, we work hard to foster cohesion structurally, which enhances a reader's understanding of our ideas.
