1. Mound object Bus distance travelled 210 miles convert miles/km Time 12 hours what is the speed​


To convert miles to kilometers, we can use the conversion factor 1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers.

Therefore, the distance travelled by the bus in kilometers is:

210 miles × 1.60934 km/mile = 337.96 kilometers

The time taken by the bus to travel this distance is given as 12 hours.

So, the speed of the bus is:

Speed = distance/time

Speed = 337.96 km/12 hours

Speed = 28.163 km/hour (approx)

Therefore, the speed of the bus is approximately 28.163 km/hour.
