5. what is the passive or active sentence for this The girl recited the poem beautifully


Answer:

The poem was beautifully recited by the girl. This is because the adverb is placed before the past participle when a sentence is in passive voice. Hence, 'beautifully recited' is the correct expression. Here, 'beautifully' is an adverb and the past participle is 'recited' of the verb 'recite'.
