26. Last september my water reading was 3588 cubic meters. The next month, the reading is 3647 cubic meters. How much will i pay water consumption If a cubic meter costs P 15.75 ? with computation​


Question:

Last September my water reading was 3588 cubic meters. The next month, the reading is 3647 cubic meters. How much will i pay water consumption If a cubic meter costs P 15.75 ? with computation​

Answer:

Use the formula below to compute the Usage

Usage = Present Reading - Previous Reading

where,

Present Reading = 3647 m³

Previous Reading = 3588 m³

Solve for Usage,

Usage = Present Reading - Previous Reading

Usage = 3647 - 3588

Usage = 59 m³

So the water consumption is 59 m³

solve for Bill,

Bill = Cost × Usage

Bill = P 15.75 × 59

Bill = Php 929.25

[tex]\therefore \large\boxed{\text{\sf The total bill is Php 929.25}}[/tex]
