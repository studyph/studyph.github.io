16. define the "traditional landscape painting" and "modern landscape painting"?​


Answer:

Traditional Landscape Painting

– Landscape painting, the depiction of natural scenery in art. Landscape paintings may capture mountains, valleys, bodies of water, fields, forests, and coasts and may or may not include man-made structures as well as people.

Modern Landscape Painting

– Driven in part by their dissatisfaction with modern urban life, many artists sought out places resembling untouched earthly paradises where they could focus on their work and observe nature closely. They generated radical artistic experiments, which might seem more likely to have been sparked by the visual and cultural stimulation of the city. But surrounded by nature and in rural communities, they began breaking the traditions of landscape painting, using, for example, non-naturalistic colors or applying paint in ways that emphasized their brushwork

23. NEED HELP List 5 geological formations which can be found in the Philippines(2) and Asia(4) and identify the type of plate boundary involved causing such natural landscape of the earth Example: Mayon Volcano - Convergent plate Boundary


Geological Formations in the Philippines

1. Philippine Sea Plate - Subduction Zones

Philippine Sea Plate is an oceanic plate surrounded by subduction zones. The plate is moving northwest at a rate of 6 – 8 cm/year towards the Eurasian Plate. Ranken and Cardwell (1984) showed that the rate of convergence increases southwards along the trench. The plate rotates with respect to the pole closed to the triple junction of the Philippine Sea, Eurasian, and Pacific Plates at the northern tip of Philippine Sea Plate. The rate of rotation is about 0.5˚/million years (Ma), yielding a total of approximately 90˚ rotation since early Tertiary. It is generally suggested that the plate motion was kept about constant since 3 – 5 Ma, but some study argued that the direction of plate propagation changed at about 1 Ma.[10]

2. Philippine Mobile Belt - Convergent Plate Boundary

The Philippine Mobile Belt (also called as Taiwan-Luzon-Mindoro Mobile Belt) is a complex tectonic zone which sits at the convergence zone of the Eurasian Plate, Philippine Sea Plate and Indo-Australian Plate. It covers the whole Philippine archipelago and extends southwards to the Molucca Sea and eastern Indonesia. The belt is seismically active associated with frequent earthquakes and active volcanism.

Geological Formations in Asia

1. The Marble Mountains - Convergent Plate Boundaries

Marble Mountains (Vietnamese: Ngũ Hành Sơn; lit. "five elements mountains") is a cluster of five marble and limestone hills located in Ngũ Hành Sơn District, south of Da Nang city in Vietnam. The five mountains are named after the five elements: Kim (metal), Thuy (water), Moc (wood), Hoa (fire) and Tho (earth).

2. Grandfather's Grandmother's Rocks - Hin Ta Hin Yai, Ko Samui

Storied natural rock formations known for bearing a resemblance to male & female genitalia.

3. Mu Ko Ang Thong National Marine Park, Ang Thong

Mu Ko Ang Thong is a marine national park in the Gulf of Thailand in Surat Thani Province. It covers 42 islands in a total area of 102 km², of which about 50 km² are land and the rest is water. The park was established on 12 November 1980. The northern tip of Ko Phaluai is also part of the marine park.

4. Seongsan Ilchulbong, Seogwipo

Seongsan Ilchulbong, also called ‘Sunrise Peak’, is an archetypal tuff cone formed by hydrovolcanic eruptions upon a shallow seabed about 5 thousand years ago.

26. kahulugan ng vegetable landscape o edible landscaping​


Ang vegetable landscape o edible landscaping ay isang uri ng paghahardin kung saan pinaghahalo ang mga bulaklak at gulay sa iisang halamanan. Sa ganitong uri ng disenyo, maaari kang magkaroon ng mga prutas at gulay, at iba pang halaman sa iyong hardin. Ang mga gulay ay maaaring lumago sa tabi ng iyong mga bulaklak.  

Sa ganitong paraan, hindi lamang nagbibigay ng disenyong magandang pagmasdan, kundi maari kang magkaroon ng mapagkukunang halaman sa likod-bahay at malimit ang gastos kung bibili pa sa palengke. Maraming uri ng gulay na maaaring isama sa vegetable landscaping sa paligid ng iyong bahay. Ang ilan sa mga pinaka-karaniwang gulay at mga halaman para sa edible landscaping ay:

Repolyo Litsugas Bell pepper/Siling pula Pechay Kamatis Mga bulaklak gaya ng Bougainvillea,  Santan, Chichirica, Dayap Citrus/Lemon Mga halamang Gamot (gaya ng mint)
