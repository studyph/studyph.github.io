15. How does Google Translate come up with these reinterpretations of the lyrics already sung by famous artists in the video parody? Explain your answer.


Answer:

Google Translate uses an algorithm to interpret the original lyrics and generate new, translated versions. The algorithm takes into account the context of the lyrics, the tone of the original lyrics, and the language of the original lyrics. It then uses this information to generate new lyrics that are similar in content and meaning to the original lyrics. Google Translate also uses machine learning to further refine its translations, allowing it to generate more accurate reinterpretations of the original lyrics.

1. Maingay - noisily

2. Mabilis - quickly

3. Malakas - loudly

4. Maganda - beautifully

5. Maaga - early

6. Madalas - frequently

7. Matagal - for a long time

8. Masipag - diligently

9. Nagluluto siya nang mabuti. (She cooks well.)

10. Kumakatok siya nang mahina. (He knocks softly.)
