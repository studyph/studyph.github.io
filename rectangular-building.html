19. A building is a rectangular prism that is 205 meters tall. Its base is 90.6 meters by 35.4 meters. What is the total surface area of the building?


Answer:

63,473 square meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the total surface area of the building, we need to find the surface area of each of the six faces of the rectangular prism and then add them together.

The surface area of the base of the building is the area of the rectangle that is its base, which is 90.6 meters by 35.4 meters, or 3174.24 square meters.

The surface area of each of the four sides of the building is the area of a rectangle that is as tall as the building and as wide as one of its sides. The building is 205 meters tall and its sides are 90.6 meters and 35.4 meters long, so the surface area of each side of the building is 18120.3 square meters or 7248.12 square meters.

The total surface area of the building is the sum of the surface area of its base and the surface area of its four sides: 3174.24 square meters + 2 * 18120.3 square meters + 2 * 7248.12 square meters = 63472.76 square meters.

So, the total surface area of the building is approximately 63,473 square meters.

22. The United Nations building in New in New York is York is a a rectangular prism that is 165m tall. Its base measures 75.2 cm by 21.5m. What is the total surface area of the building?​


Answer:

To calculate the total surface area of the United Nations building in New York, which is shaped like a rectangular prism, we need to find the area of each of the six faces and then sum them up.

Given: Height (h) = 165 m Base length (l) = 75.2 cm = 0.752 m Base width (w) = 21.5 m

Surface Area of a Rectangular Prism: The surface area of a rectangular prism is given by the formula: Surface Area = 2lw + 2lh + 2wh

Step 1: Convert base length to meters: Base length (l) = 0.752 m

Step 2: Calculate the surface area of each face: a) Top and bottom faces (2lw): Top face: 2 x 0.752 m x 21.5 m Bottom face: 2 x 0.752 m x 21.5 m

b) Front and back faces (2lh): Front face: 2 x 0.752 m x 165 m Back face: 2 x 0.752 m x 165 m

c) Left and right faces (2wh): Left face: 2 x 21.5 m x 165 m Right face: 2 x 21.5 m x 165 m

Step 3: Sum up the surface areas of all six faces: Surface Area = Top face + Bottom face + Front face + Back face + Left face + Right face

Step 4: Calculate the total surface area: Surface Area = (2 x 0.752 m x 21.5 m) + (2 x 0.752 m x 165 m) + (2 x 21.5 m x 165 m)

After performing the calculations, the total surface area of the United Nations building in New York will be obtained in square meters (m^2).

30. A rectangular building lot is 8ft. longer than it is wide and has an area of 2900f².find the dimension of the lot.​


Answer:

58ft and 50ft

Step-by-step explanation:

First let x be the width and;

x + 8 be the length.

in getting the area of a rectangle we use the formula :

A = lw

Since we already know the area, we can use it to solve for the dimensions :

A = lw

2900 = x(x + 8)

2900 = x² + 8x

I'll use Completing the Square for simple purposes.

x² + 8x = 2900

x² + 8x + 16 = 2900 + 16

(x + 4)² = 2916

√(x + 4)² = √2916

x + 4 = 54

we'll use the principal root because there are no negative dimensions.

x + 4 = 54

x + 4 - 4 = 54 - 4

x = 50

now we can use it to get the dimensions

x = width

x + 8 = length

50 = width

50 + 8 = length

58 = length

The dimensions are 58ft and 50ft.
