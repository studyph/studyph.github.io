9. How long is a leg of an isosceles trapezoid if the bases are 68 cm and 20cm, and it's perimeter is 154 cm?a. 33b. 34c. 35d. 36​


Answer:

Option a. 33

Step-by-step explanation:

An isosceles trapezoid have two congruent legs, let each leg be x , so :

[tex]154 = 68 + x + 20 + x[/tex]

[tex]154 - 68 - 20 = 2x[/tex]

[tex]2x = 66[/tex]

[tex]x = \frac{66}{2} [/tex]

[tex]{\boxed{{{x = 33}}}}[/tex]

Hence, leg of the trapezoid is 33m.
