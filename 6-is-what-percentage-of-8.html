[tex]\huge\mathfrak\pink{EXPLANATION}[/tex]

PERCENTAGE

Percentages are like fractions and decimals. They are ways to describe what proportion of a whole is represented by a number.Percentage is a term from Latin,meaning “out of one hundred”. We can consider a “whole” broken up into 100 equal parts, each part is a single percent. Percentage represents a part of the base

BASE

The base is the number that represents the whole or the 100 percent. The rate defines what part the percentage is of the base.

RATE

Rate" simply means the number of things per some other number, usually 100 or 1,000 or some other multiple of 10. A percentage is a rate per 100

17. In our solar system, 6 of the 8 planets have moons. What percentage of the planets have moons?


Hello!
.
We must first need to divide the number of planets with moons by the number of planets in our solar system.
Planets with moons = 6
Planets in the solar system = 8
6 / 8 = 0.75
.
Then, move the decimal point two places to the right and add a percent sign.
0.75 --> 75 --> 75%
.
Therefore, the percentage of the planets that have moons in our solar system is 75%.
