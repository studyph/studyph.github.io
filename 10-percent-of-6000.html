9. a poll conducted by a local newspaper asking if the governor was doing a good job.6000 people were polled.If 2400 people said they approved of the job the governor was doing,what percent of the people polled disapproved?​


Answer:

60% of the people polled disapproved of the governor's job.


Step-by-step explanation:

If 2400 people approved of the governor's job, then the remaining number of people who disapproved is:

6000 - 2400 = 3600

To find the percentage of people who disapproved, we need to divide the number of people who disapproved by the total number of people polled and then multiply by 100 to get a percentage:

(3600/6000) x 100% = 60%
