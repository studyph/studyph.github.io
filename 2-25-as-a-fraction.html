Answer:

[tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

1.) Any percent or percentage can be expressed as a fraction over 100.

⇒ [tex]25\% = \frac{25}{100} = \frac{5 \times 5}{2 \times 2 \times 5 \times 5} = \frac{1}{2 \times 2} = \frac{1}{4}[/tex]

2.) Therefore, the simplified fraction form of the percentage [tex]25\% = \frac{1}{4}[/tex].
