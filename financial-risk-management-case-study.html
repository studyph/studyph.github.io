30. an assessment on the financial management and literacy among grade 11 students. What will be the study's contribution to our society?​


Firstly, it helps to identify the level of financial literacy among students, which can serve as a basis for the development of more effective financial education programs. With the increasing complexity of financial products and services, it is essential for individuals to have a basic understanding of financial principles and concepts to make informed decisions.

Secondly, the study can lead to better financial planning and management among students, which can result in improved financial well-being and reduced debt levels. Young people today are facing several financial challenges, such as student loans, credit card debt, and the high cost of living, which can affect their future economic stability.

Thirdly, the study can contribute to creating a financially aware generation that is equipped with the knowledge and skills to navigate the complex financial landscape. This can lead to a positive impact on the economy as a whole, as financially literate individuals are better equipped to make informed decisions and participate in economic activities.

In conclusion, conducting an assessment on the financial management and literacy among grade 11 students is crucial for identifying gaps in financial education and developing effective strategies to improve financial well-being and create a more economically literate society.
