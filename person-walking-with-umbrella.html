3. why should you use umbrella or protective cover when walking under the sun


We use umbrella as a protective cover when walking under the sun to act as a shield that protect us from the harmful UV rays of the sun. Exposure to direct sunlight can lead to different skin problems like skin asthma, sun burn and rashes. Even though sunlight is good for the production of vitamin D. Prolong exposure to sunlight can lead the UV rays of the sun to damage the fibers in the skin called elastin, thus making the skin sag. It can also contribute to the risk of having skin cancer and tumor.
