4. write a paragraph long advice for your classmates who might be having difficulties this time of pandemic. Write them positive and encouraging words. Thank Youuuu in advance


Answer:

Sometimes, your friends may share information that indicates they might need help or more attention from an adult, such as that they are depressed or that they wish to harm themselves or someone else. It is important not to talk about your friends behind their backs, but talking to an adult if your friend needs help (even if they ask you not to) is sometimes necessary.

If you feel comfortable, you may also share a personal story of how you are feeling and overcoming the concerns and challenges. However, make sure not to shift the focus of the conversation, keeping your friend as the central part of it. If they aren’t ready to talk, don’t push them. You can tell them, “You don’t need to share your feelings with me if you don’t want to, but I am still happy to spend time with you and keep you company.” You can just sit beside them and reassure them that you will be there when they are ready to talk. It will make them feel that they aren’t alone and they will slowly begin to open up.

8. paano po to give words with long sound and use them in sentence Ayan na po lahat ang tanong ko[tex]pa \: help \: lang \: po[/tex]​


Explanation:

No sentence should be any longer than it needs to be. A sentence should express, in the minimum number of words, the exact thought that the writer wants to communicate. Longer sentences are called for when a writer’s thought is complex enough to require them, and when his audience is intelligent and committed enough to read them.

People usually find difficulty in learning different sentence structures and using those accurately because they go after memorising the grammatical rules. However, more than memorising, you just have to understand the position of the words in a sentence. Let me explain this with an example.

e.g. Unlike adulthood, childhood is a stage when we are not mature enough to take hard decisions.

Now try to understand the position of the words and learn the sentence formation.

Then try to make more sentence in the same formation just by changing the words.

e.g. Unlike girls, boys are not that much interested in playing with dolls.

We can observe this by:

Learn by reading more sentences.Learn by copying those sentence structures.Learn by making new sentences that you just copied.
