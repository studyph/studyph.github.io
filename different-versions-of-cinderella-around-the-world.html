In the story, the prince is interested in finding a partner with whom to share power and make a better world. Her gender-neutral parent's refusal to tie gender to any societal expectation or form of oppression reflects feminist values related to gender equality and empowerment.

3. The fair godperson in the story symbolizes those who use their power or authority to help others.
