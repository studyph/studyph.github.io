23. A mango weighs 250 grams each. If you will buy 20 mangoes and they are sold for Php60,00 perkilogram, how much will they cost?show your step by step​


Answer:

They will cost P300

Step-by-step explanation:

Since each mango weighs 250 grams and you intend to buy 20 mangoes, Multiply 250 (How much a single mango weighs) and 20 (the number of mangoes you wish to buy).

Equation: 250 x 20 = 5000g or 5 kg

Now we know the total weight of the mangoes,

Multiply P60.00 (The price of the mangoes per kg) and 5 (The total weight of the mangoes.)

Equation: 60 x 5 = 300

So the total price of the mangoes is P300
