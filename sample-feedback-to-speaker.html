Give the speaker regular feedback. (True or False)​

True.

Giving feedbacks such as "very good" and "that's nice" are good ways to show that you understand and feel what the speaker is trying to display. You can nod, occasionally. From time to time, you can also say "uh-huh," "en," and "hmmm." Doing these things will tell the speaker that you can follow what he says.
