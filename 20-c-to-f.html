What is 20 degrees Celsius in Fahrenheit?

The two different types of units or scales to measure temperature are Celsius and Fahrenheit. In other words, they are two different scales to measure the temperature.

Answer: 20° Celsius is equal to 68° Fahrenheit.

Let's look into the conversion between Celsius and Fahrenheit scales in detail.

The formula to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit is given by °F = °C × (9/5) + 32

F = [ C × (9/5) + 32 ]

Given that, C = 20

F = 20 × (9/5) + 32

F = 36 + 32

F = 68

Thus, 20 °C is equivalent to 68 °F.

Answer: 68° Fahrenheit
