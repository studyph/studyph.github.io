Which of the following statements does NOT describe the characteristics of a good melody? A good melody _____.

▶The statements does NOT describe the characteristics of a good melody is A good melody is easily forgotten .

▶A good vocal melody is one that can be sung by anyone, or at least be easily memorable as a melody. Singability is in a range of about a 5th, and should be mostly step-wise or made up of mostly chord tones. Some people can easily sing an octave, most can’t or is not within their range to do so.

▶A good non-vocal melody has less constraints, and is probably never more than 8 bars. The solos in pop songs are a good example when they are more composed and are played more or less the same in every performance. Think Elliot Easton's solo on Just What I Needed. Even at 8 bars, there are four distinct phrase/statements.

▶Good melodies will have elements of both of these. But there are memorable melodies with much less, which has more to do with vocal styling and delivery and less about the melody itself.

20. Melody's family wants to go to Hawaii, which is a 3,700 mile airplane ride. Write the number of miles in scientific notation. *


Answer:

3.7 * 10³

Step-by-step explanation:

In order to write in scientific notation, we must express the number in the form a * 10ᵇ where 0 < a < 10. In other words, we are forcing the number to only have one digit to the left of the decimal point that is NOT 0.

3,700

= 3.7 * 10ᵇ

Since the number is big and we moved the decimal point to the left 3 times, our exponent should be positive 3.

= 3,7 * 10³
