What is Exponents?

Its like the four Arithmetic Opertions but Exponents or also called Indices tells us to take a number and multiply it by it self, a certain number of times. In short its a "repeated multiplication".

Now lets see what exponent numbers look like..

2⁴

The two or the big number is called the Base. The small number or the four is called the Exponent. What are these? The base is the one who get multiplied repeatedly a certain number of times. On the other hand, the Exponent tells us how many copies of the base to multiply together.

2⁴

2×2×2×2= 16

Exponent number 2 is usually called "Squared"

Exponent number 3 is usually called "Cubed"
