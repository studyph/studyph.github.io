5. The ABC corporation gave out P38 dividend per share for its common stock the market value of the stock is P108. Determine the stock yield ratio.


To determine the stock yield ratio, you will need to divide the dividend per share by the market value of the stock.

In this case, the dividend per share is P38 and the market value of the stock is P108. So, the stock yield ratio is:

Stock yield ratio = Dividend per share / Market value of stock
= P38 / P108
= 0.35 (rounded to the nearest hundredth)

Therefore, the stock yield ratio for ABC Corporation is 0.35. This means that for every P100 worth of stock, the investor will receive P0.35 in dividends.
