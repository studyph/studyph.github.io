1. ano ang collective bargaining agreement


Ang collective bargaining agreement ay nangangahulugan at tumutukoy sa isang kontrata o kasulatan ng isang emlpleyado at ng kumpanya may kinalaman sa halaga ng sahod at oras ng trabaho. Kinakailangan ito upang maging malinaw ang usapan sa pagitan ng dalawa nang sa gayon ay hindi magkakaroon ng anumang problema sa kanila sa mga susunod na panahon.Ang Collective Bargaining Agreement o CBA ay isang kasunduan sa pagitan ng employer at ng unyon ng mga manggagawa tungkol sa sahod, oras ng pagtatrabaho at nauukol na lugar sa pagtatrabaho. Ang layunin ng CBA ay pagiging gabay o basehan kung sakaling magkaroon ng di pagkakaunwaan sa hinaharap. Pinoproteksyunan din ng CBA ang mga karapatan ng mga manggagawa laban sa mga mapang-abusong employer.

2. Ano ang kahulugan ng collective bargaining agreement


Collective Bargaining Agreement

Ang “Collective Bargaining Agreement” ay isang espesyal na uri ng kontrata at kasunduan sa pagitan ng isang kompanya at mga empleyado ng kompanya. Nakapaloob sa kasunduang ito ang tamang pasahod, oras ng trabaho at ang kaayusan ng lugar ng pinagtratrabahuhan ng isang empleyado. Ito ay isang kontrata at kasunduan na tungkulin sundin ng bawat empleyado at kompanya. Ito ay ginawa upang magkaroon na maayos at malinaw na usapan sa pagitan ng kompanya at mga empleyado nito.

3. wht do you mean by collective bargaining agreement?​


Answer:

A collective bargaining agreement (CBA) is a written legal contract between an employer and a union representing the employees. The CBA is the result of an extensive negotiation process between the parties regarding topics such as wages, hours, and terms and conditions of employment.


4. Paano nakakatulong ang Collective Bargaining Agreement sa Kaunlaran?


Answer:

Nakakatulong ang Collective Bargaining Agreement sa kaunlaran sa pamamagitan ng pagkakaroon ng kapayapaan sa pagitan ng manggagawa at ang kapitalista.

Explanation:

Ang Collective Bargaining Agreement (https://brainly.ph/question/998418) o CBA ay isang kontrata sa pagitan ng manggagawa at employer na nirerepresenta ng unyon sa panig ng manggagawa at management sa panig ng employer. Sila ang nakikipag-ayos ng terms and conditions of work, sweldo at karapatan ng bawat panig. Ito ay kanilang dinadaan sa proseso ng negosasyon at sa dulo ay magkaroon ng kasunduan na magbibigkis sa kanilang dalawa sa tagal ng tatlong (3) taon o limang (5) taon, komporme sa kanilang desisyon.

Ito ay tadhana rin ng ating Saligang Batas, sa Article 13, Section 3: Social Justice Provision, na binibigyang karapatan ang manggagawa na magkaroon ng pakikipagtawaran sa management at binigyan din ng buhay ng Labor Code natin sa Article 250. Ito ay isang napakagandang proseso ng relasyon sa pagitan ng employer at manggagawa sapagkat ang nagbibigkis sakanya ay isang kontratang napagkasunduan nila sa pamamagitan ng isang negosasyon.  

Sa 1987 Constitution natin, itinadhana na ang mga mangagawa ay bigyan ng karapatan mula sa kanyang pag-oorganisa ng mga union, pakikipagtawaran (CBA), concerted action including the right to strike, security of tenure at living wage. Isa din syang pagkilala doon sa pagkakaroon ng Industrial Peace. Ang layunin ng CBA ay magkaroon ng stability at magkaroon ng pagtaas sa benepisyo ng mga manggagawa base sa pag-unlad ng kumpanya sa panahong sila ay nasa trabaho.  

Ang laman ng CBA ay ang Economic Benefits at Political Benefits ;

Economics – Wages, Terms and Conditions of Work – pay, rest days, leave.  Political Rights – pagkilala ng unyon, management prerogative, mechanism to resolves disputes at grievance machinery  

Kapag pinagsama ang dalawa, ito ay hindi lamang para sa manggagawa kundi para din sa benepisyo ng kapitalista o employers. Anumang alitan o dispute ay pinagbibigkis ang parehong partido. Ang nilalayon ng CBA ay magkaroon ng Industrial Harmony at pagkilala sa karapatan ng manggagawa sa sahod at benepisyo, at pagkilala sa karapatan ng kapitalista sa kanyang pagpapatakbo sa kumpanya.

5. this refers to the idea that person's can we truly benefit from a decision or agreement instead of one person losing or getting the short end of the bargain​


Answer:

Social contract theory, nearly as old as philosophy itself, is the view that persons’ moral and/or political obligations are dependent upon a contract or agreement among them to form the society in which they live. Socrates uses something quite like a social contract argument to explain to Crito why he must remain in prison and accept the death penalty. However, social contract theory is rightly associated with modern moral and political theory and is given its first full exposition and defense by Thomas Hobbes. After Hobbes, John Locke and Jean-Jacques Rousseau are the best known proponents of this enormously influential theory, which has been one of the most dominant theories within moral and political theory throughout the history of the modern West. In the twentieth century, moral and political theory regained philosophical momentum as a result of John Rawls’ Kantian version of social contract theory, and was followed by new analyses of the subject by David Gauthier and others. More recently, philosophers from different perspectives have offered new criticisms of social contract theory. In particular, feminists and race-conscious philosophers have argued that social contract theory is at least an incomplete picture of our moral and political lives, and may in fact camouflage some of the ways in which the contract is itself parasitical upon the subjugations of classes of persons.

10. Discuss what you have understand of collective bargaining agreement?​


Answer:

Collective bargaining is a process of negotiation between employers and a group of employees aimed at agreements to regulate working salaries, working conditions, benefits, and other aspects of workers' compensation and rights for workers. The interests of the employees are commonly presented by representatives of a trade union to which the employees belong. The collective agreements reached by these negotiations usually set out wage scales, working hours, training, health and safety, overtime, grievance mechanisms, and rights to participate in workplace or company affairs.

What is Collective Nouns

In linguistics, a collective noun is a collection of things taken as a whole. Most collective nouns in everyday speech are not specific to one kind of thing, such as the word "group", which can be applied to people ("a group of people") or dogs ("a group of dogs") or other things.

What is Verb Agreement?

Agreement or concord happens when a word changes form depending on the other words to which it relates. It is an instance of inflection, and usually involves making the value of some grammatical category "agree" between varied words or parts of the sentence.
