What is the number for this number sentence?

4/8 × 2/6 × 6 = N

Choices

A. 1

B. 1/6

C. 8/48

D. 1/48

Solution

4/8 × 2/6 × 6 = N

4/8 × 2/6 × 6/1 = N

Because the six is whole number, we need to make it into fraction. So its Denominator is 1.

4 × 2 × 6/48

The Denominator of 8,6, and 1 is 48.

The numerator of 4,2, and 6 is 48.

48/48 or 1.

Answer

A. 1
