Explanation:

Imminent means that there is something that is likely to occur at any moment. In the scenario given, a soldier’s life is always in danger, may it be during a patrol or even while in their camp. Forthcoming is a synonym of imminent.

inevitable refers to something that cannot be stopped or prevented from happening. While dangers are inevitable in a soldier’s life, this word is not a synonym of imminent, thus this is not the correct answer.

14. Do you think that global warming Is really an imminent would threat why or why not​


Answer:

Yes, global warming is an imminent threat. The Earth's climate is changing at an unprecedented rate due to human activities such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation. This has caused temperatures to rise, sea levels to rise, and extreme weather events to become more frequent and intense. These changes are already having a negative impact on ecosystems, economies, and human health around the world. If we do not take action now to reduce emissions and mitigate the effects of climate change, the consequences could be catastrophic.

Answer:

Yes,global warming is indeed an imminent threat to the planet.

The Earth's climate is changing rapidly due to human activities, mainly the burning of fossil fuels, deforestation, and other land-use changes, which release large amounts of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. These gases trap heat from the sun, leading to rising temperatures, sea level rise, more frequent and intense extreme weather events, and other dangerous impacts on our planet.

Numerous scientific reports and studies from organizations such as the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) and NASA have provided overwhelming evidence that global warming is happening, and it is caused by human activities. The impacts of global warming are already being felt around the world and are projected to worsen in the coming decades, affecting human health, food and water security, and biodiversity.

Therefore, global warming is indeed an imminent threat that needs to be addressed urgently and collectively by individuals, governments, and organizations around the world to mitigate its impacts and ensure a sustainable future for our planet.


15. what potential mechanism can global policy maker implement to address the imminent food crisis​


Answer:

The solutions to the food crisis are those that make the lives of family famers easier: re-regulate the market, reduce the power of agri-foods industrial complex, and build ecologically resilient family agriculture. Here are some of the needed steps:

Explanation:

1. Support domestic food production.

2. Stabilize and guarantee fair prices to farmers and consumers by re establishing floor prices and publicly owned national grain reserves. Establish living wages for workers on farms, in processing facilities, and in supermarkets.

3. Halt agrofuels expansion.

4. Curb speculation in food.

5. Promote a return to smallholder farming. On a pound per acre basis, family farms are more productive than large-scale industrial farms. And they use less oil. Because 75 percent of the world's poor are farmers, this will address poverty too.

6. Support agro-ecological production.

7. Food sovereignity: Recognize the right of all people to be healthy and culturally appropriate food produced through ecologically sound methods and their own food systems.
