[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{PROBLEM}:} [/tex]

In a leadership seminar, the ratio of male delegates to the female delegates was 7:8. If there were 40 more female delegates than male delegates, how many delegates were there at the seminar?

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{ANSWER}:} [/tex]

[tex] \qquad \Large \:\: \rm{600 \: delegates} [/tex]

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION}:} [/tex]

» Let x be the number of males and (x + 40) be the number of females in the ratio of 7:8. Find x.

[tex] 7 \::\: 8 = x \::\: (x + 40) [/tex]

» Multiply the means and the extremes.

[tex] 8x = 7(x + 40) [/tex]

[tex] 8x = 7x + 280 [/tex]

[tex] 8x - 7x = 280 [/tex]

[tex] x = 280 [/tex]

» Therefore, there are 280 males. Find the number of females if it is 40 more than the number of males.

[tex] F = x + 40 [/tex]

[tex] F = 280 + 40 [/tex]

[tex] F = 320 [/tex]

» Thus, there are 320 females in the seminar. Find the total number of delegates.

[tex] 280 \:M + 320 \: F = 600 \: delegates [/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] There are 600 delegates in the seminar.

9. what do you think are some of the advantages and disadvantages of delegation​


Answer:

Advantage

The biggest benefit of delegation is that it helps the management in concentrating more on important activities of the company because routine and repetitive work can be given to lower level managers. So for example in a finance department of the company activities like daily payment of wages, payment of routine expenses are given to lower management while important decisions like preparation of monthly and yearly budget, financing decision and so on are taken by the top management.

Disadvantages

The biggest disadvantage of delegation is that the success of delegation lies in the employees to whom the task is delegated so if the employee to whom the task is delegated is incompetent then there is no use of delegation as it will harm the company more. So for example, if a company delegates recruitment process of taking new employees to lower level HR managers who are not competent and are biased towards taking their own people in the company then the whole process of the delegating the work will be a failure.

26. Which of the following can affect the span of management? Select one: a. Nature of work b. All of the given options. c. Capacity of superiors d. Delegation of authority


Answer:

Naturw of work

Explanation:

Span of management is the number of direct reports or subordinates that a manager or superior can managed effectively.

There are factors affecting span of management. They are:

1. Qualification and qualities

2. Level of management

3. Nature of work

4. Superior-subordinate relationship

5. Degree of centralization

6. Communication strategy

7. Financial position of the company

8. Clarity of plans and responsibility

So the answer to your question is Nature of work. If the work is simple or is routinary, then span of control or management is easy. If the work becomes difficult or heavy, it will be more challenging for the manager to manage.
