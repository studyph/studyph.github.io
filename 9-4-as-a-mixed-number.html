1. what is 9\4 in mixed number​


- Divide the numerator by the denominator.

- Write down the whole number result.

- Use the remainder as the new numerator over the denominator. This is the fraction part of the mixed number.

9/4 = 2 1/4

Answer:

The answer will be [tex]2\frac{1}{4}[/tex] in mixed number.

For instance, if you divide 9 by 4, this will be the result:

Quotient: 2 (Whole Number)

Remainder: 1 (Numerator)

Divisor: 4 (Denominator)

Rewriting and arranging this, it will be:

[tex]\boxed{2\frac{1}{4}}[/tex]
