23. A white-tailed deer can sprint at speeds up to 30 miles per hour. American bison can run at speeds up to 3,520 feet per minute. Which animal is faster and by how many miles per hour? There are 5,280 feet in one mile.


Answer:

[tex]\boxed{The\:American\:bison\:is\:faster\:than\:the\:white\:tailed\;deer\:by\:10\:miles\:per\:hour.}[/tex]

Computation:

Given,

[tex]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}White-tailed\:deer:&30\:mi/hr\\American\:bison:&3,520\:ft/min\\5,280\:ft=&1\:mi\end{array}\right][/tex]

Convert the speed of American bison to miles per hour.

[tex]\frac{3,520\:\diagdown\!\!\!\!\!ft}{\diagdown\!\!\!\!\!min}*\frac{60\diagdown\!\!\!\!\!min}{hr}*\frac{1\:mi}{5,280\:\diagdown\!\!\!\!\!ft}=\frac{211,200\:mi}{5,280\:hrs}=40\:mi/hr[/tex]

Comparing both speeds,

[tex]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}White-tailed\:deer:&30\:mi/hr\\American\:bison:&40\:mi/hr\end{array}\right][/tex]

We can now say that the American bison is faster than the white-tailed deer by 10 miles per hour.
