2. is magnet's concentration of force is on its side?​


Answer:

Poles

The magnetic lines of force flow from pole to pole just like in the bar magnet. However, since the poles are located closer together and a more direct path exists for the lines of flux to travel between the poles, the magnetic field is concentrated between the poles.

18. In 1938, Kristallnacht was the night when Jewish people were freed from Nazi concentration camps. Jewish communities organized protests against the Nazi Party. Nazi mobs attacked Jewish businesses, homes, and schools. Nazis gathered Jewish people and forced them to move to ghettos.


Answer:

In 1938, Kristallnacht, also known as the "Night of Broken Glass," was a night when Nazi mobs in Germany and Austria attacked Jewish businesses, homes, and synagogues. This violent pogrom resulted in the destruction of Jewish property, the arrest and imprisonment of thousands of Jews, and the death of at least 91 Jews. The name "Kristallnacht" refers to the broken glass that littered the streets from the shattered windows of Jewish-owned businesses, homes, and synagogues. The event marked a significant turning point in Nazi persecution of Jews, as it signaled the escalation of violence towards them and the beginning of the Holocaust.
