25. Group yourself by 2 and answer this activityWorksheet: Motion GraphsQuestions 1-4 refer to thevelocity-time graph of a car'smotion:1. In which section is the caraccelerating from rest?2. In which section is the car'sacceleration negative?3. How far does the car travelduring section "b"?(SAU) A127​


Answer:

1. The car is accelerating from rest in section "a", which is the straight line with a positive slope indicating a constant positive acceleration.

2. The car's acceleration is negative in section "c", which is the straight line with a negative slope indicating a constant negative acceleration.

3. The distance traveled by the car during section "b" can be determined by calculating the area under the velocity-time graph for that section. Since section "b" is a triangular region, the formula for the area of a triangle can be used:

Area = (base x height) / 2

The base of the triangle is 6 seconds (from t = 2 s to t = 8 s) and the height is 24 m/s, so the distance traveled during section "b" is:

Area = (6 s x 24 m/s) / 2 = 72 meters
