Dividing FractionsWhat is a fraction?A fraction is a numerical quantity, but it is not a whole number. It is like a proportion or a part of a whole number.Fractions can be added, subtracted, multiplied, and divided through the use of certain mathematical operators.

How to divide fractions?Dividing fractions is based on multiplication, so it is good to know how to multiply fractions first before dividing.To multiply fractions, simply get the product of the numerators over the product of the denominators.

     DIVIDING FRACTIONS

Step 1

         - identify the fractions that you are going to divide.

         example: [tex]\frac{7}{8} , \frac{1}{60}[/tex]

Step 2

         - get the reciprocal of the divisor (the divisor is found after the

         word "by")

                            [tex]\frac{7}{8} , \frac{60}{1}[/tex]

Step 3

         - multiply the fractions

                      [tex]\frac{7}{8} * \frac{60}{1} = \frac{420}{8}[/tex]

Step 4

          - simplify if possible

                    [tex]\frac{420}{8} = \frac{105}{2} = 52.5[/tex]
