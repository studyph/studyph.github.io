What are smart boards called before they were smart?

SMART BOARD

One type of interactive whiteboard is the SMART Board. An interactive whiteboard, at its most basic level, lets you project an image and 'interact' with it by writing on it or moving it around. The SMART Board is used with a projector and is connected to a computer. The projector shows what's open on the computer, and the SMART Board has a touch screen, so you can manipulate anything on the screen with your fingers rather than using a mouse or keyboard (though you can). It's comparable to how you'd use an iPad or tablet. A SMART Board comes with special pens that make writing in different colors quick and easy.

Joaquin built a circular ceiling for a living room. The radius of the ceiling is 5 feet if 1 foot=12 inches. Find the area of the ceiling in inches.

ANSWER:The ceiling has a shape of a circle. You are tasked to find the area of the ceiling given the radius.

☞ Let's first convert 5 feet to inches.

[tex] \large \rm 5 \times 12 = 60 \: inches[/tex]

☞ Then solve for the area using the formula.

[tex]\large\rm A=πr^2[/tex]

[tex]\large\rm A=(3.14)(60 \: in)^2[/tex]

[tex]\large\rm A=(3.14)(3 \: 600 \: in {}^{2} )[/tex]

[tex]\large\rm A=11 \: 304 \: in {}^{2}[/tex]

The area of the ceiling is approximately 11, 304 square inches.
