24. An auto traveled at a rate of 13 km/h for 3 min, then at 35 km/h for 4 min, and finally at 18 km/h for 2 min. Find the average speed for the complete trip in meters per second.


[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Step 1: Convert each speed in km/h to m/s and each time in minutes to seconds.

13 km/h = 3.6111 m/s

35 km/h = 9.7222 m/s

18 km/h = 5 m/s

3 min = 180 s

4 min = 240 s

2 min = 120 s

Step 2: Calculate the distances.

d1 = (3.6111 m/s)(180 s) = 650 m

d2 = (9.7222 m/s)(240 s) = 2333.3333 m

d3 = (5 m/s)(120 s) = 600 m

Step 3: Calculate the average speed.

[tex]\text{average speed} = \dfrac{\text{total distance}}{\text{total time}}[/tex]

[tex]\text{average speed} = \dfrac{\text{650 m + 2333.3333 m + 600 m}}{\text{180 s + 240 s + 120 s}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{average speed} = \text{6.64 m/s}}[/tex]
