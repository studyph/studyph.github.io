Answer:

350N-m or 350J

Explanation:

A larger cat of 7kg is lifted 5m into the air. How much GPE does it gain​?

Let's identify the given first,

mass = 7kg

gravity = 10 m/s^2

height = 5m

The formula for PE is mass x gravity x height. Substituting the given values, we get

[tex]PE=mgh=(7kg)(10m/s^2)(5m)\\\\PE=350N-m=350J[/tex]

25. What is the acceleration of 7kg trolling bag pulled by a boy with a force of 3N​


QUESTION

What is the acceleration of 7kg trolling bag pulled by a boy with a force of 3N?

ANSWERThe acceleration of the trolling bag pulled by a boy is equal to 0.43 m/s².

GIVEN

f = 3N

m = 7kg

a = ?

SOLUTION

We know that force exerted to an object is a product of the mass of the body and its acceleration. From this description, we can make a formula to find the acceleration of the given trolling bag pulled by a boy.

[tex]f = m \times a \\ \frac{f}{m} = a \\ \frac{3}{7} = a \\ 0.43 = a[/tex]

Therefore, the acceleration of the trolling bag pulled by a boy is equal to 0.43 m/s².
