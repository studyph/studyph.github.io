21. What is the percent by mass of salt in a 150 ml solution that contains 20 ml salt?


CALCULATING FOR THE PERCENT BY VOLUME

Problem:

What is the percent by volume of salt in a 150 mL solution that contains 20 mL salt?

Answer with Step-by-step explanation:

So to solve for it, we need to identify first the given values:

The total volume of the solution is 150 mL.The total amount of salt in the salt solution is 20 mL.Salt is the solute in the solution.

Since we already list down the values, we can now solve the problem using the formula:

% by volume = [tex]\frac{V_{solute} }{V_{solution}}[/tex] x 100%

where Vsolute refers to the total volume of the solute and Vsolution refers to the total volume of the solution. By direct substitution:

% by volume = 20 mL/150 mL x 100%

% by volume = 13.33%

Therefore, the percent by mass concentration of a 150-mL salt solution that contains 20 mL of salt is 13.33%.
