4. III. Read, analyze, and solve to find the area following the steps in solving wor1. A square carpet, one side of which is 5 m.2. A piece of rectangular land is 110 m long and 73 m wide.3. A classroom 9 m by 9 m.4. A window 4 feet by 2 feet.5. A trapezoid-shaped piece of wood which bases measure 128 m, 92 m and its​


Answer:

25m²8030m²81m²8ft²

Step-by-step explanation:

Problem 1:

A square carpet, one side of which is 5 m.

Asked: Area of the square carpet.

Given: 5 m

Formula: A = S²

Solution:

A = S²

A = 5²

A = 25

Answer: The area of the square carpet is 25m².

Problem 2:

A piece of rectangular land is 110 m long and 73 m wide.

Asked: Area of the rectangular land.

Given: 110 m long and 73 m wide

Formula: A = lw

Solution:

A = lw

A = 110 × 73

A = 8030

Answer: The area of the rectangular land is 8030m².

Problem 3:

A classroom 9 m by 9 m.

Asked: Area of the classroom

Given: 9 m by 9 m

Formula: A = S²

Solution:

A = S²

A = 9²

A = 81

Answer: The area of the classroom is 81m².

Problem 4:

A window 4 feet by 2 feet.

Asked: Area of the window.

Given: 4 feet and 2 feet

Formula: A = lw

Solution:

A = lw

A = 4 × 2

A = 8

Answer: The area of the window is 8ft².
