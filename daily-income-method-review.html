16. a family spends 600 pesos a day for food which is 40% of the daily income if the daily allowance of the children is 225 pesos what percent of the family daily income is for children's allowance.​​


Answer:

1,500.

Step-by-step explanation:

If you take 40 percent of a number and get 600, then what is that number? In other words, you know that 40 percent of a number is 600 and you want to know what that initial number is.

To solve this problem you multiply 600 by 100 and then divide the total by 40 as follows: (600 x 100) / 40

When we put that into our calculator, we get the following answer:

1,500

Therefore, the answer to "600 is 40 percent of what number?" is 1500, and you can also derive that 40 percent of 1500 equals 600.
