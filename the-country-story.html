8. The town mouse and the country mouse beggining story middle story and ending story


Answer:

Beginning story:

Once upon a time, there were two mice - a town mouse and a country mouse. The town mouse lived in a luxurious house in the city, while the country mouse lived in a humble home in the fields. The town mouse enjoyed a life of comfort and abundance, while the country mouse had to struggle to find food and shelter.

Middle story:

One day, the country mouse invited the town mouse to visit him in the fields. The town mouse accepted the invitation and made his way to the countryside. When he arrived, the country mouse offered him some grains and seeds to eat. The town mouse was not impressed and invited the country mouse to come and stay with him in the city, promising him a life of luxury and delicious food.

The country mouse agreed, and the two mice set off for the town. When they arrived, the town mouse showed the country mouse around his luxurious home and treated him to a feast of cakes, cheese, and wine. The country mouse was enjoying the feast when suddenly a loud noise scared them both. It was the sound of the cat, and the two mice had to run for their lives.

The country mouse realized that the town was not as safe as the countryside, and he missed his simple life. He decided to return to his humble home in the fields, and the town mouse understood his friend's decision.

Ending story:

From that day on, the town mouse and the country mouse remained good friends, but they learned to appreciate their different ways of life. The town mouse realized that while he had a life of luxury, he also had to deal with dangers such as the cat, while the country mouse had a simpler life but was safer and had more freedom.

The two mice continued to visit each other from time to time, but they were content with their own lives. They realized that the grass is not always greener on the other side and that it's important to appreciate what you have. And so, the town mouse and the country mouse lived happily ever after, each in their own way.

15. The town mouse and the country mouse beggining story middle story and ending story


Beginning: The story of the town mouse and the country mouse begins with two cousins, one who lives in the city and the other in the countryside.

Middle: One day, the town mouse invites the country mouse to come to visit and see how wonderful life is in the city. The country mouse agrees, and when they get to the city, the town mouse shows his cousin all the amazing sights and sounds of city life.

However, the country mouse quickly realizes that city life is not as great as it seems. There are loud noises, large crowds, and danger lurking around every corner. In contrast, the country mouse's peaceful and quiet life in the countryside seems much more appealing to him.

Ending: In the end, the two cousins go back to their respective homes, with a new appreciation for each other's lifestyles. The town mouse realizes that even though the countryside is quiet and peaceful, it can still be enjoyable. Similarly, the country mouse understands that although the city is fast-paced and exciting, it's not worth risking your safety and comfort. The tale concludes with the moral that there's no place like home.
