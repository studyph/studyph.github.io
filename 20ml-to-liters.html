Answer:

IN 20ML OF METHYL, 25% OF THE SOLUTION IS METHYL ALCOHOL. THIS MEANS THAT 5 ML OF THE CONTENT IS PURE METHYL ALCOHOL. IF WE TAKE 5ML OF THE LIQUID FROM THE SOLUTION, THIS MEANS THAT 6.25 ML IS THE VOLUME.

5% OF THE 20 ML SOLUTION IS 5 ML

(20 ML) (0.25) = 5 ML

25% OF THE 5 ML IS 1.25 ML

(5) (0.25) = 1.25 ML

WE NEED TO ADD BOTH TO GET THE VOLUME

5 ML + 1.25 ML = 6.25 ML
