23. If Ira can walk at an average speed of 25 km/h, how many miles Ira can walk in five hours?


• Problem:

If Ira can walk at an average speed of 25 km/h, how many miles Ira can walk in five hours?

• Solution:

The given are 25 km/h which is the speed and 5 hours as time. You are tasked to find the distance travelled in miles.

[tex]\large \boxed{ \tt Speed=distance÷time}[/tex]

[tex]\large \tt25 \: \frac{km}{h} = \frac{x}{5 \: h} \\ [/tex]

[tex]\large \tt25 \: \frac{km}{h} \times 5 \: h = x\\[/tex]

[tex]\large \tt x = 125 \:km[/tex]

Thus, the distance travelled is 125 kilometers.

Converting kilometers to miles:

One kilometer is equal to 0.621 miles. Thus, 125 kilometers multiplied by 0.621 is 77.625 miles.

• Answer:

Ira can walk 77.63 miles in five hours.
