2. Identify the 4 activities in the digestive System​


Explanation:

where is the picture bro

The four activities in the digestive system are:

1. Ingestion: This is the process of taking food into the mouth.

2. Digestion: This involves the breakdown of food into smaller molecules that can be absorbed by the body. Digestion occurs in the mouth, stomach, and small intestine.

3. Absorption: This is the process by which nutrients and other molecules are absorbed from the small intestine and transported into the bloodstream.

4. Elimination: This is the process of eliminating waste products from the body. It involves the formation and elimination of feces through the rectum and anus.

9. make a reflection of the importance of the digestive system in daily life activities​


Answer:

What is the significance of digestion? Digestion is essential for converting food into nutrients that the body can use for energy, growth, and cell repair. Food and drink must be broken down into smaller nutrient molecules before the blood can absorb and transport them to cells throughout the body.

The digestive system is vital to your overall health because it transports nutrients to your body. Without good digestive health, you will suffer from a variety of unpleasant symptoms such as abdominal pain, bloating, indigestion, and more. Furthermore, the food you eat may deprive you of proper nutrition, which can have a negative impact on your health and well-being. This is why digestive health is critical.

The gastrointestinal tract, liver, pancreas, and gallbladder comprise the digestive system. It is in charge of separating foods and liquids into their basic chemical components such as fats, proteins, and carbohydrates. The body then absorbs them as nutrients, which can then be used as energy to build and repair cells, as well as to help you function.

Otherwise, the digestive system requires adequate hydration to function properly. Make sure to drink enough water for your weight and level of physical activity. You may experience constipation, dehydration, and a variety of other unpleasant symptoms if you do not drink enough water. Drink plenty of water every day to maintain good digestive health.
