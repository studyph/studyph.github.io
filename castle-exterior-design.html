15. Venia and Paulus were making a sand castle at the beach. Venia made a castle that is 50 cm high, and Paulus make a sand castle that is 24 cm high. Find rhe difference of the height of their castles.


To solve this problem, we have to identify first the given numbers which are 50 cm (Venia's castle) and 24 cm (Paulus' castle).

Next, we have to determine which mathematical operation to use. Since the problem asks us to find the difference, we therefore have to subtract.

We now have the mathematical sentence: 50-24 = ?

We take away 24 from 50, we get 26.

Therefore, the difference of the height of the two castles is 26 cm.

20. What is castle clock?


Answer:

The Castle Clock is another example of one of al-Jazari's famous Water Clocks. In this Clock, a ball drops from the falcons' beaks into the vases, small doors open and close, a moon moves across the face of the Clock, and the musicians play their instruments every hour.

Castle Clock

-The Castle Clock is a sophisticated version of the classical water clock or clepsydra where time is measured by the regulated flow of out a vessel where the amount is then measured.
