2. what is the difference between a 2D and 3D​


Answer:

2D or 2 Dimensional (Two Dimensional) has two dimensions. You can move up and down, and left and right. 2 Dimensional is a flat surface, where you can only measure a circle's length and height.

3D or 3 Dimensional (Three Dimensional), however has three dimensions where you can move up and down, left and right, and forward and backwards. In 3D, you can measure the length, height and width of a sphere.

3D has more of a depth than 2D, which is just a flat surface.
