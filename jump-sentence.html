12. In which sentence is the underlined word a participle? The kangaroos were jumping across the plain. The jumping frog startled us as we walked by. Jumping is the preschooler’s favorite activity. He kept jumping my checkers with his king.


In which sentence is the underlined word a participle?

The kangaroos were jumping across the plain.

The jumping frog startled us as we walked by.

Jumping is the preschooler’s favorite activity.

He kept jumping my checkers with his king.

There are 2 varieties of the participle. The past and the present one. However, there could be 3 functions of a participle in a sentence.

The kangaroos were jumping across the plain. (were jumping)

- This is an example of a combination of an auxiliary verb and a participle.

The jumping frog startled us as we walked by. (jumping frog)

- The past and present participle can represent an adjective form in a sentence too.

Jumping is the preschooler’s favorite activity. (Jumping)

- Present participle can also function as a noun and this is known as a gerund
