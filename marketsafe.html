The conceptual framework for the study on the factors affecting customer satisfaction of vegetables and fruits market can be based on several factors, including product quality, pricing, customer service, and convenience.

Product quality is one of the key factors that influence customer satisfaction. The study can examine how customers perceive the quality of vegetables and fruits, including factors such as freshness, taste, and appearance. The study can also explore the impact of product quality on customer loyalty and repeat purchase intentions.

Pricing is another important factor that can affect customer satisfaction. The study can examine how customers perceive the pricing of vegetables and fruits, including factors such as affordability and perceived value for money. The study can also explore the impact of pricing on customer satisfaction and purchase intentions.

Customer service is a critical factor that can influence customer satisfaction. The study can examine how customers perceive the customer service provided by the vegetables and fruits market, including factors such as friendliness, helpfulness, and responsiveness. The study can also explore the impact of customer service on customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Convenience is another important factor that can affect customer satisfaction. The study can examine how customers perceive the convenience of shopping for vegetables and fruits, including factors such as location, opening hours, and payment options. The study can also explore the impact of convenience on customer satisfaction and repeat purchase intentions.

Overall, the conceptual framework for the study on the factors affecting customer satisfaction of vegetables and fruits market can draw from several factors, including product quality, pricing, customer service, and convenience. Linear Equation and It's Application in Real Life

In the given problem, it is an application of linear equation and solving word problem about mathematical operation. It is a combination of general mathematics and algebra.

The given problem is "Mang itik harvested 630 kgs of santol to be delivered to a market. If he puts 35 kg of santol in each kaing, how many kaings will be needed?”

Step by step solution and explanation

To solve the given problem, apply your skills in making mathematical equation in linear form.

Understand what is asked?

How many kaings will be needed?

What are the given?

630 kgs of santol to be delivered to a market Maria can puts 35 kg of santol in each kaing

Plan what operation should be used?

Division

What is the number sentence?

630 ÷ 35 = x, x represents the number of kaing

Show your solution

630 ÷ 35 = x

630 ÷ 35 = 18

Check and look back

18 x 35 = 630
