4. 4. A lawn sprinkler located at the corner of a yard rotates through 120° andsprays water 15ft. What is the area of the sector watered?A. 535.62 ft2ft²B. 435.62 ft²ft²C. 335.62D. 235.62​


Answer:

Therefore, the answer is D) 235.62 ft².

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the area of the sector watered by the sprinkler, we need to use the formula:

Area of sector = (θ/360) x πr²

where θ is the central angle of the sector in degrees, r is the radius of the circle, and π is the mathematical constant pi.

In this problem, the sprinkler rotates through 120°, so θ = 120°. The water sprays 15ft, which is the radius of the circle.

Substituting these values into the formula, we get:

Area of sector = (120/360) x π x 15²

Area of sector = (1/3) x π x 225

Area of sector ≈ 235.62 ft²

Therefore, the answer is D) 235.62 ft².
