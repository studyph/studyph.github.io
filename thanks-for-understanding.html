AC²=AB²+BC²

Next, the AB², from A to B it has number 4 so that AB² is equal to 4².

And then the last one BC², there has no number kaya wala na yan.

Copy those numbers.

5² = 4² + BC²

5²= 5 • 5 = 25

4²= 4 • 4 = 16

Answer:

PYTHAGOREAN THEOREM

In ∆ABC, the following are the given sides and their measurements:

side AC = 5side AB = 4side BC = missing length

Formula:

[tex]\tt{AC² = AB² + BC²}[/tex]

Substitute natin yung mga given length ng mga side sa formula.

[tex]\tt{5² = 4² + BC²}[/tex]

Ang exponent na 2 or squared kung tawagin, ay nangangahulugang ang isang numero ay kailangang ipag-times by itself ng dalawang beses.

[tex]\tt{(5) (5) = (4)(4) + BC²}[/tex]

Simplify.

[tex]\tt{25 = 16 + BC²}[/tex]

Ilipat ang 16 sa left side upang matira ang BC² sa pamamagitan ng Addition Property of Equality. Once na naglipat tayo ng isang number, nagbabago ang sign nito.

[tex]\tt{25 - 16 = 16 - 16 + BC²}[/tex]

[tex]\tt{25 - 16 = \cancel{16 - 16} \: BC²}[/tex]

[tex]\tt{9 = BC²}[/tex]

Upang maalis ang squared exponent sa side BC, kailangan nating gamitan ng tinatawag na Extracting the Square Root.

[tex]\tt{\sqrt{9} = \sqrt{BC}²}[/tex]

9 is a perfect square. Then, its square root is 3.

[tex]\boxed{\tt{3 = BC}}[/tex]

Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

⅞ - ¼ - ½

Find the LCM of Denominators

Multiples of 8: ( 8 ), 16, 24, 32

Multiples of 4: 4,( 8 ), 12, 16

Multiples of 2: 2, 4, 6, ( 8 )

The LCM of 8, 4 and 2 is 8,

Therefore, we use 8 as the Denominator

Use this formula:

LCM ÷ Denominator × Numerator = Numerator

8 ÷ 8 × 7 = 7

8 ÷ 4 × 1 = 2

8 ÷ 2 × 1 = 4

⅞ - 2/8 - 4/8 = ⅛

BIAS, SYMBOLISM, AND PROPAGANDA

Media (newspapers, magazine articles, videos) often has a bias or agenda behind it’s message. Being able to analyze content to determine its angle is key to staying informed. Some material is designed to inform and will try to represent all sides of an issue accurately and fairly, but other types, like propaganda, are deliberately biased or misleading and created to promote a particular point of view.
