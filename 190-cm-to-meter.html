15. The perimeter of a rectangular field is 58 meters. If the difference between the length and width is 9 meters, then the area of ​​the field is ... m^2. A. 95 C. 261 B. 190 D. 380


Answer:

[tex]{\red{B.\: 190.}}[/tex]

=> If the perimeter of a rectangle is 58 meters, it means

[tex]2(p + l) = 58 \Leftrightarrow p + l = 29.[/tex]

It is also known that the difference between the length and the width is 9 meters, meaning that it is written

[tex]p -l = 9.[/tex]

Thus, we get SPLDV

[tex]\begin{cases} p + l &= 29 && (\cdots 1) \\ p -l & = 9 && (\cdots 2) \end{cases}[/tex]

Eliminate [tex]l[/tex] from equations (1) and (2).

[tex]\begin{aligned} \! \begin{aligned} p + l & = 29 \\ p -l& = 9 \end{aligned} \\ \rule{2.3 cm}{0.6pt} + \\ \! \begin{aligned} 2p & = 38 \\ p & = 19 \end{aligned} \end{aligned}[/tex]

For [tex]p=19[/tex], we get [tex]19 - l = 9[/tex], which means [tex]l=10.[/tex]

So, the area is

[tex]{\boxed{L = pl = 19(10) = {\blue{190~\text{m}^2}}}}[/tex]

Explanation:

– Two Variable Linear Equation System (SPLDV) –

The Two Variable Linear Equation System or SPLDV is that there is more than one linear equation with two variables that have only one solution. To solve the SPLDV form, there are two ways that must be considered, which are based on the form of the equation.

A variable is a value that can change.The coefficient is the number in front of the variable.A constant is a number that is not followed by a variable.

The general form of SPLDV is as follows:

ax + by = p

cx + dy = q

While the solution of the general form above is called (xo, yo) called the solution set. Examples of SPLDV are as follows:

3x + 2y = 10

9x – 7y = 43

And the solution set is {(x,y) (4,-1)}.

One of the Solutions of the SPLDV Method is Graphics Method

The graphical method is to determine the point of intersection between two line equations so that the solution set is obtained from the linear equations of the two variables. If the equations of the two lines are parallel to each other, then the solution set is the empty set. Meanwhile, if the lines coincide, then the number of solution sets is infinite.

Problems example :

Determine the solution set below using the graphical method.

2x – y = 2

x + y = 4

Discussion:

The point of intersection of the two lines obtained is (2,2). So the solution set of the system of equations is (2,2).
