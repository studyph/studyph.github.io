[tex]2(3.14)(66)(25)+2(3.14)( 66^{2} )=10,362+27,355.68=37,717.68[/tex]

So the surface area is [tex]37,717.68[/tex] [tex]cm^{2} [/tex]

The volume of a cylinder is [tex] \pi r^{2} h[/tex]

[tex](3.14)( 66^{2} )(25)=341,946[/tex]

5. A rectangle is 5 cm longer than its width. If the perimeter of the rectangle is 66 cm, find the dimension of the rectangleSolution:​


Answer:

The dimensions of the rectangle are w(width) = 14cm and l(length) = 19cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let:

w = width

l (length) = w+5

p (perimeter) = 66

Find first the value of w.

[tex]p = 2l + 2w \\ 66 = 2(w + 5) + 2w \\ 66 = 2w + 10 + 2w \\ 66 - 10 = 2w + 2w \\ 56 = 4w \\ \frac{56}{4} = \frac{4w}{4} \\ 14 = w[/tex]

Then, substitute the value of w to get the value of l.

[tex]w = 14 \\ l = w + 5 \\ l = 14 + 5 \\ l = 19[/tex]

Therefore, the dimensions of the rectangle are w=14 and l=19.

CHECK:

Substitute the values of w and l to the equation p = 2l + 2w.

[tex]p = 2l + 2w \\ 66 = 2(19) + 2(14) \\ 66 = 38 + 28 \\ 66 = 66[/tex]

The answer is equal. Therefore, the values of w and l are correct.

Answer:

H = 32 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

L = 71 cm = 0.71 m

W = 66 cm = 0.66 m

V_ink = 40%V_container

V_container = 0.06/0.4 = 0.15 m^3
