21. 5. How is a 3/4 time signature defined?​


:: The time signature 3/4 means there should be three crotchet beats in a bar. Again, the top number tells us how many beats per bar (three in this case) and the bottom number tells us what kind of beat (crotchet beats as it’s a number four). The time signature 4/4 means there are four crotchet beats in a bar.

Question:

How is a 3/4 time signature defined?

Answer:

Every measure in the 3/4 time signature has three quarter notes (or any combination of notes that equals three quarter notes). Because there is a 4 on the bottom, the quarter note gets the beat, as we learned in the last lesson (or pulse).

Explanation:

In compound time signatures, the beat is broken down into three-part rhythms. The top number is evenly divisible by 3, with the exception of time signatures where the top number is 3. Also, each beat is divided into three components, creating a one-two-three pulse.
