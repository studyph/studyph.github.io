Nothing has changed since then: raccoon coats are no longer fashionable, but trends and fads continue to dominate the hearts and minds of people, giving them a false sense of belonging to a privileged class.

In Shulman’s story, as well as in the real world, a raccoon coat used to be a symbol of style and privilege among male college students. For Petey Bellows, the author’s roommate, a raccoon coat is a matter of life vs. death: he wants a raccoon coat above anything in the world. He knows that to have a raccoon coat means to “be in the swim” (Shulman).

Petey believes that there is no way for him to outperform his peers other than to own a piece of fashionable clothes. He does not realize that being “in the swim” is the same as being lost in a gray crowd of people. That raccoon coats are unsanitary and weight too much means nothing to Petey (Shulman). He treats clothes as the sign of his privileged position and personal well being.

In the 1920s, the revival of raccoon coats was accompanied by the returning popularity of Charleston, which rapidly grew into a social mania. Charleston was inseparable from fashion. Those who did not follow the trend would doom themselves to social oblivion, isolation, and even rejection. At that time, a young student wearing a raccoon coat and dancing Charleston would be a secret dream for dozens of girls. Undoubtedly, a fashionable car added to the picture of personal prosperity.

He tries to show that clothes, cars, and dancing skills have nothing to with personal uniqueness. Raccoon coats are no longer fashionable, but trends and fads continue to dominate people’s hearts and minds. Answer:

The Emergence of Trends and Patterns: An Essay

Trends and patterns are an intrinsic part of human culture and society. They emerge and evolve over time, shaping the way we think, behave, and interact with one another. From fashion trends to economic patterns, from societal shifts to technological advancements, trends and patterns play a significant role in various aspects of our lives. In this essay, we will explore the emergence of trends and patterns, understanding their causes, effects, and implications.

Trends and patterns are often the result of complex interactions between multiple factors. These factors can include human behavior, societal norms, technological advancements, economic forces, cultural influences, and more. The emergence of trends and patterns can be spontaneous, organic, or intentional. They can arise from individual actions or collective behaviors, and they can be driven by a variety of motivations, such as personal preferences, social pressures, economic incentives, or technological advancements.

One of the primary drivers of trends and patterns is human behavior. Human beings are social creatures who are constantly influenced by the people around them. As individuals interact and communicate with one another, they exchange ideas, opinions, and information, which can shape their perspectives and behaviors. For example, fashion trends often emerge from people's desire to conform to societal standards or express their identity. Social media platforms also play a significant role in the emergence of trends, as they facilitate the rapid spread of ideas and behaviors among a wide audience.

Societal norms and cultural influences also contribute to the emergence of trends and patterns. Society sets certain standards and expectations for its members, which influence their behaviors and choices. For instance, societal norms around beauty, gender roles, or professional success can drive trends in beauty standards, fashion choices, and career paths. Cultural influences, such as traditions, customs, and values, also shape trends and patterns in various domains, including art, music, food, and lifestyle.

Technological advancements are another significant driver of trends and patterns. As technology evolves, it can create new opportunities, disrupt existing industries, and change the way we live and work. Technological innovations can shape trends in communication, entertainment, transportation, and more. For example, the rise of social media platforms, smartphones, and streaming services has revolutionized how people interact, consume content, and share information, leading to the emergence of trends such as viral challenges, influencer culture, and online shopping.

Economic forces also play a crucial role in the emergence of trends and patterns. Economic factors, such as supply and demand, consumer preferences, and market conditions, can influence the choices people make and shape trends in industries such as fashion, entertainment, and technology. For example, changes in consumer preferences for sustainable products have led to trends in eco-friendly fashion, plant-based diets, and renewable energy.

The emergence of trends and patterns has various effects and implications on individuals, societies, and industries. Trends can impact consumer behavior, purchasing decisions, and brand preferences. They can also influence cultural norms, societal attitudes, and public policies. For instance, the #MeToo movement, which emerged as a trend on social media, has had a significant impact on public discourse, workplace policies, and social attitudes towards sexual harassment and assault.

In conclusion, the emergence of trends and patterns is a complex phenomenon driven by multiple factors, including human behavior, societal norms, technological advancements, economic forces, and cultural influences. Trends can shape the way we think, behave, and interact with one another, and they have various effects and implications on individuals and societies. Understanding the causes and dynamics of trends and patterns is essential for businesses, policymakers, and individuals alike, as it allows them to adapt to changing trends and leverage them for their benefit
