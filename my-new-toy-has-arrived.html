4. Four children have small toys. Sana has 1/10 of the toys. Mina has 12 more toys than Sana, Jihyo has one more toy of what Sana has and Tzuyu has double than Jihyo. How many toys are there ?​


Answer:

30

Step-by-step explanation:

Sana- 1/10

mina- all of Sana's toy + 12 (1/10 + 12)

Jihyo- all of Sana's toy + 1 (1/10 + 1)

Tzuyu- all of Jihyo's toy x 2 (1/10 + 1) (2) = (2/10 + 2)

There's limited information here.

1/10(Sana's toy) + 1/10(Mina's toy excluding the +12) + 1/10(Jihyo's excluding the +1) + 2/10(Tzuyu's toy excluding the +2)

so, 1/10 + 1/10 + 1/10 + 2/10

=5/10 or 1/2

It means the +12 toys of mina, +1 toy of Jihyo, and +2 toy of Tzuyu is the other 5/10 which is equal to 15

15+15=30

12. A toy blimp has a volume of 3.4 L. The pressure is 3.2 atm at a temperature of 25 oC. The toy blimp flies to a new location where the pressure is 3.5 atm and the temperature is 44 oC. What will be the volume of the toy blimp at the new location?


Answer:

Use Combined Gas Law Formula

Explanation:

Step 1 : Arrange formula

From : P1V1/T1 = P2V2/T2

To: V2 = P1V1T2/P2T1

Step 2 : Given

V1 = 3.4 L

P1 = 3.2 ATM

T1 = 25 °C + 275 (convert to kelvin +275)

P2= 3.5 ATM

T2 = 44 °C + 275 (convert to kelvin +275)

V2 = ?

Step 3 : substitute given to formula

(3.2atm)(3.4L)(309k)

-----------------------------

(3.5atm) (300k)

Step 4: cancel like terms such as "atm" and "k"

(3.2)(3.4L)(309)

-----------------------------

(3.5) (300)

Step 5: Solve

3,361.92 ÷ 1,050

= 3.20182857 L

Answer: 3.2 L
