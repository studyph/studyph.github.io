If Faizah gives Noah 40 stickers

Faizah : Noah

Faizah : Noah

13. it wasn't raining when noah built an ark make an reflection​


Answer:

Noah was building an ark not because it was raining, but because he knew that a flood was coming. He was preparing himself and his family for a disaster that hadn't yet happened. Similarly, in our own lives, we need to be proactive and take steps to prepare for the future, even if things seem to be going well at the moment.

The story of Noah's ark is a reminder that we cannot predict the future, but we can take steps to be prepared for it. We don't know when a flood will come, but we can build our own "arks" to protect ourselves from the storms of life. This could mean saving money for a rainy day, investing in our education and skills, or building strong relationships with friends and family.

It's also important to remember that just like Noah, we cannot do everything alone. Noah had the help of his family and animals to build the ark. In the same way, we need to surround ourselves with people who can support us in our preparation for the future.

In conclusion, the statement "It wasn't raining when Noah built an ark" teaches us the importance of being prepared for the future, even when everything seems to be going well at the moment. We should take proactive steps to build our own "arks" and surround ourselves with people who can support us in our preparation.
