For example, the spread of Buddhism from its traditional base in Asia to other parts of the world is a clear indication of globalization's impact on religious practices.

The words one, holy, catholic and apostolic are often called the four marks of the Church.

3. Three marks are usually enumerated: the preaching of the Word, the administration of the sacraments, and church discipline.

4. Churches Militant, Penitent, and Triumphant.
