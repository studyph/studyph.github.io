PROBLEM

The relationship between temperatures in degree Celsius (°C) and in degree Fahrenheit (°F) is given by °C 5/9 (°F – 32). What is the corresponding value in degree Fahrenheit of 37.78°C?

ANSWERThe answer is 100°F

SOLUTION

°C = 5/9 (°F -32)

9/5 °C = °F -32

9/5 °C + 42 = °F

°F = [9(37.78)] / 5+ 32

°F = 68.004 + 32

°F = 100.004
