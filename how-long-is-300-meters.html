A rice field in the shape of a parallelogram is 300 meters long. the perpendicular distance between the base and its opposite side is 120 meters. what is its area?

What is asked for the problem?

What is its area?

What facts are given?

base - 300 meters long

height - 120 meters long

What is the formula to solve the problem?

Area = bh

How is the solution done ?

Area = base × height

= 300 m × 120 m

= 36000 m²

What is the complete answer ?

The area of the rice field is 36,000 m².

Explanation:

Finding the Area of Parallelogram

Parallelogram is formed by two pairs of parallel lines. Remember that opposite sides are equal in length and opposite angles are equal in measure.

To find the area of a parallelogram, we have the formula Area = bh where b is the base and h is the height.

17. 1. A ball moved 10 meters in 3 seconds. What is the ball's speed? 2. Adrian travelled a total distance 800 meters. If he is moving It a speed OF of 4m/s How long For him to reach the final position 3. Kyla travels at a speed of 15m/s TE' she travelled for 300 seconds how Far have she travelled?​


Answer:

To calculate the ball's speed, we can use the formula:

speed = distance / time

In this case, the distance is 10 meters and the time is 3 seconds. Substituting these values into the formula, we get:

speed = 10 meters / 3 seconds

Simplifying, we get:

speed = 3.33 meters per second (rounded to two decimal places)

Therefore, the ball's speed is 3.33 meters per second.

To find the time taken by Adrian to travel a total distance of 800 meters at a speed of 4m/s, we can use the formula:

time = distance / speed

Substituting the values we know, we get:

time = 800 meters / 4 m/s

Simplifying, we get:

time = 200 seconds

Therefore, it would take Adrian 200 seconds to reach the final position.

To find how far Kyla has travelled if she travels at a speed of 15m/s for 300 seconds, we can use the formula:

distance = speed * time

Substituting the known values, we get:

distance = 15 m/s * 300 seconds

Simplifying, we get:

distance = 4500 meters

Therefore, Kyla has travelled 4500 meters.
