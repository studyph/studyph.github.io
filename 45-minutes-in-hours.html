11. what is ratio of 45 minutes to two hours


Answer:

3 : 8

Step-by-step explanation:

Ratio is a comparison of two or more values are in the same unit.

Ratio is written in its simplest form or lowest term. It is simplified by dividing the values by their greatest common factor (GCF).

Given:

45 minutes to two hours

The values must be in similar units.  Convert hours to minutes:

1 hour = 60 minutes2 hours = 2 hours × 60 minutes/hour = 120 minutes

Re-write the ratio in minutes unit:

45 minutes  to 120 minutes  ⇒  45 : 120

To simplify, divide both values by their GCF 15:

(45/15) : (120/15) = 3 : 8

Hence:

45 : 120  =  3 : 8
