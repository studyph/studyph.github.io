Find the perimeter of each of the following:

1.) A garden 15 meters long and 12 meters wide.

2.) A lot 10 meters long and 7 meters wide.

3.) A swimming pool 20 meters long and 12 meters wide.

4.) The town park 45 meters long and 32 meters wide.

5.) 15m long and 12 meter wide.

SOLUTIONS AND ANSWERS:

[tex]1.)[/tex] A garden 15 meters long and 12 meters wide.

[tex]P = 2L + 2W[/tex]

[tex]P = 30 meters + 24 meters[/tex]

P = 54 meters

P = 34 meters

10. what is the area a garden is 3 meters Long and 2 meters wide.with sollutions​


Solution:

To find the area of a rectangular garden, we must use the formula

A = lw

where:

l = length

w = width

Listing the given values

l = 3 m

w = 2 m

Solving for the area

A = lw

A = (3 m)(2 m)

A = 6 m²

Therefore, the area of the garden is 6 m².

QUESTION

What is the volume of rectangular solid 4 meters long, 3 meters wide, and 6 meters high?

Para ma-compute natin ang volume ng isang Rectangular Prism, tayo ay gumagamit ng formula na:

[tex]\purple{\boxed{V = l \times w \times h}} [/tex]

Where:

L is the length

W is the width

H is the height.

GIVEN

4 meters long - l

3 meters wide - w

6 meters high - h

SOLUTION[tex]\purple{\boxed{V = l \times w \times h}} [/tex][tex]\purple{\boxed{V = 4 \times 3 \times 6}} [/tex][tex]\purple{\boxed{V = 12 \times 6}} [/tex][tex]\purple{\boxed{V = 72m³}} [/tex]
