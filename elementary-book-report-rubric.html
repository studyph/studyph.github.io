Which of the two editions of the book contains more accurate information? Why?Answer correctly pleaseWrong❌ reportcorrect ✅brainlest​


Answer:

It is likely that the newer edition of the Science textbook, the one printed in 2018, contains more accurate information than the one printed in 2000. This is because new discoveries, research and findings have happened from the year 2000 to 2018, so the 2018 edition would have likely incorporated those advances and up-to-date information on the particular subjects.

Also, scientific understanding and knowledge change over time and textbooks are regularly updated to reflect new scientific findings and theories, so a newer edition is more likely to be more accurate.

It is important to note that to be completely certain, a comparison between the two editions should be done and even consulting with subject matter experts would provide more accurate answer.
