Title: "Ang Tunay na Kaibigan"

Characters:

Jessa, a shy and introverted girl who struggles to make friends

Mark, a outgoing and friendly boy who becomes Jessa's best friend

Act 1:

The story opens with Jessa sitting alone at lunch, struggling to make friends at her new school. She watches as the other students laugh and talk with their friends, feeling left out and isolated.

As she's about to leave the lunchroom, Mark approaches her and strikes up a conversation. Despite her initial hesitation, Jessa finds herself opening up to him and they quickly become friends.

Act 2:

Jessa and Mark spend the rest of the school year bonding over their shared interests and helping each other navigate the challenges of high school.

Jessa becomes more confident and outgoing, thanks to Mark's unwavering support and encouragement. Mark, in turn, learns the value of true friendship and loyalty.

Act 3:

The school year ends and Jessa and Mark go their separate ways, as they are heading to different colleges. They keep in touch through text and calls.

Years later, Jessa and Mark reconnect and realize that their friendship is still as strong as ever. They reflect on the impact that their friendship has had on their lives and how it has helped them to become the people they are today.

The story ends with Jessa and Mark realizing that true friends are always there for each other, no matter how much time has passed
