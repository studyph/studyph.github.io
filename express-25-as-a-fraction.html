14. Express each fraction in the lowest form. Tick the letter of the correct answer. 46/50 A. 25/25 B. 25/23 C. 23/25 D. 20/23


Answer:

C. 23/25

Solution:

Find the Greatest Common Factor (GCF) of 46 and 50, if it exists, and reduce our fraction by dividing both numerator and denominator by it. GCF = 2, and getting our simplified answer:

= 46÷2= 23

= 50÷2 = 25

= 23/25

Steps to simplifying fractions

Find the GCD (or HCF) of numerator and denominator

15 ÷ 5

25 ÷ 5

21. The probability of an event is 68%. What is this probability expressed as a fraction?a. 8/25b. 17/25c. 3/4d. 68/1​


PROBLEM:

The probability of an event is 68%. What is this probability expressed as a fraction?

ANSWER:

The problem simply tells us to convert percent into fraction. To convert percent into fraction, just put the percentage over 100 as you remove the percent sign (%) and simplify the fraction if necessary.

[tex] \sf 68 \: \%=\frac{68}{100} \div \frac{4}{4} = \frac{17}{25} \\ [/tex]

Therefore, the answer is Letter B.
