2. what are the parts of subtraction sentence give and example and label​


The parts of a subtraction sentence are:

1. Minuend: The number from which another number is subtracted. It is the first number in the subtraction sentence.

2. Subtrahend: The number being subtracted from the minuend. It is the second number in the subtraction sentence.

3. Difference: The result of subtracting the subtrahend from the minuend. It is the answer to the subtraction sentence.

Example: 7 - 3 = 4:

Minuend: 7

Subtrahend: 3

Difference: 4
