A boy moves from point P to S then went back to point R as shown in the illustration. Find the distance and displacement traveled by the boy.

Note: I can't see an illustration but is is still solvable. Distance of the points will be unknown values.

Distance = (distance from P to S) + (distance from S to R)

Displacement = distance from R to P

Explanation

The distance is the total value covered of an object from the starting point so no matter the object go, then distance keeps increasing.

The displacement however, is the distance from the starting point. So in this problem, point P was the starting point and the object stopped at point R, so the distance of points R and P is the displacement.

Answer:

In this scenario, the boy ran 50 meters to the east and then 50 meters to the west.

To determine the displacement and distance traveled by the boy, we need to first understand the difference between these two concepts.

Displacement: This is the shortest distance between the starting point and the ending point of an object. It takes into account the direction of the movement.

Distance: This is the total length of the path taken by an object, regardless of the direction of movement.

Now, let's calculate the displacement and distance traveled by the boy.

Solution:

Displacement: The boy ended up where he started, so his displacement is zero. This is because the starting point and the ending point are the same, even though he moved 50 meters to the east and then 50 meters to the west.

Displacement = 0 meters

Distance: The boy ran a total distance of 100 meters. This is because he traveled 50 meters to the east and then 50 meters to the west, covering a total distance of 100 meters.

Distance = 100 meters

Illustration:

In the illustration above, the starting point is represented by "Start." The boy ran 50 meters to the east, as indicated by the arrow pointing to the left. Then, he turned around and ran 50 meters to the west, as indicated by the arrow pointing to the right. Since he ended up where he started, his displacement is zero. However, he covered a total distance of 100 meters.
