27. An ____________ pronoun emphasizes a preceding nounA. Possessive B. ReflexiveC. Intensive D. Personal ​


Answer:

C. Intensive

Explanation:

An intensive pronoun emphasizes a preceding noun, which is often (but not always) the noun immediately before the pronoun. Intensive pronouns look exactly the same as reflexive pronouns, but they are only used for emphasis.
