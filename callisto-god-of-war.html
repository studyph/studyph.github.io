With a radius of 1,080 miles (1,738 kilometers), the Moon is the fifth largest moon in our solar system (after Ganymede, Titan, Callisto and lo). The Moon is farther away from Earth than most people realize. The Moon is an average of 238,855 miles (384,400 kilometers) away.
