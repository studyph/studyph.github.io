13. a sibling who spend most of his/her time playing online games physically,emotionally,socially,spiritually?​


Answer:

Showing Support for Others

Situation: A sibling who spends most of his/her time playing online games.

Support physically: Persuade him/her to reduce his online games screening time because it can have an impact on his/her physical body.

Support emotionally: Support him/her in his/her games, but with limitations.

Support socially: Influence him/her to focus more on himself/herself.

Support spiritually: Invite him to take some time to meditate and rest.

Helping others is not only beneficial to them and a good thing to do; it also makes us healthier and happier. Giving also attaches us to others, building stronger neighbourhoods and contributing to the creation of a happier society for all.
