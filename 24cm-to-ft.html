Area of Plane Figure and Volume of Prism

The given problem is an application of solving area of the plane figure in real life situation. You will apply your basic knowledge in mathematics and geometry especially in solving area of plane figure and how many items can be occupied in the space provided presented in algebraic expression. The given problem is "An open topped box can be created by cutting squares from the four corners of a 20cm x 30cm piece of cardboard. What dimensions will create a box with a volume of 1008 cm3?”

Step by step solution Step 1: Identify the given of the problem

four corners of a 20cm x 30cm piece of cardboard

box with a volume of 1008 cm3

Step 2: Identify what is being asked

What dimensions will create a box?

Step 3: Create mathematical equation

The volume of the rectangular prism is V = lwh.

Area of the box is A = lw

The side of the square represent the height of the box, and let x is the side of the square.

Length of the box, l = 30 – 2x

Width of the box, w = 20 – 2x

V = lwh

1008 = (30-2x)(20-2x)(x)

Step 4: Solve for x

1008 = (30-2x)(20-2x)(x)

Expand the equation, 1008 = (30-2x)(20-2x)(x)

4x3 – 100x2 + 600x = 1008

4x3 – 100x2 + 600x – 1008 = 0

Solve for x using synthetic division.

4x3 – 100x2 + 600x – 1008 = 0, it was divisible by 4. The new equation will be

x3 – 25x2 + 150x – 252 = 0

Let x is 3  

X = 3 I 1 – 25 + 150 – 252

            1 I -22 I  84 I 0

Step 5: Solve for length and width of the box

Use x = 3, it was the height of the box.

l = 30 – 2x, l = 24

w = 20 – 2x, w = 14

Final Answer

15. Find the area of 24cm 30m 5m 10m ​


Answer:

To find the area of the given figure, we need to know its shape. Assuming that the figure is a rectangle, we can use the formula for the area of a rectangle, which is:

Area = length x width

We need to convert all the measurements to the same unit before using this formula. Let's convert everything to meters:

- 24 cm = 0.24 m (divided by 100)

- 30 m = 30 m

- 5 m = 5 m

- 10 m = 10 m

Now we can use the formula:

Area = length x width = (30 m) x (5 m + 0.24 m) = 150.24 square meters

Therefore, the area of the figure is 150.24 square meters.
