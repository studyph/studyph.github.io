Answer:

A Case Study on the Single-Parenting Status on Child Development of Single-Parent Students at Jose Panganiban National High School

Introduction

The number of single-parent families in the Philippines has been increasing in recent years. This case study aims to explore the single-parenting status on the child development of single-parent students at Jose Panganiban National High School.

Research Question

How does the single-parenting status affect the child development of single-parent students at Jose Panganiban National High School?

Hypothesis

The single-parenting status will have a negative effect on the child development of single-parent students at Jose Panganiban National High School.

Methodology

The research will be conducted through a survey questionnaire that will be administered to 100 randomly selected single-parent students at Jose Panganiban National High School. The survey questionnaire will collect data on the students’ age, gender, educational attainment of the parent, monthly household income, and child development indicators such as academic achievement, emotional well-being, social relationships, and physical health. The data will be analyzed using SPSS.

Results

The results of the study will help to understand the impact of the single-parenting status on the child development of single-parent students at Jose Panganiban National High School. The findings of the study can be used to develop interventions and programs that can help improve the child development of single-parent students.
