22. Give five examples of implicit sentences and five examples of explicit sentences. ​


Examples of Implicit sentences.

It's so cold in here.

The meaning expressed by the speaker in this sentence is expressed indirectly because the speaker wants the listener to close the window of the house, or wants to borrow warm clothes.

Even though there are many people, dirty dishes are still piled up on the table.

The example sentence above is a sentence that orders someone to wash the dirty dishes that are on the table. However, the order is not explicitly spelled out because it does not directly command to wash the dishes.

They are like rocks, only silent and silent.When people are dating, they make it clear that they will not meet with others implicitly. Meeting with others is frowned upon. Examples of Explicit sentences.

Please close the window!

The words spoken by the speaker are very clear. The speaker wants or tells the listener to close the window.

Implicit and explicit are two words that have opposite meanings. If implicit is something that is implied, then explicit is something that is explicit or straightforward and clearly stated directly, these two words are clearly distinguished from their respective meanings.
