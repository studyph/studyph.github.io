12. 6. What is the mole fraction of solute in a solution that has 3.50 g sucrose (MM = 342) and 100.0 g water (MM = 18)?With solution please grade 12​


Given:

mass of solute = 3.50 g

mass of solvent = 100.0 g

molar mass of solute = 342 g/mol

molar mass of solvent = 18 g/mol

Required:

mole fraction

Solution:

Step 1: Calculate the number of moles of each component.

For solute

[tex]\text{moles of solute} = \frac{\text{mass of solute}}{\text{molar mass of solute}}[/tex]

[tex]\text{moles of solute} = \frac{\text{3.50 g}}{\text{342 g/mol}}[/tex]

moles of solute = 0.0102339 mol

For solvent

[tex]\text{moles of solvent} = \frac{\text{mass of solvent}}{\text{molar mass of solvent}}[/tex]

[tex]\text{moles of solvent} = \frac{\text{100.0 g}}{\text{18 g/mol}}[/tex]

moles of solvent = 5.5555556 mol

Step 2: Calculate the mole fraction of solute.

[tex]\text{mole fraction} = \frac{\text{moles of solute}}{\text{moles of solute + moles of solvent}}[/tex]

[tex]\text{mole fraction} = \frac{\text{0.0102339 mol}}{\text{0.0102339 mol + 5.5555556 mol}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{mole fraction} = 0.00184}[/tex]

29. What is the mole fraction of solute in a solution that has 3.50 g sucrose (MM = 342) and 100.0 g water (MM = 18)? Need procedures, ASAP


Answer:

To find the mole fraction of the solute in a solution, we first need to calculate the moles of the solute and solvent.

The moles of sucrose can be calculated as:

moles of sucrose = mass of sucrose / molar mass of sucrose

moles of sucrose = 3.50 g / 342 g/mol

moles of sucrose = 0.0102 mol

The moles of water can be calculated as:

moles of water = mass of water / molar mass of water

moles of water = 100.0 g / 18.0 g/mol

moles of water = 5.56 mol

The mole fraction of the solute (sucrose) can be calculated as:

mole fraction of sucrose = moles of sucrose / (moles of sucrose + moles of water)

mole fraction of sucrose = 0.0102 mol / (0.0102 mol + 5.56 mol)

mole fraction of sucrose = 0.00183

Therefore, the mole fraction of the solute (sucrose) in the solution is 0.00183
