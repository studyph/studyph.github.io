physical property: Any characteristic that can be determined without changing the substance’s chemical identity.

chemical property: Any characteristic that can be determined only by changing a substance’s molecular structure.

What is the difference between a chemical change and a chemical property?

A chemical property describes the ability of a substance to undergo a specific chemical change. To identify a chemical property, we look for a chemical change. A chemical change always produces one or more types of matter that differ from the matter present before the change.
