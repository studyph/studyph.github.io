7. The sum of the ages of Hero and Scarlet is 56. Herois 6 years older than Scarlet. Find the age of Hero.*​


Answer:

Hero is 31 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

H + S = 56 , 1st equation

H = S + 6 , 2nd equation

Used substitution technique, 2nd Eq to 1st Eq

S + 6 + S = 56

2S + 6 = 56

2S = 56 - 6

2S = 50

S = 25 and

H = 25 + 6 = 31
