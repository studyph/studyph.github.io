30. 1. Which is an application of the functionalist theory? A. Schools must do their part to socialize children or else children will turn out to be delinquent citizens. B. Schools must teach students to accept their status in life for there is nothing that they can do about it. C. Schools must make learners understand that in life there will always be the powerful and powerless. D. Schools must teach students to speak in clear symbols.


School's role in socializing children is one example of applying functionalist theory

Functionalist theory is a kind of theory in the subject of theoretical perspective, which defines as the relationship between institutions and society. It concerns with how each part or element in society contributes to the society's stability. In addition, in social science, functionalist theory contends that each element or institution has its own role.

As the question asks about an example of an application of functionalist theory, the correct answer would be (A), that schools have an obligation to educate children, so that they will not turn into delinquent citizens. If schools do not do their part, then there will surely be a consequence when the educated (in this case, children) will turn into parties that could disrupt the order in society.

Meanwhile, answer (B) that says that students should accept their status in life as it is portrays a very pessimistic perspective. This does not align with the mission and vision that school as an institution has: to help students to achieve a better status or future.

Answer (C) also deviates from what the role a school has in society at large. This answer emphasizes the hierarchy that exists in society, and this will make the learners lose their hope, as they will be bound by the status of the "powerful" and "powerless".

While the other two choices in this question clearly seemed incorrect, the answer (D) says otherwise. It might be true that schools should teach its students to speak in clear symbols, however, this has nothing to do with the application of functionalist theory.

All in all, functionalist theory's application is present in how the societal institution functions to help in achieving the stability desired by people or society at large.
