12. A radio wave has a frequency. Of 32 Hz. Find its wavelength.​


The formula for finding the wavelength λ is:

λ = c/f

where c is the speed of light and f is the frequency, in this case 32 Hz.

Nakadepende nalang ito sa value ng c. If simplified to 300,000 km/s ang c sa inyo, then:

300,000 km/32 Hz = 9,375 km.

However if you are precise and you define c to be exact at 299,792,458 m/s (the 1970 definition by the International System of Units), then it would be:

299,792,458 m/32 Hz = 9,374,015 meters.
