13. Convert the following to the nearest degree 1. 15°C to°F 2.40°C to °F 3. 68°F to °C 4.35°F to °C


Answer:

1.) 15°C to °F:

Using the formula °F = (°C x 1.8) + 32, we get:

°F = (15 x 1.8) + 32

°F = 59

Therefore, 15°C is approximately equal to 59°F.

2.) 40°C to °F:

°F = (40 x 1.8) + 32

°F = 104 + 32

°F = 136

Therefore, 40°C is approximately equal to 104°F.

3.) 68°F to °C:

Using the formula °C = (°F - 32) ÷ 1.8, we get:

°C = (68 - 32) ÷ 1.8

°C = 36 ÷ 1.8

°C = 20

Therefore, 68°F is approximately equal to 20°C.

4.) 35°F to °C:

°C = (35 - 32) ÷ 1.8

°C = 3 ÷ 1.8

°C = 1.67

Therefore, 35°F is approximately equal to 1.67°C.
