Introduce yourself and explain how -- if elected – you'll work with the class president to represent the interests of your class. Engage your audience with an inspirational quote about leadership, or share a short personal story about what motivated you to run for class vice president.

Part 1 of 3:

Writing the introduction

1.)Find an attention-grabbing opening statement.

2.)State the basics.

3.)List your qualifications.

Part 2 of 3

Writing the Body of the speech

1.)State your main odeas on how to improve the school.

2.)Find support for those ideas.

3.)Keep your ideas short but very strongly worded.

Part 3 of 3:

Ending with a Strong Conclusion

1.)Reiterate your main points briefly

2.)Emphasize your benefits to the audience

3.)Ask the audience for their vote

4.)Have someone look over your speech.
