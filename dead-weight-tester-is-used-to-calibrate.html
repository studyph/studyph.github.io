22. it is the type of water use in calibrating the refractometer​


Answer:

distilled water

Explanation:

Calibrate the refractometer by using distilled water. Hold the refractometer in a horizontal position. Open the refractometer's daylight plate and place two drops of distilled water on the prism. Close and press the daylight plate to spread the water evenly across the prism.
