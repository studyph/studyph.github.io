21. A box of 2kg is 0.4m.What is the potential Energy ​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The potential energy of an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{PE = mgh}[/tex]

where:

PE = potential energy

m = mass

g = acceleration due to gravity = 9.8 m/s²

h = height

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the potential energy

PE = mgh

PE = (2 kg)(9.8 m/s²)(0.4 m)

[tex]\boxed{PE = \text{7.84 J}}[/tex]

Therefore, the potential energy of the box is 7.84 J.
