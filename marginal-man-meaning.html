6. What is the meaning of marginalism? And examples?


Marginalism is a theory of economics that attempts to explain the discrepancy in the value of goods and services by reference to their secondary, or marginal, utility. ... Thus, while the water has greater total utility, the diamond has greater marginal utility.

example- Under the special case in which usefulness can be quantified, the change in utility of moving from state  to state  is{\displaystyle \Delta U=U(S_{2})-U(S_{1})\,}

Moreover, if {\displaystyle S_{1}} and {\displaystyle S_{2}} are distinguishable by values of just one variable {\displaystyle g\,} which is itself quantified, then it becomes possible to speak of the ratio of the marginal utility of the change in {\displaystyle g\,} to the size of that change:

{\displaystyle \left.{\frac {\Delta U}{\Delta g}}\right|_{c.p.}}

(where “c.p.” indicates that the only independent variable to change is {\displaystyle g\,}).

Mainstream neoclassical economics will typically assume that

{\displaystyle \lim _{\Delta g\to 0}{\left.{\frac {\Delta U}{\Delta g}}\right|_{c.p.}}}

is well defined, and use “marginal utility” to refer to a partial derivative

{\displaystyle {\frac {\partial U}{\partial g}}\approx \left.{\frac {\Delta U}{\Delta g}}\right|_{c.p.}}

18. What does the law of diminishing marginal returns mean?


As successive units of a variable resource are added to a fixed resource, beyond some point the extra, or marginal, product attributable to each additional unit of variable resource will decline.


19. meaning ng marginal thinking​


answer:

Marginal thinking is thinking about how much extra resources are worth. If you have no bananas, and you get a banana, it’s worth a lot more to you than if you already had a million of them. Compare this to thinking about the average (and people often equivocate between the two). For example, a charity might be the most effective in the world on average, but if it’s just fundraised a lot, extra (marginal) donations might be going into their less effective programs.

26. anong meaning Ng marginal unility ?​


Sa ekonomiya, ang utility ay ang kasiyahan o benepisyo na nakuha sa pamamagitan ng pag-ubos ng isang produkto; kaya ang marginal utility ng isang mahusay o serbisyo ay ang pagbabago sa utility mula sa isang pagtaas sa pagkonsumo ng mabuti o serbisyo.

Sa konteksto ng kardinal na utility, minsan ang mga ekonomista ay nagsasalita ng isang batas ng lumiliit na marginal utility , ibig sabihin na ang unang yunit ng pagkonsumo ng isang mahusay o serbisyo ay nagbibigay ng higit na utility kaysa sa pangalawang at kasunod na mga yunit, na may patuloy na pagbabawas para sa mas malaking halaga.
