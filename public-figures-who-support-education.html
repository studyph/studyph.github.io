21. what are the implications of code of ethics to the current situation of educators who actively work in both public and private educational institution​


The code of ethics for educators is important for those who work in both public and private schools. It helps ensure fair treatment of students, consistency in behavior, and avoidance of conflicts of interest. The code also emphasizes protecting students' privacy and confidentiality. By following the code of ethics, educators can maintain professional standards and build trust with students, parents, and colleagues.
