The square root of 129 in mathematical form is written with the radical sign like this √129. We call this the square root of 129 in radical form. The square root of 129 is a quantity (q) that when multiplied by itself will equal 129.

Estimating the Square Root of a Number

Square root refers to the number that when multiplied by itself is equal to the original number. On the other hand, there are non-perfect square numbers, meaning their square root are in decimal form.

Estimating the square root of the numbers deals with the finding of a number that is close enough to the original number. It is not about finding the exact value but the approximate value of the number. To estimate, you have to apply the rules in rounding off numbers.

Look for the rounding digit. Look at the digit to its right. If the digit to its right is 5, 6, 7, 8, or 9, round up. Add 1 to the rounding digit.

Look for the rounding digit. Look at the digit to its right. If the digit to its right is 0, 1, 2, 3, or 4, round down. The rounding number stays the same.
