Here are the standard form of the numbers in the original questions.

In Math, numbers are usually written as numbers. This is referred to as the standard form. However, in Elementary School, we are taught so we will be able to write numbers in the form of words. For example, the word form of 300 is three hundreds, 2000 is two thousand, etc.

In writing the standard form, we must first decide which number is the highest among others. For example, if we are to write three million and two hundred thousand, then we write the number 3 first, and followed by adding necessary amount as per the question's: 3.200.000. Most of the time, what makes a student confused in this type of question is deciding how much zeroes the standard form will have.

In this thread, I would like to try to write the standard form of the following numbers:

thirteen million, seven hundred thousand = 13.700.000.135 million,48 thousand, two hundred = 135.048.200.Fifty six million ,seven hundred twenty two thousand = 56.722.000.eighty million, ninety thousand ten = 80.900.010.257 million,12 thousand,175 = 257.012.175.

It is best that we study about the amount of zeroes a trillion, billion, a million, a thousand, and a hundred has before solving a math problem like this. It will make our thought process easier in deciding where to put the numbers. For instance, a million is written as 1.000.000 in the standard form, and we can see that it has 6 amount of zeroes, while a hundred is 100.000, which has 5, etc.

With enough exercises, I am sure that we will be able to intuitively pick up how to write the standard form of a number. Don't forget to consult your Math teacher if you are unsure.

How to Write One Hundred Million in Numbers

To write one hundred million in numbers change “one hundred million” to “100”, then multiply 100 by 106.

Alternatively, move the decimal point 6 places to the left:

100 × 106 = 100000000,

100 → 1,000 → 10,000 → 100,000 → 1,000,000 → 10,000,000 → 100,000,000.

One hundred million in numbers, generally speaking, is 100000000. In figures, 100000000 is written with thousand separators as 100,000,000.
