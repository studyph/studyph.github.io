A. 30° - 150° - 30°

Step-by-step explanation:

A complete circle is equal to 360 degrees. And a straight line is equal to 180 degrees, so if:

Angle 1 = 150 degrees

Then,

180 degrees (straight line) less 150 degrees = angle 2

Angle 2 = 30 degrees

If angle 2 = 30 degtees then,

Angle 3 + angle 2 = 180 degrees

180 degrees less 30 degrees = 150 degrees

Angle 3 = 150 degrees
