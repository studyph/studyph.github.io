A 'No Smoking' sign in front of a store is an external pressure.

The sign became an external pressure because it is found in the environment. External pressures refer to the factors that are found in or is caused by the environment.

Peer pressure is a factor brought by the people around particularly by peers. Media influence on the other hand is a factor brought exclusively by medias. Lastly, internal pressure is a factor brought by the inner self of a person and not by the environment where a person moves.
