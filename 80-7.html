11. What is the 7/8 of 80? And 1/4 of 90?​


Answer:

70 and 22.5

Step-by-step explanation:

First: Convert the fractions to decimal, just simply divide the fraction.

[tex]\frac{7}{8} =0.875[/tex]

[tex]\frac{1}{4}=0.25[/tex]

Second: Use the formula [tex]P=RB[/tex] wherein [tex]P[/tex] is the percentage or ratio we are looking for, [tex]R[/tex] is the percent, [tex]B[/tex] base, the totality.

1. [tex]\frac{7}{8}[/tex] of 80?

[tex]P=?[/tex][tex]R=\frac{7}{8} =0.875[/tex][tex]B=80[/tex]

[tex]P=(0.875)(80)[/tex]

P = 70

2. [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex] of 90?

[tex]P=?[/tex][tex]R=\frac{1}{4} =0.25[/tex][tex]B=90[/tex]

[tex]P=(0.25)(90)[/tex]

P = 22.5

Step-by-Step Explanation

Then we know

X/80 = 7/10

Multiplying both sides by 80 cancels on the left

80 × (X/80) = (7/10) × 80

X = (7/10) × 80

Therefore
