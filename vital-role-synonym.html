10. Does the sun plays a vital role?​


Does the sun play a vital role?​

Yes, sun play a vital role. In photosynthesis, sunlight plays a critical role. It is essential for plant survival.

Chloroplasts are highly significant organelles found in plant leaves. Chlorophylls are specialized pigment compounds found within chloroplasts. Chlorophyll absorbs light and converts it into chemical energy for the plant.

Because the sun emits a mixture of predominantly red and blue light, chlorophyll absorbs these colors best. Chlorophyll, on the other hand, reflects green light, which is why most plants have green leaves.

The energy absorbed by chlorophyll may be utilized to produce sugar through photosynthesis. Photosynthesis slows down when a plant receives insufficient sunshine. This might also indicate that the plant isn't getting enough sugar, which is its primary source of energy.
