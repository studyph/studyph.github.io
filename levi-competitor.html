Levi Celerio was a Filipino lyricist and composer. He was recognized by the Guinness Book Of World Records for being the only musician who could play music using a leaf. He also has over 4,000 songs to his credit. Some of his songs include Sa Ugoy ng Duyan, Gaano Ko Ikaw Kamahal, Saan Ka Man Naroroon?, Waray-Waray, Ako Ay May Singsing and Alibangbang.
