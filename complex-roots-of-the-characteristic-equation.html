19. Ifc (c≠-1) is the complex root of the cubic equation x^3+1=0, what is the value of (c-c^3+c^5-c^7+c^9-c^11+c^13)-(c^2-c^4+c^6-c^8+c^10-c^12+c^14)?


Answer:

The value of the expression is equal to 1.

Explanation:

The equation x³ + 1 = 0 can be written as x³ = -1. From x³ = -1, we get

[tex]\begin{array}{ccc}\mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^5=\textit{\textsf{c}}^3 \cdot \textit{\textsf{c}}^2= -\textit{\textsf{c}}^2} & & \mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^9=(\textit{\textsf{c}}^3)^3=(-1)^3=-1} \\ \mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^7=(\textit{\textsf{c}}^3)^2\cdot \textit{\textsf{c}}=(-1)^2\cdot \textit{\textsf{c}}=\textit{\textsf{c}}}& & \mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^{11}=\textit{\textsf{c}}^9\cdot\textit{\textsf{c}}^2=-\textit{\textsf{c}}^2} \\ & &\mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^{13}={\textit{\textsf{c}}}}\end{array}[/tex]for odd powers

and for even powers

[tex]\begin{array}{ccccc}\mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^4=-\textit{\textsf{c}}} & & \mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^8=\textit{\textsf{c}}^2} & & \mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^{12}=1}\\ \mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^6=1} & & \mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^{10}=-\textit{\textsf{c}} } && \mathsf{\textit{\textsf{c}}^{14}=\textit{\textsf{c}}^2} \end{array}[/tex]

Hence, we may write

[tex]\begin{aligned} \textit{\textsf{N}}&=\mathsf{(\textit{\textsf{c}}-\textit{\textsf{c}}^3+\textit{\textsf{c}}^5-\textit{\textsf{c}}^7+\textit{\textsf{c}}^9-\textit{\textsf{c}}^{11}+\textit{\textsf{c}}^{13})} \\ &- \mathsf{(\textit{\textsf{c}}^2-\textit{\textsf{c}}^4+\textit{\textsf{c}}^6-\textit{\textsf{c}}^8+\textit{\textsf{c}}^{10}-\textit{\textsf{c}}^{12}+\textit{\textsf{c}}^{14})} \end{aligned}[/tex]

into

[tex]\begin{aligned} \textit{\textsf{N}}=&\mathsf{\left[\textit{\textsf{c}}-(-1)-\textit{\textsf{c}}^2-\textit{\textsf{c}}-1-(-\textit{\textsf{c}}^2)+\textit{\textsf{c}} \: \right]} \\ - \ &\mathsf{\left[\textit{\textsf{c}}^2-(-\textit{\textsf{c}})+1-\textit{\textsf{c}}^2-\textit{\textsf{c}}-1-\textit{\textsf{c}}^2\right]} \end{aligned}[/tex]

which simplifies to

[tex]\mathsf{\textit{\textsf{N}}=\textit{\textsf{c}}-\textit{\textsf{c}}^2=-(\textit{\textsf{c}}^2-c+1)+1}[/tex] note that we denoted the expression as 'N '

Since c  ≠ -1 is the complex root of x³ + 1 = (x + 1)(x² - x + 1) = 0, it means that c is the root of the other factor x² - x + 1 = 0, and so c² - c + 1 = 0 as well. Thus, the desired value N becomes

[tex]\mathsf{\textit{\textsf{N}}=-(\textit{\textsf{c}}^2-c+1)+1=0+1=1}[/tex]
