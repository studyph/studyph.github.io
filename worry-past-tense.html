29. Think of situation in your life when you got tensed. You were probably worried, upset or disappointed at some time in your life. What is/are your reaction/s to this situation?​


When faced with a tense situation, people may react in a variety of ways, depending on their personality, coping mechanisms, and the nature of the situation. Some common reactions to tension include feelings of anxiety, stress, or overwhelm, physical symptoms such as headaches or stomachaches, and changes in behavior such as irritability or avoidance.

People may also use a variety of coping strategies to manage their distress, such as seeking social support, engaging in relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or meditation, or taking action to address the source of the tension. It is important to note that different coping strategies work for different people, and what works for one person may not work for another. Ultimately, the most effective way to manage tension and stress is to develop a personalized approach that works for you and to seek professional help if needed.
