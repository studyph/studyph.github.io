Renaming Fractions to Decimals  

To rename fractions to decimals, simply divide the numerator of the fraction by its denominator. The movement of the decimal point from the right depends on the number of zeros in the denominator of the fraction. For instance, if the denominator has 1 zero, the decimal point will be 1 place from the right. For two zeros, the decimal point will be moved two places. For three zeros, the decimal point is three places from the right.
