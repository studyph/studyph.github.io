24. im a prime number my digits add up to 8 im a factors of 34 but am not a factor of 36 so what number i am a.2 b. 9 c. 17 d. 34


Answer:

c. 17

Explanation (Why  "c.17" ?):1 + 7 = 8 ,therefore, it satisfies the first and second condition.

Conditions:

1st Condition:

I'm a Prime Number Meaning the number should have only 2 factors. (1 and Itself)

2nd Condition:

I'm a prime number my digits add up to 8Meaning "x + y = 8"

3rd Condition:

I'm a factors of 34 but am not a factor of 36Meaning it should be the following numbers "1, 2, 17 and 34"

Explanation (Why not "a." "b." or "d."):Although 2 is a Prime number, it has only 1 digit and doesn't satisfy the first, second and third condition.Although 9 is a Prime number, it has only 1 digit and doesn't satisfy the first, second and third condition.34 is a composite number meaning it doesn't satisfy the first condition, adding the digits doesn't give you the sum of 8 meaning it doesn't satisfy the 2nd condition.
