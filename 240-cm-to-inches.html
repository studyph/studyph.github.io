21. 5. A rectangular shipping container has a length of 7 inches, width of 12 inches and height of 6 inches. Find the surface area of the shipping container? A. 240 inches C. 250 inches B. 256 inches² D. 396 inches²​


Answer:

Ans: D. 396 in²

Step-by-step explanation:

Surface area = 2(lw + wh + hl)

Substitute the given values

A = 2[7in(12in) + 12in(6in) + 6in(7in)]

A = 2(84in² + 72in² + 42in²)

A = 2(198 in²)

A = 396 in²

26. A rectangular shipping container has a length of 7 inches, width of 12 inches and height of 6 inches. Find the surface area of the shipping container? A. 240 inches²B. 256 inches²C. 250 inches² D. 396 inches​


Answer:

Ans: D. 396in²

Step-by-step explanation:

Formula: SA = 2(lw + wh + hl)

Given:

l = 7in

w = 12in

h = 6in

Solve:

SA = 2(lw + wh + hl)

SA = 2(7in(12in) + 12in(6in) + 6in(7in))

SA = 2(84in² + 72 in² + 42in²)

SA = 2(198in²)

SA = 396in²
