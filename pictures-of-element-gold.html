2. Which elements is mixed with gold to make red gold? ​


Answer:

Red gold is a gold and copper alloy that is sometimes also referred to as pink gold or rose gold. Since copper has a bold pinkish-orange colour, adding this alloy to gold gives the gold a beautiful pinkish gold colour.

11. what is the importance of the element gold​


Answer:

Gold is a good conductor of heat and electricity and does not tarnish when it is exposed to the air, so it can be used to make electrical connectors and printed circuit boards.

Gold is also a good reflector of infrared radiation and can be used to help shield spacecraft and skyscrapers from the sun's heat.
