8. Hi po pa answer this po<3im grade 5Find the meaning of the following:1.divisibility2.odd3.even4.prime and composite​


Answer:

1. Something has divisibility if you can split it into different sections or portions. It refers to a number's quality of being divided by another number, without a remainder or leftover

2.  a whole number that is not able to be divided by two into two equal whole numbers. An odd number is a number which is not divisible by 2. The remainder in the case of an odd number is always “1”.

3.  a whole number that is able to be divided by two into two equal whole numbers. An even number is a number that can be divided into two equal groups. Even numbers end in 2, 4, 6, 8 and 0 regardless of how many digits they have (we know the number 5,917,624 is even because it ends in a 4!)

4. A prime number is a whole number with exactly two integral divisors, 1 and itself. A composite number is a whole number with more than two integral divisors.
