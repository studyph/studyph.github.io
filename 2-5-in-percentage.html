The formula to be used in finding the unknown value is P = B × R, wherein, P is the percentage, B is the base while R is the rate.

DERIVED FORMULA FOR BASE AND RATEB = P ÷ RR = P ÷ B

Note that writing percent to decimal and vice versa is important when it comes to a problem like this.

In solving for the base and percentage, you need to rewrite first the percent in decimal, for an easier solving, by moving the decimal point two units to the left.

Example:

[tex]\sf 25\% =0.25[/tex]

In solving for the rate, you need to rewrite the answer that is in decimal to a percent through multiplying the answer by 100.

Example:

[tex]\sf 0.75\times100=75\%[/tex]
