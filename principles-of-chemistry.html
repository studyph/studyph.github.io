6. in your own words, discuss how the principles and knowledge in chemistry may help solve the present problem on the COVID-19 pandemic?​


Answer:

The principles and knowledge of chemistry can play a significant role in addressing the current COVID-19 pandemic in several ways. For example, chemists can use their understanding of chemical reactions and the properties of molecules to develop new drugs and treatments for the virus. They can also use their knowledge of chemical analysis to develop accurate and reliable tests for detecting the presence of the virus in individuals. Furthermore, chemists can use their expertise in materials science to design and develop new types of personal protective equipment, such as masks and gloves, that can help prevent the spread of the virus. Overall, the principles and knowledge of chemistry can provide valuable insights and solutions for addressing the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.
