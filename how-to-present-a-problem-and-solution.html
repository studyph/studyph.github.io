4. Problem #1Concentrated sulfuric acid has aconcentration of 95% by mass. Ifthe solution has a mass of 100grams how much solute ispresent in the solution?​


Answer:

mass solute = 95 g

Explanation:

Calculating the mass of solute

percent by mass = (mass solute / mass solution) × 100

mass solute = (percent by mass / 100)(mass solution)

mass solute = (95 / 100)(100)

mass solute = 95 g
