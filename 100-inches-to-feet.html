17. Which expression converts 100 inches per minute to feet per minute?


Inches per minute and feet per minute are both units of speed. They are ratios of distance to time. The unit of time is already consistent, which is in minuted. So, the only thing we're going to convert is the unit of distance. A ruler is often called a "foot of ruler" because the length of 1 ruler is 1 foot. And we all know that a ruler consists of 12 inches. So, this means that there are 12 inches in every 1 foot. The expression of conversion from inches to foot would then be 1/12.
