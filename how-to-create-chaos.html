21. Explain People were created to be loved, and things were created to be used; the reason why the world is in so much chaos right now is because people are being used, and things are being loved


Answer:

This statement is a commentary on the state of the world today, suggesting that people have lost sight of what is truly important - valuing and loving one another - and instead have become fixated on material possessions and using others for their own gain. The first part of the statement affirms the idea that humans are inherently meant to love and be loved, while things are meant to be used for practical purposes. However, the second part of the statement suggests that people have inverted this relationship, causing chaos and discord in the world. By prioritizing things over people, individuals and societies alike have lost touch with the fundamental human values of compassion, empathy, and connection. The statement serves as a reminder that, in order to create a more harmonious and just world, we must prioritize the well-being of others and cultivate a culture of love and respect.
