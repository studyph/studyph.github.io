24. a 2,000 ml sample of helium at 300 degrees celsius and 700 torr is compressed to a volume of 850 ml at 150 degrees celsius what is the new gas​


Given:

[tex]P_{1} = \text{700 torr}[/tex]

[tex]T_{1} = \text{300°C + 273 = 573 K}[/tex]

[tex]V_{1} = \text{2000 mL}[/tex]

[tex]V_{2} = \text{850 mL}[/tex]

[tex]T_{2} = \text{150°C + 273 = 423 K}[/tex]

Unknown:

[tex]P_{2}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]\frac{P_{1}V_{1}}{T_{1}} = \frac{P_{2}V_{2}}{T_{2}}[/tex]

[tex]P_{2} = P_{1} × \frac{V_{1}}{V_{2}} × \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}[/tex]

[tex]P_{2} = \text{700 torr} × \frac{\text{2000 mL}}{\text{850 mL}} × \frac{\text{423 K}}{\text{573 K}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{P_{2} = \text{1216 torr}}[/tex]

27. Justin's rice ball recipe uses 100 grams of rice to make 1 rice ball. Justin has 700 grams of rice. How many rice balls can Justin make with 700 grams of rice?


Answer:

Justin can make 7 rice balls with 700 grams of rice.

To find the number of rice balls with 700 grams of rice, we need to use ratios to relate the different items in the problem.

Given:

Let us represent the given in ratio using the fraction form.

ratio = grams of rice / rice ball

Let x be the number of rice balls.

‌100⁄1‌

700/x

Solution:

To find the missing value, we need to cross multiply.

‌100⁄1‌ = 700/x

100 • x = 1 • 700

100x = 700

100x/100 = 700/100

x = 7

Therefore, Justin can make 7 rice balls with 700 grams of rice.

Checking:

Ratios should be proportional.

100⁄1‌ = ‌700⁄7‌

100 • 7 = 1 • 700

700 = 700 ✔

Explanation:

Ratio

Ratio is a comparison between quantities. It says how much of one thing there is compared to another thing. In simple words, it compares values.

There are three ways in expressing ratios:

Word Form (using the word "to")

Colon Form (:)

Fraction Form (/)

Example:

"Ratio of 100 grams of rice to make 1 rice ball."

Word Form: 100 to 1

Colon Form: 100:1

Fraction Form: ‌100⁄1‌

Proportion

Proportion shows that two ratios are equal. To find the unknown value in a proportion, we need to do cross multiplication.

Cross multiplication is multiplying the outer terms called the extremes, and the middle terms called the means.

Parts of a Proportion

Means are the middle terms.

Examples:

3:6 = 4:8

3/6 = 4/8

Extremes are the outer terms.

Examples:

3:6 = 4:8

3/6 = 4/8
