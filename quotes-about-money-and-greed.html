29. Explain this quote " it does not matter how much money you make. It matters what you do with your money.​


It does not matter how much money you make; what matters is what you do with your money.

The quote is an important passage that must be remembered at all times. Money is essential so that we may afford our basic needs. Money affords us health, education, happiness, and survival.

However, it is wrong to say that money is the only important thing in the world. Too often, people who do not have the right values will view money as the only factor determining a person's worth. They may place someone rich in the highest regard or turn their noses down on someone who earns less than them.

Too many people also think that earning more money than they need gives them the right to do dangerous things to their own bodies, to other living things, and to the environment. The truth is, money can help you enrich your own character and well-being. It should help you fulfill your dreams, help you live a peaceful life, and allow you to help others.

Money should not make one turn into a condescending and spiteful person.
