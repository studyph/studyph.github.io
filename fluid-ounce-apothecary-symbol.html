15. During a sale, a 16 fluid ounce container of juice costs $2.56 and a 24 fluid ounce container of juice costs $3.60. Determine the cost per fluid ounce for each container of juice, and state which container of juice has the lower cost per fluid ounce?


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

A 16-ounce container of juice costs $2.56.

2.56/16 = 0.16/oz

A 24-ounce container of the same juice costs $3.60

3.60/24 = .15/oz

The smaller size is cheaper because it costs less money per oz.
