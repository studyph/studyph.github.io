19. What is the relationship of speed to the frequency and amplitude of the wave?


Answer:

The speed of a wave is directly proportional to its frequency and inversely proportional to its amplitude. This means that as the frequency increases, the speed of the wave increases, while the amplitude decreases. As the amplitude increases, the speed of the wave decreases and the frequency remains constant.

The amplitude of a sound wave is the measure of the height of the wave.
