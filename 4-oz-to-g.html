22. Convert the following measures: with solutions 5.40.25 dag to cg 6.1/2 oz to lbs 7. 92 g to lbs 8. 1.25 kg to oz 9. 12.5 g to oz 10.28 oz to kg


Answer:

To convert from decagrams (dag) to centigrams (cg), you need to multiply by 1000 and then divide by 10, since there are 10 centigrams in a decagram.

So, to convert 40.25 dag to cg, you can use the following formula:

40.25 dag * 1000 cg/dag / 10 = 4025 cg

Therefore, 40.25 dag is equal to 4025 cg.
