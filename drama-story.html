15. It is the place and time in which a drama/story takes place.


Answer:

SETTING is the place and time in which a drama or story takes place.

Explanation:

Elements of a Story

Behind every story is a structure made up of elements of a story. There are 5 main elements of a story.

Character

Setting

Plot

Conflict

Theme

Character

Characters can be person or animal, who takes part in the action of a story. Writers use characters to perform the actions and speak the dialogue of a story. They are the who of a story.

Setting

It refers not only to the physical location, but also the time the action takes place. It is the where and the when of a story.

Plot

A plot is a series of events and character actions that relate to the central conflict. Plot can be further divided into sub-elements such as: introduction, rising action, climax, falling action, and resolution. It is the what of the story.

Conflict

It is a struggle between two people or things in a story. This conflict can be thought of as a challenge or problem that drives the action of the story. Conflict gives these events their why.

Theme

The theme is the central idea or belief in a story. It is often thought of as the message of the story.
