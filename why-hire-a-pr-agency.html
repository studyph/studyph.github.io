26. ano ang ibig sabihin ng direct hire hindi agency worker


Answer:

Direct hiring is a process in which a company offers you a job and employs you without using a third party. This structure is different from temporary (typically less than a year) or contract positions in which a staffing agency supplies and employs the talent.

You're an agency worker if you have a contract with an agency but you work temporarily for a hirer. Agencies can include recruitment agencies, for example 'temp agencies'. You're also an agency worker if you look for work through entertainment and modelling agencies.
