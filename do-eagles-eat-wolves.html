5. Animals like eagles and vultures also eat dead animals.how do they help surroundings?


They decompose it, so they stop decaying dead animals this is called scavengers or  decomposer.

The reason why vultures are found within the vicinity of deserts is because most decomposers are not thermofiles (meaning heat resistant) which makes it almost impossible for decomposers to reach a dead organism. Vultures which is in the class of scavenger decomposes these dead organisms in hot areas wherein decomposers are not present.
