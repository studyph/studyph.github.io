Answer:

107.5°

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = supplement

x+35= angle

[tex]x + (x + 35) = 180 \\ 2x + 35 = 180 \\ 2x = 180 - 35 \\ 2x = 145 \\ \frac{2x}{2} = \frac{145}{2} \\ x = 72.5[/tex]

If x is 72.5, then:

Angle = x + 35

=72.5 + 35

= 107.5

20. Which is the third angle in a given measurement of a triangle 25 degrees , 35 degrees , ___?Choices:A. 45 degreesB. 120 degreesC. 35 degreesD. 90 degrees


Answer:

i believe I can get brainleaist answer

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the third angle in a triangle, we can use the fact that the sum of the interior angles of a triangle is always 180 degrees.

So, if we add the first two angles given, we get:

25 degrees + 35 degrees = 60 degrees

To find the third angle, we can subtract the sum of the first two angles from 180 degrees:

180 degrees - 60 degrees = 120 degrees

Therefore, the third angle in this triangle is 120 degrees, and the answer is (B) 120 degrees.
