1. C= {x E N : x is divisible by 12, x < 120}


Answer and step-by-step explanation:

To find the correct set, let's observe the given hints first.

The symbol "N" means natural numbers which is {0,1,2,3,...}. So there are no negative integers in our set.

"x is divisible by 12" this means we should find numbers that can be divided by 12 and the quotient will be a whole number.

It is easier to do the opposite which is to multiply 1 to any number by 12.

12 × 1 = 12

12 × 2 = 24

12 × 3 = 36

12 × 4 = 48

12 × 5 = 60

12 × 6 = 72

12 × 7 = 84

12 × 8 = 96

12 × 9 = 108

*Let's stop here. If we multiply it further, 12 × 10 = 120. The number 120 is not part of our element because of the hint "x is less than 120 or x < 120".

Therefore, the answer is C = {12,24,36,48,60,72,84,96,108}.
