1. If (x + 4)A= x2 - 16, then A= * a. x + 4 b. X - 4 c. x+16 d. X - 16


(x+4)A = x²-16
(x+4)A = (x+4)(x-4)
A = x-4
Letter B

Answer and step-by-step explanation:

To find A, factor the right side of the equation first. Use any kinds of method in factoring a perfect square binomial.

Then subtract both sides by (x + 4) to isolate A from the left side of the equation.

(x + 4)A = x^2 - 16

(x + 4)A = (x + 4)(x - 4)

(x + 4) - (x + 4) A = (x + 4) - (x + 4)(x - 4)

A = x - 4

Therefore, the answer is b. x - 4.
