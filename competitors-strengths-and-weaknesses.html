6. If your product is an anti-dandruff shampoo with a price of about P180 and is sold in supermarkets. Identify the direct and indirect competitors of your product and discuss each competitor’s strengths, competitive advantage and weaknesses. Make sure to follow the given outline.


Sure, I can help you with that! Here's an analysis of the direct and indirect competitors of your anti-dandruff shampoo product:

Direct Competitors:

Head and ShouldersClearSelsun Blue

Head and Shoulders:

Strengths:

Head and Shoulders has been in the market for a long time and has built a strong brand name.They have a wide range of products for different hair types and dandruff conditions.Head and Shoulders has a reputation for being highly effective in treating dandruff.

Competitive Advantage:

Head and Shoulders' patented technology contains pyrithione zinc, which is highly effective in controlling dandruff.

Weaknesses:

Head and Shoulders is priced higher than your product, which may make it less accessible to price-conscious consumers.Their products may not be suitable for people with sensitive skin due to the use of harsh chemicals.

Clear:

Strengths:

Clear is known for its scalp care benefits and is positioned as a premium brand.They offer a range of shampoos for different hair types and dandruff conditions.Clear's marketing emphasizes the use of natural ingredients in their products.

Competitive Advantage:

Clear's formula contains bio-nutrium 10, a blend of 10 nutrients and botanical actives that nourish the scalp and hair.

Weaknesses:

Clear is more expensive than your product, making it less accessible to price-sensitive consumers.Some consumers may prefer products that are completely natural, rather than a blend of natural and synthetic ingredients.

Selsun Blue:

Strengths:

Selsun Blue is a well-known brand in the anti-dandruff category.They offer a range of products for different dandruff conditions, including severe dandruff.Selsun Blue's formula contains selenium sulfide, which is highly effective in treating dandruff.

Competitive Advantage:

Selsun Blue's formula is known to work quickly, providing relief from dandruff in just a few washes.

Weaknesses:

Selsun Blue is priced higher than your product, making it less accessible to price-sensitive consumers.The use of selenium sulfide may cause skin irritation in some people.

Indirect Competitors:

Herbal EssencesPanteneDove

Herbal Essences:

Strengths:

Herbal Essences offers a range of products for different hair types and concerns.They market themselves as a natural hair care brand, using plant-based ingredients in their products.Herbal Essences products are priced similarly to your product.

Competitive Advantage:

Herbal Essences' formula contains natural ingredients, which may be preferred by some consumers over synthetic ingredients.

Weaknesses:

Herbal Essences may not be as effective in treating dandruff as your product or other anti-dandruff shampoos.Their focus on natural ingredients may make them less appealing to consumers who prefer more clinically tested formulas.

Pantene:

Strengths:

Pantene is a well-established brand in the hair care category.They offer a range of products for different hair types and concerns.Pantene's marketing emphasizes their Pro-V formula, which is designed to strengthen hair.

Competitive Advantage:

Pantene's formula contains Pro-Vitamin B5, which is known to improve hair health.

Weaknesses:

Pantene may not be as effective in treating dandruff as your product or other anti-dandruff shampoos.Pantene's focus on hair strength may make them less appealing to consumers who are primarily concerned with treating dandruff.
