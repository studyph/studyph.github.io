6. How to solve this? sin 25=10/hypotenuse


Answer:

The length of the hypotenuse is 23.66

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x represent the hypotenuse.

[tex]sin25 = \frac{10}{x}[/tex]

In order to solve this, first, we will cross multiply.

[tex]xsin25 = 10[/tex]

Next, we will have to eliminate sin25 so that we can get the length of the hypotenuse or the value of x. We will have to divide both sides by sin25.

[tex]\frac{xsin25}{sin25}=\frac{10}{sin25}[/tex]

[tex]x = 23.66[/tex]

Therefore, the hypotenuse is 23.66.
