14. View points of ang probinsyano


Answer:

ANG PROBINSYANO: Issues and Viewpoints of the Social Commentary TV series

1. Issue: Negative portrayal of police officers

Viewpoints: There are scenes in the TV series that show policemen to be corrupt. In the present area for the last 6 years, the whole Philippine National Police has been in consistent scrutiny regarding immunity killings, corruption, and other crimes instead of protecting their citizens.

It is not the state knocking on your door in the middle of the night, but a policeman – a corporeal being – acting on its behalf. Whatever influence the state exerts is communicated and mediated by the police who are its agents.

The PNP general comments that the portrayal is very negative and unfair.

2. Issue: Public Attorney’s Office is portrayed wrong

In the show, the Public Attorney’s office is shown as indifferent. The office is used by citizens who cannot afford to have a lawyer. Thus, they are defended by the state.

Viewpoint: The Philippines is considered to have a weak justice system.

The lawyer complained against the TV station about it wrongly miseducating the public about how the system of public lawyering works.

They were told to follow a procedure in a meticulous matter as not to create another faux pas.

3. Issue: Misrepresentation of provinces and towns

Viewpoint: There was a part or whole season in which the protagonist is going around every provinces of the Philippines.

It is not new that Philippines is an archipelago and so the differing in provinces or islands creates new identities and cultures apart from the mainstream.

The cultural and technical habits are not yet sufficiently developed, the automatism of the technological process is not yet sufficiently advanced, the productivity of labor has not yet increased sufficiently for any man to be able to undertake successively various works.

4. Issue: Corruption from higher levels of government

Viewpoints: In another season, the show depicted a seeming reality of our own in which elite members of the government service are conniving with shady people in power or business to cheat their way and fool their citizens. In my opinion, it was a mere copycat. They should have been more creative.
