23. what property is illustrated in (83+20) +25=83+(20+25)?​


Answer:

The property illustrated in the equation (83 + 20) + 25 = 83 + (20 + 25) is the associative property of addition.

Step-by-step explanation:

The associative property of addition states that the way in which the terms in an addition expression are grouped does not affect the result of the expression. In other words, the expression (83 + 20) + 25 can be rewritten as 83 + (20 + 25) and the result will be the same. This property allows us to rearrange the terms in an addition expression for the purpose of simplification or to help us better understand the expression.

29. 83 1/3% of what number is 25?


Answer:

83.3% of 30.01 ≈ 25

Step-by-step explanation:

83.3% × x = 25

x = 25 ÷ 83.3%

x = 25 ÷ (83.3 ÷ 100)

x = (100 × 25) ÷ 83.3

x = 2500 ÷ 83.3
