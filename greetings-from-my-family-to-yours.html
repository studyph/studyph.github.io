13. thankyou for your thoughfuness that you did not even remember to greet me on my birthday a.irony b.hyperbole c.smile d.metapore


Answer:

B. Hyperbole

Explanation:

Hyperbole- An extravagant statement; the use of exaggerated terms for the purpose of emphasis or heightened effect.

ex: I could sleep for a year.

Irony- The use of words to convey the opposite of their literal meaning.

ex: The farmers marched for a week to the city hall to beg for grains.

Simile- Comparing two unlike things using like or as.

ex: Her smile is like a sweet chocolate

Metaphor- A word or phrase for one thing that is used to refer to another thing in order to show or suggest that they are similar.

ex: Drew is turtle in slowness.
