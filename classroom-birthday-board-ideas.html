27. Mrs. Jacinto asked a carpenter to construct a rectangular bulletin board for her classroom. She told the carpenter that the board’s area must be 18 square feet. What are the possible dimensions of the bulletin board? Give at least 2 pairs of possible dimensions.


Answer:

The possible dimensions of the bulletin board are, 3ft x 6ft and 2ft x 9ft.

Step-by-step explanation:

       Given:

 Area of bulletin board = 18 square feet

 Shape is rectangular

Required:

 Dimension of the rectangular bulletin board. (Length and Width)

 Give at least two pairs of possible dimension.

Solution:

 Let,

  A = area of the rectangular bulletin board

  L = Length of rectangular bulletin board

  W = Width of rectangular bulletin board

 A = L x W

Since, A= 18 ft²

 L x W = 18

If L is twice as long as the W, L = 2W

Then,

 2W(W) = 18

 2W² = 18

 W = √(18/2)

 W = 3, thus L = 2W = 2(3) = 6

Checking,

 A = L x W = 18

       6 x 3 = 18

             18 = 18, checked

If L is 7 ft longer that W, L = W+ 7

Then,  

 W(W+7) = 18

 W²+7W = 18

 W²+7W-18 = 0, roots are, W = 2 & W = -9

 Real root is positive for this application, thus, W = 2

 L = W+ 7

 L = 9

Checking,

 A = L x W = 18

        9 x 2 = 18

  18 = 18, checked

Therefore,  

  Dimensions are: 6ft x 3 ft  and,

         9 ft x 2 ft
