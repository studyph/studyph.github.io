13. What animal has the most number of teeth?


ARMADILLO

Armadillo refers to the animal that is commonly found in the thick rainforests of the continent of South America.

The Giant armadillo scientific name (Priodontes maximus) is known as the land mammal with the most number of teeth, usually over 100 teeth in its mouth at just one time.

If your question is regardless of what is the size of teeth, then the snail tops the most number of teeth with roughly 25,000 to 26,000 microscopic teeth! That it commonly uses for filtering its food.
