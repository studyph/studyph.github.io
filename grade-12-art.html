Answer:

1. line - a long narrow mark or band

2. shape - a geometric figure such as square, triangle or rectangle.

3. form - the visible shape or configuration of something.

4. space - can be positive or negative. it it includes the background, foreground, and middle ground

5. color - is an element consisting consisting of hues, of which there are three properties: hue, chroma, or intensity and value.

6. value - deals with the lightness or darkness of a color.

7. texture - refers to the surface quality quality in a work of art.
