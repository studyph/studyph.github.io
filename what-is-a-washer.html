Refrigerator: x + 250

Dryer: (1/2)x or x/2

Total: 1,800

(x) + (x+250) + x/2 = 1,800

Multiply each term by their LCD 2:

(2) [(x) + (x+250) + x/2 = 1,800] (2)

2x + 2x + 500 + x = 3,600

5x = 3,600 - 500

5x/5 = 3,100/5

x = 620

ANSWER: The washer costs $620.

Check:

Washer: 620

Refrigerator: 620 + 250 ⇒ 870

Dryer: (1/2)(620) ⇒ 310

620 + 870 + 310 = 1,800
