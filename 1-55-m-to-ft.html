11. 9. What are the measures of the sides of a kite SOFT in meters? A. ST = 11 m and TF = 53 m B. SO = 11 m and FT = 55 m C. ST = 9 m and OF = 54 mD. SO = 12 m and OF = 54 m​


______________________________________

ANSWER

A) ST = 11 m and TF = 53 m

_____________________________________

SOLUTION

Find the value of x of SO and ST.

[tex] \\ ST = SO \\ \\ 3x - 1 = 2x + 3 \\ \\ x = 4 \\ \\ [/tex]

Then, find the measure of the sides of SO and ST

[tex] \\ ST = SO \\ \\ 3x - 1 = 2x + 3 \\ \\ 3(4) - 1 = 2(4) + 3 \\ \\ 11 \: m = 11 \: m \\ \\ [/tex]

Therefore, the Measure of the Sides of SO and ST is 11 m

Find the value of x of OF and TF.

[tex] \\ OF = TF \\ \\ 8x - 11 = 6x + 5 \\ \\ x = 8 \\ \\ [/tex]

Then, find the measure of the sides of OF and TF

[tex] \\ OF = TF \\ \\ 8x - 11 = 6x + 5 \\ \\ 8(8) - 11 = 6(8) + 5 \\ \\ 53 \: m = 53 \: m \\ \\ [/tex]

Therefore, the Measure of the Sides of OF and TF is 53 m

To conclude, the Sides of SO and ST measure's 11 m and the Sides of OF and TF measure's 53 m. So, the true statement from the given choices is A) ST = 11 m and TF = 53 m.
