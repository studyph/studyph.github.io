160 minutes = 2.67 hours (rounded to two decimal places)

21 days = 3 weeks and 0 days (since 21 is not evenly divisible by 7)

20 hours = 72,000 seconds (since 1 hour = 60 minutes and 1 minute = 60 seconds, so 20 hours = 20 x 60 x 60 = 72,000 seconds)

180 seconds = 3 minutes (since 1 minute = 60 seconds, so 180 seconds = 3 minutes)

25 minutes = 1,500 seconds (since 1 minute = 60 seconds, so 25 minutes = 25 x 60 = 1,500 seconds)

30 centuries = 300 decades (since 1 century = 100 years and 1 decade = 10 years, so 30 centuries = 30 x 100 = 300 decades)

3 millenniums = 30 centuries (since 1 millennium = 1,000 years and 1 century = 100 years, so 3 millenniums = 3 x 1,000 = 3,000 years, and 3,000 years / 100 years/century = 30 centuries)

3 weeks and 1 day = 22 days (since 1 week = 7 days, so 3 weeks = 3 x 7 = 21 days, and 3 weeks and 1 day = 21 days + 1 day = 22 days)

1 week = 7 days

1 day = 24 x 60 x 60 = 86,400 seconds
