Should have these elements!!

Theme- Ex Love, Revenge, etc..

Setting - Location or time frame where and when the story happened.

Characters - People, Animals, and etc in the story .

Exposition- Background info of the character and beginning of the story.

Rising Action- Where the problem wil take it's place.

Climax- Where the problem was solved.

Falling Action- Steaming the tension inthe climax or conclusion of the climax.

Resolution- Lesson and ending of the story.
