19. Divide 50 pens to the ratio 4:5


Answer:

Pens divided to the ratio of 4:5 is 20:25.

The given number of pens is not divisible by the given ratio. There are 5 remaining pens.

Step-by-step explanation:

Ratio

A ratio shows the relative sizes of two or more values. It is a comparison between or relating of two different things. It indicates how many times one number contains another.

Going back to the problem above, the 50 pens should be divided in the ratio of 4:5, meaning in every 4 pens, there are 5 pens.

There are three steps to find the answer.

1. Add together the two halves of the ratio.

2. Divide the total number of pens by the sum of the ratio.

3. Multiply the quotient by each number in the ratio.

Solution:

Step #1

4:5 = 4 + 5 = 9

Sum is 9.

Step #2

50 ÷ 9 = 5 r.5

We cannot divide the 50 pens exactly by the given ratio. There are 5 remaining pens and those 5 pens are not part of the ratio.

There are 5 groups of every half of the ratio.

Step #3

4 × 5 = 20

5 × 5 = 25

4:5 = 20:25

Checking:

Ratios should be proportional. To check, let us cross multiply. They should have the same value.

4:5 = 20:25

5 × 20 = 4 × 25

100 = 100 ✔
