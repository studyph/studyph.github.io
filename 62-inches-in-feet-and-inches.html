11. A rectangle has a perimeter of 24 inches with a length of 4 inches and a width of[tex] \sqrt{x + 2} inches[/tex]find x.a)60b)61c)62d)63​


Answer:

Letter "c",  62 inches

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula for Perimeter of a rectangle is

P = 2L + 2W

Given:

P = 24 in

L = 4 in

W = [tex]\sqrt{x+2}[/tex] in

Required:

x = ?

Solution:

To solve this we need to substitute the values of P , L , and W to the formula

P = 2L + 2W

24 = 2(4) + 2([tex]\sqrt{x+2}[/tex])

24 = 8 + 2([tex]\sqrt{x+2}[/tex])

2([tex]\sqrt{x+2}[/tex]) = 24 - 8

2([tex]\sqrt{x+2}[/tex]) = 16

[tex]\sqrt{x+2}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{16}{2}[/tex]

[tex]\sqrt{x+2}[/tex] = 8

By squaring both sides

[tex](\sqrt{x+2})^{2}[/tex] = [tex]8^{2}[/tex]

x + 2 = 64

x = 64 -2

x = 62 in.
