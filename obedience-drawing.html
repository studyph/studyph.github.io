25. How important it is to be obedient?​


How important it is to be obedient?​

Obedience shows our respect and faith in God; obedience is the key to our success; obedience is the guaranteed and promised means to unlock rewards for our life. To truly obey, we must study His word every day and beg God to fill us with His Holy Spirit so that our lives would honor Him.

Through obedience we learn to accept and embrace His call to care for the world, we learn more about him and gain a better knowledge of his heart and goals for our life.
