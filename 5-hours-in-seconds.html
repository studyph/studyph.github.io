30. From 130 km per hour a jeepny increase its speed to 60 km per hour in 5 seconds. How much it is acceleration? A. - 6 km per hours B. 6 km per hour per seconds C. - 12 km per hours D. 12 km per hour per seconds​


Given: Vi = 130km/h

Vf = 60km/h

t = 5s

Unknown: a = ?

Formula: a = Vf-Vi/t

Solution: a = Vf - Vi / t

= 60km/h - 130km/h / 5s

= -70km/h / 5s

= -14km/h/s

Final Answer: None of the above, 'cause the answer is -14 km/h/s.
