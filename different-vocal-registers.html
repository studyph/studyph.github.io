28. What is the difference between sacred vocal music and secular vocal music?​


Sacred music is music associated with religious or spiritual worship. ... Secular music is music that does not primarily have a religious subject, though it can mention the divine or holy. Its emphasis is on ordinary, everyday content: love, work, nature, grief, and folk stories.


29. state the difference of the ambahan vocal music of mindoro and the balitaw vocal music


The Ambahan is a lyrical chant song of the pure Mangyans. It is composed of seven syllables in one line that rhyme. The number of lines found in several Ambahan ranges from three to 134. There is no specific tone style or instrument used as accompaniment. While balitaw vocal music is a debate or dialogue song in which a young woman and a young man compete to see who is better at improvising romantic verses.
