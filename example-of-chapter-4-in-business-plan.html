TYPES OF BUSINESS OWNERSHIP

SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP

Under the sole proprietorship one can operate a business, making it the simplest business form.The sole proprietorship is not a legal entity.In sole proprietorship, the person who owns the business is the one who personally is responsible for the debts.With the ease of setup and nominal cost, it became a popular business form.

PARTNERSHIP

In partnership type of business ownership, parties agree to corporate to advance their mutual interests .If a business is owned by two or more people, it is a partnership type of  business ownership.It has 3 typical classifications of for-profit partnerships: general partnerships, limited partnerships, and limited liability partnerships

CORPORATION

A separate legal entity from its owners Members of the corporation known as shareholderMembers' share in the ownership, control and profits of the corporation is determined by their portion of shares

COOPERATIVE

Cooperative is a type of business ownership which is a member-owned business structure with at least five membersA cooperative is a separate legal entity and members 3)Usually only allows a limited distribution of profits to members.

12. Levi can read 3 chapters of a story book in 3/4 hour.How many chapters he read in 4 1/4 hours?​


Answer:

Levi can read 3 chapters of a story book in 3/4 hour.How many chapters he read in 4 1/4 hours?

To solve this problem, we first need to convert the time Levi reads to a common unit, such as minutes. 3/4 hour is equal to 3/4 * 60 = 45 minutes. 4 1/4 hours is equal to 4 1/4 * 60 = 270 minutes.

Now that we have the time in minutes, we can calculate the number of chapters Levi reads in that time. In 45 minutes, Levi reads 3/4 * 3 chapters = <<3/43=2.25>>2.25 chapters. In 270 minutes, Levi reads 2.25 * 270/45 = <<2.25270/45=6>>6 chapters.

Therefore, Levi reads a total of 6 chapters in 4 1/4 hours.

16. Give some synthesis or your learnings about this chapter. and site an examples if possible.​


Answer:

When you write a literature review or essay, you have to go beyond just summarizing the articles you’ve read – you need to synthesize the literature to show how it all fits together (and how your own research fits in).

Explanation:

Synthesizing simply means combining. Instead of summarizing the main points of each source in turn, you put together the ideas and findings of multiple sources in order to make an overall point.
