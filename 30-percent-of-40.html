2. Find the rate of 30 is what percent of 40 ​


Answer:

To find the percentage, we can use the formula:

percentage = (part/whole) x 100%

Here, the part is 30, the whole is 40, and we want to find the percentage.

So, percentage = (30/40) x 100%

percentage = 0.75 x 100%

percentage = 75%

Therefore, 30 is 75% of 40.
