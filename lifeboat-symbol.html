11. During a rescue operation, two ships were sent by the coast guards to rescue the passengers trapped in a lifeboat. To make the rescue operation easier, a rectangular coordinate system was used to map the entire rescue location. Ship A has coordinates (-1, -2) and ship 8 has coordinates (-4, 11. Both ships have spotlights fixated on the same lifeboat. The light beam from ship Atravels along the line y=2x The light beam from ship 8travels along the line y=x+5. As the coast guard personnel on duty, you are tasked to determine the coordinates of the lifeboat to guide the ships. You are to submit to the team leader of the rescue operation an accurate and properly labeled sketch that shows the coordinates of the two ships and the lifeboat. ​


Answer:

To determine the coordinates of the lifeboat using the information given, we need to find the point of intersection between the two lines representing the light beams from the two ships.

The equation for the line from Ship A is y = 2x.

The equation for the line from Ship B is y = x + 5.

To find the point of intersection, we can set the two equations equal to each other and solve for x and y.

y = 2x

y = x + 5

x + 5 = 2x

x = 5

Substituting this value of x back into the first equation, we get:

y = 2(5)

y = 10

Therefore, the coordinates of the lifeboat are (5, 10).

To create a labeled sketch, you would need to plot the coordinates of the two ships and the lifeboat on a rectangular coordinate system and label them accordingly

22. bakit hindi maituturing ba mabuting halimbawa ang lifeboat exercise kung iuugnay sa kasagraduhan ng buhay


Answer:

Ang lifeboat exercise ay hindi maituturing na mabuting halimbawa kung iuugnay sa kasagraduhan ng buhay sapagkat pinakita sa istorya na may mga maiisantabi na mga buhay dahil sa sitwasyon.

Ang lifeboat exercise ay mula sa aklat ni William Kirkpatrick. May isang barko na nasiraan at malapit nang lumubog sa kalagitnaan ng dagat.

Kaya naman, hinanda na ang mga lifeboat ng mga tauhan. Ngunit hindi sapat ang lifeboat upang maisakay ang lahat ng pasahero. Ang dilemma dito ay kung sino-sino ang sasakay at sino-sino ang maiiwan sa barko?

Batid naman natin na mahalaga ang buhay kahit ano pa man ang edad mo, ang trabaho mo, o ang posisyon mo sa lipunan. Sagrado ang buhay at kahit ano pa ang sitwasyon, dapat iyun ay pahalagahan at huwag maisantabi. Dapat protektahan ang buhay ninuman.
