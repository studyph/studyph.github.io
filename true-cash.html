6. The purchase of land is classified in the statement of cash flow of operating activities, true or false? ​


Answer:

True

Explanation:

Trust on this one. Promise

Answer:

False

Explanation:

Cash flow revolves around three activities. Operating activities, Investing activities, and Financing activities.Operating activities - involves the activities/transactions directly affecting the revenue-producing activity of the company/business.

In your question, "The purchase of land is classified in the statement of cash flow of operating activities". Land is a long-term asset (non-current asset) and it does not directly affect the revenue-producing activity of a business.

To conclude, the purchase of land is classified as Investing activities, not Operating activities, so it is FALSE.
