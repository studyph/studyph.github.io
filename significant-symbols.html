15. What is the significance of the Fish symbol? ​


Answer:

In latin Christian symbology, the fish is related to Christ. Jesus told Peter (the first apostle), a fisherman by trade, that he would become a "fisher of men" if he were to follow Christ. For its connection with Christ it has also come to embody resurrection and immortality, as well as the savior.

To the early Christian community, this signified, "Iesous Christos, Theou Yios, Soter," or in the English translation of the Greek, "Jesus Christ, Son of God, Savior." Several representations of the fish symbol can be found on the walls of the Catacombs.
