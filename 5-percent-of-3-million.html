The price of a kilo of galunggong increased from 73.00 to $85.00 per kilo. Find the percent of increase.

To find the percent of increase in the price of a kilo of galunggong, we need to divide the amount of the increase by the original price and multiply by 100%. The amount of the increase is 85.00 - 73.00 = <<85.00-73.00=12.00>>12.00. Dividing this by the original price of 73.00, we get 12.00 / 73.00 = 0.164. Multiplying this by 100%, we get 0.164 * 100% = 16.4%. Therefore, the percent of increase in the price of a kilo of galunggong is 16.4%.

There were 12 pupils in a Grade 6 class who failed in the first quarterly test. In the last quarterly test, only 5 failed. What is the percent of decrease in failure?

To find the percent of decrease in failure, we need to divide the decrease in the number of pupils who failed by the original number of pupils who failed and multiply by 100%. The decrease in the number of pupils who failed is 12 - 5 = <<12-5=7>>7. Dividing this by the original number of pupils who failed, we get 7 / 12 = 0.583. Multiplying this by 100%, we get 0.583 * 100% = 58.3%. Therefore, the percent of decrease in failure is 58.3%.

The price of a lot in an exclusive subdivision is P3.1 million for every 100 square meters. This year, the price has increased to $3.348 million. What is the percent of increase in the price?

To find the percent of increase in the price of a lot in the exclusive subdivision, we need to divide the amount of the increase by the original price and multiply by 100%. The amount of the increase is 3.348 - 3.1 = <<3.348-3.1=0.248>>0.248. Dividing this by the original price of 3.1, we get 0.248 / 3.1 = 0.08. Multiplying this by 100%, we get 0.08 * 100% = 8%. Therefore, the percent of increase in the price of a lot in the exclusive subdivision is 8%.

Your small intestine is about 20 ft long. Your large intestine is 75% shorter. How long is your large intestine?

If your large intestine is 75% shorter than your small intestine, this means that it is 75% * 20 ft = <<75*.01*20=15>>15 ft shorter. Subtracting this from the length of your small intestine, we get 20 ft - 15 ft = <<20-15=5>>5 ft. Therefore, your large intestine is 5 ft long.

Place value

We can write numbers in word form or in standard form.

Every digit in a number has a place value. The position of each number determines its value.

So by understanding the value of digits, the conversion of numbers in the form of words into numeric symbols is:

1. 200,100,620,261

2. 852,674.35

3. 40% of n = 240

4. [tex]\tt 3\dfrac{1}{2}[/tex]

5. 4:8

Further explanation

Each number can be expressed in digits. The number can have one or more than two digits.

The place values for the digits can be started from trillions, billions, millions thousands, and ones.

For the whole part: millions, hundred thousands, ten thousands, thousands, etc

For the decimal part: tenthts, hundredths, thousandth, etc

So the numbers can start, for example from hundreds trillions, tens trillions, trillions and etc.
