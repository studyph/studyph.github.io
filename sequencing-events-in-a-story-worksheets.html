2. Finding the nth term of an arithmetic sequence worksheet


aₙ = nth term

a₁ = first term

d = common difference

aₙ = a₁ + (n - 1)*d

The nth term is equal to the first term plus the product of the common difference and the number of term minus one.

4. Elements of a Short Story Worksheet Directions: Accomplish this worksheet to have an overall grasp of the short story THE LEGEND OF BANYUWANGI. Reading Report I. Title: II. Author: III.Setting: a. Place: b. Time: c. Weather: d. Social Conditions: e. Mood or atmosphere: IV. Plot: a. Exposition: b. Rising Action: c. Climax d. Falling Action: e. Resolution​


I. Title: The Legend of Banyuwangi
II. Author: Unknown/Anonymous
III. Setting:
a. Place: Banyuwangi, a regency located in the easternmost part of Java Island, Indonesia
b. Time: Not specified
c. Weather: Not specified
d. Social Conditions: The story revolves around the social hierarchy and the power struggle between the king, the prince, and the common people.
e. Mood or atmosphere: Mysterious, suspenseful, and mystical.
IV. Plot:
a. Exposition: The story begins by introducing the kingdom of Banyuwangi, ruled by a powerful king who has a son, the prince.
b. Rising Action: One day, the prince disappeared without a trace. The king searched for him but to no avail. The common people then started to believe that the prince had been taken by a mystical creature called Ratu Kidul, the queen of the southern sea.
c. Climax: The king ordered his soldiers to find and bring back the prince, but they failed. Then a young fisherman came forward and offered to help. He sailed to the southern sea and found the prince with the help of Ratu Kidul, who gave him a magic potion to protect him from the creature.
d. Falling Action: The fisherman brought the prince back to the kingdom, but the king was suspicious of the young man's story and ordered him to prove it. The fisherman then sailed to the southern sea once more and returned with a piece of coral from the depths of the sea.
e. Resolution: The king was convinced of the young man's bravery and rewarded him with a high position in the kingdom. The prince was restored to his rightful place, and the people of Banyuwangi lived in peace and prosperity.
