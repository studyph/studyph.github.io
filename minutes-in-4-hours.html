20. 4. Benigno can make 100 mango grahams in 4 hours and 15 minutes, while Alyanacan make the same number of mango grahams in 2 hours and 45 minutes. Howlong will it take for Benigno and Alyana to do the job if they work together?A. 1 hour & 38 minutesC. 1 hour & 42 minutesB. 1 hour & 40 minutesD. 1 hour & 44 minutes​


Answer:

It will take 1 hour and 42 minutes or the letter c to do the job if they work together.

Step-by-step explanation:

                              Word Problem

This is an example of a word problem that involves rational expression/equation. A rational expression is of the form [tex]\frac{p(x)}{q(x)}[/tex] where [tex]q(x)[/tex] is not zero.

We can translate each person's work into an expression. Benigno: Since he can work for 4 hours and 15 minutes, so we can express it as [tex]\begin{aligned}\frac{1}{t_1}\end{aligned}[/tex], where [tex]t_1[/tex] is the time it takes for him to finish the job or [tex]\begin{aligned}\frac{1}{4\:\text{hrs and }15\:\text{mins}}\end{aligned}[/tex].Alyana: Since she can word for 2 hours and 45 minutes, so we can express it as [tex]\frac{1}{t_2}[/tex], where [tex]t_2[/tex] is the time it takes for her to finish the job or [tex]\begin{aligned}\frac{1}{2\:\text{hrs and }45\:\text{mins}}\end{aligned}[/tex].

Solution:

We want to know how long it takes for them to work together. So, the equation should be [tex]\begin{aligned}\frac{1}{t_1}+\frac{1}{t_2}=\frac{1}{t_3}\end{aligned}[/tex] where [tex]\begin{aligned}t_3\end{aligned}[/tex] is the time it takes for them to work together.

For convenience, let's convert the time in terms of hours.

       Benigno:

                   [tex]\begin{aligned}4\:\text{hrs} +\frac{15}{60}\:\text{hrs}&=4\:\text{hrs}+0.25\:\text{hrs}\\&= 4.25 \:\text{hrs}\end{aligned}[/tex]

       Alyana

                  [tex]\begin{aligned}2\:\text{hrs} +\frac{45}{60}\:\text{hrs}&=2\:\text{hrs}+0.75\:\text{hrs}\\&= 2.75 \:\text{hrs}\end{aligned}[/tex]

We have the equation, [tex]\begin{aligned}\frac{1}{4.25}+\frac{1}{2.75}=\frac{1}{t_3}\end{aligned}[/tex].

Multiply both sides by the LCD. The LCD is [tex](4.25)(2.75)(t_3)[/tex].

      [tex]\begin{aligned}(4.25)(2.75)(t_3)\left(\frac{1}{4.25}+\frac{1}{2.75}\right)&=(4.25)(2.75)(t_3)\left(\frac{1}{t_3}\right)\\2.75t_3+4.25t_3&=11.6875\end{aligned}[/tex]

Combine like terms on the right side, then apply the inverse operation to solve for the value of [tex]t_3[/tex].

      [tex]\begin{aligned}7t_3&=11.6875\\\frac{7t}{7}&=\frac{11.6875}{7}\\t_3&=1.6696\\&\text{or}\\t_3&\approx{1.70}\end{aligned}[/tex]

This means to say that it will take both of them 1.70 hours to work together to make mango grahams.

   Conversion:

                    [tex]\begin{aligned}1.70\:\text{hrs}&=1\:\text{hrs}+0.70\times{60\:\text{mins}}\\&=1\:\text{hrs}+42\:\text{mins}\\&=1\:\text{hrs and }42\:\text{mins}\end{aligned}[/tex]

In conclusion, the answer is the letter c.
