The major scale is one of the most commonly used musical scales, especially in Western music. It is one of the diatonic scales. Like many musical scales, it is made up of seven notes: the eighth duplicates the first at double its frequency so that it is called a higher octave of the same note.

noun Music

11. 6. What instructions did Daedalus give Icarus before Icarus flew away?​


Answer:

Before the flight, Daedalus gave his young son Icarus instructions in how to fly. He warned him that if he flew too close to the sea, the spray would soak the feathers, making the wings useless. If, on the other hand, he flew too close to the sun, the heat would melt the wax and destroy the wings.
