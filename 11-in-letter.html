Step-by-step explanation:

THE ANSWER IS 2:11

A. 2:11

The ratio of the letter A to all the letters in the word MATHEMATICS is 2:11.

Step-by-step explanation:

Ratio

The representation of a comparison between things or items is known as a ratio. It indicates how many times one number contains another. To give the ratio of things or items, you simply have to count or give the number of things or items indicated in the ratio. Then, express them in any of the different ways to write ratio. Remember that you can express it in colon form, word form and fraction form.

Colon Form ( a:b )Word Form ( a to b )Fraction Form ( a/b )

Going back to the question above, you have to give the ratio of the letter A to all the letters in the word MATHEMATICS. To give the ratio, count the letter A and count all the letters in the word Mathematics.

letter A - 2

letters in the word Mathematics - 11

The ratio is 2:11.
