Before explaining, a reminder of the divisibility rules needed for this-

Divisibility Rules of:

2- The number should end with zero or an even number.

3- The sum of the digits of the number should be divisible by three.

6- The number should be divisible with two and three.

9- The sum of the digits of the number should be divisible by nine.

Explanation per number:

1. 10 and 44 are not divisible by either 3, 6, or 9, as the sum of the digits of 10 is 1, and the sum of the digits of 44 is 8, neither of which are divisible by 3, or 9, which also removes the possibility of 9.

2. 54 and 18 are divisible to all. The sum of the digits of 54 is 9 and the sum of the digits of 18 is also 9. The sums are divisible by both 3, and 9. Since both numbers end with an even number, it is also divisible by 2, which makes it, divisible by 6.

3. Both 72 and 36 are divisible by 3, 6, and 9. The sums of their digits are both 9. Again, this means it is divisible to 3, and 9. We already know it's divisible by 3, so we check with 2, and since it both ends with even numbers, it is divisible to 2. Therefore making it divisible to 6.

4. 45 and 90 are only divisible to 3 and 9. The sums of their digits are also 9, which makes it divisible to 3 and 9. Although 90 ends with an even number, 45 does not, which doesn't make 45 divisible to 2, which in turn, doesn't make 45 divisible to 6.
