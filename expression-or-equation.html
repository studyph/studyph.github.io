A mathematical expression can be as simple as 2 + 4 or as complex as -4xy + 8x- 5(x/y). All mathematical expressions contain terms, and each term has a coefficient, the numerical factor. Question: Is x=0 a solution of 4x+15= 3x+15 or not?

Answer: Yes it is a Solution

Reason:

when you substitue x=0, into the equation, you should get an answer that is equal.

so, when x=0

4x+15= 3x+15

4(0) + 15 = 3(0) + 15

0 + 15 = 0 + 15

15 = 15 --> True, hence x=0 is a Solution to the equation.
