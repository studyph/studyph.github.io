5. The perimeter of a square is 84 meters. How long is each side? a. 12 m b. 20 m c. 21 m d. 24 m


Let s be the length of each side of the square.

We know that the perimeter of a square is given by:

P = 4s

We are given that the perimeter is 84 meters, so we can write:

84 = 4s

Simplifying for s:

s = 84/4 = 21

Therefore, each side of the square is 21 meters long.

The answer is (c) 21 m.
