19. The area of a trapezoid is 84 square inches and the bases are 12 inches and 16 inches. Find the height of the trapezoid.​


Answer:

6 inches

Step-by-step explanation:

Area of trapezoid= (b1 + b2)h /2

84 in2=(12+16)h /2

2(84)=(12+16)h

168= (12+16)h

h=168/ (12+16)

h=168/28

h=6 in

Answer:

6 inches

Step-by-step explanation:

A=(a+b/2)h <-- area of trapezoid

using transposition we find the value for h

A=(a+b/2)h

multiply 2 to both sides so that the 2 will cancel on the right side

2A = (a+b)h

divide both sides by (a+b)

2A/(a+b) = h <-- this is the formula to get the height

Substitute the values given in the problem:

2(84)/(12+16) = h

168/28 = 6

6= h
