Recall that the perimeter of a rectangle is given by P=2l+2w.

Given that l=3w-10 with perimeter equal to P=76, then

76=2(3w-10)+2w

76=6w-20+2w

w=12.

26. In ΔIJK, the measure of ∠K=90°, the measure of ∠J=76°, and JK = 3.6 feet. Find the length of IJ to the nearest tenth of foot


Answer:

14.9 FEET

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

The length of side IJ of a triangle IJK

What are given?

Angle K = 90 degrees

Angle J = 76 degrees

Angle I = 180 - (90+76) = 14 degrees

Side JK = 3.6 feet

Side IJ =?

HOW TO SOLVE?

We are dealing with a right triangle, so we will be using trigonometric functions (we could use Pythagorean Theorem but angles are also given so we use a different method)

We will use angle J, the adjacent side JK and the unknown side (which is also the hypotenuse) IJ.

FORMULA & SOLUTION.

cosJ = adjacent/hypotenuse

cos76 = 3.6/IJ

IJ = 3.6 / sin76

IJ = 3.6 /0.241

IJ = 14.9

CONCLUSION:

Therefore, the side IJ measure 14.9 feet.
