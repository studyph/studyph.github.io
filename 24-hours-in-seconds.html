3. how to convert 24 hours to seconds


Using the conversion factor (C.F.).

C.F.:
60 sec = 1 min
60 mins = 1 hr

EQUATION:
24 hrs to sec

SOLUTION:
[tex]24hr \times \frac{60mins}{1hr} \times \frac{60sec}{1min} [/tex]
[tex] = 86400sec[/tex]

48 x 60 = 2,880
