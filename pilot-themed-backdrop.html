14. which elements of drama refers to the technical equipment such as curtains, flats,backdrops,etc​


The elements of drama that refers to the technical equipment such as curtains, flats, backdrops, etc. is called "scenery" or "production design." Scenery and production design help to create the visual world of a play, and they are crucial in establishing the mood, setting, and atmosphere of a performance. They can also serve as visual cues for the audience, helping to identify changes in location and time. Effective scenery and production design can greatly enhance the overall impact of a theatrical performance.
