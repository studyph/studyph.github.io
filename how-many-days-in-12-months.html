12. 1. How many minutes are there in 3 hours? 2. A man stays awake for 3 days. How many hours is this? 3. How many seconds are there in 2 ½ minutes? 4. 540 minutes = hours 5. 12 weeks days 6. How many seconds are in 1 hour? 7. 7.7 years = months 8. 8. 8 months=_______ weeks ​


Answer:

There are 180 minutes in 3 hours.

3 days is equal to 72 hours (since there are 24 hours in a day).

There are 150 seconds in 2 ½ minutes (since 1 minute is equal to 60 seconds, so 2.5 x 60 = 150).

540 minutes is equal to 9 hours (since there are 60 minutes in 1 hour, so 540 ÷ 60 = 9).

12 weeks is equal to 84 days (since there are 7 days in 1 week, so 12 x 7 = 84).

There are 3,600 seconds in 1 hour (since 1 hour is equal to 60 minutes and 1 minute is equal to 60 seconds, so 60 x 60 = 3,600).

7.7 years is equal to 92.4 months (since there are 12 months in 1 year, so 7.7 x 12 = 92.4).

8 months is equal to 32 weeks (since there are 4 weeks in 1 month, so 8 x 4 = 32).

2.Three days is equivalent to 72 hours.
3.There are 150 seconds in 2 ½ minutes.
540 minutes is equal to 9 hours.
4.There are 84 days in 12 weeks.
5.There are 3600 seconds in 1 hour.
6. 7.7 years is equivalent to 92.4 months.
8 months is equal to 34.67 weeks (rounded to two decimal places).
