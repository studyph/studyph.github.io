20. A man left p5,000 to his three sons. For every peso Arlie received, Benreceived p1.50 and Carlos received p2.50. How much money was left toBen?​


Answer:

Ben received P1500.00

Step-by-step explanation:

This problem can be expressed as a linear equation.

Let x be the amount of money, in pesos, that Arlie received, therefore:

Arlie received x amount of money

Ben received 1.5x amount of money

Carlos received 2.5x amount of money.

The total amount of money is P5000, so

x + 1.5x + 2.5x = 5000

5x = 5000

Divide both sides by 5

(5x)/5 = 5000/5

x = 1000

We said earlier that Ben received 1.5x amount of money so now that we know the value of x is equal to 1000, therefore 1.5 multiplied to 1000 is equal to 1500.

Therefore Ben received P1500 amount of money.
