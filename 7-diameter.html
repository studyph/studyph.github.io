So, since 1 nanometer = 0.0000001, and the problem is asking what is the diameter of the virus when converting it from 20 nanometers to centimeters, we need to multiply:

[tex] \tt d = \sqrt{173} [/tex]

[tex] \tt d = 13.15[/tex]
