How Antivirus WorksAntivirus software scans a file, program, or an application and compares a specific set of code with information stored in its database. 13. How does the computer viruses differ from antivirus? ​


Answer:

Antivirus usually deals with the older, more established threats, such as Trojans, viruses, and worms. Anti-malware, by contrast, typically focuses on newer stuff, such as polymorphic malware and malware delivered by zero-day exploits. Antivirus protects users from lingering, predictable-yet-still-dangerous malware.

29. What's the purpose of antivirus?


The Purpose of Antivirus

As the name implies, the purpose of antivirus is to provide protection against virus and any other malware. Antivirus is a security software that scans and detects any kind of virus or infections that may cause harm to the computer system. Further, it serves as a line of defense against malware by destroying, attacking, and removing these threats and infections.
