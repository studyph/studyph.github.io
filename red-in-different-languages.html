Vietnamese

The French conquest of Vietnam began in 1858 and finished in 1884. Vietnam became part of French Indochina in 1887. Vietnam declared its independence after World War II, but France remained in power until it was defeated by the Communists led by Ho Chi Minh in 1954.

Cambodia

Laos
