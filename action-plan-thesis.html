11. explaination of the roodmap is a plan that follows the thesis statement ​


A roadmap in academic writing refers to a plan or outline that follows the thesis statement. It serves as a guide for the writer to structure their paper and present their arguments in a clear and organized manner. The roadmap typically includes the main points or sub-topics that the writer will discuss in their paper, along with the supporting details or evidence that will be presented for each point. This helps the reader to understand the overall structure of the paper and how each point is related to the thesis statement. The roadmap can also be used as a checklist for the writer to ensure that they have covered all the necessary points in their paper.
