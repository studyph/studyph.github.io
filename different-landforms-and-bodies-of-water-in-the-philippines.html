7. describe the different natural resources in benguet such as landforms,bodies of water flaura and fauna​


NATURAL RESOURCES

Question:

Describe the different natural resources in Benguet such as landforms, bodies of water, flora, and fauna.

Answer:

Benguet is a province in the Philippines known for its rich natural resources. It contains many unique landforms such as volcanic formations, peaks and mountaintops, limestone deposits, hot springs, and more. Its bodies of water include several rivers like Bued River, Acupan River and Tubao River; lakes including Tabeo Lake and Bu-os Lake; numerous creeks; two large dams - San Roque Dam and Ambuklao Dam – which produce hydroelectric power; ponds.; swamps., coastal lands with mangroves along shorelines.. When it comes to flora & fauna native to the area , visitors can spot Philippine flatwoods dominated trees species ; endemic lizards ; various fish due to abundant river channels in this region; wild boars roaming around nearby mountains ranges . Among birds there are some that migrate from neighbouring places but most commonly found birds here usually upland game bird families especially eagles variety like Philippine eagle ( pithecophaga jefferyi ).
