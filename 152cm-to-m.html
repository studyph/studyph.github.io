Question

A desk is a 46 cm wide and 152 cm long. It is 91cm above the floor. What is the volume of space under the desk?​

Given:

L = 152 cm

W = 46 cm

H = 91 cm

Formula:

V = L · W · H

Solution:

V = 152 · 46 · 91

V = 636272 cm³
