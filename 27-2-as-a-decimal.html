9. determine the digit 100 places to the right of the decimal point in the representation 2/27​


Answer:

The 100 places to the right of the decimal point in the representation 2/27​ is  0.

Step-by-step explanation:

The method applied to determine the digit 100 places to the right of the decimal point in the representation 2/27​ is  the following

1. Divide number 2/27. We will get  0.0740740740740741  

2.Then we will observe that '074' (3 digits) get repeated after the decimal point again and again in number  0.0740740740740741  

4.We want the 100th places, so we take the closest multiple of 3 in order to reach the start of the pattern again.

 here closest multiple of 3 is 96 .

So 100 = 96 + 4 = 3 x 32 + 4 (3 is repeating digit ,so divide 96 by 3 we will get 32 and when we will multiply 3*32 = 96 and add  96 + 4 , we will get 100).  

So the 96th decimal will be  4 ( 3rd digit of 0.0740740740740741)

Followed by the 97th = 0, 98th = 7, 99th = 4,

so the 100th decimal will be a 0.
