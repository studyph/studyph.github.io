7. The length of a rectangle is 52 cm and its perimeters is 200 cm. What is the area of the rectangle?


Answer:

the area of the rectangle is 2,496 cm^2

Step-by-step explanation:

A RECTANGLE is a quadrilateral with 2 pairs of opposite sides equal, and 4 right angles (90 degrees).

The GENERAL FORMULA IN FINDING FOR THE AREA OF ANY 4 SIDED POLYGON is

A = Length x Width

This is also where the formula of the area of the square is derived.

Area of square = side x side

That is the length and the width of a square are equal. So they are referred as side.

The SUM OF THE SIDES OF ANY POLYGON is also called the "perimeter".

So the formula in finding for the perimeter of a rectangle is

P = L + L + W + W

P = 2L + 2W

The problem states that the length of the rectangle is 52cm, thus the unknown is its width (W). Furthermore, the problem states the perimeter of the rectangle which is 200cm, and asks for its area.

So the perimeter of the rectangle in formula form is

P = 2L + 2W

200cm = (2)(52cm) + (2)(W)

Simplifying the right side by multiplying 2 to 52cm, we have

200cm = 104cm + 2W

Transposing 104cm to the left, we have

200cm - 104cm = 2W

Further simplifying, we have

96cm = 2W

W = 48 cm

Thus, the width of the rectangle is 48cm. We can now find for its area.

A = Length x Width

A = (52cm)(48cm)

A = 2,496 cm^2

Therefore, the area of the rectangle is 2,496 cm^2.

11. the length of a rectangle is 52 cm and its perimeter is 200 cm. What is the area of the rectangle ​


Answer:

2496 square centimeters

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's start by using the formula for the perimeter (P) of a rectangle, which is:

P = 2l + 2w

where l is the length and w is the width.

We are given that the length of the rectangle is 52 cm, and the perimeter is 200 cm. Plugging in these values, we get:

200 = 2(52) + 2w

Simplifying the right side of the equation, we get:

200 = 104 + 2w

Subtracting 104 from both sides, we get:

96 = 2w

Dividing both sides by 2, we get:

w = 48

So the width of the rectangle is 48 cm.

Now, we can use the formula for the area (A) of a rectangle, which is:

A = lw

Plugging in the values we know, we get:

A = (52)(48)

Simplifying, we get:

A = 2496

Therefore, the area of the rectangle is 2496 square centimeters
