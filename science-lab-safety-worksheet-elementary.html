1. Which scenario shows responsible behavior with regard to lab safety?1. Nathan threw out his lab safety instructional manual after reading it.2. Jessica and Elizabeth ate their lunch and evening snacks in the lab area.3. Haley poured multiple tubes of poisonous chemicals down the lab sink.4. Adam immediately cleaned up the fluids he had spilled in the lab area.(Earth Science)​


ANSWER:

Adam immediately cleaned up the fluid he had spilled in the lab area (option 4)

EXPLANATION:

If there is a spill in the lab, you should clean it, but also do not forget to also follow the safety precautions, especially with chemical spills. Spills can cause injury, like someone might slip on it or you might slip on it. It will also prevent contamination of other chemicals. Some chemicals can become volatile when mixed with other substances.

As for the other options. You should keep the manual even after reading as reference. NEVER eat in the lab. You may never know what you might ingest as well. All chemicals have guidelines when it comes to disposal. Never throw them all down the sink.

21. basic safety precautions when working in a computer lab


Enumerated below are examples of safety precautions that could be implemented when working in Computer Laboratories:

1. Remove metals such as watches and jewelries when you are going to hold computer parts and switch. Also, do not wear loose clothes.

2. Make sure that your hands are dry before you touch the power button or power on the computer.

3. Avoid bringing foods and drinks inside the laboratory.

4. Check your assigned computer terminal before you use it. When you notice a missing part or damaged computer part, call the attention of your teacher or instructor.

5. Sit only to your designated computer terminal.

6. Avoid using USBs as it may contain malware that may damage or delete your files. Seek permission first from your teacher or instructor if you really need to use USBs.

7. Avoid browsing prohibited websites while you are inside the laboratory.
