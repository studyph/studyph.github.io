1. what are the diffirent report format for hotel for arrival and departure


Answer:

The difference between the two reports is the default value preloaded into Report Type. The arrivals report defaults to Arrivals. The departures report defaults to Departures. These reports list folios within a selected date range, and they can be limited to either arrivals, departures, or those with occupancy.
