The film opens with Andy's trial, where he is convicted and sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary. As he adjusts to life in prison, Andy befriends fellow inmate Red. While The Shawshank Redemption is fiction, it is based on real-life experiences that many people can relate to.
