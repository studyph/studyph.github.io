15. Typhoon winds spiral in ____ direction. A.Clockwise in the northern hemisphere B.Clockwise in the easthern hemisphere C.Counterclockwise in the Northern Hemisphere D.Counterclockwise in the Southern hemisphere


The correct answer is C.

The wind spirals in a counter-clockwise direction going towards the center of the typhoon when it is in the Northern hemisphere.

However, the complete opposite is true for typhoons occurring in the Southern hemisphere because the direction would be clockwise towards the center of the typhoon.

22. Why is the soutern hemisphere experiencing summer when it is winter in the northern hemisphere? The southern hemisphere is


Answer:

The Earth's tilt causes the Southern Hemisphere (SH) to lean towards the Sun during SH summer. Meanwhile, it is winter in the Northern Hemisphere (NH) which leans away from the Sun. Six months later, the situation is reversed.
