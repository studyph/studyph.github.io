7. Guide Questions 1. What is the role of restriction enzymes in Recombinant DNA Technology? 2. What is the function of the DNA Ligase? 3. What is a Plasmid?​


Answer:

1. A restriction enzyme is an enzyme isolated from bacteria that cuts DNA molecules at specific sequences.

2. DNA ligases play an essential role in maintaining genomic integrity by joining breaks in the phosphodiester backbone of DNA that occur during replication and recombination, and as a consequence of DNA damage and its repair.

3. A plasmid is a small, extrachromosomal DNA molecule within a cell that is physically separated from chromosomal DNA and can replicate independently.
