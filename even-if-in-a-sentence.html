8. complex sentence even if ​


Sorry, But the question doesn't make sense

I'll just put random info about complex sentence

Complex sentence- It has one independent clause and IT atleast has 1 dependent clause

Independent clause- An independent clause is something that has a subject and a predicate, and it is a sentence that can stand on its own. An independent clause expresses or conveys the full thought or motive.

Ex.

Tom is reading.

I will rip and tear.

Dependent clause- A dependent clause is something that cant stand on its own, it is not and sentence and it can't convey or express a complete thought. It is usually connected to an independent clause and starts with marker words or subordinating conjunctions like because, after, before since and etc.

Ex.

When we arrived home

Until it is done

Combining an independent clause with a dependent clause makes a 'complex sentence'. Both clauses are usually connected through marker words or subordinating conjunctions.

Ex of a complex sentence:

I rip and tear, until it is done.

Jimmy was running away from the police, because he broke the law

Spongebob defeated Goku , because Spongebob can warp reality.

Even and Odd Numbers

A number which is divisible by 2 and generates a remainder of 0 is called an even number. An odd number is a number which is not divisible by 2. The remainder in the case of an odd number is always “1”.

The property by which we classify an integer in math as even or odd is also known as parity.  

Identifying even or odd number

1. By comprehending the number at “ones” place

In this approach, we analyze the number at “ones” place in an integer to check if the number is even or odd.  

All the numbers ending with 1,3,5,7 and 9 are odd numbers. For example, numbers such as 11, 23, 35, 47 etc. are odd numbers.

All the numbers ending with 0,2,4,6 and 8 are even numbers. For example, numbers such as 14, 26, 32, 40 and 88 are even numbers.
