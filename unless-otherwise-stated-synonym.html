1. The _________, unless otherwise stated, is an annual rate.


Rate of Annual Percentage

It is a percentage that expresses the actual annual cost of borrowing money throughout the course of a loan or the revenue from an investment.

Unless otherwise specified, the annual interest rate is charged at an annual rate.

An annual percentage rate (APR) is the yearly rate charged for a loan or earned by an investment.The annual rate charged for a loan or earned by an investment is known as an annual percentage rate (APR).Prior to any contract being executed, financial institutions are required to disclose the (APR) of a financial instrument.To shield customers from deceptive advertising, the APR offers an uniform foundation for providing annual interest rate information.Since lenders have some discretion in determining the APR, which excludes some costs, it is possible that it may not accurately reflect the cost of borrowing.Annual percentage yield, a figure that takes interest compounding into account, should not be confused with APR.

The yearly cost of the loan that a bank fees, given as a percentage, is the stated interest rate. Other fees related to borrowing the money are not factored into this interest rate.

14. Newton's first law of motion state that an object's motion will not change unless ​


Answer:

Newton's first law of motion states that an object's motion will not change unless an unbalanced force acts on the object. If the object is at rest, it will stay at rest. If the object is in motion, it will stay in motion. Inertia is the tendency of an object to resist a change in its motion.
