QUESTION:

What is m∠2, if m∠7 = 102?

Choices:

788892102

Notice m∠2 and m∠7 are same exterior angles. Same exterior angles are exterior angles on the same side of a transversal. These angles are supplementary.

Since these 2 angles are supplementary, let's equate them to 180.

[tex]\underline{\bold{Solution:}}[/tex]

[tex]\tt{x+102=180}\\\tt{x=180-102}\\\tt{x=78}[/tex]

The value of m∠2 is 78.

Answer: B. 88.32°

Step-by-step explanation:

Using "TOA" from "SOH-CAH-TOA", and creating a right triangle with 850 ft  (Opposite) and 25 ft (Adjacent). . .

Let x = measure of the angle

tan(x) = opp/adj

tan(x) = 850/25

tan(x) = 34

tan^-1(34) = x

x = 88.3151° ≈ 88.32° // (letter B)

29. suppose the rectangular table has an area of 88 M² and a perimeter of 38 M what will be its dimensions?​


Answer:

Length = 11m

Width = 8m

Step-by-step explanation:

Area = Length × Width

Possible dimensions are 11m and 8m

Substitute

A = LW

88m² = 11m × 8m is true

Perimeter of a rectangle is equal to 2L + 2W

Substitute

P = 2L + 2W

38 = 2 (11) + 2 (8)

38 = 22 + 16

38 = 38


30. <1 and <2 are supplementary angles. if m<1=82°, what is m<2? A. 8°B. 108°C. 88°D. 98°​


Answer:

D. 98

Step-by-step explanation:

Supplementary angles are angles that add up to 180°. Therefore, we can find the missing measurement by simply subtracting the given measurement from 180. 

180-82= 98
