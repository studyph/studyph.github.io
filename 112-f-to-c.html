12. Convert the following from Celsius (°C) to Fahrenheit (°F) and vice-versa. Use the formula above. Show your computations at the back of your activity sheet. 1. 60 °C 2. 112 °C 3. 10 °C 4.110 °C5. 45 °C 6.65 °F 7.59 °F 8.149 °F 9.86 °F 10.217 °F plss po need kona po to buwas ng amaga​


Answer:

To convert Celsius (°C) to Fahrenheit (°F), use the formula:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

To convert Fahrenheit (°F) to Celsius (°C), use the formula:

°C = (°F − 32) × 5/9

Using these formulas, we can convert the given temperatures as follows:

60 °C

°F = (60 × 9/5) + 32 = 140 °F

112 °C

°F = (112 × 9/5) + 32 = 233.6 °F

10 °C

°F = (10 × 9/5) + 32 = 50 °F

110 °C

°F = (110 × 9/5) + 32 = 230 °F

45 °C

°F = (45 × 9/5) + 32 = 113 °F

65 °F

°C = (65 − 32) × 5/9 = 18.33 °C

59 °F

°C = (59 − 32) × 5/9 = 15 °C

149 °F

°C = (149 − 32) × 5/9 = 65 °C

86 °F

°C = (86 − 32) × 5/9 = 30 °C

217 °F

°C = (217 − 32) × 5/9 = 102.78 °C
