19. computer system error in hardware and software


Computer system errors are because of malfunction that is happening on the computers ‘software or hardware. When something is wrong to the computer, it does not function well or commonly, it displays error on the screen.

Below are some of the Errors that we see in computer for both hardware and software:

No Video Output – nothing shows up on the screen and the light indicator on the monitor is flashing amber / yellow. Computer won’t boot up – Computer is just on the screen where it shows the processor brand “windows”. It is just stuck on that screen and will not move forward. Sound off/on Error – when you load the computer you will hear a sound but when you open an audio file, you can’t hear anything. Runtime Errors – caused by a malfunctioning or corrupted software executable files. It causes the application to close, or sometimes, it shuts down the computer and make it frozen.

24. define and describe computer hardware and software​


Answer:

HARDWARE refers to the physical elements of a computer. This is also sometime called the machinery or the equipment of the computer. Examples of hardware in a computer are the keyboard, the monitor, the mouse and the central processing unit. However, most of a computer's hardware cannot be seen; in other words, it is not an external element of the computer, but rather an internal one, surrounded by the computer's casing (tower). SOFTWARE is the programs and routines for a computer or the program material for an electronic device which make it run. An example of software is Excel or Windows or iTunes.
