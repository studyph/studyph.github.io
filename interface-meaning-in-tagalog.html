1. As used in the law of reflection the word normal means __________. A. any ray B.tangent to the interface C.parallel to the interface D.perpendicular to the interface


Answer:

D.perpendicular to the interface

Explanation:

The normal is a line that divides the angle between the incident ray and the reflected ray into two equal angles. Thus forming a 90⁰ angle to the flat interface or flat reflective surface.

16. what is the difference between Northbridge Interface and South bridge Interface?​

The main difference between northbridge and southbridge is that the northbridge is a chip in the chipset of a motherboard that directly connects to the CPU while the southbridge is a chip in the chipset of a motherboard that does not directly connects to the CPU.
