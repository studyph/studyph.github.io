1. w = 8 feet, x = 6 feet, y = 12 feet, and z = 11 feet, what is the area of the object? A. 72 square feet B. 46 square feet C. 92 square feet D. 82 square feet


Answer:

D. 82 square feet

Given:

rectangle

length = 12 ft

width = 6 ft

triangle

b = 4 ft

h = 5 ft

A = A_rectangle + A_triangle

= (l × w) + (1/2bh)

= (12 ft × 6 ft ) + [1/2(4 ft)(5 ft)]

= 72 ft² + [1/2(20 ft²)]

= 72 ft² + 10 ft²

A = 82 ft²

3. The perimeter of a rectangular concrete slab is 82 feet and its area is 364 sq feet. What is the length of the longer side of the slab


perimeter = 82 ft

2l + 2w = 82

l + w = 41

l = 41 - w

area = 364 ft²

lw = 364

(41 - w)(w) = 364

41w - w² = 364

0 = w² - 41w + 364

0 = (w - 13)(w - 28)

w = 13, 28

The longer side of the slab is 28 feet long.

24. the distance required to stop a car varies directly as the square of its speed. if 200 feet are required to stop a car traveling 70 miles per hour how many feet are required to stop a car traveling 82 miles per hour?


Answer:

Let:

s=distance in feet

v=speed in miles per hour

Thus:

s=kv²

where k= proportionality constant

k=s/v²

Hence:

k=200/70²

Therefore:

At 82 mph speed

The required distance to stop the car would be:

s=kv²

=200(82²)/70²

=200(1681/1225)

=274/22/49 feet

=274.4490 feet.
