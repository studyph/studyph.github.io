17. Quadrilateral abcd is inscribed on the circle solve for angle d if arc adc measures 210°​.

[tex] \underline{\mathbb{PROBLEM:}} [/tex]

Quadrilateral ABCD is inscribed on the circle solve for angle D if arc ADC measures 210°.

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex] \underline{\mathbb{ANSWER:}} [/tex]

[tex] \qquad\Large\rm» \:\: \green{m\angle D = 75 \degree} [/tex]

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex] \underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION:}} [/tex]

» One circle theorem suggests that if a quadrilateral is inscribed into the circle, then one of its interior angles is supplementary to its opposite angle.

[tex] \rm m\angle D + m\angle B = 180 \degree[/tex][tex] \rm m\angle A + m\angle C = 180 \degree[/tex]

» The Intercepted arc ADC inscribes the angle B. Find the measure of angle B.

[tex] \rm m\angle B = \frac12(m\overset{\frown}{ADC}) \\ [/tex]

[tex] \rm m\angle B = \frac12(210 \degree) \\ [/tex]

[tex] \rm m\angle B = 105 \degree[/tex]

» Thus, the measure of angle B is 105 degrees. Now find the measure of angle D.

[tex] \rm m\angle D + 105 \degree = 180 \degree[/tex]

[tex] \rm m\angle D = 180 \degree - 105 \degree[/tex]

[tex] \rm m\angle D = 75 \degree[/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] The measure of angle D is 75 degrees.
