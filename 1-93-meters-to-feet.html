A triangular face of the roof of the garage with two sides, StartRoot 93 EndRoot feet in length and a base of length StartRoot 186 EndRoot feet.

Now consider,

Case I

93² + 186²

= 8649 + 34596

= 43245

√ 43245 = 207. 95

Not a perfect square

Case II

186² - 93²

= 34596 - 8649

= 25947

√ 25947 = 161.08

Not a perfect square
