15. if 160 is divided into two parts in the ratio of 7:9 what are the numbers?A. 60 and 100B. 70 and 90C. 80 and 80D. 110 and 50​


Question:

If 160 is divided into two parts in the ratio of 7:9 what are the numbers?

Answer:

Let us find out so that we can wisely decide which is the correct answer for this question.

Now, since we have to use the method called "Partitive Proportion" for this, then we have to do the solution for this question.

Step 1: Add the ratio.

7 + 9 = 16

Step 2: Divide the sum to the whole unit.

160 ÷ 16 = 10

Step 3: Multiply the product to the given ratio.

7 × 10 = 70

9 × 10 = 90

Rewriting this, it will give us 70:90.

Therefore, the final answer is letter B, which is 70 and 90.
