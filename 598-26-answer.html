Answer:

To find the nearest hundreds of a number, we must first determine the tens digit.

If the tens digit is 0-4, the hundreds digit will stay the same.

If the tens digit is 5-9, the hundreds digit will increase by 1.

The tens and ones digit will change to 0.

So the answer is 600.
