20. An elevator is situated at the 50th floor of a 90 storey building.The following movements of the elevator are recorded:rises 10 floorsrises 8 floorsrises 17 floorsdrop 33 floorsdrops 9 floorsand rises 18 floorsWhat is the precint location of the elevator?​


Answer:

61th floor.

Step-by-step explanation:

The elevator is situated in 50th floor, so we will start at 50.

Rises means 'Add'.

Drop means 'Subtract'.

Rises 10 floor.

(50+10=60)

Rises 8 floor.

(60+8=68)

Rises 17 floor.

(68+17=85)

Drop 33 floor.

(85-33=52)

Drop 9 floor.

(52-9=43)

Rises 18 floor.

(43+18=61)

Answer:

The present location of elevator is on 61st floor.

Step-by-step explanation:

Rise ⇒ (+)

Drop ⇒  (-)

Begin on 50th floor.

Equation:

50 + (+10) + (+8) + (+17) + (-33) + (-9) + (+18) = Present floor of elevator

Combine the like signs:

50 + (+10) + (+8) + (+17) + (+18) + (-33) + (-9)

As a  rule in integer, add the common signs and subtract the unlike signs. For final results, copy the common sign for like sign addends and copy the sign of the larger number for unlike sign addends.

(+103) + (-42) = +61

The present location of elevator is on 61st floor.

22. If you drop your soap in the floor is the floor clean but the soap dirty?​


ANSWER:

If you drop soap on the floor, not only is the floor still dirty, it's also slippery. Soap doesn't magically remove dirt. It simply loosens dirt from whatever surface it's stuck to, so that it can be washed away. Without water, soap is useless.Instead, put it in the washing machine. If the soap has visible dirt on it, spray it off with a heavy jet of water. After that, all that will be left on the soap is microbacteria and other such dirt. DO NOT touch it with your bare hands.
