30. what is the gcf of 12 18 24 and 42​


Answer:

GCF = 6

LCM = 1008

Step-by-step explanation:

Ok so, 12, 18, 24, and 42 are all divisible to 2.

Now, we divide them with 2

12 / 2 = 6

18 / 2 = 9

24 / 2 = 12

42 / 2 = 21

So we get, 6, 9, 12, and 21, and they are divisible to 3.

Now, we divide them with 3

6 / 3 = 2

9 / 3 = 3

12 / 3 = 4

21 / 3 = 7

So we get, 2, 3, 4, and 7

They are only divisible to one plus, we can't divide them with any number.

Now, to find the GCF, we multiply 2 with 3.

2 [tex]*[/tex] 3 = 6

6 is the GCF

To find the LCM, we multiply all of them

[tex]2 * 3 * 2 * 3 * 4 * 7[/tex]

2 [tex]*[/tex] 3 = 6

6 [tex]*[/tex] 2 = 12

12 [tex]*[/tex] 3 = 36

36 [tex]*[/tex]4 = 144

144 [tex]*[/tex]7 = 1008

So, we are done!
