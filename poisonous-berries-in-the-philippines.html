29. what are the poisonous plants plants founfd in the philippines?


The Philippines is an archipelago rich in different kinds of flora. There are many poisonous plants found in the Philippines. Listed below are some of their examples:

Buta-buta - poisonous sap/latexTalumpunay/Cachubong - hallucinogenic/toxic leafDieffenbachia - plant leaves are highly toxic when chewedRosary Pea - fruits and seeds can cause burns and are highly toxic when eatenCastor plant - plant sap/oils are highly irritating and toxicWolfsbane - poisonous when eatenEnglish Yew - toxic fruitsBelladonna - all parts are toxic especially when eatenTrompeta - highly fatal leaves especially to little childrenPong-pong - highly toxic, especially the sap/oil

These are just examples of the common poisonous plants in the Philippines. There are many more.
