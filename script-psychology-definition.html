1. definition of psychology


Psychology is a study in Science that deals with the human mind. In Psychology, it studies how each individual person has a distinct set of characteristics, personality, traits, behavior, and mental thinking. In Psychology, they observe patterns which they find common with people, classify it, and use it for better understanding of the human mind’s development in different lives, age, environments, etc. Psychology allows people to have a deeper understanding of what goes through the thoughts of a person, deconstruct it, and explain it so that the person’s mind will be resolved.

29. Cyclothymia definition psychology


Answer:

Cyclothymia (sy-kloe-THIE-me-uh), also called cyclothymic disorder, is a rare mood disorder. Cyclothymia causes emotional ups and downs, but they're not as extreme as those in bipolar I or II disorder. With cyclothymia, you experience periods when your mood noticeably shifts up and down from your baseline.
