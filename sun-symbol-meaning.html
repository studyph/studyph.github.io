A horizontal blue stripe symbolize peace, truth, and justice and a horizontal red stripe for patriotism and valor. In the center of the triangle is a golden-yellow sun with eight primary rays, each rays represents a Philippine province. At each vertex of the triangle is a five-pointed anf golden-yellow star, each of it represents one of the country's three main island groups which is; Luzon, Visayas (though originally referring to Panay) and Mindanao.
