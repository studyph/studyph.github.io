Answer:

1. The world needs global health initiatives to address global health challenges and ensure that all individuals, regardless of their geographical location or socio-economic status, have access to quality healthcare services and interventions.

2. Global health initiatives can improve lives of people around the globe by reducing the burden of diseases, promoting health equity, and increasing access to healthcare services, medicines and technologies. These initiatives can also strengthen health systems and enhance the capacity of countries to respond to health emergencies and pandemics.

3. It is important for a student to know the different health initiatives because health is a fundamental human right and an important aspect of overall well-being. As future leaders and decision-makers, students have a critical role to play in shaping the global health agenda and advocating for policies and actions that promote health for all.

4. As a student, it is important to follow and engage in the implementation of health initiatives because it is a collective responsibility to ensure the health and well-being of all individuals. Students can contribute by raising awareness, advocating for policies and actions, and participating in community-based health programs and initiatives.
