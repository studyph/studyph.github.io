11. why is feedback inhibition necessary for controlling anabolic pathways​


Answer:

Feedback inhibition occurs when the end product of a reaction interferes with the enzyme that helped produce it. The inhibitor does this by binding to a second active binding site that's different from the one attached to the initial reactant. The enzyme then changes its shape and can't catalyze the reaction anymore. This type of inhibition is done as a regulatory mechanism to meet the metabolic needs of the cell or organism.

14. 1. used to acquire data and feedback in manufacturing for process control​


Answer:

Statistical Process Control (SPC)

Explanation:

Statistical Process Control (SPC) is used to acquire data and feedback in manufacturing for process control. SPC is a method of using statistical techniques to monitor and control a process. It involves collecting data on a process and using statistical tools such as control charts to identify patterns or variations in the data. These patterns or variations can then be used to identify potential problems and make adjustments to the process to improve quality and efficiency. SPC is widely used in manufacturing to improve product quality, reduce costs, and increase productivity by providing real-time feedback on a process.
