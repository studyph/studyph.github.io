1. 2. If after a massage an old lady had bruises on her arms, what area ofendangerment has the therapist has massaged?A. Medial BrachiumB. Cubital FossaC. Popliteal FossaD. Femoral Triangle​


Answer

Going by the Definitions in the explanation, the Answers is Letter A.

Explanation

If after a massage an old lady had bruises on her arms, what area of endangerment has the therapist has massaged?

A. Medial Brachium - The Brachium is the upper segment of the arm or forelimb extending from the shoulder to the elbow. Brachium is Latin for Upper Arm.

B. Cubital Fossa - The cubital fossa is the area of transition between the anatomical arm and the forearm.

C. Popliteal Fossa - The Popliteal Fossa is the diamond-shaped space behind the knee joint.

D. Femoral Triangle​ - The femoral triangle is the hollow region located in the superomedial part of the anterior(inward) thigh.

3. there is only one nasal cavity, which is divided by nasal septum into two nasal fossae? true or false??​


true

hope it helps you :)

Answer:

True

Explanation:

The nasal cavity is a large, air-filled space above and behind the nose in the middle of the face. The nasal septum divides the cavity into two cavities, also known as fossae. Each cavity is the continuation of one of the two nostrils.
