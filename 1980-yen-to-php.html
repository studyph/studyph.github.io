16. Yen is 10 years older than Eisen. In 4 years, Yen will be twice as old as Eisen. Find their both present age.​


Answer:

Eisen is 6 years old while Yen is 16 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Present ages:

Eisen: xYen: x + 10

Ages in 4 years:

Eisen: x + 4Yen: (x+10) + 4  or x+14

Equation: In 4 years, Yen will be twice as old as Eisen.

x + 14 = 2(x+4)

x + 14 = 2x + 8

2x - x = 14 - 8

x = 6

Present ages:  Subsitute 6 to x.

Eisen: x = 6 years old

Yen: (6) + 10 = 16 years old

Check:

In 4 years, Yen will be twice as old as Eisen.

(16+4) = 2(6+4)

20 = 2(10)

20 = 20  (True)
