6. The width of a rectangular lot is 3 meters less than its length, if the area of thelot is at most 54 square meters, what could be the dimension of the lot?​


9 meters by 6 meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

First, translate the word problem into an equation by using variables.

Let x be the length. If x is the length, then the width is x - 3. The area of the rectangular lot is mentioned to be 54.

By applying the formula for the area of a rectangle,

[tex]a = lw[/tex]

a = 54, l = x, w = x - 3. So, we substitute these quantities into the formula.

[tex]54 = x(x - 3)[/tex]

Distribute x to the parenthesis.

[tex]x(x - 3) = 54[/tex]

[tex] {x}^{2} - 3x = 54[/tex]

Rewrite the quadratic equation into its standard form.

[tex] {x}^{2} - 3x - 54 = 0[/tex]

Factor the left hand side.

[tex](x - 9)(x + 6) = 0[/tex]

Equate each factor to 0.

[tex]x - 9 = 0 \\ x + 6 = 0[/tex]

Then, solve for x.

[tex]x = 9 \\ x = - 6[/tex]

Next, rule out all extraneous solutions. Here, we rule out the possibility that x = -6, since measurements cannot be negative.

Hence, the value of x is 9.

Now, substitute 9 for the measurements of the length and the width.

If the length is x, then the length measures 9 meters.

If the width is x - 3, then the width measures 6 meters.

Therefore, the dimensions of the rectangular lot is 9 meters by 6 meters.
