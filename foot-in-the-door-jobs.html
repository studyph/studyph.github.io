6. 7. Suzy persuades her roommate to make her a cup of tea, then gets her roommate to cook for a week. What type of compliance technique did Suzy use? a. Low-ball technique b. Foot-in-the-door technique c. Door-in-the-face technique d. That’s Not All technique


d. That's Not All technique. The "That's Not All" technique refers to a compliance technique where a person is initially given a small request, then gradually offered additional requests or benefits until they agree to the larger, more unreasonable request. In this case, Suzy first asked her roommate to make her a cup of tea, then gradually requested bigger requests such as cooking for a week.

29. The ratio of doors to windows is 1:5. There are 186 doors and windows in a building. How many doors are there?


The ratio of doors to windows is 1:5. There are 186 doors and windows in a building. How many doors are there?

Solution:

1 + 5 = 6 parts

There are 6 parts of 186

186 ÷ 6 = 31

31 is 1 part of 186

Doors: 31 × 1 = 31

Windows: 31 × 5 = 155

Let's check if our answer was right!

31 + 155 = 186Final Answer:There are 31 doors and 155 windows in the building.
