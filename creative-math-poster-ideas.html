11. how can you show your creativeness in making a poster​


How I show my creativeness in making a poster​:

Define Topic & Purpose.Make Sentences Short and Suggestive.Use Image.Use the Right Media.

EXPLANATION:

1. Define Topic & Purpose

First, I determine what I want to discuss in the poster, whether it's about beauty products, social activities, or an animated film. Then, also determine the reasons why I made the poster interesting.

2. Make Sentences Short and Suggestive

Posters are usually skimmed by visitors. Very few pedestrians or motorists linger in one place, just to read a poster. For this reason, sentences should be kept short so that they can be read and understood in just a few seconds. Later, through this short language, the messages written by the poster makers can be conveyed properly. However, don't let these words become ambiguous and make the reader confused.

3. Use Images

Pictures are the most interesting means of conveying messages. The proportion of images that are bigger than the writing on a poster is a strategy to attract readers. I can also use striking colors as a strategy.

4. Use the Right Media

The media (where the poster is pasted) will determine whether my poster will be seen by more readers or not. If I stick posters on the walls in the market, chances are the posters will have a chance to be read by more visitors than if they were posted on the walls in the villages.
