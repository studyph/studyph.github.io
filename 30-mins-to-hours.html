ADDITION AND SUBTRACTION OF TIME

Answer with Step-by-step explanation:

1) 3 hours and 15 minutes after 12:00 noon.

Solution:

We all know that 3 hours and 15 minutes can be expressed as 3:15 while the word "after" means that we will be adding 3:15 to 12:00 noon, so:

3:15 + 12:00 = 15:15 and this is an example of military time format which is in 24-hour time format. 15:15 is just equivalent to 3:15 P.M.

2) 6 hours and 30 minutes before 11:30 P.M.

Solution:

We can express 6 hours and 30 minutes as 6:30 and the word "before" means that we will be subtracting 6:30 from 11:30, so:

11:30 - 6:30 is 5:00. Since 11:30 is P.M., 5:00 is also P.M. So the correct answer is 5:00 P.M. or equivalent to 5 o'clock in the afternoon.

3) 2 hours and 15 minutes after 12 midnight.

Solution:

So since it is 12 midnight, 2 hours and 15 minutes after it is equal to 2:15 A.M.

4) 6 hours 30 min  + 2 hours 10 min.

Solution:

6 hours 30 minutes can be converted to 6:30 while 2 hours 10 minutes can be converted to 2:10. Simply add 6:30 and 2:10, we will then have 8:40.

5) 15 hours 36 min 24 sec  + 12 hours 15 min 36 sec.

Solution:

When 15 hours 36 minutes and 24 seconds is added to 12 hours 15 minutes and 36 seconds, we can add the seconds first, then minutes and lastly, hours.

In seconds, 24 seconds plus 36 seconds is equal to 60 seconds. We all know that 60 seconds is equivalent to 1 minute.

In minutes, we have 36 minutes plus 15 minutes is equal to 51 minutes. But we still have the 1 minute above since the seconds are converted to minutes, so 51 plus 1 is 52, therefore, we have 52 minutes.

In hours, 15 hours plus 12 hours is 27 hours. But 1 day = 24 hours, so: 27 hours minus 24 hours is equivalent to 3 hours. Therefore, 27 hours is equal to 1 day and 3 hours. Therefore, the final answer is 1 day, 3 hours and 52 minutes.

5. 23. When 8 hours 30 mins. is divided by 6, the result is a) 1 hr. & 6 mins. c) 1 hr. & 35 mins. b) 1 hr. & 25 mins.


Answer:

c) 1 hr. & 35 mins.

To divide 8 hours 30 mins. by 6, we can first convert it to minutes:

8 hours x 60 minutes/hour = 480 minutes 480 minutes + 30 minutes = 510 minutes

Then, we can divide 510 by 6:

510 ÷ 6 = 85

Finally, we can convert the answer back to hours and minutes:

85 minutes = 1 hour 25 minutes

Therefore, the answer is 1 hr. & 35 mins.
