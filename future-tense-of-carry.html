ANSWER:

1. I am praying in the room.

2. He was buying a book when I called him.

3.  I will be carrying the boxes tomorrow.

4. They are losing in the game.

5. They were fleeing the facility.

EXPLANATION:PAST PROGRESSIVE TENSE-To construct a sentence using past progressive tense, use the formula  ( "was" or "were" + [present participle] )PRESENT PROGRESSIVE TENSE

-Use the formula ( "am," is," or "are" + [present participle] )

FUTURE PROGRESSIVE TENSE

-Use the formula ( "will be" + [present participle] )

WHAT IS PRESENT PARTICIPLE?-The suffix '-ing' is added to the basic form of the verb to form the Present Participle. We use the Present Participle along with a form of the verb 'to be' to create continuous (progressive) tenses.
