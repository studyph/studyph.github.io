19. A website charges a fixed amount for each song download. If you can download six songs for P4.68, then how much would it cost to download ten songs?


Let:
x-be the cost for the number of songs

6x=4.68
Divide both sides by 6
x=0.78

To cost ten songs, multiply the value of each song by 10
10(0.78)
=7.8
