30. 4. Calculate the measure of LA in the given figure a. 90° b. 130 105 c. 115 A d. 105 S 1000 1759 R T Da​


Answer:

B. 130°

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the shape is a pentagon, we need to compute first the interior angles of a pentagon

[tex](n-2)\times 180[/tex]

wherein "n" is the number of sides

[tex](5-2)\times 180[/tex]

[tex]3\times 180=540[/tex]

The interior angle of a pentagon is 540 degrees. Now that we found the interior angle of a pentagon, we can now form the formula

[tex]105+100+115+90+x=540\\[/tex]

We get 90 because of angle R which is a right angle, and right angles are 90 degrees

Add the numbers

[tex]410+x=540[/tex]

Subtract 410 from both sides of the equation

[tex]410+x-410=540-410[/tex]

[tex]x=540-410[/tex]

[tex]x=130^{\circ}[/tex]
