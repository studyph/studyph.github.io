Name opposite rays that have R as the endpoint. 4. Are RU and VO opposite rays? 5. What is the common point of US and RS? 6. Name RV in two other ways.​


Answer:

There are six segments that can be named in the figure: SU, UR, RV, VS, SR, and U V.

Ray SV is the same as ray VS.

Opposite rays that have R as the endpoint are: RS and RU, and RV and RV (which is just the same ray as RV but extended in the opposite direction).

No, RU and VO are not opposite rays because they do not have the same endpoint.

The common point of US and RS is point S.

RV can be named in two other ways as follows: VR (the order of the letters is reversed but it still represents the same line) and RU + UV (this represents the combination of two segments, RU and UV, that together form the line RV).
