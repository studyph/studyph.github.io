So if I were keeping track of my thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, I might write down the following:

Time: Saturday night

Thought: I’m alone. I’m out of step with my friends.

Feeling: Sadness

Behavior: Isolating. Not calling friends. Not picking up the phone.

17. How do the different online platforms help you as a student in your chosen track?


The Benefits of Online Platforms For Students

The Internet has provided more than just limitless learning opportunities. It's also enabled us to create online platforms that allow us to educate and learn in a holistic way from anywhere on the planet. The online portal has had a significant positive effect on our educational experiences.

Here are the ways that online platforms have helped students:  While the Internet is a place for collaboration and networking, much of the studying that took place online prior to the introduction of the online learning network was achieved by self-study. Using interactive workshops and conferencing, the online education program welcomes and allows tutors and students to interact with one another.  Online platforms have accessibility - Learners can read and practice their lessons whenever it is convenient for them. Learners can use an online education portal from any location and on any digital computer they have access to.  One of the main advantages of using an online learning program is that learners are no longer required to perform at the same speed as the rest of the class. Instead, each learner will direct their own instructional path, deciding how long to spend on each module, what to rewrite and replay, and, in certain cases, also the order in which to access material.
