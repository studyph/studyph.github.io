9. We say to divide a decimal by 1000, move the decimal point 3 places to the left. How if we divide 7.17291 by 1000? How will you move decimal point 3 times to the left if the decimal point can no longer move after 7? Defend your answer


Answer:

0.00717291

Steps:

Whenever you are moving the decimal place and you reach the end but you still need to continue, you need to keep on adding a zero(es).

0 decimal places - 7.17291 (÷1)

1 decimal place - 0.717291 (÷10)

2 decimal places - 0.0717291 (÷100)

3 decimal places - 0.00717291 (÷1,000)

So, if you want to make 7.17291 's decimal place go 3 places to the left (or divide by 1,000), your answer will be 0.0717291
