18. how many miles did a car teavel in 3 hours if it was traveling at a speed of 50 km/hr?​


Answer:

93.21 miles.

Use the formula of distance with time and speed (d = s x t)

Substitute and solve:

d = 50 km/hr x 3 hours

d = 150 kilometers

We're not done yet. Convert kilometers to miles by dividing it by 1.609 to get an approximate result.

150 km / 1.609 = 93.2057 miles

If you want round it off then do so.

93.2057 or 93.21 miles
