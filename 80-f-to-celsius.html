27. 80 degrees celsius equal blank degree fahrenheit​


CELCIUS TO FAHRENHEIT

80°C = 176°F

CONVERTING

(80°C × 9/5) + 32 = 176°F

Answer:

176°F

Step-by-step explanation:

F = C × [tex]\frac{9}{5}[/tex] + 32, where F = degree Fahrenheit, C = degree Celsius

Given:

C = 80

So,

F = 80 × [tex]\frac{9}{5}[/tex] + 32

F = 144 + 32

F = 176

Hence, 80 degree Celsius is equal to 176 degree Fahrenheit

PROBLEM

The relationship between temperatures in degree Celsius (°C) and in degree Fahrenheit (°F) is given by °C 5/9 (°F – 32). What is the corresponding value in degree Fahrenheit of 37.78°C?

ANSWERThe answer is 100°F

SOLUTION

°C = 5/9 (°F -32)

9/5 °C = °F -32

9/5 °C + 42 = °F

°F = [9(37.78)] / 5+ 32

°F = 68.004 + 32

°F = 100.004
