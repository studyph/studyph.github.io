2. Taylor pays ₱5.25 for 0.5 yards of fabric. What is the cost per yard?


Answer:

The cost of fabric per yard is ₱10.50.

Step-by-step explanation:

Money Word Problem

Let us analyze the problem.

What is asked?

The cost of fabric per yard.

What are the given?

length of yard - 0.5

cost of the given length of yard - ₱5.25

What is the number sentence?

0.5 yard is equal to ½ of the yard, meaning there are 2 of this length in one yard. So, to find the cost of one yard, we need to multiply the cost of the half of the yard by 2.

₱5.25 × 2 = N

What is the solution?

₱5.25 × 2 = ₱10.50

What is the final answer?

The cost of fabric per yard is ₱10.50.

25. the androsia factory in andros sells androsia fabric for $15 per yard calcuiate how much money will be needed to purchase​


To calculate how much money is needed to purchase a certain amount of Androsia fabric, we need to know the length of fabric that you want to buy.

For example, if you want to buy 5 yards of Androsia fabric, you would need to multiply the price per yard ($15) by the number of yards you want to buy (5):

$15 per yard x 5 yards = $75

So, you would need $75 to purchase 5 yards of Androsia fabric. If you want to buy a different amount of fabric, you can use the same formula but substitute the number of yards you want to buy for the number 5 in the example above.
