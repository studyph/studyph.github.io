#CarryOnLearning

QUESTION:

Amelie is angry. Angry is?

CHOICES:

A. Linking verb

B. Noun

C. Adjective

ANSWER:

C. Adjective

The adjective in the sentence "Amelie is angry" is 'angry'. The linking verb in the sentence is 'is'. The noun in the sentence is 'Amelie.
