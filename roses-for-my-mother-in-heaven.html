8. Mother's garden has 80 red and white roses. If 60% of the roses are red,how many are white roses?​


Answer:

32 are white roses

Step-by-step explanation:

P = B × R

P = 80 × 60%

P = 80 × 0.60

P = 48

Now , We will subtract the percentage to the base to find how many white roses are there.

White roses = P - B

White roses = 80 - 48

White roses = 32

Answer: There are 32 white roses.Solution:Identify the unknown and the formula needed

Percentage: unknown (number of red roses)

Base: 80 (total number of roses)

Rate: 60% --- turn to decimal ---> 0.6 (rate of the number of red roses)

Formula for Percentage: Base × Rate

Solve for the unknown

Base × Rate

= 80 × 0.6

= 48 (number of red roses)

Now, subtract the number of red roses to the total number of roses.

80 (total number of roses) - 48 (number of red roses)

80 - 48 = 32 (number of white roses)

Therefore, there are 32 white roses.

11. loneliness. technical definition; operational definition; mother purity black school. heaven​


Answer:

Loneliness is an unpleasant emotional response to perceived isolation. Loneliness is also described as social pain—a psychological mechanism which motivates individuals to seek social connections. It is often associated with an unwanted lack of connection and intimacy. Loneliness overlaps and yet is distinct from solitude. Solitude is simply the state of being apart from others; not everyone who experiences solitude feels lonely. As a subjective emotion, loneliness can be felt even when surrounded by other people; one who feels lonely, is lonely. The causes of loneliness are varied. They include social, mental, emotional, and environmental factors.

Research has shown that loneliness is found throughout society, including among people in marriages along with other strong relationships, and those with successful careers. Most people experience loneliness at some points in their lives, and some feel it very often. As a short term emotion, loneliness can be beneficial; it encourages the strengthening of relationships. Chronic loneliness on the other hand is widely considered harmful, with numerous reviews and meta-studies concluding it is a significant risk factor for poor mental and physical health outcomes.

Loneliness has long been a theme in literature, going back to the Epic of Gilgamesh. Yet academic study of loneliness was sparse until the late twentieth century. In the 21st century, loneliness has been increasingly recognised as a social problem, with both NGOs and governmental actors seeking to tackle it.

28. The noble subanon rose up to the clouds with their servants, who came down again and told of a heaven of gold and light.


Legendary Tale of Sandayo

In the epic legend of Sandayo, the noble Subanon ascended to the sky with their attendants, who descended and described a gold and light-filled heaven. Unique in the land, their ceremonial buklog is elevated on a special, temporary flooring that must be taken down before dusk. In the buklog, everyone participates in a circle dance, which is a representation of their unity as a people and their unification with the heavenly diwata (spirits).

Through these rituals, life is asserted and reaffirmed, including not only the survival and health of the individual and the tribe but also the equilibrium and fertility of nature. This life then enables the birth of further lives, renewing the youth's longevity, vitality, and significance.

The indigenous Philippines festivals are permeated with the spiritual side of life, and Shamanistic dances can be found practically in there. This is even more apparent in the Subanon buklog when members of the community take part in the ritual of propitiation and celebration.
