21. Working alone, it takes Stephanie seven hours to clean an attic. Shayna can clean the same attic in 12 hours. Find how long it would take them if they worked together? ​


Answer:

It would take them 4 hours, 25 minutes, and 16 seconds to finish the job if they worked together.

Step-by-step explanation:

Stephanie's working rate: 1/7 work done per hour

Shayna's working rate: 1/12 work done per hour

Time that will take them to finish the job: x

Their working rate together: 1/x work done per hour

1.) Add their working rate then equate it to their working rate together.

[tex] \frac{1}{7} + \frac{1}{12} = \frac{1}{x} [/tex]

2.) Solve for x.

[tex] \frac{12 + 7}{84} = \frac{1}{x} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{19}{84} = \frac{1}{x} [/tex]

3.) Reciprocate the equation.

[tex]x = \frac{84}{19} [/tex]

84/19 = 4 8/19 = 4.42 = 4 hours 25 mins 16 seconds

The fastest way to solve this kind of problem is by using the product over sum technique.

Product of 7 and 12= 84

Sum of 7 and 12=19

Then,

x=84/19

Just do the conversion by yourself.
