19. What is the radicands of 162 , 50,75,108


Answer:

The radicand is 2 for 162 and 50, while 3 for 75 and 108.

Step-by-step explanation:

First get the factors of the following numbers, such that on of them is a perfect square and the other one is not perfect square.

So 162 = 81 x 2

50 = 25 x 2

75 = 25 x 3

108 = 36 x 3


So for 162 and 50, the radicand is both 2. While 75 and 108, the radicands are both 3.
