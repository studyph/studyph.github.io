24. What is the speed of a car that covers a distance of 200 km in 4 hours? *a. 67 km/hr b. 50 km/hr c. 800 km/hr d. 60 km/hr ​


Answer:

B. 50 km/hr

Explanation:

The speed, as this question is phrased, may vary during the travel, so long as 200km are covered in 4 hours.

However, according to the physics formula (distance = velocity x time), the average speed will be as simple as dividing the distance covered by the amount of time. 200km divided by 4 hours equals an average speed of 50 km/h.
