10. 14. Which is paired correctly? a. Distance: Scalar c. Direction: 500km d. None of the above b. Displacement: Vector 15. Which situation shows a body in displacement? a. A pencil 5 inches from its initial position b. Students 1 m away from each other c. An iron ball tossed to 30 meters d. An arrow shot to 65 meters, East ​


Answer:

14. The correct pairing is:

b. Displacement: Vector

Displacement is a vector quantity because it has both magnitude (the distance between two points) and direction (the straight line path from the initial point to the final point).

15. The situation that shows a body in displacement is:

d. An arrow shot to 65 meters, East

Displacement is the straight-line distance between the initial and final position of an object, taking into account the direction. In this case, the arrow is shot to a distance of 65 meters towards the east, which means it has a displacement of 65 meters to the east from its initial position.
