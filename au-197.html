8. how many grams of gold (Au) are there in 15.4 mol of Au?​


The molar mass of Au is 196.967 g/mol.

To find the mass of 15.4 moles of Au, we can use the formula:

mass = moles x molar mass

Substituting the values, we get:

mass = 15.4 mol x 196.967 g/mol

mass = 3031.43 g

Therefore, there are 3031.43 grams of gold in 15.4 moles of Au.
