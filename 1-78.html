» Calculate the percentage of PRB using the:

[tex]\bold{Formula\:||\:P=R(B) }[/tex]Where p = percentage, r = rate and b = base.

'Now to find the percentage, multiply the rate by the base.Dont forget to transform the rate from a percent to a decimal form before multiplying can be done.

[tex] \huge \tt{P=\frac{50\%\:\:or\:\:0.5 \: × \: 78}{R\:\:•\:\:B}}[/tex]
