21. ho many does a polygon have if the sum of its interior angls is 1260​


[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{QUESTION:}}[/tex]

how many does a polygon have if the sum of its interior angls is 1260.

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

Using the formula of Sum of interior angles:

[tex]\sf{S_n=180(n-2)}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{1260=180(n-2)}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{\frac{1260}{180}=\frac{180}{180}(n-2)}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{7=n-2}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{7+2=n}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{9=n}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{ANSWER:}}[/tex]

The polygon have 9 sides

28. How many sides has a convex polygon if the sum of the interior angles is 1260° ? ​


it has 9 sides

hope this helps :)

Formula for sum of interior angles of a regular polygon is given as,

(n-2)×180°

where

n = # of sides of polygon

solve for n,

(n-2)×180° = 1260°

n-2 = 1260/180

n-2 = 7

n = 7 + 2

n = 9

Therefore the polygon has 9 sides.

If the 9 sided polygon is a regular polygon, it will be called a Nonagon.
