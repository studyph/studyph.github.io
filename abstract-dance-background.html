Answer & Explanation:

Introduction:

Climate change is a growing concern around the world, and it is becoming increasingly apparent that collective action is needed to address this issue. One way to do this is through community service, which can bring together individuals to work towards a common goal. In this article, we will explore how students can take the lead in community service by using reminders to promote environmentally friendly practices.

Background:

Climate change is caused by the buildup of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, primarily carbon dioxide emissions from human activities. This has led to rising temperatures, melting glaciers, and more frequent extreme weather events. The consequences of climate change are already being felt around the world, from droughts and food shortages to flooding and displacement.

Abstract:

Students can play a crucial role in addressing climate change by promoting environmentally friendly practices within their communities. By using reminders, such as posters and social media campaigns, students can encourage others to adopt behaviors that reduce carbon emissions and protect the environment.

Objectives:

The objectives of this initiative are to:

Raise awareness about the impact of climate change and the importance of taking action.Encourage individuals to adopt environmentally friendly practices in their daily lives.Mobilize students to take the lead in community service projects that promote sustainability.

Execution:

To achieve these objectives, students can use a variety of strategies, such as:

Creating posters and flyers that promote environmentally friendly practices, such as reducing energy use, conserving water, and recycling.Launching social media campaigns that spread awareness about climate change and encourage others to take action.Organizing community service projects, such as beach cleanups or tree-planting events, that promote sustainability and bring people together.Collaborating with local businesses and organizations to promote sustainable practices and reduce carbon emissions.

By using reminders and other strategies, students can help to create a culture of environmental stewardship within their communities. This, in turn, can lead to more sustainable practices and a healthier planet for future generations.

7. CAPSTONE PROJECT Theme: ENVIRONMENT Title: STUDENT’S WILL GIVE WAY TO COMMUNITY SERVICE THROUGH REMINDERS. •INTRODUCTION •BACKGROUND •ABSTRACT •OBJECTIVES •EXECUTION sana po matulong po ninyo ko dito ty​


INTRODUCTION:

The environment is an essential part of our daily lives, and it is our responsibility to protect and preserve it. Unfortunately, the rapid growth of industries and the use of non-renewable resources have caused immense damage to our planet. One way to tackle this issue is by promoting community service, especially among students who can be future leaders of society. In this capstone project, we aim to encourage students to participate in community service activities through reminders and create awareness regarding the environment.

BACKGROUND:

It is no secret that the current state of our environment is significantly challenging. The increasing population and industrialization have led to a high demand for energy, leading to deforestation, land degradation, pollution, and climate change. As a result, the needs of the present generation have been leading to environmental degradation, causing long-term implications for future generations. One way to combat this issue is through community service, especially among youth.

ABSTRACT:

This capstone project focuses on promoting community service among students for the betterment of our environment. Through reminders and awareness, we aim to instill the importance of protecting the environment and the benefits associated with community service. The students will be encouraged to participate in activities that positively impact society and have an eco-friendly approach.

OBJECTIVES:

• To create awareness among students regarding environmental degradation.

• To encourage students to participate in community service activities.

• To promote eco-friendly and sustainable practices among students.

• To foster a sense of social responsibility among students.

EXECUTION:

Initially, we will conduct a baseline survey to understand the current level of awareness and participation of students regarding community service and environmental issues. Based on the findings, we will devise reminders and campaigns that promote community service activities, especially those that align with sustainable practices. We will also collaborate with local organizations and communities to organize various activities that students can participate in, such as beach clean-ups, tree planting, and recycling drives. The project will conclude with a follow-up survey to understand the effectiveness of the initiative.

In conclusion, this capstone project aims to instill a sense of social responsibility and promote sustainable practices among students through community service activities. By participating in such activities, students can contribute towards the protection and preservation of our environment, ultimately creating a better world for generations to come.

Background:

Many students have the desire to participate in community service, but they often lack the motivation or reminders to do so. The lack of opportunities and information about community service activities in their local areas can also hinder their participation. This project aims to bridge this gap and provide students with regular reminders about community service opportunities.

Abstract:

The project will develop a mobile application that will remind students of their responsibility to the environment and encourage them to participate in community service activities. Execution:

The project will involve the following steps:

1. Conduct research on existing mobile applications for community service and environmental awareness.

2. Develop a design for the mobile application and user interface.

3. Develop the mobile application using appropriate software development tools.

4. Test the mobile application for functionality, usability, and performance.
