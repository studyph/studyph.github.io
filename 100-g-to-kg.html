5. Calculate the volume of an object with a mass of 100 kg and density of 13.6 g/cm3.


Volume is 7,352.94 cm³

Explanation:

Given:

m = 100 kg

ρ = 13.6 g/cm³

Required:

volume

Equation:

[tex]ρ \:= \:\frac{m}{v}[/tex]

where: ρ - density

m - mass

v - volume

Solution:

Convert 100 kg to g (1000 g = 1 kg)

[tex]=100\: kg\:×\:\frac{1000 \:g}{1\:kg}[/tex]

[tex]=100,000\:kg[/tex]

Substitute the value of ρ and m

[tex]ρ \:= \:\frac{m}{v}[/tex]

[tex]13.6\: g/cm³\:= \:\frac{100,000\:g}{v}[/tex]

[tex](v)\:13.6\: g/cm³\:= \:100,000\:g[/tex]

[tex]v\:= \:\frac{100,000\:g}{13.6\: g/cm³}[/tex]

[tex]v\:= \:7,352.94\:cm³[/tex]

Final Answer:Volume is 7,352.94 cm³
