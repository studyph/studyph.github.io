23. in a cement mixture ,the ratio of amount of sand to amount of cement is 24 shovels to 4 shovels how many shovel of cement should be mix in 48 shovel of sand​


Answer:

8 shovels

Step-by-step explanation:

ratio of sand to cement= 24:4

if there are 48 shovels of sand, how many shovels of cement should be added?

24:4

48:x

To find the number of shovels, we first need to simplify the ratio.

24:4 when simplified is equal to 6:1

Therefore,

24:4

48:x

is equal to

6:1

48:x

To find the ratio, we need to multiply the given values first, namely 48 and 1 since a ratio is equal to a fraction and those two values will be equal to multiplying the numerator of the first ratio to the denominator of the second.

48(1)= 48

Now, we need to find the value for x such that 6x will be equal to 48.

6x=48

x=48/6

x=8

So now, the ratio is

6:1

48:8

Which tells us that we need 8 shovels of cement for 48 shovels of sand
