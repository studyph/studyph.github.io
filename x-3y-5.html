20. x-3y=5x-3y=6it's is a inconsistent or consistent?​


Answer:

It is inconsistent.

Step-by-step explanation:

They have equal slopes (1/3)

and unequal y-intercepts (-5/3 and -2)

That's all. Check for errors too.

Answer:

The given system is inconsistent.

Step-by-step explanation:

Transform to y = mx + b:

Equation (1):

x - 3y = 5

3y = x - 5

y = x/3 - 5/3

m (slope) = 1/3b (y-intercept) = -5/3

Equation (2):

x - 3y = 6

3y = x - 6

y = x/3 - 6/3

y = x/3 - 2

m (slope) = 1/3b (y-intercept) = -2

The system is inconsistent because of the same slope at 1/3  and different y-intercepts at  -5/3  and -2.  The graphs are parallel lines and have no solution.

Consistent system have the same slopes and y-intercepts. The graphs overlap and with infinite solutions.

Independent system has different slopes and y-intercepts.  The graphs intersect at its one and only solution.
