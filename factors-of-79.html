Answer:

(a.)  Seven students scored not higher than 79.

Step-by-step explanation:

The median is the midpoint of the given ungrouped data determined by:

Median = (n + 1) / 2

Given:  

83, 72, 87, 79, 82, 77, 80, 73, 86, 81, 79, 82, 79, 74, 74Where n = 15 students

Arrange the data in ascending order:

72, 73, 74, 74. 77, 79, 79, 79, 80, 81, 82, 82, 83, 86, 87

Determine the median position:

Median =  (15 + 1) / 2

Median = (16)/2

Median = 8

The median is at 8th position:

72, 73, 74, 74, 77, 79, 79, 79, 80, 81, 82, 82, 83, 86, 87

The median is 79.
