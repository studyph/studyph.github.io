16. The area of a rectangular mirror is 45 ² and its perimeter is 28 in. What are the dimensions of the mirror?​


Answer:

The dimensions of the mirror are 9 inches × 5 inches

Step-by-step explanation:

Area of a rectangular mirror = 45 sq. inches

The perimeter of the mirror = 28 inches

Let the length of the mirror be L inches and its breadth be B inches.

Perimeter of a rectangle = 2(length + breadth)

Area = length * breadth

2(L + B) = 28

L + B = 28/2

L = 14 - B -- equation (1).

LB = 45

(14 - B)(B) = 45

14B - B² = 45

0 = B² - 14B + 45

0 = B² - 9B - 5B + 45

0 = B(B - 9) - 5(B - 9)

0 = (B - 5)(B - 9)

B - 5 = 0

B = 5 inches

B - 9 = 0

B = 9 inches

When B = 5,

L = 14 - 5 = 9 inches

When B = 9,

L = 14 - 9 = 5 inches.

∴ The dimensions of the mirror are 9 inches × 5 inches.
