22. 1. 0.07 hm to dmwith explanation​


Answer:

To convert from hectometers (hm) to decimeters (dm), you need to multiply by 10,000 because there are 10 decimeters in a meter and 100 meters in a hectometer.

So, to convert 0.07 hm to dm, you can use the following formula:

0.07 hm * 10,000 dm/hm = 700 dm

Therefore, 0.07 hm is equal to 700 dm.
