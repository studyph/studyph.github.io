28. Beef is cooked in a pressure cooker with an initial pressure of 1.5 atm at 110°C. If the volume is held constant what will be the pressure inside the cooker if the temperature increases to 120°C?​


Given:

[tex]P_{1} = \text{1.5 atm}[/tex]

[tex]T_{1} = \text{110°C + 273 = 383 K}[/tex]

[tex]T_{2} = \text{120°C + 273 = 393 K}[/tex]

Unknown:

[tex]P_{2}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]\frac{P_{1}}{T_{1}} = \frac{P_{2}}{T_{2}}[/tex]

[tex]P_{2} = P_{1} × \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}[/tex]

[tex]P_{2} = \text{1.5 atm} × \frac{\text{393 K}}{\text{383 K}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{P_{2} = \text{1.54 atm}}[/tex]
