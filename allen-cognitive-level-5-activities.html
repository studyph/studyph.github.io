22. Teacher vivian is asking her students to identify the equipment in the chemistry laboratory. under the cognitive domain, in which assessment level is this?


COGNITIVE DOMAIN

Because she is asking her students to name the equipment in the chemistry lab, teacher Vivian's rating of their performance falls below understanding level. By identifying or describing the lab apparatus, she can gauge how well her students understand the concepts and facts. She wants to gauge her students' comprehension through her assessment.

There are domains of behavior used to assess  and measure the students and these are the following: Knowledge level-  In this section, the teacher should assess the students' retention of and recognition of facts, concepts, and other information about the subject being covered.Comprehension level-  At this level, the teacher wants to make a connection between the conduct of the pupils and the fundamental concept they are studying.Application level - If the teacher wants the students to use what they've learnt in different situations or in real life. The application assessment level is being used.Analysis level  - If the teacher asks for analogies, hypotheses, and conclusions to encourage critical thinking in the students.Synthesis level- The teacher is assessing the pupils' creative thinking in this section by looking at their original approaches to a problem. Evaluation level-  The teacher seeks to gauge the pupils' viewpoints on a subject in this section.
