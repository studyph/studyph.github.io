29. Give an essay about differentiate religion from spirituality,theology and philosophy of religion.​


Philosophy and theology are academic/intellectual disciplines, whereas spirituality is more personal and experiential.

Philosophy applies logical analysis and rational argument to examine/discuss the possible meaning(s) of life, reality and existence. Theology is a branch of philosophy that applies logical analysis and rational argument to examine/discuss religious doctrines (such as the possible nature of God.).

Spirituality is a personal discovery of, and personal relationship to, the apparent meaning and purpose of life itself or reality as a whole, primarily through one’s own insights and choices rather than adherence to predefined doctrines, and often inspired by experiences of self-transcendence.
