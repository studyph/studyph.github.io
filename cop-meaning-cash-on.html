26. what is cash bond means?


In general cash bond means An arrangement wherein a party gives to another an amount of money to secure the fulfillment of an obligation. In cases where the party fails to comply with the obligation, the money is forfeited in favor of the latter.

In law cash bond means a transaction wherein a defendant deposits an amount of money to the court to secure his temporary release from jail. Defendants failing to appear before the court during their trial forfeit the cash bond.
