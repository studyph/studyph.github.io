6. Give a speech about the following quote:"Providing Learner's Resilience Through Showcasing Skills and Talents in Culture and Arts and Technology"​


Dear friends,

Today, I want to talk about the importance of resilience in learning and how showcasing skills and talents in culture and arts and technology can contribute to building this resilience.

As we all know, learning can be a challenging and sometimes even a frustrating process. We encounter difficulties and setbacks that can make us want to give up. However, resilience is the ability to bounce back from these setbacks, to persevere through challenges, and to continue moving forward towards our goals.

One way to develop resilience is through the showcasing of our skills and talents in culture, arts, and technology. When we have the opportunity to demonstrate our abilities in these areas, we are building our self-confidence and self-esteem. We are also able to receive feedback and recognition for our accomplishments, which can motivate us to continue pushing ourselves and developing our skills.

Culture and arts can provide a unique outlet for individuals to express themselves and to connect with others. By participating in cultural activities and creating art, we can develop a sense of belonging and community. These activities can also be a source of inspiration and comfort during difficult times, helping us to cope with stress and adversity.

Technology is another area where individuals can showcase their skills and talents. In today's digital age, technology is an essential part of our lives, and those who are proficient in this area have a distinct advantage. By learning to code, develop apps, or design websites, individuals can create innovative solutions to real-world problems, and make a positive impact on their communities and society.

In conclusion, showcasing skills and talents in culture and arts and technology can help individuals develop resilience in learning. By participating in these activities, we can build self-confidence, receive recognition for our accomplishments, develop a sense of community, and create innovative solutions to real-world problems. Let us embrace these opportunities and continue to grow and develop as individuals. Thank you.

30. Task 8.2 A QUOTE ON QUOTE


The phrase “quote unquote” means speaking a quotation on a word for word basis. You end the quotation with the word “unquote” to indicate that it’s finished. On the one hand, the phrase “quote on quote” is a mispronunciation of “unquote.” The correct phrase is “quote unquote.” This indicates that what you’re saying is a repetition of someone else’s statement.
