Answer:

Given:

m = 860kg

g = 9.8 m/s

h = ?

PE = 20,500J

Solution:

h = PE / mg

= 20,500J / (860kg)(9.8m/s)

= 20,500J / 8,428 kg.m/s

= 2.43m
