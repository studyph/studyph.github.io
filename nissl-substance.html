1. A 200 kg missle is launced at a speed of 1,000 m/s, east. calculate the momentum of missle.


Answer:

The correct answer is 50m/s.

Explanation:

Concept:

• Momentum is directly proportional to the mass of the object and its velocity, too.

. Therefore the greater the mass of an object or the greater its velocity, the greater its momentum.

. Momentum p is a vector, which has the same

direction as velocity v. • The momentum SI unit is kg.m/s.

p=mv

Where,

p = Momentum

m = Mass

v = Velocity

Calculations:

Given that,

Momentum (p) = 10000 kg m/s ;

Mass (m) = 200 kg

Velocity (v) = ?

We know that,

Momentum (p) = Mass (m) x Velocity (v)

10000 kg m/s = 200 kg x v

v = 10000/200

= 50 m/s
