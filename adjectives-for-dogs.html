4. find the adjective or adjective phrase10. The dog tore up a pillow of feathers.a. pillow of feathersc. tore upb. The dogd. a pillow​


Answer:

The answer is letter C. Tore up

Answer:

a. pillow of feathers is the closest answer

This is the closest answer, but technically it should just be "of feathers," modifying "pillow," which is a noun (a direct object, in this case). "Pillow of feathers" is actually a noun phrase. But by all means, go for the closest answer. Just remember that an "adjective phrase" consists only of the adjectives, not including the noun it modifies (if there's a noun, it should be correctly called a "noun phrase"). :3
