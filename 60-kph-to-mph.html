8. A car travels at 40 mph for 2 hr and 60 mph for 3 hr what is the average speed


Hello!

A car travels at 40 mph for 2 hr and 60 mph for 3 hr what is the average speed

Then, 40 mph:

40 miles ---- 1 hr

80 miles ---- 2 hr

Then, 60 mph:

60 miles ------- 1 hr

180 miles ------ 3 hr

* travel distance:

d = 80 miles + 180 miles

d = 260 miles

=> d (travel distance) = 260 miles

* travel time :

=> t (travel time) = 2 hr + 3 hr = 5 hr

We have the following data:

a (average speed) = ? (in mph)

d (travel distance) = 260 miles

t (travel time) = 5 hr

We apply the data to the average speed formula, see:

[tex]a = \dfrac{d}{t}[/tex]

[tex]a = \dfrac{260\:miles}{5\:hr}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\boxed{a = 52\:miles/hr}}\end{array}}\qquad\checkmark[/tex]

Answer:

The average speed is 52 mph

d = 60 mi
