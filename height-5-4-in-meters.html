The cylindrical water tank can hold 9.7 meters³ more water than the rectangular-prism-shaped tank.

 prism.

  Formula for cylinder - πr²h

  Formula for rectangular prism - lwh

  π - 3.1416

  r  - 2 m (divide the diameter by 2 to get the radius)

  h - 3 m

  l - 7 m

w - 2 m

 h - 2 m

3. Calculate the volume for both figures
