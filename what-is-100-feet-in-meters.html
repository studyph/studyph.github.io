3. 2.) Timi went on hiking. last week taking a trail on her feet for 100 meters A in 40 seconds. What was her total speed on that weekend?pa help po​


Answer:

2.5 meters per second

Step-by-step explanation:

To calculate Timi's speed, we need to use the formula:

Speed = Distance / Time

In this case, the distance Timi covered is 100 meters and the time it took her is 40 seconds. So, her speed can be calculated as:

Speed = 100 meters / 40 seconds

Speed = 2.5 meters per second

Therefore, Timi's speed during her hiking trip was 2.5 meters per second.
