standard deviation of the mean (mathematically, &mu; &plusmn; &sigma;, where &mu; is the

and about 99.7 percent lie within three standard deviations (&mu; &plusmn; 3&sigma;). This is

known as the 68-95-99.7 rule, or the empirical rule.
