20. 1. The circumference of a circle varies directly as its diameter. If the circumference of a circle having a diameter of 8 cm is 87 (pi)cm, what is the circumference of a circle having a diameter of 12 cm?​


Answer:

12π cm

explanation:

Given that : Circumference is equal to 8π cm when the diameter is 8 cm,

Let : C = circumference

d= diameter

k = constant of variation

The equation of variation is:

C= kd

We will use this to find the value of k (constant of variation)

C=kd

8π = (k)(8)

8π=8k

π=k

k=π
