D. 82 square feet

Given:

rectangle

length = 12 ft

width = 6 ft

triangle

b = 4 ft

h = 5 ft

A = A_rectangle + A_triangle

= (l × w) + (1/2bh)

= (12 ft × 6 ft ) + [1/2(4 ft)(5 ft)]

= 72 ft² + [1/2(20 ft²)]

= 72 ft² + 10 ft²

A = 82 ft²
