21. HOW TO CONVERT m² to mm² ?​


Answer:

Conversion rate:1 m² = 1,000,000 mm²or1 × 10⁰ m² = 1 × 10⁹  mm²

Step-by-step explanation:

1 meter (m) = 1,000 millimeter (mm)

(1 m)² = (1,000 mm)²

1 m² = 1,000,000 mm²

To convert square meters (m²) to square millimeters (mm²):

Multiply: m² × (1,00,000 mm²)/m²

Example:

Convert 3 m² to mm²:

(3 m²) × (1,000,000 mm²)m²

= 3,000,000 mm²  or  3 × 10⁹ mm²
