17. You are standing 14 feet from a water tower. The distance from you to the point oftangency on the tower is 28 feet. What is the radius of the water tower?​


Answer:

r= 21 ft

Step-by-step explanation:

(14+r)² = r²+28²

(14+r)(14+r)= 196+14r+14r+r²

196+14r+14r+r²=r²=28²

Cancel the two r², add the two 14r and simplify 28²: 196+ 28r = 784

Subtract 196 to 196 and 784: 28r=588

Divide both sides with 28: r=21

So the radius is 21 ft.
