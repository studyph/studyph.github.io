Explanation:

A proper fraction is a fraction whose numerator is smaller than its denominator.

A improper fraction is a fraction in which the numerator (top number) is greater than or equal to the denominator (bottom number).

A mixed fraction is simply an improper fraction written as the sum of a whole number and a proper fraction.
