Answer:

A. 15

Step-by-step explanation:

To convert square centimeters to square meters, you need to divide the number of square centimeters by 10,000 (since 1 square meter is equal to 10,000 square centimeters). Therefore, to convert 150,000 square centimeters to square meters, you would divide 150,000 by 10,000:

150,000 sq cm ÷ 10,000 = 15 sq m

So the answer is a) 15m.
