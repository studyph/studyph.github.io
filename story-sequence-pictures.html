As the sun began to set over the quiet town, 12-year-old Jamie sat on her front porch, staring out into the distance. She felt a sense of unease in her gut but couldn't put her finger on what was causing it. Suddenly, she saw movement out of the corner of her eye and turned to see an old man slowly walking down the street.

As he drew nearer, she could see the deep lines etched into his weathered face and the sadness in his eyes. Instead of turning away, Jamie decided to approach him and offer her help.
