23. What kind of speech do you find the most interesting to listen to Persuasive?Expostory? Or Entertaining? Why?​


Answer:

Entertaining

Explanation:

In general terms, an entertaining speech is intended to hold an audience's attention and entertain or regale them while conveying a point. While entertaining presentations, like more traditional informative or persuasive speeches, should transmit a clear message, their delivery style is often distinct. Persuasive speaking is the most common style of communication. This style of discourse might range from political debates to discussions about what to eat for dinner. Persuasive speaking is inextricably linked to the audience, as the speaker must meet the audience halfway. Persuasion is not totally under the speaker's hands; influence occurs when an audience agrees with what a speaker says. As a result, persuasive speaking involves increased attention to audience analysis.
