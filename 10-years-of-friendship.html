3. A log cabin quilt is 24 years old and a friendship quilt is 6 years old. In how many years will the log cabin quilt be three times as old as the friendship quilt? Let y be the years. Write an equation.​


Let 'y' be the number of years
Log cabin quilt = 24 years old
Friendship quilt = 6 years old

Solution:
24 + y = 3(6 + y)
24 + y = 18 + 3y
24 - 18 = 3y - y
6 = 2y
y = 3 years

Check:
24 + 3 = 27
6 + 3 = 9
27 is 3 times 9

Therefore 3 years is the answer

28. 10 brilliant things friendship​


Answer:

1. Friendship constitutes an invaluable source of emotional succor and a profound sense of membership: the presence of steadfast companions can assist us in effectively managing the vicissitudes of life, while also conferring upon us a resolute sense of solace and protection.

2. The association of friendship is considered as a motivator for an individual's personal development as it pushes individuals to engage in new experiences, strive for personal objectives and manifest the optimal facets of their existence.

3. The fostering of friendships has been found to facilitate felicity and a state of good health: Devoting time to socializing with companions may enhance one's emotional state, alleviate psychological strain, and augment our general sentiment of physical and mental prosperity.

4. The association of friendship has been shown to occasion the manifestation of qualities such as empathy and comprehension. Through engaging in social interactions with individuals from diverse backgrounds and varying perspectives, one has the potential to cultivate a heightened sense of empathy and understanding towards others.

5. In the realm of social interactions, the rapport of friendship has the capacity to forge enduring etchings within the memories of individuals. Specifically, numerous instances of felicity and recollections that remain indelible are often concomitant with the shared activities among friends.

6. Interpersonal bonds forged through friendship offer a profound sense of community and connection. Participation in a social circle can foster the sensation of being linked to a greater whole, imbue us with a sense of direction and value, and instill a feeling of having found a rightful place.

7. Friendship fosters constructive communication and conflict-resolution skills: Proactive friends engage in transparent and sincere exchanges, and collaborate effectively to mitigate conflicts through constructive means.

8. The establishment of friendships has been shown to promote a positive and healthy lifestyle through the provision of social support, which can encourage and motivate individuals to engage in exercise, adhere to healthy dietary practices, and attend to their overall well-being.

9. The bond of friendship affords prospects for recreational activities and exploration: Be it traversing diverse destinations, sampling novel cuisines, or delving into uncharted pastimes, companions can facilitate the discovery of fresh and stimulating opportunities.

10. Friendship is deemed as an ardent source for the provision of unconditional love and acceptance.
