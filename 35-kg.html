7. The distance between a 50-kg person and a 35-kg person is 3 m. What is the amount ofgravitational force between the two?​


Calculating the gravitational force between two objects.

Given

Mass of Person 1 (m₁) = 50 kgMass of Person 2 (m₂) = 35 kgDistance between the objects (r) = 3 mGravitational Constant (G) = 6.67 x 10⁻¹¹ N • m² / kg²Gravitational Force (Fɢ) = ?

Fɢ = Gm₁m₂ / r²

Fɢ = (6.67 x 10⁻¹¹ N • m² / kg²)(50kg)(35kg) / (3 m)²

Fɢ = (6.67 x 10⁻¹¹ N • m² / kg²)(1750 kg²) / 9 m²

Fɢ = (1.16725 x 10⁻⁷ N • m²) / 9 m²

Fɢ = 1.2967 x 10⁻⁸ N
