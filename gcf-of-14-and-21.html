14. what is the common factor GCF LCM of 14 21 28 35 ​


Answer:

420

Step-by-step explanation:

The prime factors of 14 are 2 and 7The prime factors of 21 are 3 and 7.The prime factors of 28 are 2, 2 and 7.The prime factors of 35 are 5 and 7.

The least common multiple is the product of all factors in the greatest number of their occurrence.

LCM = 2*2*3*5*7

LCM = 2^2*3*5*7

LCM = 420

The least common multiple of 14, 21, 28 and 35 is 420.
