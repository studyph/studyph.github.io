21. Jenna's mouth is one huge metal factory.: explanation of the sentences


Answer:

Jenna’s mouth is one huge metal factory means that Jenna is using braces for her teeth therefore her mouth looks like one huge metal factory.

Explanation:

The words “Jenna’s mouth is one huge metal factory” is metaphor. To know what is the meaning of these words, we should understand what is metaphor first. Metaphor is a figure of speech that describes an object or action in a way that is not the literal true (exact meaning of the words). Metaphor helps explain an idea or make a comparison. We can not take metaphor literally because if we do, the words would sounds strange. The definition of metaphor is a word or a phase for one thing that is used to refer to another thing in order to show or suggest that they are similar. It contains an object, activity, or idea that is used as symbol of something else. An example of metaphor is “Love is a battlefield” means that love is hard.
