22. why do you think smaller size particles dissolve faster than bigger one?​


Answer:

A given quantity of solute dissolves faster when it is ground into small particles than if it is in the form of a large chunk because more surface area is exposed. The packet of granulated sugar exposes far more surface area to the solvent and dissolves more quickly than the sugar cube.

Answer:

Softball gloves are larger because the ball is bigger than a baseball. For example, the average size of a baseball outfielders glove is 13 inches, whereas the average size of a softball outfielders glove is 15 inches.
