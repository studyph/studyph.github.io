13. 6. Four interior angles of a hexagon measure 110", 120°, 130 and 140. Themeasure of the 5th angle is 20 less than the 6 angle. Find the measure ofthe 5th and the 6th angle.a. 110° and 130° b. 120° and 140" C. 100% and 120° d. 90° and 110- ​


Answer:

b. 120°

Sum of the interior angles of a hexagon is 720°

Let the sixth angle be x°

The given angles are : 100° , 110° , 120°, 130° , 140°

∴ 100 + 110 + 120 + 130 + 140 + x° = 720

∴ 600 + x = 720

∴ x = 720 - 600 = 120 °

Ans : the sixth angle of the hexagon is 120°
