6. In a restaurant, the mean annual salary of the 4 chefs is P68,000, and the mean annual salary of the 8 waiters is P47,000. What is the mean annual salary of all 12 employees?


Mean  = [tex] \frac{ x_{1} + x_{2} + ... + x_{n} }{n} [/tex]

total salry of chefs = 4 * 68000 = 272000
total salary of waiters = 8 * 47000 = 376000

mean annual salary of all 12 employees = ((4*68000)+(8*47000))/12 = 54000

7. salary origin:old meaning:new meaning:​


Answer:

Origin: Salary comes from the Latin word salarium, which also means "salary" and has the root sal, or "salt." In ancient Rome, it specifically meant the amount of money allotted to a Roman soldier to buy salt, which was an expensive but essential commodity.

Old Meaning:salarium

New Meaning:a fixed regular payment, typically paid on a monthly or biweekly basis but often expressed as an annual sum, made by an employer to an employee, especially a professional or white-collar worker.
