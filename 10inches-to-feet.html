Answer:

Area of the Circle

The area of its surface is 78.54 inches^2.

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the surface of a tambourine is a circle, use the formula A = (\piπ /4 X D^2. Given that the diameter is 10 inches then A = (\piπ /4) (10 inches^2). A = (3.1416/4) (100 inches^2). A = (0.7854) (100 inches^2). Therefore, A = 78.54 inches^2.
