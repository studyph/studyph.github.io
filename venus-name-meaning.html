22. Venus has a mean distance from the sun of 67,240,000 miles while Mars has a mean distance from the sun of 4,213,000,000 miles. How much farther is Mars from the sun than Venus?


Step-by-step explanation:

To find out how much farther Mars is from the sun than Venus, we can subtract Venus' mean distance from the sun (67,240,000 miles) from Mars' mean distance from the sun (4,213,000,000 miles).

4,213,000,000 miles (Mars) - 67,240,000 miles (Venus) = 4,145,760,000 miles

So Mars is 4,145,760,000 miles farther from the sun than Venus.

24. what is the origin name of planet venus​


THE PLANET VENUS

Planet Venus was named after the Roman Goddess of beauty and love Venus or Aphrodite in Greek Mythology. It is the nearest planet next to Earth, and just a little bit smaller but almost as big as Earth.

Venus is wrapped in a very thick atmosphere that contains carbon dioxide and other green house gases. Its atmosphere is thick that the pressure on the planet's surface is 90 times greater than of the Earth enough to crush a moving car.

The thick atmosphere and large amount of carbon dioxide causes the planet to trap the Sun's radiation that Venus making it the hottest planet in the solar system, with temperature soaring up to more than 480°C.

Venus is also known as the morning star, because it is the only star visible during day due to its distance from the Earth.
