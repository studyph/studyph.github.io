16. Solve for the situation below. A boy who is on the 2nd floor of their house watches his dog lying on the ground. The angle between his eye level and his line of sight is 32°. a. Which angle is identified in the problem: angle of elevation or angle of depression? Justify your answer. b. If the boy is 3 meters above the ground, approximately how far is the dog from the house? a. The angle identified in the problem is the angle of depression. This is because the boy is looking downwards from his position on the 2nd floor towards his dog on the ground.

b. Let's denote the distance between the boy and his dog by d. From the problem, we can form a right triangle where the boy's eye level is the height of the triangle, the distance between the house and the dog is the base of the triangle, and the line of sight is the hypotenuse of the triangle. We can use trigonometry to solve for d.

First, we need to find the complement of the angle of depression, which is 90 - 32 = 58 degrees. Using the tangent function, we have:

tan(58) = 3/d

Solving for d, we get:

d = 3/tan(58) ≈ 2.45 meters

Therefore, the dog is approximately 2.45 meters away from the house.

c. Let's denote the height of the boy above the ground by h. We can use the same right triangle formed in part b and the distance between the house and the dog (which is given as 7 meters) to solve for h.

Using the tangent function again, we have:

tan(32) = h/7

Solving for h, we get:

h = 7*tan(32) ≈ 4.12 meters

Therefore, the boy is approximately 4.12 meters above the ground.

22. Solve for the situation below. A boy who is on the 2nd floor of their house watches his dog lying on the ground. The angle between his eye level and his line of sight is 32°. a. Which angle is identified in the problem: angle of elevation or angle of depression? Justify your answer. b. If the boy is 3 meters above the ground, approximately how far is the dog from the house? Make a sketch of the visualization. c. If the dog is 7 meters away from the house, approximately how high is the boy above the ground? Try to sketch your visualization.​hi gaiz morning naknang​


a. The angle identified in the problem is the angle of depression because the boy is looking down at the dog from a higher point.

b. Let the distance between the boy's eyes and the ground be x. We can use tangent since we know the angle of depression and one of the sides.

tangent(32°) = distance from the boy's eyes to the ground / distance from the boy's eyes to the dog + 3 m

tan(32°) = x / (x + d + 3)

where d is the distance from the house to the dog.

Rearranging this formula, we get:

d = (x + 3) * tan(32°) - x

Assuming the boy is about 1.5 meters tall, his eyes are about 1.5 + 3 = 4.5 meters above the ground. So x = 4.5 - 3 = 1.5 meters.

Therefore,

d = (1.5 + 3) * tan(32°) - 1.5

d ≈ 7.1 meters

The dog is approximately 7.1 meters away from the house.

c. This time, we need to use the distance between the boy's eyes and the dog and the tangent function again.

tangent(32°) = height difference / (distance from the boy's eyes to the dog)

tan(32°) = h / d

where h is the height difference between the boy's eyes and the ground.

Rearranging this formula gives:

h = d * tan(32°)

If d = 7 meters, we get:

h ≈ 4 meters

The boy is approximately 4 meters above the ground.

29. What sketching pencil point are recommended for sketching?​


Answer:

The best pencils for drawing and sketching are a HB, 2B, 6B and 9B. Far better than buying a whole set of pencils, most of which will never be used.

Explanation:

So the short answer is, an HB Pencil is generally lighter than a 2B pencil because a 2B pencil is softer and will, therefore, leave more graphite on the drawing surface with each line than the HB pencil.
