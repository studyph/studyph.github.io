1. You are told buy some foods in the market you walk 12 m east from home 12 m north 6 m west 6 m south 6 m west and finally 8 m north to reach the market


Based on the given information, here is the summary of the directions taken to reach the market:

Walk 12 m east from home

Walk 12 m north

Walk 6 m west

Walk 6 m south

Walk 6 m west

Walk 8 m north

To determine the total distance traveled, we can add up the distances traveled in each direction:

Distance traveled east: 12 m

Distance traveled north: 12 m + 8 m = 20 m

Distance traveled west: 6 m + 6 m = 12 m

Distance traveled south: 6 m

The total distance traveled is therefore:

12 m + 20 m + 12 m + 6 m = 50 meters

So, you walked a total of 50 meters to reach the market.

3. Suppose you are to buy some foods in the market.You walked 12 m East from home,12 m North,6 m West,6 m South,6 m West ,and finally 8 m North to reach the market.​


Based on the information provided, you walked in a zigzag pattern to reach the market. Here is a summary of the directions you took:

Walked 12 m East from home

Walked 12 m North

Walked 6 m West

Walked 6 m South

Walked 6 m West

Walked 8 m North to reach the market

To return home, you can follow the same path in reverse:

Walk 8 m South from the market

Walk 6 m East

Walk 6 m North

Walk 6 m East

Walk 12 m South

Walk 12 m West to return home

Note that you may need to adjust your route based on obstacles or other factors that may have changed since you walked to the market.

Answer:

Based on the given directions, we can draw a diagram to visualize the path:

      8m N

        |

6m W --+-- 6m W

        |

        |

6m S --+-- Market

        |

       12m N

        |

       12m E

        |

       Home

Starting from home, we first walked 12 meters East, then 12 meters North to reach a point that is 12 meters away from home in the North-East direction. Then we walked 6 meters West, 6 meters South, and 6 meters West again, which brought us to a point that is 12 meters away from home in the South-West direction. Finally, we walked 8 meters North to reach the market.

Therefore, the total distance we walked is the sum of the distances in each direction, which is:

12 + 12 + 6 + 6 + 6 + 8 = 50 meters

So we walked a total of 50 meters to reach the market.
