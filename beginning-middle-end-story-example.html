1. example of story with beginning middle and end​


Answer:

Title: The Lost Key

Beginning: Sara arrived home from work and reached into her purse to grab her house key, but it wasn't there. She checked every pocket in her purse, but the key was nowhere to be found. She started to panic, realizing she must have lost it somewhere.

Middle: Sara retraced her steps from work to home, but the key was nowhere to be found. She asked the security guard at work, but he hadn't seen it either. Sara spent the entire evening looking for the key, but she had no luck.

As night fell, Sara was getting desperate. She had no way to enter her home and had no spare key. She started to feel like she might have to spend the night outside when her neighbor, Mr. Johnson, approached her. "Is everything alright, Sara?" he asked.

Sara explained her situation, and Mr. Johnson offered to help. He had a spare key to Sara's house, and he kindly offered to let her stay at his place for the night. Sara was incredibly grateful for his kindness and accepted the offer.

End: The next day, Sara found her lost key in the bottom of her purse, tucked away in a corner. She couldn't believe it had been there the whole time. Sara went over to Mr. Johnson's to thank him for his help and offer to pay him back for his kindness. Mr. Johnson simply smiled and told her it was no problem at all, and he was just happy to help. From that day on, Sara made sure to keep a spare key in a safe place, just in case she ever lost her key again.
