1. What is the Chemical Formula of Cocoa Butter?​


Answer:

Cocoa butter contains a high proportion of saturated fats as well as monounsaturated oleic acid, which typically occurs in each triglyceride. The predominant triglycerides are POS, SOS, POP, where P = palmitic, O = oleic, and S = stearic acid residues.[6][7][8][9] Cocoa butter, unlike non-fat cocoa solids, contains only traces of caffeine and theobromine.[10]
