20. what is the lcm of 12 and 56?​


Answer:

The LCM of 12 and 56 is 168.

Step-by-step explanation:

Find the prime factorization of 12 :

12 = 2 × 2 × 3

Find the prime factorization of 56

56 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 7

Multiply each factor the greater number of times it occurs in steps i) or ii) above to find the LCM:

LCM = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 7

LCM = 168
