4. 4.A room is 11 m by 9 m. There is a 5 m by 4 m carpet inmiddle.What area of the room is not covered by the carpet?​


[tex]\underline{\mathbb{EQUATION}}:\\\\\text{Area of rectangle} =\text{length}\times\text{breadth}[/tex]

Area not covered by carpet = (area of floor) - (area of carpet)

Area of the floor:

The floor of the room is a rectangle. Given that the dimension of the floor(synonymously room) is [tex](11\times9)m[/tex].

length of the floor = 11 m

breadth of the floor = 9 m

[tex]\text{Area of the floor}= 11\times9=99\;m^2[/tex]

Area of the carpet:

The carpet is a rectangle of dimension [tex](5\times4)m[/tex].

length of the carpet = 5 m

breadth of the carpet = 4 m

[tex]\text{Area of the carpet}= 5\times4=20\;m^2[/tex]

Area not covered by carpet:

Area not covered by carpet = 99-20 = 79 m²
