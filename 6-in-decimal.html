GCF(4,6) = 2

4÷26÷2=23

We know that

23

is the same as

2÷3

Then using

Long Division for 2 divided by 3

gives us

0.66667

Converting our number to a percentage:

0.66667×100

=66.67%

Solution (Decimal):

Here, we simply divide the numerator by the denominator using long division.

In conclusion, 4 ÷ 6 = 0.6666666667.

Solution (Percent):

Again, divide the numerator by the denominator using long division. So the answer again is 0.6666666667. Now, what we're going to do is to multiply it by 100 then just add a percentage sign (%) to it.

= 0.66666666667 × 100 = 66.6666666667
