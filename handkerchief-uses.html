20. solve the following problem1. Jewel bought two handkerchiefs. She was given a change of Php 32.00 when she paid Php 150.00 to the cashier. Let p be the price of a handkerchief. Write an equation that can be used to solve for the price of a handkerchief.​


Answer:

The equation that can be used to solve for the price of a handkerchief is:

2p + 32 = 150

Explanation:

Let p be the price of one handkerchief.

Jewel bought two handkerchiefs, so the cost of these two handkerchiefs is 2p.

She paid Php 150.00 to the cashier and was given a change of Php 32.00, so the total amount of money she gave to the cashier is:

Php 150.00 + Php 32.00 = Php 182.00

Therefore, we can write the equation:

2p + 32 = 182

Simplifying the equation, we get:

2p = 150

Dividing both sides by 2, we get:

p = 75

Therefore, the price of one handkerchief is Php 75.00.

25. How was the impact force lessened by the use of the handkerchief?


In reference to egg volleyball, the impact force was lessened by the use of the handkerchief because the handkerchief served as cushion. It means that there is more surface area in which the egg came into contact with. In addition, since the handkerchief is flexible, it can follow the shape of the egg. In contrast, if the egg dropped on the ground, the impact point would serve as the convergence point of all the energy which would result in the breaking of the egg. Below are other factors as to why the impact was lessened by the use of the handkerchief.

Gravity

Gravity is the force the brings or attracts something with mass towards the center of the earth or toward any other physical body that has mass. Think of it as a giant magnet. Hence, in egg volleyball, as the egg is tossed up, it will always go down because of gravity.  

Acceleration due to Gravity

When an object is falling towards the earth, it will gain momentum despite the presence of air resistance. This kind of acceleration has both direction and magnitude. So you will notice that when the egg is going down, it will accelerate.

Air resistance

This is the force that slows things down as they pass through air because of drag. Drag is the force that acts opposite to the motion of any object, thereby causing it to decelerate.

29. tina has 1/2 meter of cloth to be made into handkerchiefs how many handkerchiefs can she make if she uses 1/8 m for each?​


PROBLEM

Tina has 1/2 meter of cloth to be made into handkerchiefs how many handkerchiefs can she make if she uses 1/8 m for each?

ANSWER

Tina would be able to make 4 handkerchiefs.

SOLUTION

We need to divide the total length (1/2 meter) of cloth to length that Tina will use (1/8 meter) for every handkerchief

[tex] \frac{1}{2} \div \frac{1}{8} \\ = \frac{1}{2} \times 8 \\ = \frac{8}{2} \\ = 4[/tex]

Therefore, Tina would be able to make 4 handkerchiefs.
