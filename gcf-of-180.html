1. Find the GCF of 180 and 216​


Answer:

the answer to your question is 36

Answer:

36

Step-by-step explanation:

Find the prime factorization of 180

180 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 5

Find the prime factorization of 216

216 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 3

To find the GCF, multiply all the prime factors common to both numbers:

Therefore, GCF = 2 × 2 × 3 × 3

GCF = 36
