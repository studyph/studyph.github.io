15. how to use triangle ruler?


Start by locating the scale on the drawing's legend, and then find the corresponding side on the triangular ruler. Line up the "0" mark with the beginning of the line on the drawing, read the major mark at the other end of the line, and add minor marks as tenths. Multiply the total by 10 to get the actual reading.
