29. The diagonals of a kite measure 12cm 8cm. What is the area of the kite? ​


Step-by-step explanation:

The area of a kite can be calculated using the formula:

Area = (d1 x d2) / 2

where d1 and d2 are the lengths of the diagonals.

In this case, the diagonals measure 12cm and 8cm. So, we can plug these values into the formula to get:

Area = (12cm x 8cm) / 2

Area = 96cm² / 2

Area = 48cm²

Therefore, the area of the kite is 48 square centimeters.
