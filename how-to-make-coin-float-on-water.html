16. Why does ship/boat float and a 10 centavos coin sink in water?​


Ship/boats float because they are made of materials that are less dense than water, such as wood or steel. The air inside the boat also helps it to stay afloat. A 10 centavos coin sinks in water because it is made of metal, which is denser than water and therefore heavier.

18. what makes the water hyacinth float on water? how about water lettuce ​


Answer:

Water hyacinth has a special type of parenchyma tissue which is known as the aerenchyma. Aerenchyma is the spongy tissue that has air spaces or channels where the air is trapped, especially in the stem of the hyacinth. The stem part becomes buoyant and due to this, the water hyacinth floats on the surface of the water.
