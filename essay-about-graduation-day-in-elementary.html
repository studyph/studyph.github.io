14. how to cook malunggay pancake? please give answers i want to graduate elementary ​


Answer:

Malunggay Pancake {Moringa Pancake}

You can only mix the pancake mixture and malunggay leaves when you are about to cook. This will prevent the pancake from the unpleasant smell of the leaves.Do not soak malunggay leaves to pancake mixture for more than 30 minutes to preserve the vanilla flavor of your pancake.

Ingredients

1 pack of maya pancake mix

1 cup fresh malunggay leaves, chopped

Follow instruction in the packaging.

Add malunggay right before cooking.

Directions

HERE is the secret to have a palatable pancake; no taste of malunggay and no smell of leaves:

Mix your old pancake recipe in a separate container. You can have any kind of the usual pancake mixture. Have your own way.

Chop fresh malunggay leaves, then set aside on a separate container.

DO NOT MIX MALUNGGAY AND PANCAKE MIXTURE IMMEDIATELY.

Prepare for cooking.

30. elementary essay about gender equality​


Explanation:

Gender equality is the principle of treating individuals of all genders fairly and equitably. It is the belief that all genders deserve the same rights, opportunities, and treatment regardless of their gender identity. Gender equality is important because it ensures that all people have the opportunity to reach their full potential and live a fulfilling life.

In the past, society has been unequal and unfair towards women and other marginalized genders. Women have been denied the same opportunities as men in education, employment, and politics. They have also been paid less than men for the same work, faced harassment and violence, and have not been represented fairly in positions of power. This discrimination has also affected people who identify as non-binary or transgender, who have been denied their rights and freedoms.

Gender equality seeks to challenge these unfair practices and create a world where all genders are treated equally. This means that all individuals, regardless of gender, should have access to the same education, job opportunities, and political representation. It also means that gender-based violence and discrimination should be eliminated.

To achieve gender equality, we need to change the way we think about gender roles and stereotypes. We need to recognize that everyone has the right to express their gender identity and be accepted for who they are. We need to teach our children about gender equality from an early age and challenge any sexist attitudes and behaviors we encounter.

In conclusion, gender equality is an essential aspect of a just and fair society. It is not only a human right, but it is also crucial for social and economic development. Everyone should be treated equally, regardless of their gender identity. By promoting gender equality, we can create a better world for ourselves and for future generations
