5. 15 g of sodium chloride is dissolved in 225 grams of water what is the mass percent of sodium chloride in the solution​


Answer:

mass percent = 6.25%

Explanation:

Solving for the mass of the solution

mass solution = mass solute + mass solvent

mass solution = 15 g + 225 g

mass solution = 240 g

Solving for the mass percent

mass percent = (mass solute / mass solution) × 100

mass percent = (15 g / 240 g) × 100

mass percent = 6.25%
