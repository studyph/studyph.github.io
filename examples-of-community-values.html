9. base on your personal experience or observations in your community, give examples of claims of fact, value and policythat you may have observed or used in the ccommunit. claims of factclaims of valueclaims of policy​


Explanation:

Claims of fact are statements that are verifiable and can be either true or false. Here are some examples:

- The total population of our city is 100,000.

- The number of COVID-19 cases in our community has decreased by 20% over the last month.

- The literacy rate in our town is 98%.

Claims of value express personal beliefs or opinions about whether something is right or wrong, good or bad. Here are some examples:

- It is important for the community to invest in education and lifelong learning.

- Art and culture are important for the development of a healthy and vibrant community.

- Protecting the environment and natural resources is crucial for future generations.

Claims of policy propose a course of action that should be taken to address a particular issue or problem. Here are some examples:

- The local government should increase funding for public transportation to reduce traffic congestion and air pollution.

- The community should implement a recycling program to reduce waste and promote sustainability.

- The school board should implement a policy to promote inclusion and diversity in schools.
