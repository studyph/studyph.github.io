4. what will be the acceleration of the soccer ball that rolls in a field which is 500 g and has three newton to the left?___________pa help po​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The acceleration of an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{a = \frac{F}{m}}[/tex]

where:

a = acceleration

F = force

m = mass

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the acceleration

Note: 500 g = 0.5 kg

[tex]a = \dfrac{F}{m}[/tex]

[tex]a = \dfrac{\text{3 N}}{\text{0.5 kg}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{a = \text{6 m/}\text{s}^{2}}[/tex]

Therefore, the acceleration of the soccer ball is 6 m/s².

27. An object with a mass of 2300 g has a force of 6.5 newtons applied to it. What is the resulting acceleration of the object?​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The force acting on an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{F = ma}[/tex]

where:

F = force

m = mass

a = acceleration

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for a

Note: 2,300 g = 2.3 kg

F = ma

[tex]\dfrac{F}{m} = \dfrac{ma}{m}[/tex]

[tex]a = \dfrac{F}{m}[/tex]

[tex]a = \dfrac{\text{6.5 N}}{\text{2.3 kg}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{a = \text{2.83 m/}\text{s}^{2}}[/tex]

Therefore, the acceleration of the object is 2.83 m/s².

30. calculate the work done by a 2.45 newton force pushing a 400 g sandwich across a table 0.75 m wide​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The work done by an object exerting a force F at a distance d is given by

[tex]\boxed{W = Fd}[/tex]

where:

W = work

F = force

d = distance

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for W

W = Fd

W = (2.45 N)(0.75 m)

[tex]\boxed{W = \text{1.8375 J}}[/tex]

Therefore, the work done is 1.8375 J.
