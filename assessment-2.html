Answer:

Formative assessment

Formative assessment, formative evaluation, formative feedback, or assessment for learning, including diagnostic testing, is a range of formal and informal assessment procedures conducted by teachers during the learning process in order to modify teaching and learning activities to improve student attainment.

Placement Assessment

Placement assessments are used to “place” students into a course, course level, or academic program. For example, an assessment may be used to determine whether a student is ready for Algebra I or a higher-level algebra course, such as an honors-level course.

Diagnostic assessment

A diagnostic assessment is a form of pre-assessment where teachers can evaluate students' strengths, weaknesses, knowledge and skills before their instruction. ... A diagnostic assessment refers to an assignment written at the beginning and end of a course.

Complex-Performance Assessment

A complex Performance assessment is the one that requires a rubric and an expert examiner. Example items for complex assessment include thesis, funding proposal, etc. The complexity of assessment is due to the format implicitness.

26. 1.Why is self-assessment important? 2.What makes peer assessment important?​


Answer:

1.) Self-assessment is important to us students because it can provide insight and can help us to determine our understanding of the certain task or activity.

2.) Because It can help the students develop lifelong skills in assessing and providing feedback to others, and also equips them with skills to self-assess and improve their own work.

Explanation:

What is the purpose of self assessment?

The purpose of self-assessment is to help the individual know the extent of his abilities and to improve upon them without the need of a performance appraiser.

Why is peer assessment important?

Peer assessment or peer review provides a structured learning process for students to critique and provide feedback to each other on their work. It helps students develop lifelong skills in assessing and providing feedback to others, and also equips them with skills to self-assess and improve their own work.
