12. How can we write into equation the statement "the cost (c) of chicken varies directly as it's weight(w)"please help me​


• Problem:

How can we write into equation the statement "the cost (c) of chicken varies directly as it's weight(w)"?

• Answer:

If a situation produces pairs of numbers in which their ratio is constant, then there is a direct variation. This simply means that if there's an increase in c, there will be an increase in w also. Similarly a decrease in c causes a decrease in w.

Thus, the equation is

[tex] \huge \boxed{ \tt c = kw}[/tex]

In which k is the constant and c varies directly as w.

Answer:

9 men wil be able to paint in 10 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

18 hour for 5 people. 1 hour for 5x18 people . 10 hour for 5x18÷10=9 people

• Problem:

Word problem: If 5 men can paint a house in 18 hours, how many men will be able to paint in 10 hours?

• Solution:

We can solve this by applying what we learn in ratio and proportion.

[tex] \tt5:18 = x:10[/tex]

To find the value of the unknown, multiply 18 by 5 and then divide the answer by 10.

[tex] \tt5 \times 18 = 90 \div 10 = 9[/tex]

In fraction form,

[tex] \tt x = \frac{18 \times 5}{10} \\ \tt x= \frac{90}{10} = 9[/tex]

Thus, the answer is 9.

They are indirectly proportional with each other, or we call it inverse relationship between quantities.

• Conclusion:

If 5 men can paint a house in 18 hours, then 9 men can paint the same house in 10 hours.
