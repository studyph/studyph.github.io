11. Which sentence uses the pronoun "her" as a direct object?a. The teacher gave the assignment to herb. The teacher gave her the assignmentc. The teacher called her​Help please


Answer:

b. The teacher gave her the assignment

Explanation:

diko alam, feel ko lang yan yung sagot.

Answer:

The answer is both B and C.

A direct object is always located after the verb.  The basic construction works like this: Subject + Verb + Who or What. In this case, "gave" and "called" are verbs both followed by "her" which makes it a direct object. The "her" in A is an indirect object, where "the assignment" is instead the direct object.

23. Complete the following sentences using the infinitive as the object. The teacher reminds us ___________


Infinitives

Answer:

Using Infinitives is pretty simple as long as the student understands its uses and its role in a sentence. While also not forgetting to watch out for wrong grammars and such, to write Infinitive Verbs you combine the word “To” and the base form of the verb you are going to use in a sentence. The Infinitive Verb can have various of uses but not that complex to be classified as a hard topic.

Examples of Infinitives:

To mend To sew To practice To perform To compose
