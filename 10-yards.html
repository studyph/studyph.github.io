18. if it takes 5/10 yards to make a dress,then how many yards will it takes to make 10 dresses


Answer:

5 yards

Explanation :

5/10 plus another 5/10 it'll become 10/10 or 1 whole.. Continue the process and you'll get 5 whole, meaning 5 yards will be used to create 10 dresses

Answer:

5/10×10=5

Calculation steps:

5/10×10= 510×10=

 5 × 1010 × 1= 5010

= 50 ÷ 1010 ÷ 10= 5

The result is 5

Step-by-step explanation:

Further explanation

For the problem:

510×10 = ?

Convert any mixed numbers to fractions:

10=10

Multiply all of the numerators by each other. Do the same with the denominators and form a new fraction with these values:

5 × 1010 × 1=5010

The Greatest Common Factor (GCF) of 50 and 10 is 10. The result can be simplified by dividing both the numerator and denominator by 10.

5010=50 ÷ 1010 ÷ 10=5

The result is 5
