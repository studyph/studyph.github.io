25. what is the percent by mass concertration of a soup solution that contains 10 grams of salt in 150 grams of waterplease pakisagot please ​


Given: 10g NaCl

150g H2O

Unknown: % by mass = ?

Formula: %M = Msol / Mtot × 100%

where in, %M = percentage by mass

Msol = mass of solute

Mtot = total mass

Note: I don't know what's the real variables for this formula, so I decided to create my own.

Solution:

Mtot = mass of solute + mass of solvent

= 10g NaCl + 150g H20

= 160g

%M = Msol / Mtot × 100%

= 10g NaCl / 160g × 100%

= 0.0625g × 100%

= 6.25%

Final Answer: The mass percentage of the solution is 6.25%
