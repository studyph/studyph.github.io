ℎ = 6

Answer:

h=6

Step-by-step explanation:

Since Ben bought 4 packs of balloon, and the total quantity of balloon is 24, we are to solve for how many balloons are there inside each pack.

4h=24

Because 4 packs times (multiplication) h (which represents how many balloons there are inside), is equal to 24.

Applying the process in linear equation:

4h/4=24/4

Cancel the 4 in both denominator and numerator (4h/4).

Then divide 24 by 4, the result is 6.

so h=6.
