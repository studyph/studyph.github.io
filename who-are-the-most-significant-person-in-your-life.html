It is a time when individuals are transitioning from childhood to adulthood, which can bring about physical, emotional, and psychological changes. Therefore, understanding and meeting the challenges and demands of adolescence is essential for healthy development and successful transition into adulthood. Some of these challenges include navigating changes in the body, developing a sense of identity, establishing healthy relationships, making decisions about education and career paths, and dealing with peer pressure and societal expectations.

2. I can say that some challenges that adolescents commonly face include managing emotions, coping with stress, establishing independence while still relying on caregivers, dealing with social media and technology use, and navigating romantic relationships.
