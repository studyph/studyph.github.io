A list of Actions that show SelflessnessGiving someone your seat on the bus/train.Letting your siblings take the bigger piece of that food you wanted to share.Allotting time in your schedule to help your parents with house-work.Giving gifts to your friends.Helping elderly people cross the street.Carrying someone else's heavy baggage.Saying sorry after a fight.Listening and waiting for your turn to talk.Donating money or old items that can still be used.Giving tips to the waiter/waitress after a meal.
