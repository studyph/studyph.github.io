1. What sketching pencil point are recommended for sketching?​


Answer:

The best pencils for drawing and sketching are a HB, 2B, 6B and 9B. Far better than buying a whole set of pencils, most of which will never be used.

Explanation:

So the short answer is, an HB Pencil is generally lighter than a 2B pencil because a 2B pencil is softer and will, therefore, leave more graphite on the drawing surface with each line than the HB pencil.

4. What are the three common points of pencil used in freehand sketching?​


Answer:

Pencil points may be sharpened into three different shapes: the conical, the chisel, and the elliptical. For sketching purposes, the first two are recommended. The conical pointed soft pencil can make different thicknesses of lines by varying the pressure of the pencil against the paper surface.
