5. A cube has width of 5 cm. What is the volume of the cube?

Explanation and Answer:

[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c}Length = 5cm\\Width = 5cm\\Height = 5cm\\\\V= 5cm\:×\:5cm\:×\:5cm =\\ 125\:cm \end{array}}[/tex]

Therefore the volume will be 125cm.

6. What is the volume of a figure with that is 5 inches wide, 8 inch and 7 inches long?

Explanation and Answer:

V = L x W x HV = 8 in x 7 in x 7 inV = 392 in³Therefore the answer will be 392 in³

7. Cube has height of 11 cm. What is the volume of the cube?

Explanation and Answer:

[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c}Length = 11cm\\Width = 11cm\\Height = 11cm\\\\V=11cm\:×\:11cm\:×\:11cm \\V=1331 \:cm \end{array}}[/tex]

Therefore the volume will be 1331 cm.

8. A rectangular prism has a width of 4 cm, a height of 8 cm and of 7 cm. What is the volume of the prism?

Explanation and Answer:

[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c}Length = 7cm\\Width = 4cm\\Height = 8cm\\Volume=?\\\\V=7cm\:×\:4cm\:×\:8cm \\V=200\:cm \end{array}}[/tex]

Therefore the volume is 200 cm

9. What is the volume of a figure that is 6 inches wide, 4 inches to 5 inches long?

V = 6 × 4 × 5V = 72 in³

10. What is the volume of a figure that is 9 inches wide, 7 inches 8 inches long?​

V = 9in × 7in × 8V = 504 in³
